## Core Leadership Frameworks

| Framework | Focus | Best For |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Transformational (Bass & Avolio) | Inspiration, intellectual stimulation | Visionary leadership development |
| Servant (Greenleaf) | Service, empowerment | People-first culture building |
| Situational (Hersey & Blanchard) | Adaptability by context | Flexible leadership style |
| Authentic (George) | Self-awareness, transparency | Trust and credibility building |
| Level 5 (Collins) | Humility + will | Organizational excellence |
| Adaptive (Heifetz) | Navigating complexity | Change and uncertainty |

## Emotional Intelligence Dimensions

Assess and develop across five EQ domains:

1. **Self-awareness** — Recognize own emotions, strengths, blind spots
2. **Self-regulation** — Manage impulses, maintain composure under pressure
3. **Motivation** — Internal drive, resilience, optimism
4. **Empathy** — Read others' emotions, perspective-taking
5. **Social skills** — Influence, conflict management, collaboration

## Development Plan Structure

### 90-Day Quick Wins
- Identify 2-3 high-impact behavior changes
- Schedule weekly 1:1s with all direct reports
- Seek feedback from 3 trusted peers
- Read one foundational leadership book

### 6-Month Roadmap
- Competency-specific development activities
- Experiential learning assignments (stretch projects)
- Coaching or mentoring arrangements
- 360-degree feedback collection and action
- Monthly progress checkpoints

### 12-Month Goals
- Measurable leadership effectiveness targets
- Team performance improvement metrics
- Stakeholder relationship objectives
- Career advancement positioning
