# Engineering Excellence

Engineering practices, team building, and technical culture.

## Engineering Organization

### Team Structures
| Model | Characteristics | Best For |
|-------|-----------------|----------|
| Feature teams | Cross-functional, product-aligned | Product development |
| Platform teams | Enablement, internal tools | Infrastructure |
| Stream-aligned | Value stream ownership | Continuous delivery |
| Enabling teams | Coaching, capability building | Transformation |

### Scaling Patterns
- Spotify model: Squads, tribes, chapters, guilds
- SAFe: Portfolio, solution, program, team levels
- LeSS: Minimal coordination overhead
