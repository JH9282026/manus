# Technology Strategy

Frameworks for technology vision, roadmap, and portfolio management.

---

## Strategic Planning Process

### 1. Business Context
- Corporate strategy and goals
- Industry trends and disruption
- Competitive technology landscape
- Customer technology expectations

### 2. Current State Assessment
- Technology inventory and health
- Capability maturity analysis
- Technical debt assessment
- Skill gap identification

### 3. Future State Vision
- Target architecture
- Capability roadmap
- Innovation priorities
- Partnership strategy

### 4. Roadmap Development
- Initiative prioritization
- Resource planning
- Milestone definition
- Risk management

## Technology Portfolio Management

### Portfolio Categories
| Category | Characteristics | Action |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| Invest | Strategic, growing | Increase funding |
| Maintain | Stable, essential | Sustain current level |
| Migrate | Legacy, needed | Plan transition |
| Retire | Obsolete, costly | Phase out |

### Investment Criteria
- Strategic alignment score
- Business value potential
- Technical risk level
- Implementation complexity
- Resource requirements
