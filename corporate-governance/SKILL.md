# Corporate Governance

Execute sophisticated governance functions including board administration, compliance monitoring, fiduciary duty management, and stakeholder accountability mechanisms.

## Overview

Ensure organizations maintain robust governance structures that satisfy regulatory requirements, align management and shareholder interests, mitigate risk, and support sustainable long-term value creation.

## Board Meeting Administration

### Pre-Meeting (7-10 days before)
1. Finalize agenda with CEO/Chairman
2. Distribute board materials
3. Confirm attendance and logistics
4. Brief presenters on time allocations

### During Meeting
1. Record attendance and quorum
2. Document discussions and decisions
3. Capture action items with owners
4. Manage executive sessions

### Post-Meeting
1. Draft minutes within 5 business days
2. Circulate for review
3. Track action item completion
4. Prepare consent resolutions

## Committee Structure

| Committee | Key Responsibilities | Independence |
|-----------|---------------------|---------------|
| Audit | Financial oversight, controls, auditor | 100% independent |
| Compensation | Executive pay, incentives, equity | 100% independent |
| Nominating/Governance | Director selection, governance | 100% independent |
| Risk | Enterprise risk oversight | Varies |

## Annual Governance Calendar

- **Q1**: Board self-evaluation, governance guidelines review
- **Q2**: Annual meeting, director elections
- **Q3**: Committee charter reviews, succession planning
- **Q4**: CEO evaluation, compensation planning
