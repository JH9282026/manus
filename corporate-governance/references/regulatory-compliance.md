# Regulatory Compliance

Governance requirements under SEC rules and stock exchange standards.

---

## SEC Requirements

### Proxy Statement (Schedule 14A)
- Director backgrounds and qualifications
- Board leadership structure
- Risk oversight description
- Committee composition and charters
- Director independence determinations
- Executive compensation disclosure

### Form 8-K Triggers
- Director resignation/appointment
- Executive officer changes
- Auditor changes
- Amendments to charter/bylaws
- Code of ethics waivers

### Section 16 Compliance
- Forms 3, 4, 5 filings
- Insider trading policies
- Trading windows
- Pre-clearance procedures

---

## NYSE Listed Company Manual

### Board Requirements
- Majority independent directors
- Regular executive sessions
- Corporate governance guidelines
- Code of business conduct

### Committee Requirements
- Audit committee charter and requirements
- Compensation committee charter
- Nominating committee charter
- Annual committee self-evaluation

---

## NASDAQ Listing Rules

### Similar to NYSE with variations:
- Majority independent board
- Independent committee requirements
- Code of conduct
- Audit committee composition

---

## State Law (Delaware)

### Fiduciary Duties
- Duty of Care: Informed decision-making
- Duty of Loyalty: Company interests first
- Duty of Good Faith: Honest purpose

### Business Judgment Rule
Protection when directors:
- Act in good faith
- Are reasonably informed
- Believe action is in company's best interest
