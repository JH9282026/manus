# Board Administration

Best practices for board meeting management and director matters.

---

## Meeting Preparation

### Agenda Development
1. Standing items (minutes approval, committee reports)
2. Strategic discussion topics
3. Action items requiring board approval
4. Executive session
5. Time allocations

### Board Materials
- Distribute 7-10 days in advance
- Include agenda, supporting documents, presentations
- Use board portal for secure distribution
- Highlight items requiring action

---

## Meeting Minutes

### Content Requirements
- Date, time, location
- Directors present and absent
- Quorum confirmation
- Matters discussed (summary level)
- Resolutions adopted (verbatim)
- Voting results
- Executive session notation

### Best Practices
- Document process, not debate
- Avoid attributing statements to individuals
- Reference materials considered
- Clear action items with owners

---

## Director Independence

### NYSE Standards
Director is NOT independent if:
- Employee of company in last 3 years
- Family member is executive officer
- Received >$120K compensation (other than board fees)
- Affiliated with auditor in last 3 years
- Executive of another company with interlocking compensation

### Documentation
- Annual questionnaire
- Relationship analysis
- Board determination
- Proxy disclosure

---

## Board Evaluation

### Components
- Full board effectiveness
- Committee performance
- Individual director assessment
- Chair/Lead Director evaluation

### Process
1. Design evaluation questionnaire
2. Collect responses (anonymous)
3. Synthesize findings
4. Report to board
5. Develop improvement actions
