# Committee Governance

Committee structure, charters, and oversight responsibilities.

---

## Audit Committee

### Responsibilities
- Financial statement oversight
- Internal control assessment
- External auditor relationship
- Whistleblower procedures
- Risk oversight (financial)

### Independence Requirements
- 100% independent directors
- Financial literacy required
- At least one "audit committee financial expert"

### Key Activities
- Quarterly earnings review
- Annual audit planning
- Internal audit oversight
- Related party transaction review

---

## Compensation Committee

### Responsibilities
- CEO compensation determination
- Executive compensation approval
- Incentive plan oversight
- Equity grant administration
- Say-on-Pay preparation

### Independence Requirements
- 100% independent directors
- "Non-employee director" for SEC purposes
- "Outside director" for 162(m)

### Key Activities
- Annual CEO evaluation
- Peer group benchmarking
- Incentive plan design
- CD&A disclosure review

---

## Nominating/Governance Committee

### Responsibilities
- Director nomination
- Board composition planning
- Governance guidelines oversight
- Board evaluation process
- Succession planning

### Key Activities
- Director search and selection
- Annual governance review
- Board refreshment planning
- Shareholder engagement on governance

---

## Charter Elements

### Required Content
1. Purpose and scope
2. Composition and qualifications
3. Authority and responsibilities
4. Meeting requirements
5. Resources and access
6. Performance evaluation
7. Charter review process
