# Infographic Marketing & Distribution

Strategically distribute infographics to maximize reach, engagement, backlinks, and lead generation.

## Overview

This skill covers pre-launch optimization, multi-channel distribution, outreach, repurposing, and ROI measurement for infographic marketing campaigns.

## Channel Selection

| Goal | Channel | Strategy | Reference |
|------|---------|----------|-----------|
| SEO/backlinks | Blog + outreach | Embed in post, pitch publishers | `/references/distribution-channels.md` |
| Social engagement | Platform posts | Resize per platform, carousels | `/references/distribution-channels.md` |

| Authority | PR/influencer | Pitch journalists, exclusives | `/references/seo-measurement.md` |
| Ongoing traffic | Repurposing | Slides, video, social posts | `/references/seo-measurement.md` |

## Pre-Launch Checklist
1. Keyword-rich file name
2. Compress under 1MB
3. Platform-specific dimensions
4. Alt text with primary keyword
5. Embed code for sharing
6. Landing page with 500+ words context
7. Schema markup (ImageObject)
