# Product Photography

Create professional product images for e-commerce, catalogs, and marketing across all platforms.

## Overview

Product photography captures items for commercial sale, focusing on accurate representation, appealing presentation, and technical quality. This skill covers lighting techniques, camera settings, background options, styling, and post-processing for e-commerce and marketing applications.

## White Background Photography

### Setup

**Equipment:**
- Camera on tripod
- White background (seamless paper, PVC board, fabric)
- 2-3 lights (key, fill, background)
- Reflectors for fill light

**Lighting:**
- Key light: 45\u00b0 from product, softbox
- Fill light: Opposite side, lower power
- Background lights: Illuminate background to pure white

### Camera Settings

- ISO: 100-200 (low noise)
- Aperture: f/8-f/11 (sharp focus, depth of field)
- Shutter speed: Adjust for proper exposure
- White balance: Manual (5500K or custom)
- Shoot RAW for flexibility

## Product Types

| Product Type | Lighting Approach | Considerations |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Reflective (jewelry, glass) | Large, soft sources; control reflections | Avoid harsh reflections, use diffusion |
| Textured (fabric, wood) | Side lighting for texture emphasis | Show tactile details |
| Dark products | More light power, white reflectors | Prevent underexposure |
| Light products | Controlled lighting, avoid overexposure | Maintain detail in highlights |
| Transparent | Backlighting for transparency | Show material properties |

## Styling and Composition

**Product Placement:**
- Center in frame with 10-15% margin
- Eye-level or slightly above
- Multiple angles (front, side, top, detail)

**Styling:**
- Clean and dust-free
- Props for context (minimal, not distracting)
- Lifestyle shots for use-case demonstration

## Post-Processing

**Essential Edits:**
- Background cleanup (pure white: RGB 255,255,255)
- Color correction for accuracy
- Remove dust and imperfections
- Sharpen for detail
- Resize for platform requirements

**Platform Specifications:**
- Amazon: 1000px minimum, white background
- Shopify: 2048x2048px recommended
- Instagram: 1080x1080px square
