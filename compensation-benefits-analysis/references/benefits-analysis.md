# Benefits Program Analysis

Methodology for benefits competitive analysis and cost optimization.

---

## Benefits Inventory

### Health & Welfare
- Medical (PPO, HMO, HDHP)
- Dental and vision
- Life and disability insurance
- FSA/HSA accounts
- EAP programs

### Retirement
- 401(k) with match
- Pension plans (if applicable)
- Deferred compensation

### Time Off
- PTO/vacation
- Sick leave
- Holidays
- Parental leave

### Perquisites
- Wellness programs
- Tuition reimbursement
- Commuter benefits
- Remote work stipends

---

## Competitive Analysis

### Benchmarking Sources
- SHRM Benefits Survey
- Mercer National Survey
- Kaiser Family Foundation
- Industry-specific surveys

### Comparison Metrics
- Employer contribution percentage
- Deductible and out-of-pocket limits
- Coverage breadth
- Employee cost share

---

## Cost Projection Modeling

### Trend Factors
- Medical: 6-8% annual trend
- Dental: 3-4% annual trend
- Pharmacy: 8-10% annual trend

### Scenario Analysis
- Base case: Current plan design
- High deductible shift
- Contribution strategy changes
- Network narrowing

---

## Optimization Strategies

### Cost Reduction
- Plan design changes (higher deductibles)
- Contribution strategy shifts
- Vendor renegotiation
- Wellness ROI programs

### Value Enhancement
- Voluntary benefits (no cost to employer)
- Financial wellness programs
- Flexible work arrangements
- Recognition programs
