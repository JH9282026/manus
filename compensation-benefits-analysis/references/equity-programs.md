# Equity Compensation Programs

Design and administration of stock-based compensation.

---

## Equity Vehicle Comparison

| Vehicle | Tax Event | Risk | Upside |
|---------|-----------|------|--------|
| Stock Options | Exercise | Lower (no downside) | Full appreciation |
| RSUs | Vesting | Higher (value delivery) | Full value |
| PSUs | Vesting | Performance-contingent | Performance multiplier |
| ESPP | Purchase | Moderate | 15% discount typical |

---

## Grant Guidelines

### By Level
- Individual Contributor: 0.01-0.05% (early stage)
- Senior IC: 0.05-0.15%
- Manager: 0.1-0.25%
- Director: 0.25-0.5%
- VP: 0.5-1.5%
- C-Level: 1-3%+

### Grant Sizing
- Market data (Option Impact, Carta)
- Value-based (target $ value)
- Percentage of outstanding

---

## Valuation Methods

### Black-Scholes Model
Inputs:
- Current stock price
- Strike price
- Time to expiration
- Risk-free rate
- Volatility assumption

### 409A Valuation
- Required for private companies
- Independent appraisal
- Updated with material events
- Safe harbor protection

## Administration

### Vesting Tracking
- Vesting schedules by grant
- Termination treatment
- Change of control provisions

### Dilution Management
- Share pool sizing
- Annual burn rate (grants/shares outstanding)
- Overhang (outstanding + available/shares outstanding)
- Refresh grant philosophy

### Tax Considerations
- ISOs vs. NSOs
- 83(b) elections
- AMT implications
- International equity taxation
