# Equity Compensation Programs

Design and manage stock-based compensation programs.

---

## Equity Program Types

### Stock Options

**Incentive Stock Options (ISO):**
- Tax-advantaged for employees (no tax at exercise if held 2 years from grant, 1 year from exercise)
- $100K annual limit on exercisable ISOs
- Must be employees (not contractors)
- Exercise price must equal FMV at grant

**Non-Qualified Stock Options (NSO):**
- Ordinary income tax at exercise on spread
- No annual limits
- Available to employees, contractors, advisors
- More flexible design

### Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)

- Employee receives shares on vesting (no purchase required)
- Taxed as ordinary income at vesting based on share FMV
- No exercise price — always has value (unlike options)
- Most common equity vehicle at public and late-stage companies
- Withholding typically via sell-to-cover at vesting

### Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

- Section 423 qualified plan
- Employees purchase shares at discount (up to 15% off lower of offering or purchase price)
- Typical structure: 6-month purchase periods, lookback provision
- Broad-based participation (available to most employees)
- Annual contribution limit: $25,000 in FMV

## Equity Budget Management

### Burn Rate Targets

| Company Stage | Annual Equity Burn Rate | Dilution Range |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-IPO/startup | 3-5% of fully diluted shares | 15-25% total option pool |
| Growth stage | 2-4% annually | Refresh grants + new hires |
| Public — small/mid cap | 1.5-3% annually | ISS guidelines |
| Public — large cap | 0.5-1.5% annually | ISS guidelines |

### Grant Guidelines by Level

| Level | Equity Grant Range | Vesting |
|---|---|---|
| Individual contributor | 0.01-0.10% (startup); $5K-$50K annual value (public) | 4-year standard |
| Senior IC / Lead | 0.05-0.25% (startup); $25K-$150K (public) | 4-year standard |
| Manager / Director | 0.10-0.50% (startup); $50K-$300K (public) | 4-year standard |
| VP | 0.25-1.00% (startup); $150K-$750K (public) | 4-year standard |
| C-suite | 0.50-3.00% (startup); $500K-$5M+ (public) | 4-year, may include performance |
