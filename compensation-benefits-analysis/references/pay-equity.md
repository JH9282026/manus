# Pay Equity Analysis

Conduct rigorous pay equity audits and develop remediation strategies.

---

## Pay Equity Analysis Methodology

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Define scope** — Which employees, pay elements, and protected classes to analyze
2. **Prepare data** — Clean employee data, validate job architecture, code variables
3. **Select model** — Multiple regression (most common) or cohort analysis
4. **Run analysis** — Test for unexplained pay gaps after controlling for legitimate factors
5. **Interpret results** — Identify statistically and practically significant gaps
6. **Root cause analysis** — Investigate sources of identified gaps
7. **Develop remediation** — Design pay adjustments to close unjustified gaps
8. **Implement** — Execute adjustments, update processes to prevent recurrence
9. **Monitor** — Ongoing monitoring at least annually

### Regression Model Specification

**Dependent variable:** Total cash compensation (log-transformed)

**Independent variables (legitimate factors):**
- Job family and level (categorical)
- Geography (cost of labor index or categorical)
- Years of relevant experience
- Time in current role/grade
- Performance rating (if validated and non-discriminatory)
- Education level (if genuinely job-related)
- Relevant certifications

**Test variables (protected characteristics):**
- Gender (binary or multi-category)
- Race/ethnicity
- Age
- Intersectional terms (gender × race) for advanced analysis

### Interpreting Results

| Finding | Statistical Test | Practical Test | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Significant gap ≥ 2% | p < 0.05 | Meaningful dollar difference | Remediate — adjust pay |
| Significant gap < 2% | p < 0.05 | Small dollar difference | Monitor — investigate root cause |
| Not significant | p ≥ 0.05 | N/A | No action — continue monitoring |

## Pay Transparency Compliance

### Emerging Legislation

| Jurisdiction | Key Requirements |
|---|---|
| Colorado | Salary ranges in all job postings |
| New York City | Salary ranges in job postings (4+ employees) |
| California | Salary ranges on request and in postings (15+ employees) |
| Washington | Salary ranges in postings (15+ employees) |
| EU Pay Transparency Directive | Salary ranges in postings, gender pay gap reporting |

### Compliance Preparation

1. Establish formal salary bands for all positions
2. Document compensation philosophy and pay-setting criteria
3. Train managers on pay decision rationale and communication
4. Audit current pay against published ranges
5. Develop pay communication talking points for employees
