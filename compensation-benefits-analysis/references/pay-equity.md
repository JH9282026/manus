# Pay Equity Analysis

Methodology for identifying and remediating pay disparities.

---

## Regression Analysis Framework

### Legitimate Factors (Controls)
- Job level/grade
- Tenure (time in role, company tenure)
- Performance ratings
- Geographic location
- Education (if job-related)
- Relevant experience

### Protected Classes
- Gender
- Race/ethnicity
- Age
- Other jurisdiction-specific categories

---

## Statistical Methodology

### Multiple Regression Model
```
Log(Salary) = β0 + β1(JobLevel) + β2(Tenure) + β3(Performance) + β4(Location) + β5(Gender) + ε
```

### Interpretation
- Coefficient on protected class indicates pay gap
- Statistical significance at p < 0.05
- Practical significance: gaps > 2-3%

### Confidence Levels
- 95% confidence standard
- Consider 90% for smaller populations
- Document methodology choices

---

## Remediation Strategies

### Immediate Remediation
- Adjust salaries to close gaps
- Calculate total remediation budget
- Prioritize by risk level

### Phased Approach
- Spread adjustments over 2-3 cycles
- Target highest-risk gaps first
- Monitor for regression

### Prevention
- Structured salary ranges
- Offer letter calibration
- Manager training
- Regular monitoring
