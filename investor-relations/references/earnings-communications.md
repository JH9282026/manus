# Earnings Communications

## Press Release Structure
1. Headline with key financials
2. Summary table (GAAP + non-GAAP)
3. CEO commentary (2-3 paragraphs)
4. CFO commentary (1-2 paragraphs)
5. Segment results
6. Guidance with safe harbor
7. Non-GAAP reconciliations
8. Call details

## Call Script
- Moderator: safe harbor, intros, format
- CEO: strategic highlights (8-10 min)
- CFO: financial detail (8-10 min)
- Q&A: bridging statement, analyst queue
- Q&A prep: question matrix with responses

## Investor Deck
- Investment thesis (1-2 slides)
- Business model and market (3-4)
- Competitive positioning (2-3)
- Financial performance (3-4)
- Growth strategy (2-3)
- ESG (1-2)
- Appendix: tables, reconciliations
