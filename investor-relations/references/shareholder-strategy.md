# Shareholder Strategy

## Ownership Analysis
- Breakdown by style: growth, value, GARP, index
- Geographic distribution and concentration
- QoQ changes (13F analysis)
- Gaps vs. peers; institutional vs. retail mix

## Investor Targeting
- Gap analysis to find prospects
- Score: style fit, sector expertise, capacity
- Outreach: conferences, roadshows, 1-on-1s

## Activist Response
1. Detect: monitor 13D/13G, unusual trading
2. Assess: review activist playbook
3. Prepare: messaging, board materials, outreach
4. Engage: determine approach
5. Communicate: holding statements, shareholder contact

## Analyst Relations
- Monitor consensus and model assumptions
- Track rating changes and targets
- Facilitate access within Reg FD
- Conduct perception studies

## IR Metrics
| Metric | Measures |
|--------|---------|
| Price vs. peers | Relative performance |
| Volume/liquidity | Market interest |
| Estimate accuracy | Guidance effectiveness |
| Ownership changes | Targeting success |
