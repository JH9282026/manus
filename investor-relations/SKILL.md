# Investor Relations

Manage strategic communications between a public company and its investment community.

## Overview

This skill covers earnings communications, investor presentations, shareholder analysis, analyst relations, regulatory compliance (Reg FD), activist defense, and IR program optimization.

## Activity Selection

| Situation | Activity | Deliverable | Reference |
|-----------|----------|------------|-----------|

| Annual meeting | Proxy Season | Proxy materials | `/references/shareholder-strategy.md` |

## Key Regulations

| Regulation | Requirement | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Reg FD | No selective material disclosure | SEC enforcement |
| 10-K/10-Q/8-K | Timely SEC filings | Delisting risk |
| Insider Trading | No MNPI trading | Criminal prosecution |
