# Amazon Seller Strategy

Optimize Amazon FBA operations for profitability in 2026's evolving marketplace with increased fees and stricter inventory limits.

## Overview

Amazon FBA in 2026 requires strategic adaptation to rising fees, tighter inventory limits, and evolving policies. Success demands comprehensive listing optimization, efficient PPC management, precise inventory forecasting, and strategic pricing to maintain profitability amidst platform changes.

## 2026 FBA Landscape Changes

### Fee Increases and Structure

| Fee Type | 2026 Change | Impact |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Per-unit fulfillment | +$0.08 average | Higher for thin-margin products |
| Small standard ($10-$50) | +$0.25 | Significant for mid-price items |
| High-value (>$50) | +$0.51 | Premium product impact |
| Under $10 | Increased discount | Benefit for low-price items |
| Inbound placement | +$0.05 (single location) | Waived for optimized splits |
| Defect penalty | $0.60 per unit | Labeling/routing errors |

### Inventory Limit Changes

- Storage allowance reduced from 6 months to 5 months of forecasted sales
- Low-Inventory-Level fee calculated at FNSKU level
- Requires 28+ days supply based on 30-day and 90-day rolling averages
- Increased stockout risk during peak seasons (Prime Day, Q4)

### Service Discontinuations

**FBA Prep and Labeling (Ended 2026):**
- Sellers must handle all prep work before shipment
- Options: Internal prep or third-party logistics (3PL) partners
- Includes labeling, poly bagging, bundling, kitting

## Listing Optimization

### Product Title Optimization

**Formula:** Brand + Key Feature + Product Type + Size/Variant

**Best Practices:**
- Primary keyword within first 80 characters
- Include 5-7 high-value keywords naturally
- Avoid keyword stuffing
- Follow category-specific guidelines
- Mobile-optimized (displays first 80 chars)

### Image Optimization

**Requirements:**
- Main image: White background, product fills 85% of frame
- Minimum 1000x1000px (enables zoom)
- Recommended: 2048x2048px for best quality
- 7+ images (30% better conversion)

**Image Types:**
1. Main product image (white background)
2. Lifestyle images (product in use)
3. Infographics (features, benefits)
4. Size/scale comparison
5. Packaging/what's included
6. Detail shots
7. Comparison charts

### Bullet Points Strategy

**Structure:**
- 5 bullet points, 200-250 characters each
- Lead with benefit, follow with feature
- Address common objections
- Include secondary keywords naturally
- Focus on customer outcomes

**Example:**
- ❌ "Made with high-quality materials"
- ✅ "DURABLE CONSTRUCTION - Premium stainless steel withstands daily use for 10+ years, backed by lifetime warranty"

### A+ Content (Enhanced Brand Content)

**Impact:** 5-10% conversion increase

**Elements:**
- Brand story module
- Comparison charts
- Feature highlights with images
- How-to guides
- Customer testimonials
- Product specifications table

## PPC Management

### Campaign Structure

**Tiered Approach:**

1. **Auto Campaigns** (Discovery)
   - Budget: 20% of total PPC
   - Purpose: Keyword discovery
   - Duration: 2-4 weeks
   - Harvest successful keywords for manual campaigns

2. **Manual Exact** (High-Intent)
   - Budget: 40% of total PPC
   - Bid: Aggressive on proven converters
   - Target: Bottom-funnel keywords

3. **Manual Phrase** (Mid-Funnel)
   - Budget: 25% of total PPC
   - Bid: Moderate
   - Target: Related searches

4. **Manual Broad** (Top-Funnel)
   - Budget: 15% of total PPC
   - Bid: Conservative
   - Target: Discovery and reach

### Bid Optimization

**Placement Adjustments:**
- Top of Search: +50-100% (high-intent)
- Product Pages: +20-40% (complementary)
- Rest of Search: Baseline bid

**Day-Parting Strategy:**
- Analyze hourly conversion data
- Increase bids during peak conversion hours
- Reduce bids during low-performance periods
- Typical peak: 7-9 PM local time

### Budget Allocation

**Starting Point:** 10-15% of target revenue

**Optimization Goals:**
- Launch phase: ACoS 30-50% (acceptable)
- Growth phase: ACoS 20-30% (target)
- Mature phase: ACoS 15-20% (optimized)
- TACoS: Keep under 10% of total revenue

## Inventory Management

### Forecasting Formula

**Basic Calculation:**
```
Reorder Point = (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time) + Safety Stock
Safety Stock = Average Daily Sales × Safety Days (7-14)
Order Quantity = (Average Daily Sales × Restock Period) - Current Inventory
```

**Seasonal Adjustments:**
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): 2-3x normal inventory
- Prime Day (July): 1.5-2x normal inventory
- Back-to-School (Aug-Sep): Category-dependent
- Account for 5-month storage limit

### Hybrid Fulfillment Strategy

**FBA + FBM Combination:**
- FBA: Fast-moving, Prime-eligible products
- FBM: Slow-moving, oversized, high-margin items
- 3PL: Prep, overflow storage, multi-channel

**Benefits:**
- 10-20% reduction in total fulfillment costs
- Risk mitigation (platform outages)
- Flexibility during inventory limit constraints
- Better control over margins

## Pricing Strategy

### Dynamic Repricing

**Repricing Rules:**
- Stay within 5-10% of target price
- Never go below minimum profitable price
- Match/beat Buy Box price when possible
- Adjust based on inventory levels (higher when low stock)

**Repricing Tools:**
- RepricerExpress
- Informed.co
- Seller Snap
- BQool

### Margin Protection

**Minimum Price Calculation:**
```
Minimum Price = COGS + FBA Fees + Referral Fee + Target Profit Margin
```

**2026 Fee Considerations:**
- Include new fee structures in calculations
- Account for inbound placement fees
- Factor in prep costs (no longer Amazon-provided)
- Add buffer for potential fee increases

## Account Health Management

### Key Metrics to Monitor

| Metric | Target | Critical Threshold |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Order Defect Rate | <0.5% | >1% (suspension risk) |
| Pre-Fulfillment Cancel Rate | <2.5% | >2.5% |
| Late Shipment Rate | <4% | >4% |
| Valid Tracking Rate | >95% | <95% |
| Return Dissatisfaction Rate | <10% | >10% |

### Reimbursement Management

**2026 Policy:** Reimbursement based on sourcing cost (not selling price)

**Action Items:**
- Maintain accurate COGS data in Seller Central
- Document all costs (product, shipping, prep)
- File claims within 18 months
- Use reimbursement tools (Getida, Refunds Manager)
