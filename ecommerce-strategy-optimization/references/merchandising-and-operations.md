# Digital Merchandising and Operations

## Product Catalog Optimization

### Product Taxonomy
- Organize into logical categories and subcategories
- Limit depth to 3 levels maximum
- Use customer-centric language (not internal jargon)
- Implement faceted navigation for filtering
- Tag products with multiple attributes for cross-discovery

### Search Optimization
- Implement autocomplete with product suggestions
- Handle synonyms and typos gracefully
- Show "Did you mean?" for zero-result searches
- Personalize search results based on browsing history
- Track and optimize for top search queries with no results

### Product Recommendations

| Algorithm Type | Placement | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Collaborative filtering | Product page | "Customers also bought" |
| Content-based | Category page | "Similar products" |
| Recently viewed | Homepage, cart | "Continue browsing" |
| Trending | Homepage | "Popular right now" |
| Personalized | Email, homepage | "Recommended for you" |
| Complementary | Cart, post-purchase | "Complete the look" |

## Pricing Strategy

### Dynamic Pricing Approaches
- **Competitive pricing**: Monitor and match/beat competitor prices
- **Demand-based**: Adjust based on inventory levels and demand signals
- **Time-based**: Flash sales, happy hours, seasonal adjustments
- **Segmented**: Different prices for different customer segments
- **Bundle pricing**: Discount for buying complementary products together

### Pricing Psychology
- Charm pricing ($9.99 vs $10.00)
- Anchor pricing (show original price crossed out)
- Decoy pricing (add a "bad deal" option to make preferred option attractive)
- Free shipping threshold (set above AOV to increase basket size)
- Subscription discount (save 10-15% with recurring purchase)

## Fulfillment Operations

### Fulfillment Models

| Model | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Self-fulfillment | Full control, branding | Labor-intensive, space | Low volume, custom products |
| 3PL | Scalable, expertise | Less control, cost | Growing brands |
| Dropship | No inventory risk | Low margins, no quality control | Testing new products |
| FBA (Amazon) | Prime badge, fast shipping | Fees, Amazon dependency | Amazon-focused sellers |
| Hybrid | Flexibility | Complexity | Multi-channel brands |

### Shipping Strategy
- Free shipping on orders above threshold (most effective incentive)
- Flat-rate shipping for simplicity
- Real-time carrier rates for accuracy
- Same-day/next-day for competitive advantage
- Ship-from-store for omnichannel retailers
- Display estimated delivery date prominently

## Analytics and Measurement

### Essential E-commerce Dashboards
1. **Revenue dashboard**: Daily/weekly/monthly revenue, AOV, orders
2. **Conversion funnel**: Traffic → PDP → ATC → checkout → purchase
3. **Product performance**: Best sellers, underperformers, margin analysis
4. **Customer dashboard**: New vs returning, LTV, cohort analysis
5. **Marketing attribution**: Channel performance, ROAS, CAC by channel
6. **Inventory dashboard**: Stock levels, sell-through rate, days of supply
