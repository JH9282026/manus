## Cart Abandonment Recovery

### Why Carts Are Abandoned (Industry Averages: 70% abandonment)

| Reason | % of Abandoners | Solution |
|---|---|---|
| Extra costs (shipping, tax) | 48% | Free shipping threshold, transparent pricing |
| Required account creation | 26% | Guest checkout option |
| Complex checkout | 22% | Reduce form fields, progress indicator |
| Couldn't calculate total | 21% | Show running total with shipping estimate |
| Didn't trust site | 18% | Trust badges, SSL, reviews |
| Delivery too slow | 16% | Expedited options, clear delivery dates |

### Recovery Sequence
1. **Email 1** (1 hour): "You left something behind" — show cart contents
2. **Email 2** (24 hours): "Still interested?" — add social proof
3. **Email 3** (48-72 hours): "Last chance" — add incentive (discount/free shipping)
4. **SMS** (4-6 hours): Quick reminder with cart link
5. **Retargeting ads** (1-14 days): Dynamic product ads on social/display

## Customer Retention Strategy

### Retention Metrics to Track
- **Repeat purchase rate**: % of customers who buy again
- **Purchase frequency**: Average orders per customer per year
- **Customer lifetime value (LTV)**: Total revenue per customer
- **Net revenue retention**: Revenue from existing customers over time
- **Churn rate**: % of customers who don't return

### Retention Programs
- **Loyalty program**: Points, tiers, exclusive benefits
- **Subscription**: Recurring delivery for consumables
- **VIP program**: Early access, exclusive products for top customers
- **Win-back campaign**: Re-engage lapsed customers with incentive
- **Post-purchase flow**: Thank you, usage tips, review request, cross-sell

### Email Lifecycle Automation
1. Welcome series (days 0-7)
2. First purchase follow-up (day 3-7 post-delivery)
3. Review request (day 14 post-delivery)
4. Cross-sell recommendation (day 30)
5. Replenishment reminder (based on product cycle)
6. Win-back sequence (60-90 days inactive)
7. Birthday/anniversary rewards
