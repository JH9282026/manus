# E-commerce Strategy & Optimization

Develop and execute comprehensive e-commerce strategies that drive traffic, convert visitors, and maximize customer lifetime value.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for building and optimizing online retail businesses across the full funnel — from customer acquisition and product discovery through conversion, fulfillment, and retention. Cover platform strategy, merchandising, CRO, pricing, and analytics.

## E-commerce Funnel Metrics

| Stage | Key Metrics | Benchmarks |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Traffic, impressions, reach | Varies by channel |
| Discovery | Bounce rate, pages/session | Bounce <40%, 3+ pages |
| Consideration | Product views, add-to-cart rate | ATC 8-12% |
| Conversion | Conversion rate, AOV | CR 2-4%, AOV varies |
| Retention | Repeat rate, LTV, NPS | 25-40% repeat |

## CRO Priority Framework

| Page | Impact | Quick Wins |
|---|---|---|
| Product page | Highest | Better images, reviews, urgency |
| Cart page | High | Trust badges, shipping clarity, upsells |
| Checkout | High | Guest checkout, fewer fields, progress bar |
| Category page | Medium | Filters, sort options, quick view |
| Homepage | Medium | Clear value prop, hero optimization |
| Search | Medium | Autocomplete, typo tolerance, filters |

## Platform Selection Guide

| Platform | Best For | Price Range |
|---|---|---|
| Shopify | SMB, DTC brands | $39-399/mo |
| WooCommerce | WordPress sites, flexibility | Free + hosting |
| BigCommerce | Mid-market, B2B | $39-399/mo |
| Magento/Adobe Commerce | Enterprise, complex catalogs | $$$$ |
| Salesforce Commerce Cloud | Enterprise, omnichannel | $$$$$ |
| Headless (custom) | Unique UX requirements | Custom build |

## Product Page Optimization Checklist

- [ ] High-quality images with zoom (5+ angles)
- [ ] Compelling product title with key attributes
- [ ] Clear pricing with any savings shown
- [ ] Prominent add-to-cart button (above fold)
- [ ] Social proof (reviews, ratings, purchase count)
- [ ] Shipping info and delivery estimate
- [ ] Return policy clearly stated
- [ ] Size/fit guide (apparel)
- [ ] Cross-sell and upsell recommendations
- [ ] Mobile-optimized layout and touch targets
