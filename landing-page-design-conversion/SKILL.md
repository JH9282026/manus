# Landing Page Design & Conversion

Design high-converting landing pages with optimized hero sections, copy, trust signals, and conversion elements.

## Hero Section Essentials
- **Headline**: value proposition in 6-10 words; benefit-focused
- **Subheadline**: supporting detail expanding the promise
- **Visual**: relevant image or video reinforcing message
- **CTA**: clear action button with specific language
- **Trust**: social proof elements for immediate credibility

## Conversion Principles
1. Single focused goal per page
2. Message match between ad and landing page
3. Remove navigation and distractions
4. Reduce form friction (fewer fields = higher conversion)
5. Social proof near conversion points
6. Urgency and scarcity when legitimate
7. Mobile-first design
