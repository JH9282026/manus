# Conversion Optimization

## A/B Testing
- Test one element at a time for clear results
- Priority order: headline -> CTA -> hero image -> form -> layout
- Run until statistical significance (95%+ confidence)
- Document all tests and results

## Form Optimization
- Fewer fields = higher conversion (test 3 vs. 5 vs. 7)
- Multi-step forms for complex lead capture
- Smart defaults and autofill support
- Inline validation (real-time error feedback)
- Progress indicators for multi-step forms

## CTA Best Practices
- Action-oriented: "Get My Free Guide" not "Submit"
- First person often wins: "Start my trial" vs "Start your trial"
- Contrasting color from page palette
- Large enough to tap easily on mobile (44x44px min)
- Repeat throughout long pages

## Trust Signals
- Customer testimonials with names and photos
- Security badges near forms and payment
- Client/media logos
- Star ratings and review counts
- Money-back guarantees
- Privacy assurance near email fields

## Mobile Optimization
- Thumb-friendly CTAs in bottom zone
- Simplified forms
- Fast loading (<3 seconds)
- No horizontal scroll
- Click-to-call for phone conversions

## Key Metrics
| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| Landing page conversion | 2-5% | 10%+ |
| Form completion | 20-40% | 50%+ |
| Bounce rate | <60% | <40% |
| Time to convert | <2 min | <1 min |
