## Key Metrics

### Engagement Metrics

**Impressions**:
- Definition: Number of times AR Lens is shown
- Importance: Reach and visibility
- Benchmarks: Varies by campaign size

**Plays**:
- Definition: Number of times AR Lens is activated
- Importance: Initial engagement
- Calculation: Plays / Impressions = Play Rate

**Play Rate**:
- Definition: Percentage of impressions resulting in plays
- Importance: Creative appeal
- Benchmarks: 5-15% typical

**Average Play Time**:
- Definition: Average duration of Lens usage
- Importance: Engagement depth
- Benchmarks: 10-30 seconds typical
- High performers: 30+ seconds

**Completion Rate**:
- Definition: Percentage completing full experience
- Importance: Experience quality
- Calculation: Completions / Plays

**Share Rate**:
- Definition: Percentage of users sharing Lens
- Importance: Virality and organic reach
- Calculation: Shares / Plays
- Benchmarks: 10-30% typical

### Reach Metrics

**Unique Users**:
- Definition: Number of individual users reached
- Importance: Audience size
- Deduplication: Across devices and sessions

**Frequency**:
- Definition: Average impressions per user
- Importance: Message reinforcement
- Calculation: Impressions / Unique Users
- Optimal: 2-5 for most campaigns

**Geographic Reach**:
- Definition: Distribution across locations
- Importance: Market coverage
- Analysis: Performance by region

**Demographic Reach**:
- Definition: Distribution across demographics
- Importance: Audience alignment
- Analysis: Performance by age, gender

### Conversion Metrics

**Swipe-Ups**:
- Definition: Number of swipe-up actions
- Importance: Intent and interest
- Calculation: Swipe-Ups / Plays = Swipe-Up Rate

**Website Visits**:
- Definition: Visits to destination URL
- Importance: Traffic generation
- Tracking: Snap Pixel

**Conversions**:
- Definition: Desired actions completed
- Types: Purchases, signups, downloads
- Tracking: Snap Pixel, conversion events

**Cost Per Conversion**:
- Definition: Average cost to achieve conversion
- Calculation: Total Spend / Conversions
- Importance: Efficiency metric

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**:
- Definition: Revenue generated per dollar spent
- Calculation: Revenue / Ad Spend
- Benchmarks: 3:1 to 5:1 typical for e-commerce

### Brand Metrics

**Brand Awareness Lift**:
- Definition: Increase in brand awareness
- Measurement: Brand lift study
- Calculation: (Exposed - Control) / Control

**Ad Recall Lift**:
- Definition: Increase in ad recall
- Measurement: Brand lift study
- Importance: Message retention

**Brand Favorability Lift**:
- Definition: Increase in positive brand perception
- Measurement: Brand lift study
- Importance: Brand health

**Purchase Intent Lift**:
- Definition: Increase in purchase consideration
- Measurement: Brand lift study
- Importance: Conversion likelihood

**Message Association**:
- Definition: Association of message with brand
- Measurement: Brand lift study
- Importance: Communication effectiveness

## Performance Analysis

### Engagement Analysis

**Play Rate Analysis**:
- Low play rate (<5%): Creative appeal issue
- Medium play rate (5-15%): Typical performance
- High play rate (>15%): Strong creative

**Optimization**:
- Improve thumbnail
- Enhance preview
- Test different concepts
- Refine targeting

**Play Time Analysis**:
- Short play time (<10s): Engagement issue
- Medium play time (10-30s): Typical engagement
- Long play time (>30s): High engagement

**Optimization**:
- Improve interactions
- Add engaging elements
- Simplify experience
- Enhance value
