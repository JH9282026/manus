# AR Campaign Strategy and Planning

## Introduction

Successful AR advertising campaigns require strategic planning that aligns AR experiences with business objectives, target audiences, and the customer journey. This guide covers campaign strategy, planning, execution, and optimization for Snapchat AR ads.

## Campaign Objectives

### Awareness Objectives

**Goals**:
- Brand discovery
- Reach and impressions
- Brand recall
- Top-of-mind awareness

**AR Strategies**:
- Gamified Entertainment Lenses (12% brand differentiation)
- Viral, shareable experiences
- Broad targeting
- High-frequency exposure

**Metrics**:
- Impressions
- Reach
- Play rate
- Share rate
- Brand lift (awareness, recall)

### Consideration Objectives

**Goals**:
- Product understanding
- Feature exploration
- Brand preference
- Purchase intent

**AR Strategies**:
- World Lenses (8% purchase intent, 7% brand relevance)
- Interactive Entertainment Lenses (9% memorability)
- Product demonstrations
- Virtual try-on

**Metrics**:
- Average play time
- Engagement rate
- Swipe-up rate
- Brand lift (consideration, preference)

### Conversion Objectives

**Goals**:
- Purchases
- App installs
- Lead generation
- Store visits

**AR Strategies**:
- Shoppable AR Lenses (8% search intent increase)
- Front-facing Lenses (5% uniqueness, 4% relevancy)
- Direct purchase integration
- Clear CTAs

**Metrics**:
- Conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition
- Return on ad spend
- Purchase lift

## Conclusion

Successful AR campaign strategy requires aligning AR experiences with business objectives, understanding target audiences, and executing with precision.
