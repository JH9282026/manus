## Sales Presentation Design Considerations


### Visual Design for Sales

**Brand Consistency**
- Use company templates and colors
- Include appropriate logos
- Maintain professional appearance
- Balance brand presence with content focus

**Data Visualization**
- Charts showing ROI and value
- Before/after comparisons
- Process improvements
- Customer results

**Visual Hierarchy**
- One key point per slide
- Clear headlines
- Supporting visuals
- Minimal text


### Slide Structure

**Title Slides**
- Clear, benefit-focused titles
- Prospect-centric language
- Attention-grabbing when needed

**Content Slides**
- Maximum 3-5 bullet points
- Supporting visuals
- Consistent layout
- Easy to follow

**Data Slides**
- Clear chart labels
- Key takeaways highlighted
- Source citations
- Visual simplicity
