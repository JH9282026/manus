## Best Practices for Sales Presentations


### Preparation Best Practices

1. **Research thoroughly** before every presentation
2. **Customize content** for each prospect
3. **Practice delivery** until confident
4. **Prepare for objections** in advance
5. **Test technology** before presenting


### Delivery Best Practices

6. **Start strong** with an attention-grabbing opener
7. **Tell stories** that resonate emotionally
8. **Focus on value**, not features
9. **Listen actively** and respond accordingly
10. **Read the room** and adjust in real-time


### Engagement Best Practices

11. **Ask questions** throughout the presentation
12. **Involve the audience** in the discussion
13. **Use names** when addressing individuals
14. **Create dialogue**, not monologue
15. **Respond to cues** both verbal and non-verbal


### Closing Best Practices

16. **Summarize value** before asking for commitment
17. **Address objections** confidently
18. **Create appropriate urgency** when genuine
19. **Ask for the business** directly
20. **Define clear next steps** every time
