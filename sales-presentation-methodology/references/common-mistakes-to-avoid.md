# Common Mistakes To Avoid

Detailed reference content for the Sales Presentation Methodology skill.

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid


### Preparation Mistakes

1. **Insufficient research** on the prospect
2. **Generic presentations** not customized for the audience
3. **Overloaded slides** with too much information
4. **Unprepared for technical failures** or interruptions
5. **Ignoring stakeholder dynamics** and politics


### Delivery Mistakes

6. **Leading with features** instead of value
7. **Talking too much** and listening too little
8. **Reading from slides** instead of engaging
9. **Ignoring audience reactions** and signals
10. **Rushing through content** without connection


### Content Mistakes

11. **No clear value proposition** articulated
12. **Missing proof points** and social proof
13. **Forgetting to address objections** proactively
14. **Presenting irrelevant information** or features
15. **Failing to differentiate** from alternatives


### Closing Mistakes

16. **Never asking for the business** directly
17. **Ending without clear next steps** defined
18. **Ignoring buying signals** when present
19. **Weak follow-up** after presentations
20. **Giving up too early** on complex deals
