## Measuring Presentation Effectiveness


### Key Metrics

**Conversion Metrics**
- Presentation-to-proposal rate
- Proposal-to-close rate
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length

**Engagement Metrics**
- Question frequency
- Stakeholder participation
- Follow-up response rates
- Content sharing

**Feedback Metrics**
- Post-presentation surveys
- Win/loss analysis feedback
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Referral rates


### Continuous Improvement

**Presentation Analysis**
- Record and review presentations
- Identify successful patterns
- Note areas for improvement
- Track what resonates

**A/B Testing**
- Test different openings
- Try various proof points
- Experiment with closes
- Compare virtual vs. in-person
