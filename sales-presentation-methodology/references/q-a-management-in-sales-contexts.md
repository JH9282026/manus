## Q&A Management in Sales Contexts


### Q&A Facilitation

**Creating Space for Questions**
- "What questions do you have?"
- "What would you like me to clarify?"
- "What concerns can I address?"

**Encouraging Participation**
- Ask specific individuals
- Acknowledge the importance of questions
- Reward questions with positive responses


### Answering Techniques

**SEER Method**
1. **Summary**: Restate the question
2. **Elaborate**: Provide your answer
3. **Example**: Give a concrete illustration
4. **Redirect**: Connect to a key point or benefit

**Handling Difficult Questions**
- Stay calm and professional
- Acknowledge valid concerns
- Be honest when you don't know
- Commit to follow-up when needed

**Turning Questions into Opportunities**
- Use questions to reinforce value
- Connect answers to pain points
- Build credibility through expertise
- Create urgency through responses
