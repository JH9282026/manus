## Follow-Up Presentation Strategies


### Post-Presentation Follow-Up

**Immediate Follow-Up (Within 24 Hours)**
- Thank you email
- Summary of key points
- Answers to outstanding questions
- Promised materials

**Structured Follow-Up**
- Recap document
- Relevant case studies
- ROI calculations
- Proposal or quote


### Multi-Meeting Strategy

**Sequential Presentations**
- Discovery → Pitch → Demo → Proposal → Close
- Each presentation builds on previous
- Maintain momentum between meetings
- Involve additional stakeholders strategically

**Stakeholder Expansion**
- Executive presentations
- Technical deep-dives
- User demonstrations
- Finance reviews
