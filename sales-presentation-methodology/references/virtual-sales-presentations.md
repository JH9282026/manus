## Virtual Sales Presentations


### Virtual Presentation Challenges

**Attention Management**
- Competing distractions
- Multi-tasking temptation
- Screen fatigue
- Limited non-verbal feedback

**Technical Considerations**
- Platform reliability
- Audio/video quality
- Screen sharing issues
- Internet connectivity


### Virtual Presentation Best Practices

**Technical Setup**
- Professional background
- Good lighting
- Quality audio
- Camera at eye level

**Engagement Techniques**
- Use polls and questions
- Call on individuals by name
- Share control when appropriate
- Use chat for participation

**Pacing and Structure**
- Shorter segments
- More frequent breaks
- Interactive elements every 5-10 minutes
- Clear agenda and timing

**Visual Optimization**
- Larger fonts
- Higher contrast
- Simplified slides
- More frequent transitions
