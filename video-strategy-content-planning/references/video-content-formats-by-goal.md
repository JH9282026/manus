# Video Content Formats By Goal

Detailed reference content for the Video Strategy Content Planning skill.

---

## Video Content Formats by Goal


### Brand Awareness Formats

**Viral-potential content**:
- Challenges and trends participation
- Commentary on current events
- Entertaining, shareable content
- Collaborative content

**Thought leadership**:
- Industry insights and predictions
- Original research and data
- Expert interviews
- Conference talks and keynotes


### Lead Generation Formats

**Educational content**:
- How-to tutorials
- Comprehensive guides
- Free mini-courses
- Live workshops

**Problem-solution content**:
- Common mistake videos
- Troubleshooting guides
- Comparison and review content
- FAQ videos


### Sales and Conversion Formats

**Product-focused content**:
- Demo videos
- Feature walkthroughs
- Use case showcases
- Customer success stories

**Trust-building content**:
- Behind-the-scenes
- Company culture
- Team introductions
- Testimonial compilations
