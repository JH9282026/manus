## Content Repurposing Strategy


### The Repurposing Pyramid

From one piece of flagship content, create:

**Tier 1**: Full-length video (YouTube, website)
**Tier 2**: Clips and highlights (Shorts, Reels, TikTok)
**Tier 3**: Audio extraction (podcast)
**Tier 4**: Written content (blog post, newsletter)
**Tier 5**: Social snippets (quotes, images, threads)


### Repurposing Tactics


#### Video to Short-Form
- Extract key moments
- Add captions
- Optimize vertical format
- Create multiple clips per video


#### Video to Audio
- Extract audio track
- Add intro/outro
- Publish as podcast
- Distribute to audio platforms


#### Video to Written
- Transcribe content
- Edit into blog format
- Add headers and formatting
- Optimize for SEO


#### Video to Social
- Quote cards
- Behind-the-scenes photos
- Key statistics
- Discussion prompts
