# Evergreen Vs Timely Content

Detailed reference content for the Video Strategy Content Planning skill.

---

## Evergreen vs. Timely Content


### Evergreen Content

Content that remains relevant long-term:

**Characteristics**:
- Addresses persistent problems or interests
- Doesn't reference specific dates or events
- Searchable and discoverable over time
- May need periodic updates

**Examples**:
- "How to Start a Podcast"
- "Beginner's Guide to Photography"
- "Understanding Credit Scores"

**Benefits**:
- Compounds views over time
- SEO and search value
- Library asset building
- Efficient long-term ROI


### Timely Content

Content tied to current events or trends:

**Characteristics**:
- References specific moments or events
- Peak performance immediately after publishing
- Short relevance window
- Newsjacking and trend response

**Examples**:
- "iPhone 16 First Impressions"
- "My Reaction to [Current Event]"
- "Trends to Watch in 2025"

**Benefits**:
- Rapid view accumulation
- Algorithm boost from timeliness
- Relevance and current positioning
- Community engagement


### Content Mix Strategy

Recommended balance by channel type:

| Channel Type | Evergreen | Timely |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| Tutorial/Education | 80% | 20% |
| News/Commentary | 20% | 80% |
| Lifestyle/Vlog | 50% | 50% |
| Business/Professional | 70% | 30% |
