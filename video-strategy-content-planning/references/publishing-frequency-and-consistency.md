## Publishing Frequency and Consistency


### Finding Your Optimal Frequency

Factors to consider:

| Factor | Higher Frequency | Lower Frequency |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Resources | Well-funded team | Solo creator |
| Content Type | News, trends | Evergreen, high-production |
| Platform | TikTok, Twitter | YouTube, LinkedIn |
| Audience | Daily consumers | Weekly deep-divers |
| Goals | Rapid growth | Quality positioning |


### Frequency Guidelines by Platform

| Platform | Minimum | Recommended | High-Volume |
|----------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| YouTube (long-form) | 1/week | 2-3/week | Daily |
| YouTube Shorts | 3/week | 1/day | 3+/day |
| TikTok | 1/day | 3/day | 5+/day |
| Instagram Reels | 3/week | 1/day | 3/day |
| LinkedIn | 2/week | 3-5/week | Daily |


### The Consistency Principle

Consistency matters more than frequency:
- Viewers develop expectations
- Algorithms reward predictability
- Production becomes sustainable
- Quality remains high

**Better**: 1 great video every week for a year
**Worse**: 5 videos one week, then nothing for a month


### Managing Consistency

- Build a content buffer (2-4 weeks ahead)
- Plan for sick days and vacations
- Have "emergency" content ready
- Communicate schedule changes to audience
- Use scheduling tools to maintain rhythm
