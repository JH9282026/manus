# Common Mistakes

Detailed reference content for the Video Strategy Content Planning skill.

---

## Common Mistakes

1. **No clear strategy**: Creating without purpose or plan
2. **Inconsistent posting**: Unpredictable schedule hurts growth
3. **Ignoring analytics**: Not learning from performance data
4. **Chasing trends exclusively**: No evergreen foundation
5. **Poor audience understanding**: Content that doesn't resonate
6. **Burnout from overcommitment**: Unsustainable pace
7. **Copying competitors**: No differentiation
8. **Neglecting SEO**: Missing search opportunities
9. **Single platform dependence**: No diversification
10. **Perfectionism paralysis**: Not publishing good-enough content
