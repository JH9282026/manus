# Resource Planning

Detailed reference content for the Video Strategy Content Planning skill.

---

## Resource Planning


### Budget Considerations


#### Equipment Investment
- Camera and lenses
- Lighting equipment
- Audio equipment
- Editing software
- Studio/set


#### Ongoing Costs
- Software subscriptions
- Music licensing
- Stock footage/images
- Freelancer fees
- Hosting and tools


#### Budget Tiers

**Beginner ($0-500)**:
- Smartphone camera
- Natural lighting + $50 ring light
- Lapel microphone ($30-50)
- Free editing software

**Intermediate ($500-2,500)**:
- Entry DSLR/mirrorless
- Three-point lighting
- USB microphone
- Paid editing software

**Professional ($2,500-10,000+)**:
- Professional camera system
- Professional lighting
- XLR microphone setup
- Full Adobe Creative Suite


### Team Planning


#### Solo Creator Roles
One person handles:
- Strategy and planning
- Scripting and research
- Filming and production
- Editing and post-production
- Publishing and promotion


#### Small Team Roles
- **Creator/Host**: On-camera talent and vision
- **Editor**: Post-production
- **Manager**: Strategy and publishing


#### Full Team Roles
- Executive Producer
- Content Strategist
- Scriptwriter
- Videographer
- Editor
- Graphic Designer
- Social Media Manager
- Community Manager
