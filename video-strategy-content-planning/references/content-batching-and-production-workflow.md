### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Content Length | Audience | Best For |
|----------|---------------|----------|----------|
| YouTube | 8-20 min (standard), <60 sec (Shorts) | Broad, search-driven | Tutorials, evergreen, long-form |
| TikTok | 15-60 sec, up to 10 min | Young, trend-driven | Entertainment, trends, discoverability |
| Instagram Reels | 15-90 sec | Visual, lifestyle-focused | Behind-the-scenes, aesthetic content |
| LinkedIn | 30 sec-10 min | Professional, B2B | Thought leadership, career content |
| Facebook | 1-5 min | Older demographic, shares | News, family-friendly, local |
| Twitter/X | 2:20 max | News-focused, commentary | Quick takes, reactions, clips |


### Platform-First vs. Repurposing


#### Platform-First Approach
Create original content optimized for each platform:
- Maximum optimization for each algorithm
- Higher resource requirements
- Best performance potential
- Requires multiple content strategies


#### Repurposing Approach
Adapt content across platforms:
- Efficient content multiplication
- Consistent messaging
- Lower resource requirements
- Must adapt format appropriately


### Multi-Platform Strategy


#### Hub-and-Spoke Model
- **Hub**: Primary platform for flagship content (e.g., YouTube)
- **Spokes**: Secondary platforms for distribution and discovery (TikTok, Instagram)
- Drive traffic from spokes to hub
- Build deep community on hub


#### Platform-Specific Optimization

**YouTube Optimization**:
- Searchable titles with keywords
- Detailed descriptions
- Custom thumbnails
- End screens and cards
- Chapter markers

**TikTok/Reels Optimization**:
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- Native captions
- Trending sounds when relevant
- Strong call-to-action
- Hashtag strategy

**LinkedIn Optimization**:
- Professional tone
- Native video upload
- Thought leadership angle
- Engagement-focused captions
- Career/business relevance
