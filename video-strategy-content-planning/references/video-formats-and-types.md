## Video Formats and Types



### Educational Formats



#### Tutorial/How-To
Step-by-step instruction on completing tasks:
- High search potential
- Evergreen value
- Clear structure
- Strong retention when well-paced



#### Explainer
Breaking down complex concepts:
- Thought leadership positioning
- Animation/visual aid heavy
- Moderate search potential
- Authority building



#### Course/Workshop
Multi-part educational content:
- Deep topic coverage
- Lead generation potential
- High production value
- Premium positioning



### Entertainment Formats



#### Vlog
Personal, documentary-style content:
- Parasocial relationship building
- Lower production requirements
- Personality-driven
- Authentic connection



#### Challenge
Attempting tasks with specific rules:
- High engagement potential
- Collaboration opportunities
- Trending format participation
- Entertainment-first approach



#### Reaction
Responding to other content:
- Trend commentary
- Lower preparation needs
- Personality showcase
- Must add genuine value
