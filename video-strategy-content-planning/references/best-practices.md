# Best Practices

Detailed reference content for the Video Strategy Content Planning skill.

---

## Best Practices

1. **Document your strategy**: Write it down, review regularly
2. **Know your audience deeply**: Beyond demographics to psychographics
3. **Build content pillars**: Focused, recognizable themes
4. **Create a sustainable calendar**: Realistic commitments
5. **Batch production**: Efficient creation processes
6. **Mix evergreen and timely**: Balanced content portfolio
7. **Regular audits**: Review and optimize continuously
8. **Build content buffer**: Ahead-of-schedule production
9. **Repurpose strategically**: Maximize content value
10. **Track and iterate**: Data-informed decisions
