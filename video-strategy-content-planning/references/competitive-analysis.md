## Competitive Analysis


### Competitor Identification


#### Direct Competitors
- Same niche and audience
- Similar content formats
- Competing for same viewers


#### Indirect Competitors
- Adjacent niches
- Different formats, same audience
- Same topics, different angle


#### Aspirational Competitors
- Larger channels in your space
- Where you want to be
- Benchmark for success


### Analysis Framework

For each key competitor, analyze:


#### Content Analysis
- What topics do they cover?
- What formats do they use?
- How often do they post?
- What's their content quality?


#### Performance Analysis
- Average views per video
- Engagement rates (likes, comments)
- Subscriber count and growth
- Best-performing content


#### Positioning Analysis
- How do they position themselves?
- What makes them unique?
- Who is their target audience?
- What's their brand voice?


### Competitive Differentiation

Find your unique angle:

- **Depth**: Go deeper than competitors
- **Breadth**: Cover more comprehensively
- **Format**: Present differently
- **Personality**: Unique voice and style
- **Audience**: Serve underserved segment
- **Quality**: Higher production value
