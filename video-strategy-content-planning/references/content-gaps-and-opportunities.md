## Content Gaps and Opportunities


### Identifying Content Gaps


#### Your Channel Gaps
- Topics your audience asks about but you haven't covered
- Sub-topics within your pillars not yet explored
- Formats you haven't tested
- Audience segments not addressed


#### Market Gaps
- Topics competitors don't cover well
- Underserved niches
- Poor quality existing content
- Emerging topics without coverage


### Gap Analysis Process

1. **List all topics** in your niche
2. **Audit your content**: What have you covered?
3. **Research competitors**: What have they covered?
4. **Analyze search volume**: What are people searching for?
5. **Survey audience**: What do they want?
6. **Identify gaps**: Where are the opportunities?
