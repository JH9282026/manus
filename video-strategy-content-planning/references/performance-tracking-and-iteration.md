## Performance Tracking and Iteration


### Key Metrics to Track


#### Reach Metrics
- Impressions
- Views
- Unique viewers
- Traffic sources


#### Engagement Metrics
- Watch time
- Average view duration
- Retention rate
- Likes, comments, shares


#### Growth Metrics
- Subscribers gained
- Subscriber conversion rate
- Channel growth rate


#### Conversion Metrics
- Click-through rate
- Link clicks
- Lead generation
- Revenue


### Analytics Review Cadence

- **Daily**: Quick health check
- **Weekly**: Content performance review
- **Monthly**: Strategy assessment
- **Quarterly**: Major strategy decisions
- **Annually**: Comprehensive channel audit


### Iteration Process

1. **Analyze**: Review performance data
2. **Identify**: Find patterns and insights
3. **Hypothesize**: What changes might improve results?
4. **Test**: Implement changes on limited content
5. **Measure**: Compare to baseline
6. **Scale**: Apply successful changes broadly
