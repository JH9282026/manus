## Seasonal and Trending Content Planning


### Seasonal Content Opportunities


#### Major Holidays
- Plan 4-8 weeks in advance
- Create evergreen seasonal content
- Update and re-promote annually
- Consider cultural variations


#### Industry Events
- Trade shows and conferences
- Product launches
- Annual reports and announcements
- Award seasons


#### Calendar Events
- Back-to-school
- Tax season
- Summer/winter activities
- Sports seasons


### Trend Integration Strategy


#### Types of Trends

**Micro-trends**: Hours to days
- Platform-specific challenges
- Breaking news
- Viral moments

**Short-term trends**: Days to weeks
- Meme cycles
- Product launches
- Celebrity moments

**Medium-term trends**: Weeks to months
- Seasonal interests
- Industry developments
- Cultural conversations

**Long-term trends**: Months to years
- Technology shifts
- Social movements
- Economic changes


#### Trend Response Framework

1. **Monitor**: Set up alerts and follow trend sources
2. **Evaluate**: Does this fit your brand and audience?
3. **Decide**: Quick response or let it pass?
4. **Create**: Add unique value, don't just replicate
5. **Publish**: Speed matters for trends
6. **Analyze**: Learn what works for future trends
