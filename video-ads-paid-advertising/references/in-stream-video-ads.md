### In-Stream Video Ads

**Pre-Roll:**
- Plays before content
- Highest attention
- Skippable or non-skippable
- Most common format

**Mid-Roll:**
- Plays during content
- Often non-skippable
- Higher completion rates
- Content-dependent availability

**Post-Roll:**
- Plays after content
- Lower attention
- Less common
- Retargeting value
