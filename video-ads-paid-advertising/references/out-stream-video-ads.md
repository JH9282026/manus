### Out-Stream Video Ads

**Definition:**
Video ads that appear outside of video content (in-article, in-feed, in-banner)

**Formats:**
- In-article (within editorial content)
- In-feed (social feed placement)
- In-banner (within display units)
- Interstitial (full-screen between content)

**Characteristics:**
- Reaches non-video content viewers
- Often sound-off default
- Scroll-to-play behavior
- Broader inventory
