### Testing Methodology

**A/B Testing:**
- Change one variable
- Equal budget split
- Sufficient sample size
- Clear success metric

**Multivariate Testing:**
- Multiple variables
- Algorithm optimization
- Larger sample needs
- Platform features


---

### Testing Methodology

**Hypothesis-Driven Testing:**
1. Observe current performance
2. Form hypothesis ("If X, then Y")
3. Design test to prove/disprove
4. Implement with controls
5. Analyze results
6. Scale or iterate

**Statistical Rigor:**
- Sufficient sample size
- Clear success metrics
- Controlled variables
- Statistical significance
- Confidence intervals
