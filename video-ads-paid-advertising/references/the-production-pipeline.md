### The Production Pipeline

**Phase 1: Strategy**
- Objective clarity
- Audience definition
- Competitive review
- Creative brief
- Success metrics

**Phase 2: Creative Development**
- Concept ideation
- Script writing
- Storyboarding
- Production planning
- Stakeholder approval

**Phase 3: Production**
- Filming/animation
- Multiple versions
- Platform variations
- Asset capture

**Phase 4: Post-Production**
- Editing
- Motion graphics
- Color grading
- Audio design
- Caption creation

**Phase 5: Testing**
- Internal review
- A/B variations
- Platform compliance
- Quality assurance
