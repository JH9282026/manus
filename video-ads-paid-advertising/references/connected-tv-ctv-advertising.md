### Connected TV (CTV) Advertising

**Definition:**
Video ads served on internet-connected television devices (smart TVs, streaming devices, gaming consoles).

**Platforms:**
- Roku
- Amazon Fire TV
- Apple TV
- Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, etc.)
- Streaming services (Hulu, Peacock, etc.)

**Characteristics:**
- Full-screen, non-skippable
- Premium content environments
- Household targeting
- Living room attention
- Growing rapidly

**Targeting:**
- Household demographics
- Content genres
- First-party data
- Cross-device matching
