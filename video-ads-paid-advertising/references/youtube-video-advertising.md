### YouTube Video Advertising

**Ad Formats:**
- Skippable in-stream (5-sec skip)
- Non-skippable in-stream (15-20 sec)
- Bumper ads (6 sec)
- Discovery ads
- Masthead ads
- YouTube Shorts ads

**Targeting Options:**
- Demographics
- Interests and affinity
- In-market audiences
- Custom intent
- Remarketing
- Placement targeting
- Topic targeting

**Bidding Strategies:**
- CPV (Cost per View)
- CPM (Cost per Thousand Impressions)
- Target CPA
- Maximize conversions
