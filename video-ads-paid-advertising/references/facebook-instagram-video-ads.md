### Facebook & Instagram Video Ads

**Placements:**
- Feed (Facebook/Instagram)
- Stories
- Reels
- In-stream (FB video content)
- Messenger
- Audience Network

**Format Specifications:**
- Feed: 1:1, 4:5, 16:9
- Stories: 9:16
- Reels: 9:16
- Length: Up to 240 minutes (recommend 15 sec or less)

**Targeting:**
- Core audiences (demographics, interests)
- Custom audiences (website, customer lists)
- Lookalike audiences
- Detailed targeting
