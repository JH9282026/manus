### Connected TV (CTV) Advertising Deep Dive

**Platform Options:**
- Streaming services (Hulu, Peacock, etc.)
- OTT devices (Roku, Amazon Fire TV)
- Smart TV manufacturers
- Programmatic CTV

**Creative Considerations:**
- TV-quality production value
- 15-second and 30-second formats
- Full-screen, lean-back viewing
- QR codes for response
- Sound-on environment

**Measurement Challenges:**
- Cross-device attribution
- Household vs. individual
- Limited direct response tracking
- Brand lift studies important
