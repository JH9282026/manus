### Custom Audience Strategies

**Website Behavior Segmentation:**
- All visitors (broad)
- Product page viewers (interested)
- Cart abandoners (high intent)
- Past purchasers (retention)
- High-value customers (VIP)

**Video Viewer Segmentation:**
- 3-second viewers (minimal engagement)
- 25% viewers (some interest)
- 50% viewers (engaged)
- 75% viewers (very engaged)
- 95% viewers (completers)

**Engagement Segmentation:**
- Page fans/followers
- Post engagers
- Form submitters
- Event responders
- App users
