### Video Creative Frameworks

**AIDA Framework:**
- **Attention:** Hook in first seconds
- **Interest:** Build engagement with relevance
- **Desire:** Create want through benefits
- **Action:** Clear CTA

**PAS Framework:**
- **Problem:** Present the pain point
- **Agitation:** Amplify the problem
- **Solution:** Introduce your offering

**BAB Framework:**
- **Before:** Current state (problem)
- **After:** Future state (solution applied)
- **Bridge:** How you get them there

**Hero's Journey (Simplified):**
- Ordinary world
- Call to adventure
- Guide/mentor (your brand)
- Transformation
- New world
