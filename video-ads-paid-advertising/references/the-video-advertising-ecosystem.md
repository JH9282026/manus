### The Video Advertising Ecosystem

**Major Platforms:**
- YouTube (Google Video Partners)
- Facebook & Instagram (Meta)
- TikTok
- LinkedIn
- Connected TV (CTV)
- Programmatic networks

**Ad Types:**
- In-stream (before, during, after content)
- Out-stream (standalone video units)
- Social feed ads
- Stories ads
- Interactive and shoppable
