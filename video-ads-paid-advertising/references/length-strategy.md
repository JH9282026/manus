### Length Strategy

**Short-Form (6-15 seconds):**
- Awareness/reach campaigns
- Retargeting
- Simple messages
- High frequency

**Medium-Form (15-60 seconds):**
- Consideration
- Product demonstration
- Story delivery
- Primary ad length

**Long-Form (60+ seconds):**
- Complex stories
- Educational content
- Highly engaged audiences
- Remarketing warm audiences
