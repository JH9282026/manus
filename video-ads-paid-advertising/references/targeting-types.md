### Targeting Types

**Demographic:**
- Age
- Gender
- Location
- Language
- Income (where available)
- Education

**Interest-Based:**
- Affinity audiences (lifestyle)
- In-market audiences (purchase intent)
- Topic targeting
- Content targeting

**Behavioral:**
- Past purchases
- Website behavior
- App activity
- Engagement history

**Contextual:**
- Content alignment
- Keyword targeting
- Placement targeting
- Category targeting
