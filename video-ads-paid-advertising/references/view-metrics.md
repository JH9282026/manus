### View Metrics

**Views:**
- Platform-specific definitions
- YouTube: 30 sec or completion (whichever first)
- Facebook: 3 seconds
- TikTok: 2 seconds

**View Rate (VTR):**
- Views / Impressions
- Indicates ad appeal
- Benchmark by platform

**Completion Rate:**
- Completed views / Total views
- Measures content effectiveness
- Critical for understanding engagement

**Average View Duration:**
- How long viewers watch
- Indicates content quality
- Helps identify drop-off points
