### Skippable vs Non-Skippable

**Skippable Ads:**
- User can skip after 5 seconds
- Longer formats allowed (up to 3 min)
- Pay only when viewed (often)
- Requires strong hook
- Respects user choice

**Non-Skippable Ads:**
- Forced viewing (typically 15-20 seconds)
- Guaranteed completion
- Higher CPM
- Can frustrate viewers
- Complete message delivery
