### LinkedIn Video Ads

**Formats:**
- Sponsored content (feed video)
- Video ads in LinkedIn Audience Network

**Specifications:**
- Horizontal or square
- 3 seconds to 30 minutes
- Recommended 15-30 seconds

**Targeting:**
- Job title
- Company
- Industry
- Skills
- Seniority
- Company size

**Best For:**
- B2B marketing
- Thought leadership
- Recruitment
- Professional services
