### Platform-Optimized Production

**YouTube-Specific:**
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Hook critical (5-second skip)
- Longer form acceptable
- End screens and cards
- Thumbnail optimization

**Meta (Facebook/Instagram):**
- Multiple aspect ratios
- Sound-off design
- Captions essential
- Mobile-first
- Fast hook

**TikTok-Specific:**
- Vertical (9:16)
- Native aesthetic
- Sound-on culture
- Trend awareness
- Creator style

**LinkedIn-Specific:**
- Professional context
- B2B focus
- Educational value
- Thought leadership
- Credibility emphasis
