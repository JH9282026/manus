### Lookalike/Similar Audience Mastery

**Seed Quality:**
- Use highest-value customers
- Sufficient seed size (1,000+)
- Recent data preferred
- Exclude low-value segments

**Percentage Strategy:**
- 1% = Most similar, smallest reach
- 5% = Balance of similarity and reach
- 10% = Broader, less similar
- Test multiple percentages

**Source Optimization:**
- Test different seed sources
- Compare performance
- Refresh regularly
- Combine high-performers
