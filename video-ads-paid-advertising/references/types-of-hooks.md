### Types of Hooks

**Question Hooks:**
- "Have you ever..."
- "What if you could..."
- "Why do most people..."
- Engage curiosity

**Statement Hooks:**
- Surprising facts
- Bold claims
- Contrarian views
- Pattern interrupts

**Visual Hooks:**
- Unexpected imagery
- Movement and action
- Faces and emotion
- Before/after glimpse

**Story Hooks:**
- "I used to..."
- "Last year we..."
- Personal narrative
- Customer story
