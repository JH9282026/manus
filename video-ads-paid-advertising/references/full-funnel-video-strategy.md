### Full-Funnel Video Strategy

**Top of Funnel (Awareness):**
- Broad targeting
- Brand storytelling
- Emotional connection
- Long-form when engaging
- Frequency for recall

**Middle of Funnel (Consideration):**
- Retargeting engaged viewers
- Product demonstrations
- Social proof
- Educational content
- Comparison content

**Bottom of Funnel (Conversion):**
- Retargeting intent signals
- Direct response creative
- Strong CTAs
- Limited-time offers
- Testimonials

### Full-Funnel Video Strategy

**Integrated Campaign Planning:**
Build video advertising that moves audiences through the funnel:

**Awareness Phase:**
- Broad targeting
- Brand storytelling
- Emotional connection
- Longer formats acceptable
- Frequency for recall

**Consideration Phase:**
- Retargeting aware audiences
- Product/service education
- Feature demonstration
- Testimonial integration
- Comparison content

**Conversion Phase:**
- Direct response creative
- Strong CTAs
- Limited-time offers
- Social proof
- Friction reduction

**Retention/Loyalty:**
- Customer appreciation
- Cross-sell/upsell
- Referral encouragement
- Community building
