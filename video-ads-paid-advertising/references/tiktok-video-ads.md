### TikTok Video Ads

**Ad Formats:**
- In-Feed ads
- TopView (first thing users see)
- Brand Takeover
- Branded Hashtag Challenges
- Branded Effects
- Spark Ads (boost organic content)

**Creative Requirements:**
- Vertical (9:16)
- 9-60 seconds (15-30 optimal)
- Native, entertaining content
- Sound-on expected
- Trend-aware

**Best Practices:**
- Don't make ads, make TikToks
- Hook in first second
- Native creator aesthetic
- Trending sounds/formats
- User-generated style
