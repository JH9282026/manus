# Change Metrics and Measurement

## Measurement Framework

### Leading Indicators
Predict future success and enable proactive intervention.

| Metric | Measurement Method | Target |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Awareness levels | Survey, quiz | >90% aware |
| Training completion | LMS tracking | >95% complete |
| Communication reach | Open rates, attendance | >80% reached |
| Sponsor engagement | Activity tracking | Active sponsorship |
| Change agent activation | Activity metrics | Fully activated |

### Lagging Indicators
Confirm actual adoption and behavior change.

| Metric | Measurement Method | Target |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Adoption rate | System usage, observation | >85% adoption |
| Proficiency levels | Assessment, observation | Meeting standard |
| Process compliance | Audit, data analysis | >95% compliance |
| Error rates | Quality metrics | Reduced from baseline |
| Productivity | Performance data | Meeting targets |

---

## ADKAR Measurement

### Assessment Approach
Measure each ADKAR element on a 1-5 scale:

- **1**: No awareness/desire/knowledge/ability/reinforcement
- **3**: Partial—room for improvement
- **5**: Full achievement

### Barrier Point Identification
The lowest ADKAR element indicates the barrier point requiring focus.

## Change Health Scorecard

### Scorecard Components

| Dimension | Metrics | Weight |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Sponsorship | Visibility, advocacy | 20% |
| Communication | Reach, clarity, feedback | 20% |
| Training | Completion, proficiency | 20% |
| Adoption | Usage, compliance | 25% |
| Sustainability | Reinforcement, regression | 15% |

### Health Assessment
- **Green (80-100%)**: On track
- **Yellow (60-79%)**: At risk—intervention needed
- **Red (<60%)**: Critical—immediate action required

## Dashboard Design

### Essential Elements
- Overall change health score
- ADKAR progress by stakeholder group
- Adoption metrics trending
- Risk and issue summary
- Key activities completed/upcoming

### Reporting Cadence
- Weekly: Operational metrics and issues
- Monthly: Executive summary and trends
- Quarterly: Strategic assessment and adjustments
