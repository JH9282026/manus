# Resistance Management

## Understanding Resistance

Resistance is a natural human response to change. Understanding root causes enables effective intervention.

### Common Resistance Sources

| Source | Indicators | Intervention |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Fear of unknown | Anxiety, questions, avoidance | Communication, involvement |
| Loss of control | Pushback, complaints | Participation, choices |
| Concern about competence | Hesitation, questions about training | Training, coaching, support |
| Increased workload | Time concerns, overwhelm | Resources, prioritization |
| Past change failures | Skepticism, cynicism | Quick wins, transparency |
| Disagreement with change | Vocal opposition, alternatives | Listening, addressing concerns |

---

## Resistance Identification

### Visible Signs
- Vocal opposition or complaints
- Reduced participation
- Missed deadlines or deliverables
- Negative comments in meetings

### Hidden Signs
- Passive compliance without commitment
- Lack of engagement in training
- Workarounds and shortcuts
- Increased absenteeism

---

## Resistance Management Strategies

### Prevention
- Involve stakeholders early in design
- Communicate transparently and frequently
- Address concerns proactively
- Build sponsor and coalition support

### Intervention
- Listen actively to concerns
- Acknowledge feelings as valid
- Provide additional information or support
- Adjust approach where possible
- Escalate persistent issues

### Escalation
- Document resistance patterns
- Engage sponsors and leaders
- Provide coaching to managers
- Address through performance management if needed

---

## Manager Coaching for Resistance

### Conversation Framework

1. **Acknowledge** the resistance without judgment
2. **Explore** the underlying concerns
3. **Empathize** with the feelings
4. **Explain** the rationale and benefits
5. **Engage** in problem-solving together
6. **Agree** on next steps and support needed

### Key Coaching Questions
- "What concerns you most about this change?"
- "What would help you feel more confident?"
- "What support do you need from me?"
- "What would success look like for you?"
