# Governance & Stakeholders

Board governance and stakeholder management frameworks.

---

## Board Governance

### Board Structure
| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Chair | Board leadership, agenda, CEO relationship |
| Independent Directors | Oversight, governance, objectivity |
| Inside Directors | Operational knowledge, execution |
| Committee Chairs | Committee leadership, expertise |

### Common Committees
- **Audit**: Financial oversight, risk, compliance
- **Compensation**: Executive pay, incentives
- **Nominating/Governance**: Board composition, governance
- **Strategy**: Strategic oversight (sometimes)

### Board Meeting Best Practices
- Advance materials (5-7 days prior)
- Clear agenda with time allocations
- Executive session without management
- Action items and follow-up tracking

---

## Board Reporting

### CEO Report Structure
1. **Strategic Progress**: Key initiative updates
2. **Financial Performance**: Results vs. plan
3. **Operational Highlights**: Key metrics
4. **People/Culture**: Talent, engagement
5. **Risk Update**: Key risks and mitigation
6. **Outlook**: Forward view and priorities

### Board Deck Best Practices
- Executive summary upfront
- Data-driven with clear insights
- Balanced (good news and challenges)
- Clear recommendations for decisions
- Concise (aim for 20-30 pages core)

### Information Quality
- Accurate and verified
- Timely and relevant
- Complete context
- Clear implications
- Actionable insights

## Investor Relations

### Investor Communication Channels
| Channel | Frequency | Audience |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Earnings call | Quarterly | All investors |
| Investor day | Annual | Institutional |
| One-on-ones | Ongoing | Major holders |
| Conferences | Periodic | Prospective investors |
| SEC filings | Required | All stakeholders |

### Earnings Call Structure
1. Safe harbor statement
2. CEO strategic overview
3. CFO financial review
4. Q&A session

### Investor Messaging
- Consistent with strategy
- Balanced optimism and realism
- Forward-looking guidance (if provided)
- Clear capital allocation priorities

## Stakeholder Management

### Stakeholder Mapping
| Stakeholder | Interest | Influence | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|-----------|----------|
| High Interest/High Influence | Manage closely |
| High Interest/Low Influence | Keep informed |
| Low Interest/High Influence | Keep satisfied |
| Low Interest/Low Influence | Monitor |

### Employee Engagement
- Town halls and all-hands
- Skip-level meetings
- Engagement surveys
- Recognition programs
- Open door policies

### Customer Engagement
- Customer advisory boards
- Executive sponsor programs
- NPS and satisfaction tracking
- Customer visits and feedback

### Community Relations
- Corporate social responsibility
- Local community involvement
- Environmental initiatives
- Charitable giving programs
