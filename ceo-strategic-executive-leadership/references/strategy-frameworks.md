# Strategy Frameworks

Strategic planning frameworks and tools for executive leadership.

---

## Strategic Analysis Tools

### PESTLE Analysis
| Factor | Considerations |
|--------|----------------|
| Political | Government policy, stability, trade |
| Economic | Growth, inflation, exchange rates |
| Social | Demographics, culture, trends |
| Technological | Innovation, disruption, infrastructure |
| Legal | Regulations, compliance, litigation |
| Environmental | Climate, sustainability, resources |

### Porter's Five Forces
1. **Competitive Rivalry**: Intensity among existing competitors
2. **Threat of New Entrants**: Barriers to entry
3. **Threat of Substitutes**: Alternative solutions
4. **Supplier Power**: Supplier concentration and switching costs
5. **Buyer Power**: Customer concentration and alternatives

### SWOT Analysis
| Internal | External |
|----------|----------|
| Strengths | Opportunities |
| Weaknesses | Threats |

## Strategic Options

### Competitive Strategies (Porter)
- **Cost Leadership**: Lowest cost producer

## Strategic Planning Process

### Phase 1: Situation Analysis
- External environment assessment
- Internal capability review
- Competitive landscape mapping
- Performance baseline

### Phase 2: Strategy Formulation
- Vision and mission refinement
- Strategic options identification
- Option evaluation and selection
- Strategic pillar definition

### Phase 3: Strategy Articulation
- Goals and objectives setting
- Initiative identification
- Resource allocation
- Success metrics definition

### Phase 4: Implementation Planning
- Initiative prioritization
- Timeline development
- Accountability assignment
- Change management planning

---

## Strategic Plan Document

### Structure
1. **Executive Summary** (2-3 pages)
2. **Strategic Context** (5-10 pages)
3. **Vision, Mission, Values** (2-3 pages)
4. **Strategic Pillars** (10-15 pages)
5. **Goals and Initiatives** (10-15 pages)
6. **Financial Projections** (5-10 pages)
7. **Implementation Roadmap** (5-10 pages)
8. **Risk Assessment** (3-5 pages)

### Strategic Pillar Format
- Pillar statement
- Strategic rationale
- Key goals (3-5)
- Priority initiatives
- Success metrics
- Resource requirements
