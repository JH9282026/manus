# ABM Orchestration and Execution

Master multi-channel campaign orchestration, personalization tactics, and coordinated engagement strategies to deliver seamless account experiences.

## ABM Orchestration Framework

Orchestration is the strategic coordination of all sales and marketing activities across channels to create unified, personalized experiences for target accounts.

### Orchestration vs. Multi-Channel Marketing

**Traditional Multi-Channel Marketing:**
- Parallel campaigns across channels with limited integration
- Lead-centric focus on individual conversions
- Channel-specific metrics and optimization
- Siloed execution by channel specialists
- Generic messaging with basic personalization

**ABM Orchestration:**
- Strategically coordinated touchpoints across all channels
- Account-centric focus on buying committee engagement
- Unified account-level metrics and attribution
- Cross-functional collaboration and shared account plans
- Deep personalization based on account intelligence

### The Orchestration Cycle

ABM orchestration follows a continuous, data-driven cycle:

**1. Signal Detection and Monitoring**
- **Intent Data:** Monitor topic research, competitive analysis, solution exploration
- **Behavioral Data:** Track website visits, content consumption, email engagement
- **CRM Activity:** Sales interactions, meeting outcomes, opportunity progression
- **Third-Party Insights:** News, funding events, leadership changes, hiring patterns
- **Engagement Scoring:** Aggregate signals into account-level engagement scores

**2. Trigger Activation**
- **Threshold-Based Triggers:** Activate when engagement score reaches predefined level
- **Event-Based Triggers:** Launch campaigns based on specific actions (demo request, pricing page visit)
- **Time-Based Triggers:** Scheduled touchpoints based on buying stage or campaign timeline
- **Manual Triggers:** Sales-initiated campaigns for strategic accounts or opportunities

**3. Play Selection and Execution**
- **Play Library:** Pre-built, tested campaign sequences for common scenarios
- **Personalization Layer:** Customize messaging, content, and offers based on account attributes
- **Channel Selection:** Choose optimal channels based on persona preferences and buying stage
- **Sequencing:** Coordinate timing and order of touchpoints across channels

**4. Multi-Channel Delivery**
- **Coordinated Launch:** Simultaneous or sequenced activation across channels
- **Consistent Messaging:** Unified value proposition and positioning across all touchpoints
- **Progressive Engagement:** Build momentum through increasing levels of interaction
- **Multi-Threading:** Engage multiple stakeholders with persona-specific messaging

**5. Real-Time Feedback and Response**
- **Engagement Tracking:** Monitor opens, clicks, visits, downloads, meeting requests
- **Behavioral Analysis:** Identify patterns indicating buying stage progression or stalling
- **Dynamic Adjustment:** Modify campaign based on account responses (advance, pause, pivot, restart)
- **Sales Notification:** Alert sales team when accounts reach engagement thresholds

**6. Optimization and Learning**
- **Performance Analysis:** Evaluate campaign effectiveness by account tier and segment
- **A/B Testing:** Test messaging, offers, channels, and timing variations
- **Play Refinement:** Update playbooks based on what works and what doesn't
- **Best Practice Sharing:** Document and replicate high-performing approaches

## Multi-Channel Engagement Tactics

### Digital Channels

#### Account-Based Advertising

Target specific accounts and stakeholders with personalized ads:

**LinkedIn Advertising**
- **Account Targeting:** Upload target account list for precise targeting
- **Contact Targeting:** Target specific job titles and roles within accounts
- **Matched Audiences:** Retarget website visitors and email list contacts
- **Ad Formats:** Sponsored content, message ads, dynamic ads, video ads
- **Best Practices:**
  - Use account-specific messaging for Tier 1 accounts
  - Create industry/vertical-specific creative for Tier 2
  - Test multiple ad variations and optimize based on engagement
  - Coordinate ad messaging with email and sales outreach

**Display and Programmatic Advertising**
- **IP-Based Targeting:** Target company IP addresses to reach employees
- **Reverse IP Lookup:** Identify companies visiting your website for retargeting
- **Contextual Targeting:** Show ads on sites target accounts visit
- **Platforms:** RollWorks, Demandbase, Terminus, Metadata.io
- **Best Practices:**
  - Use display for awareness and brand building
  - Retarget engaged accounts with more specific offers
  - Frequency cap to avoid ad fatigue
  - Align creative with account's buying stage

**Social Media Advertising**
- **Facebook/Instagram:** Reach decision-makers in professional and personal contexts
- **Twitter:** Engage with industry conversations and thought leaders
- **YouTube:** Video content for product education and thought leadership
- **Best Practices:**
  - Use social for top-of-funnel awareness and engagement
  - Create educational, value-driven content (not overtly promotional)
  - Engage with account stakeholders' posts and content
  - Monitor social listening for account insights

#### Personalized Web Experiences

Customize website content for target accounts:

**Dynamic Landing Pages**
- **Account-Specific Pages:** Custom URLs with account name, logo, and tailored messaging
- **Industry Pages:** Vertical-specific content, case studies, and value propositions
- **Role-Based Pages:** Persona-specific messaging and content journeys
- **Tools:** Mutiny, Foundry, Demandbase, Drift

**Website Personalization**
- **Hero Message Customization:** Change headline and value prop based on account
- **Case Study Matching:** Show relevant customer stories from same industry
- **CTA Personalization:** Tailor calls-to-action based on buying stage
- **Content Recommendations:** Suggest relevant resources based on account profile

**Conversational Marketing**
- **Account-Based Chat:** Prioritize chat routing for target accounts
- **Personalized Greetings:** Welcome visitors from target accounts by name/company
- **Intelligent Chatbots:** Qualify and route based on account tier and engagement
- **Meeting Scheduling:** Streamline booking for high-priority accounts
- **Tools:** Drift, Qualified, Conversica

#### Email Orchestration

Coordinate personalized email campaigns:

**Email Segmentation Strategies**
- **Account Tier:** Different cadences and personalization levels by tier
- **Buying Stage:** Stage-appropriate content and messaging
- **Persona:** Role-specific value propositions and pain points
- **Engagement Level:** Adjust frequency based on responsiveness

**Email Campaign Types**

**Awareness Campaigns:**
- **Objective:** Introduce brand and establish credibility
- **Content:** Industry insights, research reports, thought leadership
- **Cadence:** Monthly or bi-weekly
- **Personalization:** Industry/vertical-specific

**Nurture Campaigns:**
- **Objective:** Build relationship and demonstrate value
- **Content:** Use cases, customer stories, educational resources
- **Cadence:** Weekly or bi-weekly
- **Personalization:** Account and persona-specific

**Engagement Campaigns:**
- **Objective:** Drive specific action (demo, event, meeting)
- **Content:** Product information, ROI calculators, invitations
- **Cadence:** Multi-touch over 2-4 weeks
- **Personalization:** Account-specific offers and messaging

**Re-Engagement Campaigns:**
- **Objective:** Revive dormant accounts
- **Content:** New features, customer success stories, special offers
- **Cadence:** 3-5 touch campaign
- **Personalization:** Reference past engagement and interests

**Email Best Practices:**
- Coordinate email timing with sales outreach (not same day)
- Use plain-text emails for high-touch, personalized outreach
- A/B test subject lines, messaging, and CTAs
- Monitor engagement and adjust send frequency
- Ensure mobile optimization
- Include clear, singular call-to-action

### Direct and Experiential Channels

#### Direct Mail and Gifting

Create memorable physical touchpoints:

**Strategic Direct Mail**
- **Personalized Packages:** Custom boxes with account-specific messaging and gifts
- **Dimensional Mailers:** 3D items that stand out and get opened
- **Handwritten Notes:** Personal touches from executives or account teams
- **Triggered Sends:** Automated based on engagement milestones or events
- **Platforms:** Sendoso, Alyce, Postal.io, Reachdesk

**Gifting Strategies**

**Tier 1 Strategic Accounts:**
- **Budget:** $200-$1,000+ per account
- **Approach:** High-value, highly personalized gifts
- **Examples:** Custom artwork, premium experiences, executive gifts
- **Timing:** Key milestones, holidays, relationship building

**Tier 2 Accounts:**
- **Budget:** $50-$200 per account
- **Approach:** Thoughtful, relevant gifts with personalization
- **Examples:** Books, branded items, local specialties, gift cards
- **Timing:** Campaign launches, event invitations, thank-yous

**Tier 3 Accounts:**
- **Budget:** $10-$50 per account
- **Approach:** Scalable, brand-building items
- **Examples:** Branded swag, digital gift cards, small treats
- **Timing:** Automated triggers, bulk campaigns

**Direct Mail Best Practices:**
- Research recipient interests for personalization
- Include clear next step or call-to-action
- Coordinate with digital touchpoints (email, ads, sales call)
- Track delivery and follow up promptly
- Respect company policies on gift acceptance
- Measure impact on engagement and pipeline

#### Executive Events and Experiences

Build relationships through exclusive experiences:

**Executive Dinners and Roundtables**
- **Format:** Intimate gatherings (8-15 attendees) with peer-to-peer discussion
- **Topics:** Industry trends, business challenges, best practices (not product pitches)
- **Attendees:** C-level and VP-level from target accounts plus customers
- **Facilitation:** Moderated discussion with thought-provoking questions
- **Follow-Up:** Personalized thank-you, summary of insights, next steps

**VIP Experiences**
- **Sporting Events:** Premium seats at major games or tournaments
- **Entertainment:** Concerts, theater, exclusive performances
- **Culinary:** Chef's table dinners, wine tastings, cooking classes
- **Adventure:** Golf outings, fishing trips, unique local experiences
- **Best For:** Relationship building with key decision-makers

**Industry Conferences and Trade Shows**
- **Pre-Event:** Personalized invitations, meeting scheduling, VIP passes
- **During Event:** Private meetings, hospitality suite, exclusive sessions
- **Post-Event:** Follow-up meetings, content sharing, relationship nurturing
- **ABM Booth Strategy:** Prioritize target account visitors, fast-track demos

**Workshops and Collaborative Sessions**
- **Format:** Working sessions to solve specific business challenges
- **Approach:** Consultative, value-first (not sales-focused)
- **Deliverables:** Custom recommendations, roadmaps, frameworks
- **Participants:** Cross-functional teams from account and vendor
- **Outcome:** Demonstrate expertise, build trust, identify opportunities

### Sales Channels

#### Account-Based SDR Outreach

Coordinate sales development with marketing campaigns:

**SDR-Marketing Alignment**
- **Shared Account Lists:** SDRs focus exclusively on ABM target accounts
- **Campaign Coordination:** SDR outreach timed with marketing campaigns
- **Intelligence Sharing:** Marketing provides account insights to SDRs
- **Engagement Handoffs:** Marketing-engaged accounts prioritized by SDRs
- **Feedback Loop:** SDRs report account insights back to marketing

**Multi-Channel SDR Tactics**
- **Phone:** Personalized calls referencing marketing engagement
- **Email:** Coordinated with marketing emails (different days/messaging)
- **LinkedIn:** Connection requests, InMail, engagement with posts
- **Video:** Personalized video messages for high-priority accounts
- **Social Selling:** Engage with account content, share relevant insights

**SDR Cadence Design**

**Tier 1 Accounts (High-Touch):**
- **Duration:** 4-6 weeks
- **Touchpoints:** 15-20 attempts across channels
- **Personalization:** Highly customized messaging and research
- **Channels:** Phone, email, LinkedIn, video, direct mail

**Tier 2 Accounts (Medium-Touch):**
- **Duration:** 3-4 weeks
- **Touchpoints:** 10-12 attempts
- **Personalization:** Industry/role-specific messaging
- **Channels:** Phone, email, LinkedIn

**Tier 3 Accounts (Low-Touch):**
- **Duration:** 2-3 weeks
- **Touchpoints:** 6-8 attempts
- **Personalization:** Template-based with dynamic fields
- **Channels:** Email, LinkedIn

#### Executive Engagement

Leverage executive relationships for strategic accounts:

**Executive-to-Executive Outreach**
- **Approach:** C-level to C-level communication
- **Format:** Personalized emails, LinkedIn messages, phone calls
- **Messaging:** Strategic business insights, peer introductions, exclusive invitations
- **Timing:** Key account milestones, strategic opportunities, relationship building

**Executive Sponsorship Programs**
- **Model:** Assign executive sponsors to Tier 1 strategic accounts
- **Responsibilities:** Quarterly business reviews, escalation point, strategic advisor
- **Benefits:** Demonstrates commitment, builds trust, differentiates from competitors
- **Cadence:** Quarterly touchpoints minimum, more frequent for active opportunities

**Executive Content and Thought Leadership**
- **Formats:** Bylined articles, speaking engagements, podcast interviews, video messages
- **Distribution:** Share with target accounts, promote on social media, include in campaigns
- **Topics:** Industry trends, business strategy, innovation, leadership insights
- **Impact:** Builds credibility, generates engagement, creates conversation starters

## Campaign Playbooks

Pre-built, tested campaign sequences for common ABM scenarios:

### Playbook 1: Net-New Account Acquisition

**Objective:** Engage cold accounts with no prior relationship

**Duration:** 12 weeks

**Week 1-2: Awareness**
- LinkedIn ads with thought leadership content
- Display ads for brand awareness
- Email 1: Industry insights and research report

**Week 3-4: Education**
- Retargeting ads for engaged accounts
- Email 2: Use case and customer success story
- Social selling: Engage with stakeholder posts

**Week 5-6: Engagement**
- Personalized direct mail package
- Email 3: Invitation to webinar or event
- SDR outreach: Phone and LinkedIn

**Week 7-8: Consideration**
- Email 4: Product overview and demo offer
- Personalized landing page with account-specific content
- Sales outreach: Meeting request

**Week 9-12: Conversion**
- Executive outreach for non-responsive accounts
- Retargeting with demo and trial offers
- Final email: Exclusive offer or limited-time opportunity

### Playbook 2: Pipeline Acceleration

**Objective:** Accelerate active opportunities through sales stages

**Duration:** 6-8 weeks

**Stage: Discovery/Qualification**
- Email: Educational content on business challenges
- Ads: Customer success stories and ROI data
- Sales: Discovery call and needs assessment

**Stage: Solution Presentation**
- Email: Product guides and technical documentation
- Personalized demo and proof of concept
- Sales: Solution presentation and stakeholder meetings

**Stage: Evaluation**
- Email: Competitive comparison and differentiation
- ROI calculator and business case template
- Sales: Executive briefing and reference calls

**Stage: Negotiation**
- Email: Implementation roadmap and success plan
- Executive engagement and relationship building
- Sales: Proposal, negotiation, and contract finalization

### Playbook 3: Customer Expansion

**Objective:** Drive upsell and cross-sell within existing accounts

**Duration:** Ongoing

**Quarterly Business Reviews:**
- Review performance metrics and value delivered
- Identify new use cases and expansion opportunities
- Present product roadmap and upcoming features

**Product Launch Campaigns:**
- Email: New feature announcements and benefits
- Personalized demos of new capabilities
- Exclusive early access or beta programs

**Usage-Based Triggers:**
- Monitor product adoption and usage patterns
- Identify accounts approaching plan limits
- Proactive outreach with upgrade recommendations

**Advocacy and Referral:**
- Request case studies and testimonials
- Invite to speak at events or participate in content
- Referral program with incentives

## Personalization at Scale

### Personalization Levels

**Level 1: Segment Personalization (Tier 3)**
- Industry/vertical-specific messaging
- Company size-based content
- Geographic localization
- **Effort:** Low (template-based)
- **Impact:** Moderate

**Level 2: Account Personalization (Tier 2)**
- Company name and logo
- Account-specific pain points
- Relevant case studies from similar companies
- Custom ROI calculations
- **Effort:** Medium (semi-automated)
- **Impact:** High

**Level 3: Stakeholder Personalization (Tier 1)**
- Individual name and role
- Persona-specific value propositions
- Personalized video messages
- Custom research and insights
- **Effort:** High (manual)
- **Impact:** Very High

### AI-Powered Personalization

**Content Recommendations:**
- Analyze engagement patterns to suggest relevant content
- Predict next-best content based on buying stage
- Personalize email content blocks dynamically

**Dynamic Messaging:**
- Adjust value propositions based on account attributes
- Insert relevant statistics and data points
- Customize competitive positioning

**Predictive Engagement:**
- Identify optimal send times for each account
- Predict channel preferences by persona
- Recommend next-best actions for sales and marketing

- 6sense: AI-powered orchestration and personalization
- Drift: Conversational AI and chatbots
- Mutiny: Website personalization
