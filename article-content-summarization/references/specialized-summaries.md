# Specialized Document Summaries

Handle legal, technical, and domain-specific content.

---

## Legal Document Summaries

### Plain-Language Translation

Convert legal language to accessible terms:
- Replace jargon with everyday words
- Explain legal concepts
- Highlight key obligations

### Structure

**Parties**
- Who is involved

**Key Terms**
- Obligations of each party
- Payment terms
- Duration

**Important Clauses**
- Termination conditions
- Liability limitations
- Dispute resolution

**Action Items**
- What you need to do
- Deadlines
- Review recommendations

### Flagging Concerns

Mark areas requiring legal review:
- Unusual terms
- High-risk clauses
- Ambiguous language

---

## Technical Document Summaries

### Preserving Accuracy

- Keep technical terms when necessary
- Define terms on first use
- Maintain precision

### Structure

**Purpose**
- What problem this solves

**Key Specifications**
- Critical parameters
- Requirements
- Limitations

**Implementation**
- How it works (high-level)
- Dependencies
- Constraints

---

## Medical/Healthcare Summaries

### Patient-Friendly Language

- Explain medical terms
- Use analogies
- Focus on practical implications

### Clinical Summary

- Diagnosis
- Treatment plan
- Expected outcomes
- Follow-up required

---

## Financial Report Summaries

### Key Metrics Focus

- Revenue and growth
- Profitability
- Key ratios
- Guidance/outlook

### Structure

**Performance Snapshot**
- Headline numbers
- Comparison to prior period

**Key Drivers**
- What caused results

**Outlook**
- Forward guidance
- Risks and opportunities

---

## Policy Document Summaries

### Structure

**Purpose**
- Why this policy exists

**Key Requirements**
- What you must do
- What you must not do

**Procedures**
- How to comply

**Consequences**
- What happens if violated
