# Brand Governance & Audits

Frameworks for managing brand consistency and conducting audits.

---

## Governance Framework

### RACI Matrix

| Activity | Brand Team | Marketing | Agencies | Executives |
|----------|-----------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Strategy | A/R | C | C | A |
| Identity | R | C | C | I |
| Guidelines | R | I | I | A |
| Campaigns | C | R | R | I |
| Compliance | R | A | A | I |

R=Responsible, A=Accountable, C=Consulted, I=Informed

---

## Brand Audit Framework

### Audit Scope

1. **Visual Consistency**
   - Logo usage compliance
   - Color accuracy
   - Typography adherence
   - Imagery style alignment

2. **Verbal Consistency**
   - Messaging alignment
   - Tone of voice
   - Terminology usage
   - Tagline application

3. **Touchpoint Review**
   - Website
   - Social media
   - Advertising
   - Sales materials
   - Product/packaging
   - Physical spaces

### Audit Scoring

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 90-100% | Excellent | Maintain |
| 75-89% | Good | Minor refinements |
| 60-74% | Fair | Training needed |
| <60% | Poor | Intervention required |

## Global Brand Management

### Localization Framework

| Element | Global Mandate | Local Adaptation |
|---------|---------------|------------------|
| Logo | Fixed | None |
| Colors | Primary fixed | Secondary flexible |
| Typography | Latin fixed | Non-Latin approved alternatives |
| Messaging | Core pillars fixed | Local language/tone |
| Imagery | Style fixed | Local representation |

### Regional Governance

- Global brand council (strategy)
- Regional brand managers (execution)
- Local marketing (adaptation)
- Quarterly alignment reviews

## Digital Asset Management

### DAM System Requirements

- Centralized asset repository
- Version control
- Access permissions by role
- Search and filtering
- Usage tracking
- Automatic expiration

### Asset Categories

- Logos (all formats, sizes)
- Templates (presentations, documents)
- Photography library
- Icons and illustrations
- Video assets
- Audio/music
