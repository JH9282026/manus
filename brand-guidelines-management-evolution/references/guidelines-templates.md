## Logo Section Template

### Logo Anatomy

- Construction grid with proportions
- Primary lockup specifications
- Secondary variations
- Icon/symbol standalone

### Clear Space

```
Minimum clear space = X (height of symbol element)
Apply X to all sides of logo
```

### Minimum Sizes

| Format | Minimum Width |
|--------|---------------|
| Print | 1 inch (25mm) |
| Digital | 80 pixels |
| Favicon | 16x16 pixels |

### Usage Rules

**DO:**
- Use approved logo files only
- Maintain clear space
- Use on approved backgrounds

**DON'T:**
- Stretch or distort
- Add effects (shadows, glows)
- Change colors outside palette
- Rotate or flip
- Place on busy backgrounds
