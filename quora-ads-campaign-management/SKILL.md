# Quora Ads Campaign Management

Manage comprehensive Quora Ads campaigns to reach high-intent audiences actively researching solutions on the knowledge platform.

## Overview

Quora Ads enables advertisers to connect with users at the moment they're seeking information and solutions. This skill covers all ad formats (text, image, video, promoted answers, lead gen forms, app installs), targeting options (contextual, audience, behavioral, broad), bidding strategies (CPC, CPM, conversion-optimized), and campaign optimization for various business objectives.

## Ad Formats

| Format | Best For | Key Benefits | Specs |
|--------|----------|--------------|-------|
| **Text Ads** | Direct response, testing | Quick creation, cost-effective, native appearance | Headline: 65 chars, Body: 105 chars |
| **Image Ads** | Conversion, product showcase | High visibility, multiple placements | 16:9 to 4:5 ratio, 580px min width |
| **Video Ads** | Brand awareness, storytelling | Strong engagement, higher recall | 3-60 sec, 16:9, MP4/MOV |
| **Promoted Answers** | Thought leadership, traffic | Establishes authority, evergreen content | 200+ chars (200-600 words recommended) |
| **Lead Gen Forms** | Lead generation | Frictionless capture, pre-populated forms | Appends to any format |
| **App Install Ads** | App downloads | Direct app store linking | Appends to any format |

### Text Ads

**Appearance:** Simple headline and description blending into Quora interface

**Placements:**
- User feeds
- Topic feeds
- Question pages
- Quora Digest newsletter

**Specifications:**
- Business Name: 40 characters max
- Headline: 65 characters max
- Body Text: 105 characters max
- Display URL: 30 characters max
- Call-to-Action: 16 options available

**Best Practices:**
- Keep messaging clear and concise
- Test multiple headline variations
- Use question-style headlines
- Include specific value propositions

### Image Ads

**Appearance:** Visual ads with headline and description, converting to text ads in text-only placements

**Placements:**
- User feeds (image)
- Question pages (text fallback)
- Quora Digest (text fallback)

**Specifications:**
- Headline: 65 characters max
- Body Text: 105 characters max
- Image Type: PNG, JPG
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 to 4:5
- Dimensions: Minimum 580 pixels width (1200x628 recommended for 16:9)
- Logo: PNG/JPG, 500x500 pixels (displayed as circle)
- Call-to-Action: 16 options

**Best Practices:**
- Use high-quality, relevant images
- Include text overlay for clarity
- Test different aspect ratios
- Ensure logo is clear at small sizes

### Video Ads

**Appearance:** Immersive video content in user and topic feeds

**Placements:**
- User feeds (desktop and mobile)
- Topic feeds
- NOT on question pages

**Specifications:**
- File Type: MOV, MP4
- Audio Format: AAC
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Resolution: 1280x720 recommended (min: 640x360, max: 1920x1080)
- Frame Rate: ≤ 30 fps
- File Size: ≤ 70 MB
- Duration: 3-60 seconds
- Headline: 65 characters max
- Description: 105 characters max
- Business Name: 40 characters max
- Logo: PNG/JPG, 500x500 pixels
- Captions & Sound: Recommended
- Call-to-Action: 16 options

**Best Practices:**
- Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
- Design for sound-off viewing (use captions)
- Keep videos concise (15-30 seconds ideal)
- Show product/service in action
- Include clear call-to-action

### Promoted Answers

**Unique to Quora:** Promote detailed, helpful answers to relevant questions

**Appearance:** Clearly marked as "promoted by [brand]" within answer threads

**Specifications:**
- Body Text: Minimum 200 characters (200-600 words recommended)
- Business Name: 40 characters max
- Company Logo: PNG/JPG, 500x500 pixels

**Best Practices:**
- Provide genuine value (not just promotion)
- Answer the question thoroughly
- Include relevant examples and data
- Link to relevant resources
- Establish thought leadership
- Create evergreen content

**Benefits:**
- Builds long-term brand authority
- Drives qualified traffic
- Positions brand as expert
- Content remains discoverable over time

### Lead Gen Forms

**Functionality:** Collect user information directly on Quora without leaving platform

**Can Append To:** Image, Text, Video, and Promoted Answers

**Placements:**
- Home feeds
- Topic feeds
- Question pages

**Specifications:**
- Headline: 65 characters max
- Contact Fields: First/Last Name, Email, Business Email, Phone Number
- Employment Fields: Job Title, Company Name, Company Size
- Location Fields: City, State, Zip Code, Country
- Confirmation Message: 65 characters max
- Logo: PNG/JPG, 500x500 pixels
- Landing Page: Optional confirmation message and URL

**Benefits:**
- Frictionless lead capture
- Pre-populated forms (higher completion rates)
- Direct CRM integration
- Detailed lead quality reporting

**Best Practices:**
- Request only essential information
- Clear value proposition in headline
- Compelling confirmation message
- Follow up promptly with leads

### App Install Ads

**Functionality:** Drive mobile app downloads directly from Quora

**Can Append To:** Any ad format (Image, Text, Video, Promoted Answers)

**Specifications:**
- Headline: 40-60 characters recommended
- Body Text: 80+ characters
- Call-to-Action: 16 options (tailored for app installs)

**Benefits:**
- Direct app store linking
- Device targeting capabilities
- Seamless user experience

**Best For:**
- App user acquisition
- Re-engagement campaigns
- App feature promotion

## Targeting Options

### Contextual Targeting

Place ads alongside relevant content:

**Topic Targeting:**
- Target questions and feeds tagged with specific topics
- Range from broad to niche subjects
- Balance scale with relevance
- Use for: Brand awareness, reaching interested audiences

**Keyword Targeting:**
- Ads appear next to questions containing specified keywords
- Reach users at moment of interest
- Target questions in bulk
- Use for: High-intent traffic, specific solutions

**Question Targeting:**
- Place ads on specific question pages
- Hyper-targeted messaging
- Highest relevance
- Use for: Boosting conversions, niche audiences

### Audience Targeting

Leverage existing data:

**Website Traffic Targeting:**
- Reconnect with users who visited your website
- Requires Quora Pixel installation
- Use for: Retargeting, conversion optimization

**List Match Targeting:**
- Upload email lists to target/exclude users
- Build lookalike audiences
- Use for: Customer targeting, exclusions, scaling

**Lookalike Targeting:**
- Find new users similar to existing audiences
- Based on website visitors or email lists
- Use for: Scaling campaigns, new customer acquisition

**B2B & ABM Targeting (via Bombora partnership):**
- Job title
- Company size
- Industry
- Use for: Account-Based Marketing, B2B campaigns

### Behavioral Targeting

Target based on past engagement:

**Interest Targeting:**
- Users who engaged with specific content on Quora
- Indicates interest in topics
- Wide audience reach
- Use for: Broad awareness, interest-based campaigns

**Keyword History Targeting:**
- Users who previously read questions with specified keywords
- Follow-up messaging
- Use for: Nurturing interested users

**Question History Targeting:**
- Users who read specific questions
- Deliver follow-up messages
- Use for: Deepening engagement, conversion

**Answer History Targeting:**
- Users who read Promoted Answers or other answers
- Re-engage interested users
- Use for: Moving users down funnel

### Broad Targeting

**Auto-targeting:**
- Quora's machine learning determines best targeting
- Optimizes for efficiency over time
- Widest reach
- Use for: Discovery, letting algorithm optimize

### Secondary Targeting Filters

Layer on top of primary targeting:

**Location:**
- Country, state, city, zip codes
- Use for: Geographic campaigns, local businesses

**Platform & Browser:**
- Desktop or mobile
- Specific browsers
- Use for: Device-specific campaigns

**Gender:**
- Male, female, or all
- Use for: Gender-specific products/services

**Exclusions:**
- Exclude specific questions, keywords, locations, audiences
- Account and ad set levels
- Use for: Brand safety, avoiding irrelevant traffic

## Bidding Strategies

### Cost Per Click (CPC)

**How It Works:**
- Bid and pay per click on ad
- Minimum bid: $0.01
- Quora provides suggested bid based on targeting

**When to Use:**
- Traffic campaigns
- Conversion campaigns (with sufficient budget)
- Testing new audiences

**Best Practices:**
- Start with suggested bid
- Bid your true value
- Monitor click quality
- Adjust based on conversion data

### Cost Per Impression (CPM)

**How It Works:**
- Bid for 1000 impressions
- Pay per impression
- Minimum bid: $0.20

**When to Use:**
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Broad branding initiatives
- High CTR expected

**Best Practices:**
- Use for awareness objectives
- Ensure creative is compelling
- Monitor frequency

### Conversion Optimized Bidding (Target CPA)

**How It Works:**
- Bid for target Cost Per Action
- Pay per impression
- Machine learning optimizes delivery

**Requirements:**
- Quora Pixel installed
- At least 20 conversions per ad set

**When to Use:**
- Conversion campaigns with sufficient data
- Scaling proven campaigns
- Automating bid management

**Best Practices:**
- Don't start with this (need conversion history)
- Set realistic target CPA
- Allow time for algorithm to learn
- Actual CPA may differ from target

### Auto-Bidding

**How It Works:**
- Machine learning dynamically adjusts bids
- Optimizes for performance
- Available for CPA and CPC

**When to Use:**
- Campaigns with sufficient data
- Hands-off optimization
- Scaling campaigns

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign Level

**Set:**
- Campaign objective (Conversions, App Installs, Traffic, Awareness, Lead Generation)
- Budget (daily or lifetime)
- Campaign name

**Best Practices:**
- Organize by budget allocation
- Use daily budgets for consistent spend
- Separate campaigns for different products/initiatives
- Naming convention: "Category - Targeting Type - Geography"

### Ad Set Level

**Set:**
- Primary targeting (Contextual, Audience, Behavioral, Broad)
- Secondary filters (Location, platform, gender, exclusions)
- Bidding strategy and bid amount
- Schedule (optional)

**Best Practices:**
- Separate ad sets for performance analysis (e.g., mobile vs desktop)
- Use "Bulk Add" for cross-platform keywords
- Convert Topics to Interests for greater scale
- Bid based on true value

### Ad Level

**Create:**
- Ad format (Video, Image, Text, Promoted Answer)
- Creative assets
- Call-to-action
- Landing page URL
- Optional: Lead Gen Form or App Install link

**Best Practices:**
- Test 2-4 ads per ad set
- Use unique UTM parameters for tracking
- Prepare text ad fallbacks for image ads
- Test Promoted Answers in separate campaigns
- Ensure ad-landing page consistency
- Follow Quora ad guidelines
