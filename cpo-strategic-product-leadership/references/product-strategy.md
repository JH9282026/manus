# Product Strategy

Frameworks for product vision, portfolio strategy, and competitive positioning.

---

## Vision Development

### Vision Statement Template
```
For [target customer]
Who [has this need/problem]
Our [product name]
Is a [product category]
That [key benefit/value proposition]
Unlike [competitive alternative]
Our product [key differentiator]
```

### Vision Characteristics
- Inspiring and aspirational
- Clear and memorable
- Guides decision-making
- Durable but adaptable

## Market Analysis

### TAM/SAM/SOM
- **TAM**: Total available market
- **SAM**: Serviceable available market
- **SOM**: Serviceable obtainable market

### Competitive Analysis
- Direct competitors
- Indirect competitors
- Substitutes
- Emerging threats

## Portfolio Strategy

### Three Horizons Model
- **H1**: Core business (70%)
- **H2**: Adjacent growth (20%)
- **H3**: Transformational (10%)

### Product Lifecycle
| Stage | Focus | Investment |
|-------|-------|------------|
| Introduction | Market education | High |
| Growth | Scale and expand | High |
| Maturity | Optimize and defend | Moderate |
| Decline | Harvest or sunset | Low |
