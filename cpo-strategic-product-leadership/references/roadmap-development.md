# Roadmap Development

Prioritization frameworks and roadmap communication strategies.

---

## Prioritization Frameworks

### RICE Scoring
```
Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort
```

| Factor | Scale |
|--------|-------|
| Reach | # of users in time period |
| Impact | 0.25 (min), 0.5, 1, 2, 3 (massive) |
| Confidence | 100%, 80%, 50% |
| Effort | Person-months |

### WSJF (Weighted Shortest Job First)
```
WSJF = Cost of Delay / Job Size

Cost of Delay = User Value + Time Value + Risk Reduction
```

### MoSCoW
- **Must Have**: Critical requirements
- **Should Have**: Important but not critical
- **Could Have**: Nice to have
- **Won't Have**: Not in scope

---

## Roadmap Formats

### NOW/NEXT/LATER
- **Now**: In progress (high confidence)
- **Next**: Coming soon (medium confidence)
- **Later**: Future consideration (low confidence)

### Outcome-Based Roadmap
Focus on outcomes, not features:
- "Reduce churn by 20%"
- "Increase activation rate"
- "Expand to new market"

---

## Stakeholder Communication

### By Audience
| Audience | Focus | Detail Level |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Board | Strategy and outcomes | High-level |
| Executives | Priorities and timelines | Summary |
| Sales | What's coming and when | Moderate |
| Engineering | Technical requirements | Detailed |
| Customers | Value and timing | Selective |

### Communication Cadence
- Board: Quarterly
- Leadership: Monthly
- Team: Sprint planning
- Public: Major releases
