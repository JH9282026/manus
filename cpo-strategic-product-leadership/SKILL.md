Deliver comprehensive product strategy, innovation leadership, and product portfolio management to create market-winning products and drive sustainable revenue growth.

## Product Strategy Framework

### Vision Development
1. Market opportunity analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM)
2. Customer segment prioritization
3. Competitive differentiation
4. Long-term product direction

### Portfolio Strategy
- Core products (revenue drivers)
- Growth products (scaling investments)
- Venture products (future bets)
- Sunset products (managed decline)

## Roadmap Prioritization

### RICE Framework
- **R**each: How many users impacted
- **I**mpact: Degree of impact (0.25, 0.5, 1, 2, 3)
- **C**onfidence: Evidence strength (%)
- **E**ffort: Person-months required
- **Score**: (R × I × C) / E

### Roadmap Formats
| Format | Audience | Time Horizon |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| NOW/NEXT/LATER | All stakeholders | Flexible |
| Quarterly | Leadership | 4 quarters |
| Release | Engineering | 1-2 sprints |
| Theme-based | Strategy | Annual |

## Product-Market Fit Indicators

- 40%+ "very disappointed" if product gone (Sean Ellis test)
- Organic growth and word-of-mouth
- Increasing retention curves
- Customer willingness to pay
- Repeat usage patterns
