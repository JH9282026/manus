
### Pre-Flight Procedures

**Location Assessment**:
1. Check airspace restrictions (apps: AirMap, B4UFly, UAV Forecast)
2. Identify potential hazards (power lines, trees, birds)
3. Assess weather conditions (wind, precipitation)
4. Plan flight path and emergency landing zones
5. Identify people and property that could be affected

**Equipment Checks**:
- Battery charge (drone and controller)
- Propeller condition and security
- Gimbal function and calibration
- Memory card space and speed rating
- Firmware updates
- Lens cleanliness

**Checklist Before Takeoff**:
- [ ] Airspace authorization confirmed
- [ ] Weather within operating limits
- [ ] Home point set correctly
- [ ] Return-to-home altitude appropriate
- [ ] Obstacle avoidance enabled
- [ ] Camera settings configured
- [ ] Batteries fully charged
- [ ] Visual observers briefed (if applicable)

### Basic Flight Maneuvers

**Fundamental Controls**:
- Throttle (left stick up/down): Altitude
- Yaw (left stick left/right): Rotation
- Pitch (right stick up/down): Forward/backward
- Roll (right stick left/right): Left/right

**Essential Maneuvers**:
- Hover: Maintaining stable position
- Orbit: Circling a subject
- Fly-through: Moving through spaces
- Reveal: Starting obscured, revealing subject
- Tracking: Following moving subjects

**Flight Modes**:
- Normal/Position Mode: GPS-stabilized, obstacle avoidance
- Sport Mode: Increased speed, reduced stabilization
- Cinematic Mode: Slower, smoother movements
- Tripod Mode: Precision slow movements
- Manual/Attitude Mode: No GPS, full manual control

### Advanced Flight Techniques

**Coordinated Movements**:
- Combining multiple axis inputs smoothly
- Gimbal control during flight
- Speed and direction matching
- Altitude changes while moving

**Obstacle Navigation**:
- Flying through windows and gaps
- Under structures
- Around objects
- Close proximity to subjects

**Weather Considerations**:
- Wind compensation techniques
- Flying in varying light conditions
- Temperature effects on batteries
- Moisture and precipitation limits

### Safety Protocols

**Emergency Procedures**:
- Return-to-home activation
- Manual landing procedures
- Signal loss protocols
- Low battery management
- Flyaway prevention

**Risk Mitigation**:
- Visual observer use for blind spots
- Maintaining safe distances
- Avoiding crowds and vehicles
- Emergency landing zone identification
- Insurance coverage

**Battery Safety**:
- Never charge unattended
- Store at appropriate charge level (40-60%)
- Inspect for damage before use
- Dispose of damaged batteries properly
- Follow temperature guidelines
