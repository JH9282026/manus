# De-Escalation Toolkit

Techniques for managing high-emotion conflict situations.

---

## Immediate Calming Techniques

### Acknowledgment Phrases
- "I can see this is really frustrating for you."
- "It sounds like you've been dealing with this for a while."
- "Your concerns are valid and worth discussing."

### Pace and Tone
- Lower your voice slightly
- Slow your speech
- Use calm, measured responses
- Pause before responding

### Physical Interventions
- Suggest a short break
- Offer water
- Change seating arrangement
- Move to neutral space

---

## Emotional Management

### Recognizing Escalation Signs
- Raised voice
- Personal attacks
- Repetitive arguments
- Physical tension
- Withdrawal or shutdown

### Redirection Strategies
- "Let's step back for a moment..."
- "I want to make sure I understand..."
- "Can we focus on the specific issue of..."
- "What would help right now?"

### Face-Saving Opportunities
- Acknowledge good intentions
- Recognize past efforts
- Reframe mistakes as learning
- Focus forward, not backward

---

## Difficult Personality Strategies

### Aggressive Behavior
- Stay calm and don't match energy
- Set firm boundaries on behavior
- Focus on specific issues
- Take breaks as needed

### Passive-Aggressive Behavior
- Name the behavior gently
- Ask direct questions
- Document agreements clearly
- Follow up consistently

### Stonewalling
- Ask open-ended questions
- Offer breaks to think
- Use silence comfortably
- Try written communication
