# Category 6: Brand & Identity (10%) - Detailed Reference

Detailed reference content for design scoring rubric.

---

## Category 6: Brand & Identity (10%)
### 6.1 Brand Expression (5%)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 1-2 | No brand identity, generic appearance |
| 3-4 | Weak brand presence, inconsistent application |
| 5-6 | Basic brand elements present |
| 7-8 | Strong brand expression throughout |
| 9-10 | Powerful brand identity, memorable, differentiated, emotionally resonant |

---

### 6.2 Emotional Design (5%)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 1-2 | Cold, uninviting, no emotional connection |
| 3-4 | Generic feel, limited personality |
| 5-6 | Pleasant but forgettable |
| 7-8 | Engaging personality, appropriate tone |
| 9-10 | Delightful experience, strong emotional resonance, memorable moments |

---

## Calculating Final Score
### Weight Distribution

| Category | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Visual Aesthetics | 25% |
| User Experience | 25% |
| Consistency & Systems | 15% |
| Responsiveness | 15% |
| Accessibility | 10% |
| Brand & Identity | 10% |
| **Total** | **100%** |

### Scoring Formula

```
Final Score = (Visual × 0.25) + (UX × 0.25) + (Consistency × 0.15) + 
              (Responsive × 0.15) + (Accessibility × 0.10) + (Brand × 0.10)
```
