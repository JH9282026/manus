# Event Types and Format-Specific Copy

## Conference Copy

### Conference Naming Approaches
- **Descriptive**: "Digital Marketing Summit 2026" (clear, searchable)
- **Aspirational**: "Elevate" or "Converge" (branded, memorable)
- **Hybrid**: "SPARK: The Innovation Conference" (branded + descriptive)
- Add year for annual events
- Include tagline for context: "SPARK 2026: Where Ideas Ignite"

### Conference Description Template
"[Event Name] brings together [audience] for [duration] of [experiences]. Discover [key topics/themes] through [format: keynotes, workshops, panels]. Join [number] [audience type] from [industries/companies] at [venue/platform] on [dates]."

### Speaker Bio Formula
"[Name] is [title] at [company], where [he/she/they] [key responsibility]. With [X years/notable achievement], [Name] has [credential/accomplishment]. [He/She/They] will present [session title], exploring [key takeaway]."

## Webinar Copy

### Webinar Title Formulas
- "How to [Achieve Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
- "[Number] Strategies for [Desired Result]"
- "The [Audience]'s Guide to [Topic]"
- "[Mistake/Challenge] That's Costing You [Loss] — And How to Fix It"

### Webinar Registration Page Copy
- Headline: Benefit-driven title
- Subhead: Date, time, duration
- 3-5 bullet points: What attendees will learn
- Speaker photo and brief bio (1-2 sentences)
- Registration button above the fold
- "Can't make it live? Register for the recording"

---

## Workshop Copy

### Workshop Value Proposition
Emphasize transformation: "In this [duration] workshop, you'll go from [current state] to [desired state]."

### Workshop Agenda Copy
- Label sessions as "hands-on," "interactive," "exercise"
- Include what participants will create/take away
- Show before/after: "Bring your [current work], leave with [improved version]"
- Note: "Limited to [X] participants for personalized attention"

---

## Gala/Fundraising Event Copy

### Gala Invitation Elements
- Formal event name and host organization
- Honoree(s) or cause being celebrated
- Date, time, dress code, venue
- Ticket levels with benefits at each tier
- RSVP deadline with response mechanism
- Tax-deductibility information

### Fundraising Urgency Copy
- "Every dollar raised tonight supports [specific impact]"
- "Join [X] community leaders supporting [cause]"
- "Table sponsorship includes [specific benefits]"

---

## Trade Show Copy

### Booth Messaging Hierarchy
1. **3 seconds**: Company name + one-line value proposition (readable from 10 feet)
2. **10 seconds**: Key benefit statements or product categories (readable from 5 feet)
3. **30 seconds**: Detailed product information, demos, handout copy

### Pre-Show Email to Prospects
"Visiting [Trade Show]? Stop by Booth #[XX] to see [specific demo/launch]. Book your private demo at [link] — spots are limited."
