# Event Email Sequences

## Pre-Event Email Campaign (Conference)

### Email 1: Save the Date (6 months before)
- Subject: "Save the Date: [Event Name] — [Date]"
- Brief event overview and key highlight
- Early-bird registration link
- Add-to-calendar button

### Email 2: Speaker Announcement (4 months before)
- Subject: "Announcing [Headline Speaker] at [Event]"
- Speaker photo and brief compelling bio
- Session topic and what attendees will learn
- Registration CTA with early-bird pricing

### Email 3: Agenda Release (3 months before)
- Subject: "[Event] agenda is live — see what's in store"
- Highlight 3-5 must-attend sessions
- Categorize by track or audience
- Registration CTA (early-bird deadline approaching)

### Email 4: Early-Bird Deadline (2 months before)
- Subject: "Early-bird pricing ends [date] — save $[amount]"
- Price comparison: early-bird vs regular
- Testimonial from past attendee
- Strong CTA with countdown element

### Email 5: Social Proof (6 weeks before)
- Subject: "Join [number]+ [audience] at [Event]"
- Company logos of registered attendees
- Attendee quote about past event value
- Registration CTA

### Email 6: Final Push (2 weeks before)
- Subject: "Last chance to register for [Event]"
- Recap key highlights and speakers
- FOMO element: "Over [X]% sold out"
- Final registration CTA

## Attendee Nurture Sequence (Post-Registration)

### Confirmation Email (Immediate)
- Thank them for registering
- Key logistics (date, time, location/platform)
- What to expect / how to prepare
- Add to calendar links

### Pre-Event Prep (1 week before)
- Agenda and session selection guide
- Speaker highlights to build excitement
- Networking tips or app download
- Logistics (parking, hotel, virtual platform access)

### Day-Before Reminder
- Final logistics and schedule
- Last-minute tips
- Excitement builder: "Tomorrow is the day!"

---

## Post-Event Follow-Up Sequence

### Email 1: Thank You (Day 1 after)
- Subject: "Thank you for joining [Event]!"
- Key highlights and memorable moments
- Survey link for feedback (keep short: 5-7 questions)
- Tease upcoming content (recordings, slides)

### Email 2: Resources (Day 3-5 after)
- Subject: "Your [Event] recordings and resources are ready"
- Links to session recordings, slide decks, resources
- Key takeaways or summary document
- Next steps or related content

### Email 3: Community (Day 7-14 after)
- Subject: "Continue the [Event] conversation"
- Community or networking group invitation
- Save the date for next event
- Related content or upcoming webinars
