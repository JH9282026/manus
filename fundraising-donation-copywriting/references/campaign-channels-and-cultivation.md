# Fundraising Campaigns and Donor Cultivation

## Email Fundraising Sequences

### Year-End Campaign (4-Week Sequence)

**Email 1 — Impact Story (Week 1)**
- Subject: "Meet [Name] — the reason we do this work"
- Content: Compelling beneficiary story
- Soft CTA: "Read [Name]'s full story"

**Email 2 — Year in Review (Week 2)**
- Subject: "What YOUR support made possible in 2025"
- Content: Impact statistics, milestones, achievements
- CTA: "Help us do even more in 2026"

**Email 3 — The Ask (Week 3)**
- Subject: "[Name], we need your help before December 31"
- Content: Direct ask with gift table and matching opportunity
- CTA: "Make your tax-deductible gift today"

**Email 4 — Matching Gift Urgency (Dec 28-30)**
- Subject: "48 hours left: Your gift will be DOUBLED"
- Content: Short, urgent, matching gift deadline
- CTA: "Double your impact now"

**Email 5 — Last Chance (Dec 31)**
- Subject: "Last chance for your 2025 tax deduction"
- Content: Very short, final push
- CTA: "Give now before midnight"

## Major Gift Cultivation

### Cultivation Cycle (12-24 months)

| Phase | Activities | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Identification | Research, wealth screening, connection mapping | Ongoing |
| Qualification | Discovery meeting, interest assessment | Month 1-3 |
| Cultivation | Events, tours, volunteer opportunities, updates | Month 3-12 |
| Solicitation | Face-to-face meeting with specific ask | Month 12-18 |
| Stewardship | Thank you, impact reporting, recognition | Ongoing |

### Major Gift Ask Meeting Structure
1. **Thank** them for their time and previous support
2. **Share** vision and specific project/initiative
3. **Present** the case and impact opportunity
4. **Ask** for a specific amount: "Would you consider a gift of $[amount]?"
5. **Listen** — pause after the ask (silence is powerful)
6. **Respond** to questions or concerns
7. **Agree** on next steps regardless of answer

## Crowdfunding Campaign Best Practices

### Campaign Page Elements
- Compelling headline (benefit-focused)
- Video (2-3 minutes, emotional story + ask)
- Goal amount with progress bar
- Reward/impact tiers
- Timeline with updates
- Social sharing buttons
- FAQ section

### Crowdfunding Timeline

| Phase | Day | Activities |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-launch | -14 to 0 | Build email list, prepare assets, recruit ambassadors |
| Launch | 1-3 | Personal outreach to inner circle (target 30% in first 48 hours) |
| Mid-campaign | 4-21 | Social media push, press outreach, ambassador activation |
| Final push | Last 3 days | Urgency emails, countdown, stretch goals |

### The 30/90 Rule
- 30% of goal should come from inner circle in first 48 hours
- 90% of crowdfunding success is determined by pre-launch preparation
- If you don't hit 30% in 48 hours, the campaign is unlikely to succeed

## Donor Stewardship Program

### Stewardship Touchpoints by Gift Level

| Level | Touchpoint | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| All donors | Thank-you email/letter | Within 48 hours |
| $100+ | Impact update email | Quarterly |
| $500+ | Personal phone call from staff | Semi-annually |
| $1,000+ | Annual impact report (printed) | Annually |
| $5,000+ | Personal meeting with ED | Annually |
| $10,000+ | Named recognition opportunity | As appropriate |
| $25,000+ | Board member personal contact | Quarterly |
