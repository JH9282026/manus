# Fundraising & Donation Copywriting

Craft compelling fundraising copy that inspires individuals and organizations to give generously to charitable causes, nonprofit organizations, and social initiatives.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for every fundraising communication format — from annual appeal letters and email campaigns to major gift solicitations and crowdfunding. Cover donor psychology, emotional storytelling, the case for support, and multi-channel campaign design.

## Fundraising Campaign Types

| Campaign Type | Ask Range | Timeline | Best Channel |
|---|---|---|---|
| Annual appeal | $25-500 | Ongoing, year-end push | Direct mail, email |
| Capital campaign | $1,000-$10M+ | 2-5 years | In-person, print |
| Crowdfunding | $10-500 | 30-60 days | Online platform |
| Peer-to-peer | $25-250 | Event-based | Social media, email |
| Major gift | $10,000+ | 6-24 month cultivation | In-person meetings |
| Planned giving | Estate-level | Multi-year relationship | Personal letters, meetings |

## Donor Psychology Principles

| Principle | Application |
|---|---|
| Individual impact | "Your $50 provides school supplies for one child for a year" |
| Identified victim effect | Tell one person's story, not statistics |
| Social proof | "Join 5,000 donors who support this cause" |
| Reciprocity | Provide value first (report, impact update) before asking |
| Identity | "As a parent/educator/community leader, you understand..." |
| Urgency | Matching gift deadline, year-end tax benefit |
| Tangibility | Show specific, concrete outcomes for each gift level |

## Appeal Letter Structure

1. **Opening hook** — Compelling individual story or urgent need
2. **Problem statement** — The challenge your organization addresses
3. **Solution** — How your organization makes a difference
4. **Impact evidence** — Results, data, testimonials
5. **The ask** — Specific dollar amounts with impact descriptions
6. **Gift table** — Show what each level provides
7. **Urgency** — Deadline, matching gift, immediate need
8. **Call to action** — How to give (multiple channels)
9. **P.S.** — Restate key impact or deadline

## Gift Table Format

| Gift Level | Impact | Recognition |
|---|---|---|
| $25 | Provides 50 meals | Thank-you email |
| $50 | Supplies one student for a year | Handwritten note |
| $100 | Funds one week of counseling | Annual report listing |
| $250 | Sponsors a family for a month | Impact video update |
| $500 | Trains one community health worker | Personal phone call |
| $1,000+ | Transforms a classroom | Named recognition |
