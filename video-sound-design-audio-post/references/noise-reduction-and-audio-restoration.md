## Noise Reduction and Audio Restoration



### Types of Noise

**Broadband Noise:**
- Hiss, air conditioning, wind noise
- Present across frequency spectrum
- Often consistent, making it easier to profile

**Tonal Noise:**
- Hum (electrical interference, typically 50/60Hz)
- Whine, buzz, hum from equipment
- Usually at specific frequencies

**Transient Noise:**
- Clicks, pops, crackles
- Brief, irregular disturbances
- Often from digital artifacts or physical damage

**Reverb/Room Sound:**
- Reflections from recording environment
- Challenging to remove without affecting source
- Prevention is better than cure



### Audio Restoration

**Click and Pop Removal:**
- Automatic detection and removal tools
- Manual pencil tool for spectral editing
- Replace with surrounding audio
- Interpolation algorithms

**Clipping/Distortion Repair:**
- Declipping tools attempt to reconstruct waveforms
- Limited effectiveness for severe clipping
- Prevention through proper gain staging is essential

**Missing Audio:**
- Spectral layers can separate elements
- AI-based tools for audio recovery
- Creative solutions (room tone, sound effects)
