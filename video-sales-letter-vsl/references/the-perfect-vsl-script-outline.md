## The Perfect VSL Script Outline



### Pre-Production Planning

Before writing your VSL script, document:

1. **Target Avatar**: Detailed profile of your ideal customer
2. **Problem Hierarchy**: Primary, secondary, and tertiary problems your product solves
3. **Unique Mechanism**: The specific method or approach that makes your solution different
4. **Core Transformation**: The before/after state change customers experience
5. **Proof Elements**: Testimonials, case studies, credentials, and data points
6. **Objections List**: Every reason someone might not buy
7. **Competitive Positioning**: How you differ from alternatives
