## Problem Agitation and Amplification



### The Art of Agitation

Effective agitation doesn't create problems—it reveals the full scope of problems the viewer already has. The goal is to help viewers feel the weight of their situation and understand why status quo is unacceptable.



### Balancing Agitation and Hope

Effective VSLs agitate without depressing. The key is to:

1. **Validate**: Acknowledge this isn't the viewer's fault
2. **Explain**: Show why previous solutions haven't worked
3. **Bridge**: Transition to hope by introducing the possibility of change
4. **Deliver**: Present your solution as the path forward
