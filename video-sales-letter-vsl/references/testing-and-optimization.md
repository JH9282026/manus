## Testing and Optimization


### Elements to Test

**Opening Hook**
- Different hook styles (question, statement, story)
- Different opening visuals
- Time to first engagement prompt

**Offer Presentation**
- Order of components
- Value stacking approach
- Bonus combinations

**Price Point**
- Different price levels
- Payment plan options
- Bundle variations

**Length**
- Full version vs condensed
- Section order changes
- Pacing variations

**Visual Style**
- Presenter vs voiceover
- B-roll intensity
- Text overlay frequency


### Testing Methodology

1. **Baseline Measurement**: Establish current performance metrics
2. **Hypothesis Development**: Form specific theories about improvements
3. **Variable Isolation**: Test one element at a time
4. **Statistical Significance**: Ensure enough data before conclusions
5. **Implementation**: Roll out winning variations
6. **Continuous Testing**: Always have a test running


### Key Metrics

- **Play Rate**: Percentage who start video
- **Average Watch Time**: How far viewers get
- **Completion Rate**: Percentage watching to end
- **Click-Through Rate**: Clicks on CTA
- **Conversion Rate**: Purchases divided by viewers
- **Revenue Per Viewer**: Total revenue divided by views
- **Cost Per Acquisition**: Ad spend per customer
