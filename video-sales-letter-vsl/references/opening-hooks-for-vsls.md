## Opening Hooks for VSLs



### The Contrarian Hook

Challenge conventional wisdom to immediately capture attention:

"Everything you've been told about [topic] is wrong—and I have the proof..."

"What if the 'experts' in [industry] have been lying to you this whole time?"

"The [industry] doesn't want you to know what I'm about to reveal..."



### The Curiosity Hook

Create an information gap that compels viewers to keep watching:

"In the next few minutes, I'm going to show you a simple [number]-step method that [impressive result]..."

"What you're about to discover has been responsible for [big number/result]..."

"There's one secret that separates [successful group] from everyone else—and it has nothing to do with [common assumption]..."



### The Story Hook

Open with a dramatic moment that pulls viewers into a narrative:

"It was 3 AM, and I was staring at [dramatic situation]..."

"The doctor looked at me and said words that changed everything..."

"I'll never forget the day [pivotal moment happened]..."



### The Question Hook

Ask a question that your target audience can't help but answer "yes" to:

"Have you ever felt like you're working harder than ever but falling further behind?"

"What if you could [achieve desire] in half the time, without [painful process]?"

"Are you tired of [common frustration] only to end up [negative outcome]?"



### The Demonstration Hook

Show a result immediately to create instant credibility:

"Watch as I [demonstrate result] in real-time..."

"Before we go any further, let me show you something that took me [time] to discover..."

"In the next 30 seconds, you'll see proof that [claim]..."



### The Numbers Hook

Lead with impressive statistics or specific results:

"$47,321 in 30 days—that's what happened when I discovered this..."

"87% of [group] fail at [goal]. Here's why you won't..."

"After testing this on 3,247 people, the results were undeniable..."
