## Scarcity and Urgency Tactics



### Legitimate Scarcity Types

**Quantity Scarcity**
- Limited seats/spots available
- Limited inventory
- Exclusive membership caps

**Time Scarcity**
- Enrollment periods
- Sale prices with deadlines
- Bonus availability windows

**Conditional Scarcity**
- First X buyers get additional bonus
- Price increases after milestone
- Offer changes after feedback period



### Creating Authentic Urgency

**External Deadlines**
Tie offers to real external events:
- "Before the algorithm update takes effect..."
- "In time for the [seasonal event]..."
- "While this technology is still cutting-edge..."

**Logical Urgency**
Make a reasoned case for acting now:
- "Every day you wait costs you [amount]..."
- "Your competitors aren't waiting..."
- "The best time to start was yesterday; the second best is now..."

**Price-Based Urgency**
- "After this launch, the price goes to [higher price]..."
- "The introductory rate ends at [specific time]..."
