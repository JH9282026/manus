# Common Vsl Mistakes

Detailed reference content for the Video Sales Letter Vsl skill.

---

## Common VSL Mistakes


### Script Mistakes

1. **Starting with credentials instead of hook**: Lead with value, not your resume
2. **Weak or missing unique mechanism**: Differentiation is essential
3. **Insufficient problem agitation**: Don't rush to the solution
4. **Feature-focused rather than benefit-focused**: Always translate to viewer impact
5. **No story or weak story**: Narrative is a primary persuasion tool
6. **Objections unaddressed**: Anticipate and answer concerns
7. **Unclear or weak CTA**: Tell viewers exactly what to do
8. **No urgency**: Give reasons to act now


### Production Mistakes

1. **Poor audio quality**: The #1 killer of VSL effectiveness
2. **Distracting visuals**: Keep focus on the message
3. **Inconsistent branding**: Maintain visual coherence
4. **Overproduction**: Polish is good; slickness can feel inauthentic
5. **Underproduction**: Quality signals credibility
6. **Wrong pacing**: Match energy to content needs
7. **Ignoring mobile viewers**: Optimize for all devices


### Strategic Mistakes

1. **Wrong audience targeting**: The best VSL can't save bad targeting
2. **Misaligned traffic**: Match message to audience awareness
3. **Price-value disconnect**: Ensure perceived value exceeds price
4. **Weak offer**: The VSL can't overcome a bad offer
5. **No testing**: Always optimize based on data
6. **Ignoring analytics**: Watch behavior data, not just conversion
7. **Set-and-forget mentality**: VSLs need ongoing optimization
