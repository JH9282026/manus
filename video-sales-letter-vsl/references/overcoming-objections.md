## Overcoming Objections



### Common Objections Framework

**Time Objections**
"I know you're busy. That's exactly why I designed this to take only [time]. In fact, [customer] implemented this in just [shorter time]..."

**Money Objections**
"I understand [price] is an investment. But let me ask you—what is [problem] costing you every [month/year]? When you look at it that way..."

**Skepticism Objections**
"You might be thinking, 'I've tried things like this before.' And I get it. But here's why this is different..."

**Relevance Objections**
"Maybe you're wondering if this will work for your specific situation. Let me share what happened with [similar customer]..."

**Timing Objections**
"You might think, 'I'll do this later.' But here's the truth about waiting..."



### Preemptive Objection Handling

Address objections before they fully form in the viewer's mind:

"Now, I know what you might be thinking right now..."
"At this point, some people wonder..."
"The natural question is..."



### Objection Reframing

Transform objections into reasons to buy:

"The fact that you're hesitating actually shows [positive trait]..."
"Your concern about [objection] is exactly why [product] is perfect for you..."
