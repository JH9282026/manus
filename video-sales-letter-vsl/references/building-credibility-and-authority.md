## Building Credibility and Authority



### Establishing Expertise

**Credentials**: Degrees, certifications, professional achievements

"As a [credential holder] with [X years] of experience in [field]..."

**Results**: Measurable outcomes you've achieved

"Over the past [time], I've helped [number] people achieve [result]..."

**Associations**: Notable clients, publications, media appearances

"I've been featured in [publication] and worked with companies like [names]..."

**Social Proof**: Testimonials, case studies, user numbers

"Join the [number] people who have already [achieved result]..."



### Borrowed Authority

Reference respected sources to transfer credibility:

"According to a Harvard study..."
"As [famous person] once said..."
"[Industry leader] recently stated..."



### Demonstrable Expertise

Show don't tell:

- Share specific, actionable insights that prove knowledge
- Demonstrate the product or method in action
- Provide unique perspectives that only an expert would know
- Answer anticipated questions before they're asked
