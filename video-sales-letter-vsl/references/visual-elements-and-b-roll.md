## Visual Elements and B-Roll


### Types of Visual Content

**Presenter on Camera**
- Builds personal connection
- Demonstrates authenticity
- Allows for emotional delivery

**Screen Recordings**
- Software demonstrations
- Process walkthroughs
- Evidence and proof

**B-Roll Footage**
- Lifestyle imagery
- Product shots
- Emotional reinforcement

**Graphics and Animation**
- Data visualization
- Concept illustration
- Text emphasis

**Testimonial Videos**
- Customer success stories
- Before/after documentation


### B-Roll Best Practices

- Use B-roll to illustrate points, not decorate
- Match B-roll emotion to script emotion
- Avoid generic stock footage when possible
- Use custom footage for authenticity
- Transition B-roll smoothly
- Keep B-roll clips short (2-5 seconds typically)


### Visual Pacing

**Opening**: High visual energy to capture attention
**Middle**: Mix of presenter and supporting visuals
**Offer**: Clear product shots and graphics
**Close**: Return to presenter for personal connection
