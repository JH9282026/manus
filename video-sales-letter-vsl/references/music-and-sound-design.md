## Music and Sound Design


### Background Music Selection

**Purpose of Music:**
- Set emotional tone
- Maintain energy and pacing
- Cover audio imperfections
- Signal transitions

**Music Guidelines:**
- Keep volume low enough for voice clarity
- Match music energy to content energy
- Use copyright-free or licensed music
- Avoid music with lyrics under speech
- Change music to signal section changes


### Sound Effects

Use sound effects sparingly for:
- Transitions between sections
- Emphasis on key points
- Pattern interrupts
- CTA moments


### Audio Quality Essentials

- Record in quiet environment
- Use quality microphone (lavalier, shotgun, or condenser)
- Process audio for consistency
- Remove background noise
- Normalize volume levels
- Apply subtle compression
