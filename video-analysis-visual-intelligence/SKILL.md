# Video Analysis & Visual Intelligence

Process, understand, and manipulate video content with advanced visual perception capabilities.

## Overview

Transform raw video data into structured, actionable intelligence while enabling sophisticated video manipulation operations. Achieve human-level video understanding through perceptual analysis, temporal intelligence, emotional detection, and professional-grade manipulation.

## Core Competencies

| Pillar | Capabilities | Applications |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| Perceptual Understanding | Object, scene, action recognition | Content categorization |
| Temporal Intelligence | Change tracking, event detection | Narrative analysis |
| Emotional Analysis | Facial expressions, sentiment | Audience response prediction |
| Manipulation | Extraction, compositing | Video editing, VFX |

## Analysis Modes

| Mode | Function | Output |
|------|----------|--------|
| Scene Detection | Identify scene boundaries | Timestamps, transitions |
| Object Tracking | Follow objects across frames | Trajectories, IDs |
| Face Analysis | Detect faces, expressions | Landmarks, emotions |
| Action Recognition | Identify activities | Action labels, timestamps |
| Speech Transcription | Convert speech to text | Timestamped transcript |
| Audio Analysis | Detect music, effects | Sound classification |
| Narrative Analysis | Understand story structure | Arc identification |
| Quality Assessment | Technical evaluation | Quality scores |

## Object Detection & Tracking

### Detection Categories
- People and faces
- Vehicles and transportation
- Animals and wildlife
- Sports equipment
- Consumer goods
- Furniture and fixtures
- Natural elements
- Tools and instruments

### Tracking Outputs
- Frame-by-frame bounding boxes
- Persistent object IDs
- Movement trajectories
- Entry/exit timestamps
- Occlusion handling

## Extraction & Manipulation

### Extraction Methods
| Method | Best For | Quality |
|--------|----------|--------|
| AI Rotoscoping | Complex edges | Highest |
| Chroma Key | Green/blue screen | High |
| Depth Segmentation | 3D scenes | High |
| Motion Segmentation | Moving subjects | Medium |

### Output Formats
- Transparent video (WebM, ProRes 4444)
- Image sequences (PNG with alpha)
- Mask sequences (grayscale)
- Isolated clips (MP4)
