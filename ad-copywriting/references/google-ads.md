# Google Ads Copywriting

Comprehensive guide to creating high-performing Google Search, Display, and Performance Max ad copy.

---

## Responsive Search Ads (RSA)

### Character Limits

- **Headlines**: 15 required, 30 characters each
- **Descriptions**: 4 required, 90 characters each
- **Display Path**: 2 fields, 15 characters each

### Headline Diversity Strategy

Create headlines across these categories:

1. **Brand + Keyword** (2-3 headlines): "[Brand] | [Keyword] Solutions"
2. **Primary Benefit** (2-3 headlines): "Save 40% on [Product]"
3. **Features** (2-3 headlines): "Free Shipping | 24/7 Support"
4. **Social Proof** (1-2 headlines): "Trusted by 10,000+ Customers"
5. **Urgency/Offer** (1-2 headlines): "Limited Time: 50% Off"
6. **CTA-Focused** (2-3 headlines): "Get Your Free Quote Today"
7. **Question Format** (1-2 headlines): "Looking for [Solution]?"

### Keyword Insertion

Syntax: `{KeyWord:Default Text}`

Examples:
- `{KeyWord:Running Shoes}` — Capitalizes each word
- `{keyword:running shoes}` — All lowercase
- `{KEYWORD:RUNNING SHOES}` — All uppercase

### Pinning Strategy

Pin strategically, not excessively:
- Pin brand name to Position 1 for brand queries
- Pin strongest CTA to Headline Position 3
- Avoid pinning more than 2-3 headlines

## Description Best Practices

### Structure Formula

**Description 1**: Value proposition + primary benefit + CTA
**Description 2**: Features + social proof + secondary CTA
**Description 3**: Offer details + urgency + trust signal
**Description 4**: Alternative angle or segment-specific messaging

### Description Examples

**B2B SaaS**:
- "Streamline your workflow and save 10+ hours per week. Trusted by 5,000 teams. Start your free 14-day trial today."

**E-commerce**:
- "Free shipping on orders over $50. 30-day returns, no questions asked. Shop our bestsellers now."

**Local Service**:
- "Licensed and insured professionals. Same-day appointments available. Call now for a free estimate."

## Quality Score Optimization

### Ad Relevance Factors

1. Include target keywords in headlines
2. Match ad messaging to search intent
3. Use language from the keyword itself
4. Segment ad groups tightly

### Expected CTR Factors

1. Compelling headlines that stand out
2. Clear value proposition
3. Strong, relevant CTAs
4. Ad extensions that add value

### Landing Page Experience

1. Message match between ad and landing page
2. Above-the-fold content mirrors ad promise
3. Clear conversion path
4. Mobile-optimized experience

## Google Display Network

### Asset Specifications

- **Short Headline**: 25 characters
- **Long Headline**: 90 characters
- **Description**: 90 characters
- **Business Name**: 25 characters

### Display Copy Tips

1. Lead with benefit, not brand
2. Use numbers and specifics
3. Create urgency without false scarcity
4. Match visual creative messaging

---

## Performance Max

### Asset Group Strategy

Create asset groups for:
- Different audience segments
- Different product categories
- Different funnel stages

### Text Asset Variety

- 3-5 short headlines (15-30 chars)
- 1-5 long headlines (up to 90 chars)
- 1-5 descriptions (up to 90 chars)
- Diverse CTAs across assets

## Policy Compliance

### Prohibited Content

- Excessive capitalization
- Misleading claims
- Trademark violations
- Prohibited products/services

### Restricted Industries

- Healthcare: Require certification
- Financial services: Disclosure requirements
- Alcohol: Age-gating required
- Gambling: License verification
