# Display Ads Copywriting

Guide to creating display advertising and programmatic ad copy.

---

## Display Ad Specifications

### Common Sizes

| Size | Name | Headlines | Description |
|------|------|-----------|-------------|
| 300x250 | Medium Rectangle | 25 chars | 90 chars |
| 728x90 | Leaderboard | 25 chars | 90 chars |
| 160x600 | Wide Skyscraper | 25 chars | 90 chars |
| 320x50 | Mobile Banner | 20 chars | 60 chars |
| 300x600 | Half Page | 30 chars | 90 chars |

### Responsive Display Ads

- Short headline: 25 characters
- Long headline: 90 characters
- Description: 90 characters
- Business name: 25 characters

---

## Copy Strategy by Size

### Leaderboard (728x90)

- Horizontal layout requires concise copy
- Logo + headline + CTA format
- Single benefit focus
- Large, readable CTA button

### Medium Rectangle (300x250)

- Most versatile size
- Room for headline + description
- Visual-heavy with supporting copy
- Clear CTA placement

### Skyscraper (160x600)

- Vertical reading pattern
- Stack messages vertically
- Multiple message points possible
- CTA at bottom

### Mobile Banner (320x50)

- Extremely limited space
- 3-5 words maximum
- Brand + CTA only
- No detailed messaging

### Curiosity-Driven

- "Discover What Works"
- "See the Difference"

### Offer-Driven

- "Free Shipping"

## Retargeting Copy

### Cart Abandonment

**Headline**: "Still Thinking It Over?"
**Description**: "Your cart is waiting. Complete your order and save 10%."

### Product View Retargeting

**Headline**: "Back in Stock"
**Description**: "The [product] you viewed is available. Shop now before it sells out."

### Site Visitor Retargeting

**Headline**: "We Miss You"
**Description**: "Come back and see what's new. Special offer inside."

### Sequence Strategy

1. **Day 1-3**: Remind of visited products
2. **Day 4-7**: Introduce incentive (discount, free shipping)
3. **Day 8-14**: Create urgency (limited time offer)
4. **Day 15-30**: Broader brand messaging

## Dynamic Creative

### Product Feed Integration

Use placeholders for:
- Product name
- Price
- Sale price
- Image
- Description

### Dynamic Copy Templates

- "[Product Name] - Now [Sale Price]"
- "Your [Category] is waiting"
- "Complete your [Category] collection"

---

## Programmatic Best Practices

### Contextual Alignment

Match messaging to:
- Publisher content
- Audience segment
- Time of day
- Device type

### Frequency Management

Rotate creative to prevent:
- Ad fatigue
- Banner blindness
- Negative brand association

### A/B Testing Elements

1. Headline variations
2. CTA button text
3. Offer presentation
4. Color schemes
5. Image styles

---

## Native Advertising

### In-Feed Ads

- Match publisher editorial style
- Informative vs. promotional
- Longer headlines allowed
- Discovery-focused CTAs

### Content Recommendation

- Article-style headlines
- Curiosity-driving copy
- Soft sell approach
- Educational value

---

## Compliance and Quality

### Quality Standards

- Clear branding
- Readable text sizes
- Professional imagery
- Accurate claims

### Policy Guidelines

- No misleading buttons
- No fake system alerts
- No deceptive animations
- Clear advertiser identification
