# Meta Ads Copywriting

Guide to writing high-converting Facebook and Instagram ad copy.

---

## Ad Format Specifications

### Single Image/Video Ads

| Element | Optimal | Maximum |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Primary Text | 125 chars | 500 chars |
| Headline | 27 chars | 40 chars |
| Description | 27 chars | 30 chars |

### Carousel Ads

- Primary text: Same as single image
- Card headlines: 32 characters each
- Card descriptions: 18 characters each
- 2-10 cards per carousel

### Stories/Reels

- Text overlays: Minimal, 2-3 words
- CTA: Built into format
- Duration: 15 seconds optimal

## Primary Text Strategies

### Hook-First Writing

The first line must grab attention (visible before "See More"):

**Problem Hook**: "Struggling to [pain point]?"
**Curiosity Hook**: "We discovered something surprising about [topic]..."
**Benefit Hook**: "Imagine [desired outcome] in just [timeframe]."
**Social Proof Hook**: "Join 50,000+ people who have already..."

### Body Structure

1. **Hook** (Line 1): Attention-grabbing opener
2. **Problem** (Lines 2-3): Agitate the pain point
3. **Solution** (Lines 4-5): Introduce your offer
4. **Proof** (Line 6): Social proof or credibility
5. **CTA** (Final line): Clear next step

### Emoji Usage

Use emojis strategically:
- 1-3 emojis per ad maximum
- Place at line beginnings for bullet points
- Match emoji to message tone
- Test emoji vs. no emoji performance

## Headline Best Practices

### Headline Formulas

1. **Direct Benefit**: "Sleep Better Tonight"
2. **Offer Focus**: "50% Off - This Week Only"
3. **Curiosity**: "The Secret Top Athletes Use"
4. **Question**: "Ready for Your Best Skin?"
5. **Command**: "Transform Your Home Today"

### Character Efficiency

Headlines may be truncated on mobile:
- Front-load key words
- Avoid articles (a, the, an) when possible
- Use numerals instead of words

## CTA Button Selection

Choose CTAs that match funnel stage:

| Stage | Best CTAs |
|-------|-----------|
| Awareness | Learn More, Watch More |
| Consideration | Sign Up, Get Offer |
| Conversion | Shop Now, Buy Now |
| Engagement | Send Message, Call Now |

## Audience-Specific Copy

### Cold Audiences

- Focus on problem awareness
- Introduce brand credibility
- Offer low-commitment CTAs
- Use educational content

### Warm Audiences

- Reference previous touchpoints
- Highlight specific features
- Address common objections
- Push toward conversion

### Hot Audiences (Retargeting)

- Create urgency
- Offer incentives
- Use cart abandonment reminders
- Personalize messaging

---

## Creative Concepts

### Copy-Visual Integration

Ensure copy and creative work together:
- Headline reinforces visual message
- Primary text expands on visual story
- CTA aligns with creative direction

### Testing Framework

Test these copy elements:
1. Different hook styles
2. Long vs. short primary text
3. Emoji vs. no emoji
4. Question vs. statement headlines
5. Different CTA buttons

---

## Policy Compliance

### Prohibited Practices

- Personal attributes ("Are you overweight?")
- Before/after imagery claims
- Misleading buttons or UI elements
- Sensational or exaggerated claims

### Special Ad Categories

- Housing, employment, credit require restrictions
- Political ads require authorization
- Alcohol ads have targeting limitations
