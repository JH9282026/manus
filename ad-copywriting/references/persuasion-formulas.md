# Persuasion Formulas

Proven copywriting frameworks for ad copy.

---

## AIDA Framework

**A**ttention → **I**nterest → **D**esire → **A**ction

### Application
- Attention: Bold claim or question
- Interest: Relevant benefit
- Desire: Proof or urgency
- Action: Clear CTA

---

## PAS Framework

**P**roblem → **A**gitate → **S**olve

### Application
- Problem: Name the pain point
- Agitate: Emphasize consequences
- Solve: Present your solution

### Example
"Tired of slow websites? (Problem)
Every second costs you customers. (Agitate)
Speed up 3x with our CDN. (Solve)"

---

## BAB Framework

**B**efore → **A**fter → **B**ridge

### Application
- Before: Current painful state
- After: Desired future state
- Bridge: Your solution connects them

---

## Social Proof Patterns

- "Join [X] others who..."
- "Trusted by [Notable Names]"
- "[X] 5-star reviews"
- "#1 rated in [Category]"
- "As seen in [Media]"

---

## Urgency Triggers

### Time-Based
- "Limited time offer"
- "Ends [Date]"
- "Today only"

### Scarcity-Based
- "Only [X] left"
- "Limited spots"
- "While supplies last"

### FOMO-Based
- "Don't miss out"
- "Selling fast"
- "Join now"

---

## CTA Power Words

### Action Verbs
- Get, Start, Try, Discover
- Claim, Unlock, Access, Join
- Download, Learn, Explore

### First Person
- "Get My Free Guide"
- "Start My Trial"
- "Claim My Discount"

---

## Headline Formulas

1. How to [Achieve Result]
2. [Number] Ways to [Benefit]
3. The Secret to [Outcome]
4. Stop [Pain Point] Today
5. Finally, [Solution] That Works
6. [Result] Without [Objection]
