# LinkedIn Ads Copywriting

Guide to creating professional B2B advertising for LinkedIn.

---

## Ad Format Specifications

### Sponsored Content

| Element | Character Limit |
|---------|-----------------|
| Introductory Text | 150 optimal, 600 max |
| Headline | 70 characters |
| Description | 100 characters |

### Message Ads

| Element | Character Limit |
|---------|-----------------|
| Subject Line | 60 characters |
| Message Body | 1,500 characters |
| CTA Button | 20 characters |

### Text Ads

| Element | Character Limit |
|---------|-----------------|
| Headline | 25 characters |
| Description | 75 characters |

---

## B2B Tone Guidelines

### Professional Voice

Maintain credibility through:
- Industry-appropriate terminology
- Data-driven claims with specifics
- Thought leadership positioning
- Confident but not aggressive tone

### Avoid

- Overly casual language
- Excessive exclamation points
- Clickbait tactics
- Unsubstantiated superlatives

---

## Audience Targeting Copy

### By Seniority

**C-Suite**: Focus on strategic outcomes, ROI, competitive advantage
**Directors/VPs**: Emphasize efficiency, team performance, scalability
**Managers**: Highlight practical solutions, ease of implementation
**Individual Contributors**: Address daily pain points, skill development

### By Industry

Customize messaging for:
- Industry-specific challenges
- Relevant case studies and examples
- Appropriate terminology
- Regulatory considerations

## Sponsored Content Strategies

### Introductory Text

Lead with business impact:

**Data Hook**: "Companies using [solution] see 40% faster growth."
**Question Hook**: "What if your team could close deals 2x faster?"
**Insight Hook**: "The top-performing sales teams share one thing in common."
**Challenge Hook**: "Managing remote teams shouldn't be this hard."

### Content Types

Match copy to content format:
- **Blog Posts**: Educational, curiosity-driving
- **Case Studies**: Results-focused, specific outcomes
- **Webinars**: FOMO, expert positioning
- **White Papers**: Thought leadership, comprehensive solutions
- **Product Demos**: Problem-solution, feature benefits

## Message Ads Best Practices

### Subject Lines

Create urgency without spam triggers:
- "[Name], quick question about [topic]"
- "Invitation: Exclusive [event/opportunity]"
- "New research on [industry challenge]"

### Message Body Structure

1. **Personal connection** (1-2 sentences)
2. **Value proposition** (2-3 sentences)
3. **Social proof** (1 sentence)
4. **Clear CTA** (1 sentence)

### Personalization Tokens

Use LinkedIn data:
- First name
- Company name
- Job title
- Industry

---

## Lead Gen Forms Copy

### Form Headlines

Keep focused and benefit-driven:
- "Get Your Free Industry Report"
- "Access the Complete Guide"
- "Request Your Custom Demo"

### Privacy Messaging

Address data concerns:
- "Your information is secure"
- "No spam, unsubscribe anytime"
- "We respect your privacy"
