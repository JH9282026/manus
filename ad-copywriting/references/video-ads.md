# Video Ads Copywriting

Guide to scripting YouTube, TikTok, and video advertising content.

---

## YouTube Ad Formats

### Skippable In-Stream (Pre-Roll)

- Skip available after 5 seconds
- Hook must land in first 5 seconds
- 15-30 seconds optimal length
- Companion banner copy required

### Non-Skippable In-Stream

- 15-20 seconds maximum
- Entire message must be compelling
- CTA at end required

### Bumper Ads

- 6 seconds maximum
- Single message focus
- Brand reinforcement

---

## The 5-Second Hook

### Hook Strategies

**Question Hook**: "Ever wonder why [curiosity]?"
**Problem Hook**: "Tired of [pain point]?"
**Statistic Hook**: "Did you know [surprising stat]?"
**Direct Address**: "Hey [target audience]!"
**Visual Hook**: Start with unexpected imagery + supporting text

### Hook Elements

Include in first 5 seconds:
1. Visual attention-grabber
2. Target audience identification
3. Promise of value or solution
4. Reason to keep watching

---

## Script Structure

### 15-Second Format

- **0-5 sec**: Hook + problem identification
- **5-10 sec**: Solution introduction
- **10-15 sec**: CTA + brand

### 30-Second Format

- **0-5 sec**: Hook
- **5-15 sec**: Problem + agitation
- **15-25 sec**: Solution + benefits
- **25-30 sec**: CTA + brand

### 60-Second Format

- **0-5 sec**: Hook
- **5-20 sec**: Story/problem development
- **20-40 sec**: Solution demonstration
- **40-55 sec**: Social proof + benefits
- **55-60 sec**: CTA + brand

---

## Script Writing Tips

### Conversational Tone

Write for spoken delivery:
- Use contractions
- Short sentences
- Active voice
- Natural rhythm

### Visual Integration

Script visuals alongside dialogue:
- Describe key visual moments
- Align text overlays with speech
- Note when to show product
- Indicate demonstration points

## TikTok Ad Copywriting

### Format Specifications

- 9:16 vertical format
- 15-60 seconds optimal
- Sound-on environment
- Native aesthetic required

### TikTok-Specific Hooks

- Trend participation
- "POV: [scenario]"
- "Things I wish I knew about [topic]"
- Pattern interrupt visuals

### Caption Best Practices

- 2-3 lines maximum
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant
- CTA in caption
- Emojis sparingly

---

## Companion Text

### YouTube Companion Banner

- 300x60 pixels
- 15-20 characters ideal
- Match video CTA
- Brand + offer focus

### End Screen Copy

- Clear CTA
- Subscribe invitation
- Related video tease

### Video Description

- First 2 lines visible
- Primary CTA above fold
- Keywords for discovery
- Links and timestamps

---

## Call-to-Action Scripting

### Verbal CTAs

- "Click the link below"
- "Visit [URL] now"
- "Tap Shop Now to get started"
- "Subscribe for more [value]"

### On-Screen CTAs

- Button text: 2-4 words
- Contrasting color
- Appear on final frame
- Animate for attention

---

## Platform Compliance

### YouTube Policies

- No misleading content
- No sensational thumbnails
- Appropriate content ratings
- Clear sponsorship disclosure

### TikTok Policies

- Community guidelines compliance
- No prohibited products
- Age-appropriate content
- Authentic creator style
