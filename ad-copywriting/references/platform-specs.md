# Platform Specifications

Detailed requirements for each advertising platform.

---

## Google Ads

### Responsive Search Ads (RSAs)
- Headlines: Up to 15 (30 characters each)
- Descriptions: Up to 4 (90 characters each)
- Display Path: 2 fields (15 characters each)
- Minimum required: 3 headlines, 2 descriptions

### Display Ads
- Short headline: 25 characters
- Long headline: 90 characters
- Description: 90 characters
- Business name: 25 characters

### Performance Max
- Headlines: Up to 5 (30 characters)
- Long headlines: Up to 5 (90 characters)
- Descriptions: Up to 5 (90 characters)

---

## Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

### Single Image/Video Ads
- Primary text: 125 characters (500 max)
- Headline: 40 characters
- Link description: 30 characters

### Carousel Ads
- Primary text: 125 characters
- Headline per card: 40 characters
- Cards: 2-10

### Stories/Reels
- Primary text: 125 characters
- Consider vertical format

---

## LinkedIn Ads

### Sponsored Content
- Introductory text: 150 characters optimal
- Headline: 70 characters
- Description: 100 characters

### Message Ads
- Subject line: 60 characters
- Message body: 1,500 characters

### Text Ads
- Headline: 25 characters
- Description: 75 characters

---

## Twitter/X Ads

### Promoted Tweets
- Tweet copy: 280 characters
- Website card headline: 70 characters

---

## YouTube Ads

### Video Ad Companion Copy
- Headline: 15 characters
- Description: 2 lines, 35 characters each
- Display URL: 35 characters

### Bumper Ads
- 6 seconds max video
- Focus on single message

---

## TikTok Ads

### In-Feed Ads
- Ad text: 100 characters
- CTA button: Platform options
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
