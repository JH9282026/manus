# Pinterest Creative Specifications

Detailed technical specifications for all Pinterest ad formats.

---

## Standard Pins (Promoted Pins)

### Image Specifications

**File Format**:
- Accepted: .PNG, .JPEG
- Recommended: .PNG for graphics with transparency, .JPEG for photographs

**Dimensions**:
- Aspect Ratio: 2:3 (recommended), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (vertical video format)
- Recommended Size: 1000 x 1500 pixels (2:3)
- Minimum Width: 600 pixels
- Maximum Width: 4000 pixels
- Maximum Height: 10000 pixels

**File Size**:
- Maximum: 20 MB
- Recommended: Under 10 MB for faster loading

**Quality Requirements**:
- Resolution: 72-150 DPI
- Color Space: RGB
- No pixelation or blurriness
- Clear focal point

### Text Specifications

**Headline**:
- Maximum Length: 100 characters
- Recommended: 40-60 characters for mobile display
- Appears below Pin image
- Truncates on mobile if too long

**Description**:
- Maximum Length: 500 characters
- Recommended: 200-300 characters
- First 50-60 characters most important (visible without expansion)
- Supports basic formatting (line breaks)

**Destination URL**:
- Required for all Promoted Pins
- Must be HTTPS
- Must match domain claimed in Pinterest Business account
- Can include UTM parameters for tracking

### Design Requirements

**Text Overlay**:
- Recommended: 20% or less of image area
- Must be readable (large font, high contrast)
- Avoid covering key visual elements
- No excessive text (may reduce delivery)

**Branding**:
- Logo placement: Corner or subtle integration
- Not required but recommended
- Should not dominate image
- Consistent across campaign

**Prohibited Content**:
- Excessive text (more than 20% of image)
- Blurry or low-quality images
- Misleading or clickbait content
- Prohibited products (weapons, tobacco, etc.)
- Sexually suggestive content
- Before/after images (health/beauty claims)

## Video Pins

### Video Specifications

**File Format**:
- Accepted: .MP4, .MOV, .M4V
- Recommended: .MP4 (H.264 encoding)

**Dimensions**:
- Aspect Ratios: 1:1 (square), 2:3 (vertical), 9:16 (full vertical)
- Recommended: 1080 x 1920 pixels (2:3) or 1080 x 1080 pixels (1:1)
- Minimum: 240p resolution
- Maximum: 4K resolution

**Duration**:
- Minimum: 4 seconds
- Maximum: 15 minutes (900 seconds)
- Recommended: 6-15 seconds for optimal engagement
- Videos under 3 seconds will loop automatically

**File Size**:
- Maximum: 2 GB
- Recommended: Under 1 GB for faster upload and processing

**Encoding**:
- Video Codec: H.264 or H.265
- Frame Rate: 25-30 fps (recommended)
- Bitrate: Variable bitrate (VBR) recommended
- Keyframe Interval: 2 seconds maximum

**Audio**:
- Audio Codec: AAC
- Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- Bitrate: 128 kbps minimum
- Channels: Stereo (2 channels)
- Note: Videos autoplay muted; design for sound-off viewing

### Video Types

**Standard Video**:
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 2:3
- Appears in feed like standard Pins
- Autoplay on scroll

**Max-Width Video (Mobile Only)**:
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 16:9
- Takes full width of mobile screen
- Higher impact, mobile-optimized

### Design Requirements

**Visual Quality**:
- High resolution (1080p recommended)
- Good lighting and focus
- Stable footage (use stabilization)
- Professional editing

**Sound-Off Optimization**:
- Captions or text overlays for key information
- Visual storytelling (show, don't tell)
- Large, readable text
- High contrast for legibility

**First 3 Seconds**:
- Hook viewer immediately
- Show product or key benefit
- Dynamic visuals and motion
- No slow intros or logos

**Prohibited Content**:
- Excessive flashing or strobing
- Misleading thumbnails
- Clickbait tactics
- Low-quality or pixelated video
- Prohibited products or content

## Idea Ads (Story Pins)

### Page Specifications

**Number of Pages**:
- Minimum: 2 pages
- Maximum: 20 pages
- Recommended: 5-10 pages for optimal completion rate

**File Formats**:
- Images: .JPEG, .PNG, .BMP, .TIFF, .WEBP
- Videos: .MP4, .MOV, .M4V

**Dimensions**:
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16 (vertical, full-screen)
- Recommended Size: 1080 x 1920 pixels
- Minimum: 720 x 1280 pixels

**File Size**:
- Images: Maximum 20 MB per image
- Videos: Maximum 100 MB per video

**Duration (Video Pages)**:
- Minimum: 3 seconds per page
- Maximum: 60 seconds per page
- Recommended: 15-30 seconds per page

### Text Specifications

**Title**:
- Maximum: 100 characters
- Appears at top of Idea Ad
- Required field

**Description per Frame**:
- Maximum: 250 characters per page
- Optional but recommended
- Provides context for each page

### Interactive Elements

**Product Tags**:
- Tag products from your catalog
- Up to 10 products per page
- Enables direct shopping from Idea Ad

**Topic Tags**:
- Add relevant topic tags for discoverability
- Helps Pinterest categorize content
- Improves search visibility

**Stickers and Text Overlays**:
- Add text, stickers, and graphics
- Use Pinterest's built-in tools
- Maintain brand consistency

**Clickable Links**:
- Supported as of 2025
- Add destination URLs
- Drive traffic to website

### Design Requirements

**Visual Consistency**:
- Consistent style across all pages
- Unified color palette
- Cohesive branding
- Logical flow and progression

**Content Structure**:
- Page 1: Hook and introduction
- Middle Pages: Step-by-step content
- Final Page: CTA and link

**Prohibited Content**:
- Excessive promotional content
- Misleading information
- Low-quality images or videos
- Prohibited products

## Carousel Ads

### Card Specifications

**Number of Cards**:
- Minimum: 2 cards
- Maximum: 5 cards
- Recommended: 3-4 cards for optimal engagement

**File Format**:
- Accepted: .PNG, .JPEG
- Recommended: .PNG for consistent quality

**Dimensions**:
- Aspect Ratios: 1:1 (square) or 2:3 (vertical)
- Square: 1000 x 1000 pixels (recommended)
- Vertical: 1000 x 1500 pixels (recommended)

**File Size**:
- Maximum: 20 MB per card (some sources indicate 32 MB)
- Recommended: Under 10 MB per card

### Text Specifications

**Headline per Card**:
- Maximum: 100 characters per card
- Can be unique for each card
- Appears below card image

**Description**:
- Maximum: 500 characters (applies to entire carousel)
- Shared across all cards
- Appears below carousel

**Destination URL**:
- Optional per card
- Can be unique for each card (track individual card performance)
- Or single URL for entire carousel

### Design Requirements

**Visual Cohesion**:
- Consistent style across all cards
- Same aspect ratio for all cards
- Unified color palette and branding
- Complementary content

**Card Order**:
- Left-to-right sequence
- Logical progression
- Best-performing image first
- CTA or brand message last (optional)

**Prohibited Content**:
- Inconsistent aspect ratios within carousel
- Low-quality images
- Excessive text overlay
- Misleading content

## Shopping Ads

### Product Feed Specifications

**File Format**:
- Accepted: .CSV, .TSV, .XML
- Recommended: .CSV for simplicity

**Feed Delivery**:
- Upload: Manual upload to Pinterest
- Scheduled Fetch: Pinterest fetches from URL on schedule
- API: Programmatic feed management

**Update Frequency**:
- Recommended: Daily
- Minimum: Weekly
- Critical for price and availability accuracy

### Required Feed Fields

| Field | Description | Format | Example |
|-------|-------------|--------|----------|
| id | Unique product ID | Alphanumeric | SKU12345 |
| title | Product name | Text, max 500 chars | Blue Running Shoes |
| description | Product details | Text, max 10,000 chars | Lightweight running shoes... |
| link | Product page URL | URL (HTTPS) | https://example.com/product |
| image_link | Product image URL | URL (HTTPS) | https://cdn11.bigcommerce.com/s-eeul26hjka/images/stencil/590x590/products/4681/141332/media__58141.1742220832.jpg?c=1 |
| price | Product price | Number + currency | 49.99 USD |
| availability | Stock status | in stock, out of stock, preorder | in stock |

### Recommended Feed Fields

| Field | Description | Format | Example |
|-------|-------------|--------|----------|
| brand | Brand name | Text | Nike |
| condition | Product condition | new, used, refurbished | new |
| product_type | Category hierarchy | Text, > separated | Shoes > Running > Men's |
| google_product_category | Google taxonomy | Number or text | Apparel & Accessories > Shoes |
| sale_price | Discounted price | Number + currency | 39.99 USD |
| age_group | Target age | adult, kids, infant | adult |
| gender | Target gender | male, female, unisex | male |
| color | Product color | Text | Blue |
| size | Product size | Text | 10 |
| material | Product material | Text | Mesh |

### Image Specifications

**Dimensions**:
- Minimum: 1000 x 1500 pixels
- Recommended Aspect Ratio: 2:3
- Maximum: 4000 x 6000 pixels

**File Format**:
- Accepted: .JPEG, .PNG
- Recommended: .JPEG for photographs

**Quality Requirements**:
- High-resolution, professional photography
- Clean background (white or neutral preferred)
- Product clearly visible
- No watermarks or promotional text
- Accurate color representation

### Feed Validation

**Common Errors**:
- Missing required fields
- Invalid URLs (not HTTPS)
- Incorrect price format
- Low-quality images
- Mismatched product data

**Validation Tools**:
- Pinterest Catalog Diagnostics
- Feed preview before publishing
- Error reports in Ads Manager

## AR Try-On Pins

### 3D Model Specifications

**File Format**:
- iOS: .USDZ
- Android: .GLB
- Must provide both formats for cross-platform support

**Model Requirements**:
- Accurate product representation
- Realistic textures and materials
- Optimized polygon count (under 100,000 polygons)
- Proper UV mapping
- Correct scale and proportions

**File Size**:
- Maximum: 10 MB per model
- Recommended: Under 5 MB for fast loading

### Preview Image Specifications

**Dimensions**:
- Minimum: 1000 x 1000 pixels
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (square)
- High-resolution product image

**File Format**:
- Accepted: .JPEG, .PNG
- Recommended: .PNG for transparency

### Supported Product Categories

**Currently Supported**:
- Lipstick and lip products
- Eyewear (glasses, sunglasses)
- Select beauty products (eyeshadow, foundation)
- Home decor (furniture, wall art - limited)

**Requirements**:
- Product must be suitable for AR try-on
- 3D model must accurately represent product
- Multiple variations (colors, styles) recommended

### Technical Requirements

**Device Compatibility**:
- iOS devices with ARKit support (iPhone 6S and later)
- Android devices with ARCore support
- Minimum OS: iOS 11, Android 7.0

**Product Tagging**:
- Products must be tagged in Pin
- Linked to product catalog
- Accurate product information required

## General Technical Requirements

### File Naming

**Best Practices**:
- Use descriptive names (product-name-blue-1000x1500.jpg)
- Avoid special characters
- Use hyphens or underscores, not spaces
- Include dimensions for organization

### Color and Quality

**Color Space**:
- RGB (not CMYK)
- sRGB color profile recommended

**Resolution**:
- 72-150 DPI for web
- Higher resolution for large images

**Compression**:
- Balance quality and file size
- Avoid over-compression (artifacts, pixelation)
- Use lossless compression for graphics

### URL Requirements

**Destination URLs**:
- Must be HTTPS (secure)
- Must match claimed domain in Pinterest Business account
- Can include UTM parameters for tracking
- Must lead to relevant, functional landing page

**Image URLs** (for feeds):
- Must be HTTPS
- Must be publicly accessible
- Must be direct image links (not HTML pages)
- Should be stable (not change frequently)

### Accessibility

**Alt Text**:
- Recommended for all images
- Descriptive, keyword-rich
- Helps with SEO and accessibility

**Captions** (for videos):
- Recommended for all videos
- Improves accessibility
- Enhances sound-off viewing

### Ad Review Process

**Review Time**:
- Typically 24 hours
- Can be faster (few hours) or slower (48 hours)

**Common Rejection Reasons**:
- Low-quality images or videos
- Excessive text overlay
- Misleading or clickbait content
- Prohibited products or content
- Technical specification violations
- Landing page issues (slow load, broken links)

**Resubmission**:
- Fix issues and resubmit
- Contact Pinterest support if unclear
- Review Pinterest Advertising Guidelines
