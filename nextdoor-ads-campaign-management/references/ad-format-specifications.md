# Nextdoor Ad Format Specifications
Complete technical specs for all Nextdoor ad formats across placements.
## Newsfeed Image Ad
- Resolution: 1200x628 (16:9) or 1200x1200 (1:1)
- Format: JPEG or PNG, max 150MB
- Headline: 70 chars, Body: 1500 chars (90 before truncation)
- CTA: 20 chars
## Newsfeed Video Ad  
- Length: 2-120s, Format: MOV or MP4, max 500MB
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or 1:1
- Headline: 70 chars, Body: 1500 chars
## Carousel Ad
- 2-10 images, 1200x1200 each, max 150MB per image
- Multi-destination URLs supported
## Lead Gen Ad
- 2-11 form fields (pre-filled + custom questions)
- Privacy policy URL required
