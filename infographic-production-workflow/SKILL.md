# Infographic Production Workflow

Manage the complete infographic production process from concept through delivery.

## Overview

A structured 7-phase workflow ensuring infographics are created efficiently and meet quality standards.

## Phases

| Phase | Duration | Deliverable | Reference |
|-------|----------|------------|-----------|
| 1. Discovery | 2-3 days | Brief, data | `/references/discovery-design-phases.md` |
| 2. Content Strategy | 1-2 days | Story, architecture | `/references/discovery-design-phases.md` |
| 3. Wireframing | 1-2 days | Layout sketches | `/references/discovery-design-phases.md` |
| 4. Visual Design | 3-5 days | Full design | `/references/discovery-design-phases.md` |
| 5. Review | 2-3 days | Approved design | `/references/review-delivery-phases.md` |
| 6. Finalization | 1-2 days | Production files | `/references/review-delivery-phases.md` |
| 7. Delivery | 1 day | Handoff complete | `/references/review-delivery-phases.md` |

## Roles
- Project Manager, Content Strategist, Researcher, Copywriter, Designer, Stakeholder

## Timelines
- Standard: 2-4 weeks
- Rush: 1-2 weeks
- Complex: 4-8 weeks
