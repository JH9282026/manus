# Geopolitical Research & Analysis

Systematic geopolitical investigation to identify risks, opportunities, and strategic implications for organizations operating internationally.

## Overview

This skill provides frameworks for analyzing geopolitical landscapes including country risk assessment, scenario planning, competitive intelligence on state actors, sanctions analysis, and regional power dynamics. It integrates classical geopolitical theories with contemporary risk methodologies to produce actionable intelligence.

## Core Analytical Domains

### Country Risk Assessment
- Evaluate political stability via regime change probability, governance quality, corruption indices
- Assess economic risks: debt-to-GDP, currency stability, sovereign credit ratings (S&P, Moody's, Fitch)
- Measure social cohesion: civil unrest potential, regulatory predictability, foreign investment attitude
- Use ICRG 22-variable methodology across political, financial, and economic dimensions

### Regional Dynamics
- Map territorial disputes and border conflict escalation potential
- Analyze resource competition vectors: energy security, water scarcity, critical minerals
- Assess strategic locations: maritime choke points (Hormuz, Malacca, Suez), trade routes
- Apply classical frameworks: Mackinder's Heartland, Spykman's Rimland, Mahan's sea power
