## Templates and Examples


### Promotional SMS Templates

**Flash Sale:**
> "[Brand]: ⚡ FLASH SALE! [X]% off [category] for [time]. Shop: [link] Reply STOP to opt out"

**Holiday:**
> "[Brand]: 🎄 Holiday special! [Offer details]. [Deadline]. Shop: [link] Text STOP to unsubscribe"

**Back in Stock:**
> "[Brand]: Good news! [Product] is back in stock. Get yours before it's gone: [link] Reply STOP to opt out"


### Transactional SMS Templates

**Order Shipped:**
> "[Brand]: Your order #[number] has shipped! Track: [link]. Arrives [date]. Questions? Reply here."

**Delivery Confirmation:**
> "[Brand]: Delivered! Your package was left [location]. How was your experience? Reply 1-5."

**Appointment Reminder:**
> "[Business]: Reminder - [Service] appt [date] at [time]. [Location]. Reply C to confirm, R to reschedule."


### Conversational SMS Templates

**Welcome:**
> "Welcome to [Brand]! 👋 Reply anytime with questions—real humans here to help. For deals, text DEALS."

**Support:**
> "Thanks for reaching out! A team member will reply shortly. For faster help, try our FAQ: [link]"

**Feedback:**
> "Thanks for your recent [purchase/visit]! How would you rate your experience? Reply 1-5 (5 = best)."
