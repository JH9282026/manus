## Transactional SMS

Transactional messages inform customers about orders, accounts, and services.



### Order Confirmations

**Essential Information:**
- Order number
- Confirmation of items/services
- Expected timeline
- How to track or modify
- Contact information

**Example:**
> "[Brand] Order #12345 confirmed! 3 items, total $127.50. Ships in 1-2 days. Track: [link] Questions? Reply here."



### Shipping Updates

**Include:**
- Carrier and tracking number (or link)
- Expected delivery date
- Delivery instructions if needed

**Examples:**
> "Your order is on the way! 📦 Track with USPS: [link]. Expected delivery: Thursday Oct 21."
> "Delivered! Your [Brand] order was left at your front door. How was your experience? Reply 1-5."



### Appointment Communications

**Confirmation:**
> "Confirmed: [Service] with [Provider], Wed Oct 20 at 2 PM. Location: [Address]. Reply C to confirm, R to reschedule."

**Reminder (24 hours):**
> "Reminder: Your appointment is tomorrow at 2 PM. Reply C to confirm. Need to reschedule? Reply R or call 555-1234."

**Day-of Reminder:**
> "See you today at 2 PM! Address: [Location]. Running late? Reply here to let us know."
