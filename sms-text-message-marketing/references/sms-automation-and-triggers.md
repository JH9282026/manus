## SMS Automation and Triggers



### Behavior-Triggered Messages

**Abandoned Cart:**
> Trigger: Item in cart, no purchase in 2 hours
> Message: "Forget something? Your cart is waiting: [link]. Complete your order for free shipping!"

**Browse Abandonment:**
> Trigger: Viewed product, left site
> Message: "Still thinking about [Product]? It's going fast! Shop now: [link]"

**Purchase Follow-Up:**
> Trigger: 7 days after purchase
> Message: "How are you enjoying [Product]? We'd love to hear your thoughts. Leave a review: [link]"

**Win-Back:**
> Trigger: No purchase in 90 days
> Message: "We miss you! Here's $10 off your next order: code COMEBACK. Shop: [link]"



### Event-Triggered Messages

**Birthday:**
> "[Name], happy birthday! 🎂 Here's a gift: 20% off your next purchase. Use code BDAY20 by [date]."

**Anniversary:**
> "It's been 1 year since you joined [Brand]! Thanks for being with us. Enjoy 15% off: code THANKYOU"

**Loyalty Milestone:**
> "Congrats! You've earned Gold status. 🏆 Enjoy free shipping on all orders. Shop now: [link]"
