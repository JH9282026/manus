## SMS Marketing Trends


### Rich Communication Services (RCS)

RCS is the evolution of SMS, offering richer features.

**RCS Features:**
- High-resolution images and video
- Interactive carousels
- Suggested replies and actions
- Read receipts
- Typing indicators
- Branding and logos

**Current Adoption:**
- Supported on Android devices
- Growing carrier adoption
- Apple adding support
- Not yet universal


### AI and SMS

Artificial intelligence is enhancing SMS marketing.

**AI Applications:**
- Predictive send time optimization
- Automated personalization
- Sentiment analysis for responses
- Chatbot-driven conversations
- Content optimization


### Conversational Commerce

SMS evolving from broadcast to conversation.

**Two-Way SMS:**
- Customer service via text
- Personal shopping assistance
- Appointment scheduling
- Order modifications
- Feedback collection

**Example Conversation:**
```
Business: "Thanks for your order! Reply here with any questions."
Customer: "Can I change my shipping address?"
Business: "Of course! What's the new address?"
Customer: "123 Main St, Apt 4, Boston MA 02101"
Business: "Updated! Your order will now ship to 123 Main St. Anything else?"
Customer: "No, thanks!"
Business: "You're welcome! Have a great day. 📦"
```
