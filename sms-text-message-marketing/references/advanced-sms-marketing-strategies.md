## Advanced SMS Marketing Strategies


### Segmentation Strategies

Effective SMS marketing requires sophisticated segmentation to deliver relevant messages.

**Behavioral Segmentation:**
- Purchase history (bought vs. browsed)
- Engagement level (active vs. dormant)
- Response history (clickers vs. non-clickers)
- Cart abandoners
- VIP customers (high spenders)

**Demographic Segmentation:**
- Location (city, region, timezone)
- Age group
- Gender
- Income level

**Lifecycle Segmentation:**
- New subscribers (welcome series)
- Active customers (engagement messages)
- Lapsed customers (win-back campaigns)
- Loyal customers (exclusive offers)

**Segmented Campaign Example:**

**VIP Customers:**
> "VIP EXCLUSIVE: Early access to our Black Friday sale starts NOW for you only. Shop before everyone else: [link]"

**Dormant Customers:**
> "We miss you! Here's 25% off your next order—just for you. Use code COMEBACK25: [link]"

**Local Customers:**
> "Hey Chicago! Pop into our downtown store today for an extra 15% off. Show this text at checkout."


### Personalization at Scale

Move beyond first-name personalization to truly relevant messaging.

**Advanced Personalization:**
- Product recommendations based on purchase history
- Restocking reminders based on purchase cycle
- Birthday and anniversary offers
- Weather-triggered messages
- Event-based triggers

**Examples:**

**Restock Reminder:**
> "Running low on [Product]? Reorder now and get free shipping: [link]"

**Weather-Triggered:**
> "☀️ It's going to be 85° in Austin this weekend! Stock up on sunscreen: [link]"

**Purchase Anniversary:**
> "It's been 1 year since you got your [Product]! Here's 20% off your next upgrade: [link]"


### A/B Testing for SMS

Optimize performance through systematic testing.

**What to Test:**
- Message copy and tone
- Offer type and amount
- CTA wording
- Send time
- Personalization elements
- Emoji usage

**Testing Process:**
1. Form hypothesis (e.g., "Urgency will increase CTR")
2. Create variants (A: standard, B: with urgency)
3. Split audience randomly (at least 1,000 per variant)
4. Send simultaneously
5. Measure results (CTR, conversion, opt-out)
6. Apply learnings to future campaigns

**Test Example:**

**Variant A:**
> "New arrivals just dropped. Shop now: [link]"

**Variant B:**
> "🚨 New arrivals selling fast! Shop now before they're gone: [link]"

**Results:**
- A: 8% CTR, 2.1% conversion
- B: 12% CTR, 2.8% conversion, 0.3% higher opt-out
- **Winner:** B (higher engagement, acceptable opt-out increase)


### SMS and Email Integration

Combine channels for maximum impact.

**Coordinated Campaigns:**
- Email for detailed content and storytelling
- SMS for urgency, reminders, and time-sensitive offers
- Use each channel for its strengths

**Integration Strategies:**

**Sequential:**
1. Day 1: Email announces sale
2. Day 3: SMS reminder
3. Day 5: Email with product highlights
4. Day 6: SMS "ending soon" reminder

**Preference-Based:**
- Ask subscribers their preferred channel
- Heavy email engagers get more emails
- SMS-responsive users get more texts

**Fallback:**
- If email unopened after 48 hours, send SMS
- Critical alerts always go to both channels
