## Best Practices Summary


### 15 SMS Marketing Best Practices

1. **Obtain proper consent** before sending any messages
2. **Identify yourself** in every message
3. **Keep messages under 160 characters** when possible
4. **Personalize** with names and relevant offers
5. **Include clear CTAs** in every message
6. **Time messages appropriately** (business hours, local time)
7. **Limit frequency** to avoid fatigue
8. **Provide value** with every message
9. **Make opt-out easy** and honor requests immediately
10. **Test before sending** (links, codes, timing)
11. **Segment your audience** for relevance
12. **Track and measure** results
13. **Integrate with other channels** for cohesive campaigns
14. **Use automation** for triggered messages
15. **Stay compliant** with TCPA, GDPR, and other regulations

### Platform Selection Criteria

- **Integration capabilities** with your existing tools
- **Compliance features** (consent management, opt-out handling)
- **Automation capabilities** for triggered messages
- **Segmentation options** for targeted messaging
- **Analytics and reporting** depth
- **Pricing structure** (per message vs. subscription)
- **Support quality** and availability


### Essential Features
