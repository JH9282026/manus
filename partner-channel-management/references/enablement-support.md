# Partner Enablement and Support

Comprehensive frameworks for building partner enablement programs, creating training content, and designing tiered support models.

---

## Partner Enablement Framework

### Enablement Objectives

Partner enablement aims to equip partners with the knowledge, skills, tools, and resources to effectively:

1. **Identify and Qualify** target customers and opportunities
2. **Articulate Value** through compelling messaging and positioning
3. **Demonstrate Solutions** via effective demos and presentations
4. **Navigate Sales Process** from discovery through close
5. **Implement and Support** customers for success and retention
6. **Generate Demand** through marketing and outreach activities

### Enablement Pillars

**Product Knowledge**
- Features, capabilities, and use cases
- Technical architecture and integrations
- Pricing, packaging, and licensing
- Roadmap and future direction
- Competitive differentiation

**Sales Skills**
- Discovery and qualification techniques
- Value-based selling methodology
- Demo delivery and storytelling
- Objection handling and negotiation
- Closing strategies and techniques

**Market and Customer Understanding**
- Target customer profiles and personas
- Industry trends and challenges
- Buyer journey and decision process
- Competitive landscape and positioning
- Customer success stories and references

**Tools and Resources**
- Sales playbooks and scripts
- Marketing materials and campaigns
- Demo environments and assets
- Proposal and quote templates
- ROI calculators and business case tools

**Processes and Systems**
- Deal registration and protection
- Quoting and contracting procedures
- Order fulfillment and provisioning
- Support escalation and resources
- Partner portal and PRM usage

## Sales Enablement

### Core Sales Training

**Module 1: Company and Product Overview** (2 hours)

**Content**:
- Company history, mission, and values
- Product portfolio and positioning
- Target markets and customers
- Competitive landscape
- Partner program overview

**Delivery**: Live webinar or recorded video

**Assessment**: 10-question quiz (80% to pass)

**Module 2: Value Proposition and Messaging** (2 hours)

**Content**:
- Core value propositions by persona
- Key messages and talk tracks
- Elevator pitch and positioning statements
- Customer pain points and solutions
- Differentiation from competitors

**Delivery**: Live workshop with practice

**Assessment**: Deliver 2-minute pitch to partner manager

**Module 3: Discovery and Qualification** (3 hours)

**Content**:
- Qualification frameworks (BANT, MEDDIC, CHAMP)
- Discovery question bank
- Active listening and probing techniques
- Identifying stakeholders and decision process
- Red flags and disqualification criteria

**Delivery**: Live training with role-play

**Assessment**: Conduct mock discovery call

**Module 4: Product Demonstration** (4 hours)

**Content**:
- Demo best practices and structure
- Feature-to-benefit translation
- Storytelling and use case scenarios
- Handling technical questions
- Demo environment setup and navigation

**Delivery**: Hands-on demo training and practice

**Assessment**: Deliver 30-minute demo to partner manager

**Module 5: Proposal and Closing** (2 hours)

**Content**:
- Proposal development and customization
- Pricing and packaging recommendations
- ROI and business case building
- Negotiation strategies and tactics
- Closing techniques and next steps

**Delivery**: Live training with examples

**Assessment**: Create sample proposal for case study

**Module 6: Competitive Positioning** (2 hours)

**Content**:
- Competitive landscape overview
- Head-to-head comparisons and battlecards
- Competitive traps and landmines
- Displacement strategies
- Handling competitive objections

**Delivery**: Live training with role-play

**Assessment**: Handle competitive scenario

**Total Core Sales Training**: 15 hours over 2-3 weeks

### Advanced Sales Training

**Enterprise Sales Methodology** (8 hours)
- Complex sales process and stakeholder management
- Executive engagement and C-level selling
- Multi-threaded account strategies
- Consensus building and political navigation
- Large deal negotiation and closing

**Vertical/Industry Specialization** (4 hours per vertical)
- Industry trends, challenges, and regulations
- Vertical-specific use cases and solutions
- Industry terminology and language
- Key players and competitive landscape
- Customer references and case studies

**Solution Selling Workshop** (4 hours)
- Consultative selling approach
- Business problem identification

### Sales Playbooks

**Playbook Components**:

**1. Situation**: When to use this playbook
- Customer profile and characteristics
- Buying signals and triggers
- Opportunity type and stage

**2. Objectives**: What success looks like
- Desired outcomes and next steps
- Key decisions or commitments
- Metrics and success criteria

**3. Preparation**: What to do before engagement
- Research and discovery
- Stakeholder identification
- Materials and resources needed
- Internal team alignment

**4. Execution**: Step-by-step approach
- Meeting agenda and flow
- Key questions to ask
- Messages and positioning
- Demo or presentation outline

**5. Objection Handling**: Common pushback and responses
- Typical objections by type
- Recommended responses and rebuttals
- Proof points and evidence
- When to concede vs. persist

**6. Next Steps**: How to advance the opportunity
- Recommended next actions
- Timeline and milestones
- Stakeholder engagement plan
- Internal follow-up and support

**Example Playbooks**:
- New customer acquisition
- Competitive displacement
- Expansion/upsell to existing customer
- Renewal and retention
- Executive engagement
- Proof of concept/pilot
- Procurement and legal negotiation

## Marketing Enablement

### Co-Branded Marketing Materials

**Customization Levels**:

**Level 1: Partner Logo Addition**
- Vendor-created materials with partner logo added
- Minimal customization, fast turnaround
- Suitable for: Brochures, one-pagers, presentations

**Level 2: Co-Branded Templates**
- Templates with both vendor and partner branding
- Partner can customize content within guidelines
- Suitable for: Email campaigns, social posts, blog posts

**Level 3: Fully Custom**
- Partner creates materials with vendor approval
- Maximum flexibility and personalization
- Suitable for: Case studies, whitepapers, webinars

**Brand Guidelines**:
- Logo usage and placement rules
- Color palette and fonts
- Messaging and positioning guidelines
- Approval process and timeline
- Examples and templates

### Technical Certification

**Certification Levels**:

**Associate Certification**
- Prerequisites: Foundational technical training
- Format: 50-question multiple choice exam
- Passing score: 80%
- Validity: 1 year
- Demonstrates: Basic product knowledge and implementation skills

**Professional Certification**
- Prerequisites: Associate certification + 3 implementations
- Format: Hands-on lab exam (4 hours)
- Passing score: 85%
- Validity: 2 years
- Demonstrates: Advanced configuration and troubleshooting skills

**Expert Certification**
- Prerequisites: Professional certification + 10 implementations
- Format: Project review and oral exam
- Passing score: 90%
- Validity: 3 years
- Demonstrates: Architecture design and complex implementation expertise

**Certification Benefits**:
- Listed in partner directory
- Certification badge for marketing
- Access to advanced resources and support
- Eligibility for complex deals and projects
- Higher partner tier qualification

## Partner Support Model

### Tiered Support Structure

**Tier 1: Self-Service (All Partners)**

**Resources**:
- Partner portal and knowledge base
- Documentation and user guides
- Video tutorials and webinars
- Community forums and discussions
- Chatbot and automated support

**Response Time**: Immediate (self-service)

**Availability**: 24/7

**Tier 2: Partner Help Desk (Registered Partners+)**

**Resources**:
- Email and ticket-based support
- Shared partner success team
- Standard SLA for response and resolution
- Access to technical support for escalations

**Response Time**:
- P1 (Critical): 4 hours
- P2 (High): 1 business day
- P3 (Medium): 2 business days
- P4 (Low): 5 business days

**Availability**: Business hours (8am-6pm local time)

**Tier 3: Dedicated Partner Manager (Silver Partners+)**

**Resources**:
- Named partner manager (shared across 10-20 partners)
- Regular check-ins (monthly)
- Proactive support and guidance
- Deal coaching and strategy
- Escalation and advocacy

**Response Time**:
- P1 (Critical): 2 hours
- P2 (High): 4 hours
- P3 (Medium): 1 business day
- P4 (Low): 2 business days

**Availability**: Extended hours (7am-7pm local time)

**Tier 4: Strategic Partner Support (Gold/Platinum Partners)**

**Resources**:
- Dedicated partner manager (1:5-10 partner ratio)
- Weekly check-ins and pipeline reviews
- Executive sponsor and escalation
- Priority access to resources and specialists
- Custom enablement and support programs

**Response Time**:
- P1 (Critical): 1 hour
- P2 (High): 2 hours
- P3 (Medium): 4 hours
- P4 (Low): 1 business day

**Availability**: 24/7 for P1, extended hours for others
