# Taboola Targeting Strategies

Advanced targeting techniques for Taboola campaigns.

## Geographic Targeting

- **Country-level**: Broad reach
- **State/Province**: Regional campaigns
- **City**: Local businesses
- **Postal Code**: Hyper-local

## Platform Targeting

- **Desktop**: Higher engagement, B2B
- **Mobile**: Higher volume, impulse purchases
- **Tablet**: Middle ground

## Audience Targeting

**Behavioral Segments:**
- Pre-built segments by interest
- Categories: Auto, Finance, Travel, Health

**Custom Audiences:**
- Customer lists (email, phone)
- Website visitors
- Converters

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- 1%: Most similar
- 3-5%: Balanced
- 10%: Broadest reach

## Publisher Targeting

- Auto-targeting: Algorithm optimizes
- Whitelist: Approved publishers only
- Blacklist: Exclude poor performers
- Bid modifiers: Adjust bids per publisher
