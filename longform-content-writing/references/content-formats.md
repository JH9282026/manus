# Long-Form Content Formats

## White Paper Structure

### Purpose
Persuade readers through authoritative, research-backed content that positions the organization as a thought leader while subtly guiding toward a solution.

### Standard Structure
1. **Title page** — Compelling title, subtitle, author, date, company
2. **Executive summary** — Problem statement, key findings, 200-300 words
3. **Introduction** — Context, scope, why this matters now
4. **Problem definition** — Detailed exploration of the challenge
5. **Current landscape** — Market analysis, trends, statistics
6. **Solution framework** — Methodology, approach, best practices
7. **Case evidence** — Examples, data, proof points
8. **Implementation** — Practical steps, considerations
9. **Conclusion** — Summary, call to action
10. **References** — Sources, citations, further reading

### White Paper Types
| Type | Focus | Tone |
|------|-------|------|
| Problem-Solution | Address specific pain point | Educational, consultative |
| Backgrounder | Product/technology deep dive | Technical, detailed |
| Numbered List | Best practices or trends | Accessible, scannable |
| Research Report | Original findings | Data-driven, analytical |

## Ebook Structure

### Chapter Organization
- 5-10 chapters for standard ebook
- Each chapter: 1,000-2,500 words
- Opening hook + context + content + transition to next chapter
- Visual chapter dividers

### Design Integration
- Professional cover design
- Consistent page layout template
- Pull quotes and callout boxes
- Chapter summary sections
- Interactive table of contents
- Author bio and company info

## Industry Report Structure

### Methodology Section
- Research methodology description
- Sample size and demographics
- Data collection period
- Statistical methods used
- Limitations acknowledgment

### Data Presentation
- Executive summary with key findings
- Infographic overview of main statistics
- Detailed findings by category
- Year-over-year comparison (if applicable)
- Expert commentary and analysis
- Actionable recommendations
- Methodology appendix
