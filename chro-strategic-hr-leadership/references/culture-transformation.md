# Culture Transformation

## Understanding Culture

### Culture Elements
- **Artifacts**: Visible symbols, office layout, rituals
- **Espoused values**: Stated beliefs and policies
- **Underlying assumptions**: Deep beliefs driving behavior

### Culture Dimensions
- Innovation vs. stability
- Internal vs. external focus
- Control vs. flexibility
- Individual vs. collective

---

## Culture Assessment

### Assessment Methods

| Method | Strengths | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Surveys | Quantitative, scalable | Baseline, tracking |
| Focus groups | Rich qualitative data | Deep understanding |
| Interviews | Leadership perspective | Executive alignment |
| Observation | Actual vs. stated | Reality check |

### Assessment Dimensions
- Values alignment
- Behavior patterns
- Leadership style
- Communication norms
- Decision-making approach
- Risk tolerance
- Customer orientation

## Culture Change Process

### Phase 1: Define Target Culture
- Articulate cultural attributes needed
- Link to business strategy
- Define behavioral anchors
- Create culture vision

### Phase 2: Assess Current State
- Measure current culture
- Identify gaps from target
- Understand root causes
- Map subcultures

### Phase 3: Design Interventions

| Lever | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| Leadership | Role modeling, messaging |
| Structure | Reporting, decision rights |
| Systems | Performance management, rewards |
| Processes | How work gets done |
| Talent | Hiring, development, promotion |
| Symbols | Office design, rituals, stories |

### Phase 4: Execute Systematically
- Phase interventions strategically
- Enable leaders as culture carriers
- Embed in daily operations
- Maintain visible commitment

### Phase 5: Measure and Sustain
- Track culture metrics
- Celebrate progress
- Address regression
- Integrate into ongoing management

---

## Leader's Role in Culture

### Critical Behaviors
- **Model** desired behaviors personally
- **Communicate** culture vision consistently
- **Recognize** cultural behaviors when observed
- **Address** counter-cultural behaviors directly
- **Embed** culture in decisions and processes

### Culture Carrier Enablement
- Define expected leader behaviors
- Provide coaching and feedback
- Hold accountable through performance
- Share success stories
