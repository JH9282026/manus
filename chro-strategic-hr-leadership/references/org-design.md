# Organizational Design

## Design Principles

### Alignment Principles
- Structure follows strategy
- Enable customer focus
- Support speed and agility
- Optimize for efficiency
- Build accountability

### Design Elements
- Reporting relationships
- Span of control
- Decision rights
- Coordination mechanisms
- Performance metrics

---

## Structure Options

| Structure | Best For | Trade-offs |
|-----------|----------|------------|
| **Functional** | Efficiency, expertise | Silos, slow coordination |
| **Divisional** | Customer/product focus | Duplication, inconsistency |
| **Matrix** | Complex coordination | Confusion, conflict |
| **Network** | Flexibility, innovation | Complexity, accountability |
| **Hybrid** | Balancing multiple needs | Design complexity |

---

## Span of Control Guidelines

### Factors Affecting Optimal Span
- Work complexity and variability
- Employee experience and capability
- Manager capacity and skill
- Geographic dispersion
- System and process maturity

### Benchmark Ranges

| Role Type | Typical Span | Considerations |
|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| Executive | 5-8 | Strategic complexity |
| Middle management | 6-10 | Coordination needs |
| Front-line management | 8-15 | Work standardization |
| Highly routine work | 15-25 | Process maturity |

## Role Design

### Role Components
- Purpose and outcomes
- Key responsibilities
- Decision authority
- Relationships and interfaces
- Success measures
- Required capabilities

### RACI Framework
- **Responsible**: Does the work
- **Accountable**: Owns the outcome
- **Consulted**: Provides input
- **Informed**: Kept aware

---

## Organization Transformation

### Transformation Steps

1. **Current State Assessment**
   - Document existing structure
   - Identify pain points
   - Assess effectiveness

2. **Future State Design**
   - Develop design options
   - Model implications
   - Select preferred design

3. **Transition Planning**
   - Create migration approach
   - Plan talent moves
   - Design change management

4. **Implementation**
   - Execute in phases
   - Manage people impacts
   - Monitor and adjust

### Change Management Integration
- Communicate vision and rationale
- Address individual impacts
- Enable managers to lead change
- Provide support resources
