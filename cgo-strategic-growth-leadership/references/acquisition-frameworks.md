# Acquisition Frameworks

Strategies and frameworks for customer acquisition.

---

## Channel Strategy

### Channel Economics

| Channel | CAC Range | Best For | Scalability |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Organic/SEO | Low | Long-term, sustainable | High |
| Content Marketing | Low-Medium | Thought leadership | Medium |
| Paid Search | Medium-High | Intent-driven | High |
| Paid Social | Medium | Awareness, targeting | High |
| Direct Sales | High | Enterprise, complex | Low-Medium |
| Channel Partners | Variable | Coverage, leverage | High |
| Events | High | Relationship, brand | Low |

### Channel Mix Optimization

| Stage | Recommended Mix |
|-------|----------------|
| Early | Founder sales, outbound, content |
| Growth | Diversified paid + organic |
| Scale | Optimized multi-channel |
| Mature | Efficiency focus, brand |

## Funnel Optimization

### Full-Funnel Framework

| Stage | Goal | Tactics | Metrics |
|-------|------|---------|--------|
| Awareness | Drive traffic | Content, SEO, paid, PR | Visitors, reach |
| Interest | Capture leads | Lead magnets, email capture | Leads, conversion |
| Consideration | Nurture | Email, retargeting, demos | MQLs, engagement |
| Evaluation | Qualify | Sales engagement, trials | SQLs, pipeline |
| Purchase | Convert | Proposals, negotiation | Deals, revenue |

### Conversion Optimization

| Element | Testing Approach |
|---------|------------------|
| Headlines | A/B test messaging |
| CTAs | Button text, placement, color |
| Forms | Field count, friction |
| Social Proof | Testimonials, logos, reviews |
| Pricing Display | Presentation, anchoring |

## Product-Led Growth (PLG)

### PLG Motion Design

| Element | Design Question |
|---------|----------------|
| Free Tier | What's valuable enough to attract? |
| Activation | What creates "aha" moment? |
| Conversion Trigger | What drives paid upgrade? |
| Expansion | What increases usage/seats? |
| Virality | What drives sharing/invites? |

### Freemium Design

| Approach | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Feature-Limited | Core features free, advanced paid | Slack, Zoom |
| Usage-Limited | Free to threshold | Dropbox, Mailchimp |
| Time-Limited | Free trial period | Most SaaS |
| Seat-Limited | Free for small teams | Notion, Asana |

### PLG Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Activation Rate | % completing key action | >40% |
| Free-to-Paid Conversion | % upgrading to paid | 2-5% |
| Time to Value | Days to activation | <7 days |
| Viral Coefficient | Invites per user | >0.5 |
| Product Qualified Leads | Users showing buying signals | Growing |

## Sales Motions

### Motion Selection

| ACV Range | Motion | Characteristics |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| <$1K | Self-Serve | No touch, PLG |
| $1K-$10K | Inside Sales | Phone/video, transactional |
| $10K-$100K | Field Sales | Relationship, consultative |
| >$100K | Enterprise | Strategic, multi-threaded |

### Sales-Marketing Alignment

| Element | Alignment Point |
|---------|----------------|
| Lead Definition | Agreed MQL/SQL criteria |
| Handoff Process | When and how leads transfer |
| SLA | Response time expectations |
| Feedback Loop | Win/loss, lead quality |
| Shared Metrics | Pipeline, revenue contribution |
