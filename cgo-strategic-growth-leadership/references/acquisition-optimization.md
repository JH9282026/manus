# Acquisition Optimization

Strategies for optimizing customer acquisition across channels.

---

## Channel Deep Dives

### Paid Search (SEM)
**Best For:** High-intent prospects actively searching

**Optimization Levers:**
- Keyword targeting and match types
- Ad copy testing
- Landing page optimization
- Bid strategy (manual vs. automated)
- Quality score improvement

**Key Metrics:**
- CPC (Cost per Click)
- CTR (Click-through Rate)
- Conversion Rate
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

### Paid Social
**Best For:** Awareness, consideration, retargeting

**Platforms:**
| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| Facebook/Instagram | B2C, broad targeting |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional targeting |
| Twitter | Tech, media, real-time |
| TikTok | Gen Z, viral content |

**Optimization Levers:**
- Audience targeting
- Creative formats and testing
- Placement optimization
- Bid and budget strategy
- Retargeting sequences

### Content Marketing
**Best For:** Organic growth, authority building

**Content Types:**
| Type | Funnel Stage |
|------|-------------|
| Blog posts | Awareness |
| Ebooks/guides | Consideration |
| Case studies | Decision |
| Webinars | Consideration/Decision |

**SEO Optimization:**
- Keyword research and targeting
- On-page optimization
- Technical SEO
- Link building
- Content refresh strategy

### Outbound Sales
**Best For:** Enterprise, high-value accounts

**Components:**
- Account selection (ICP definition)
- Contact identification
- Outreach sequences (email, phone, social)
- Meeting booking
- Handoff to AE

## Funnel Optimization

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

**Framework:**
1. **Analyze** current funnel data
2. **Hypothesize** improvement opportunities
3. **Prioritize** based on impact/effort
4. **Test** with A/B experiments
5. **Implement** winning variations
6. **Iterate** continuously

### Landing Page Optimization

**Elements to Test:**
| Element | Variations |
|---------|------------|
| Headline | Value prop, problem, benefit |
| CTA | Text, color, placement |
| Form | Length, fields, steps |
| Social proof | Testimonials, logos, numbers |
| Images | Hero image, product shots |

### Lead Nurturing

**Email Sequence Structure:**
1. Welcome and value delivery
2. Educational content
3. Case study/social proof
4. Product introduction
5. Conversion offer
6. Urgency/scarcity

## Attribution Modeling

### Attribution Models
| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| First Touch | All credit to first interaction | Awareness focus |
| Last Touch | All credit to last interaction | Conversion focus |
| Linear | Equal credit to all touches | Balanced view |
| Time Decay | More credit to recent touches | Long sales cycles |
| Position | 40% first, 40% last, 20% middle | Comprehensive |
| Algorithmic | Data-driven attribution | Sophisticated analytics |

### Multi-Touch Attribution
- Track all touchpoints
- Assign weighted credit
- Understand full journey
- Optimize channel mix

## CAC Optimization

### CAC Reduction Strategies
| Strategy | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| Improve conversion rates | Funnel optimization |
| Channel mix optimization | Shift to lower-CAC channels |
| Targeting improvement | Better audience selection |
| Creative optimization | Higher-performing ads |
| Referral programs | Leverage existing customers |
| Product-led growth | Product as acquisition channel |

### Payback Period Optimization
- Reduce CAC
- Increase ARPU
- Improve gross margin
- Accelerate time to revenue
