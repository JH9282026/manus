# Revenue Optimization

Strategies for pricing, packaging, and revenue growth.

---

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Approaches

| Approach | Method | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Value-Based | Price based on value delivered | Differentiated products |
| Competitive | Based on market pricing | Commoditized markets |
| Cost-Plus | Margin over costs | Low differentiation |
| Penetration | Low price for market share | New market entry |
| Premium | High price for exclusivity | Luxury, innovation |

### Value-Based Pricing Process

1. **Identify value drivers** — What do customers value?
2. **Quantify value** — Calculate economic value delivered
3. **Segment by value** — Different value = different price
4. **Set price** — Capture fair share of value
5. **Communicate value** — Justify price with value

### Pricing Research Methods

| Method | Description | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Van Westendorp | Survey-based price sensitivity | Early exploration |
| Conjoint Analysis | Feature/price tradeoffs | Packaging decisions |
| A/B Testing | Live price tests | Optimization |
| Competitive Analysis | Benchmark vs market | Positioning |

## Packaging Design

### Package Structures

| Structure | Description | Best For |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Good-Better-Best | Three tiers | Clear segmentation |
| Per-Seat | Price per user | Collaboration tools |
| Usage-Based | Pay for consumption | Variable usage |
| Feature-Based | Price by capability | Diverse needs |
| Hybrid | Combination | Complex value |

### Good-Better-Best Design

| Tier | Role | Pricing |
|------|------|--------|
| Good | Entry point, volume | Lowest price |
| Better | Most popular, balanced | Target margin |
| Best | Premium, full featured | Highest margin |

### Feature Gating

| Gating Approach | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Capability | Features available by tier |
| Capacity | Limits (users, storage, API calls) |
| Support | Service level by tier |
| Access | Integrations, customization |

## Expansion Revenue

### Expansion Mechanisms

| Type | Mechanism | Tactics |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Upsell | Higher tier | Feature gates, usage limits |
| Cross-Sell | Additional products | Bundling, recommendations |
| Usage Growth | More consumption | Usage-based pricing |
| Seat Expansion | More users | Viral mechanics, team growth |
| Price Increases | Annual adjustment | Value communication |

### Net Revenue Retention

**NRR = (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting MRR**

| NRR Range | Indication |
|-----------|------------|
| <100% | Shrinking revenue base |
| 100-110% | Healthy, stable |
| 110-120% | Strong expansion |
| >120% | Excellent, growth even without new customers |

### Expansion Playbook

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| Identify | Usage signals, health scores |
| Qualify | Expansion potential, timing |
| Engage | CSM or sales outreach |
| Propose | Tailored expansion offer |
| Close | Upgrade or add-on |
