# CGO Strategic Growth Leadership

Execute comprehensive growth strategy for market expansion, customer acquisition, and revenue optimization.

## Overview

This skill delivers Chief Growth Officer strategic capabilities: market expansion frameworks, customer acquisition optimization, revenue growth mechanisms, and data-driven growth analytics. Applicable across B2B, B2C, and hybrid business models for sustainable organizational growth.

## Growth Metrics

### Core Growth KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| CAC | Acquisition spend / New customers | Declining |
| LTV | Revenue × Margin × Lifetime | Growing |
| LTV:CAC | LTV / CAC | >3:1 |
| Payback | CAC / Monthly revenue | <12 months |
| NRR | (Start MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Start MRR | >100% |
| GRR | (Start MRR - Churn) / Start MRR | >85% |

### Growth Rate Metrics

| Metric | Calculation |
|--------|-------------|
| MoM Growth | (This Month - Last Month) / Last Month |
| YoY Growth | (This Year - Last Year) / Last Year |
| CAGR | (End Value / Start Value)^(1/n) - 1 |
| Rule of 40 | Revenue Growth Rate + Profit Margin |
