# CGO Strategic Growth Leadership

Provide comprehensive Chief Growth Officer capabilities including growth strategy, market expansion, customer acquisition, and data-driven growth analytics.

## Overview

This skill enables execution of strategic growth functions across B2B, B2C, and hybrid business models. Use this for developing growth strategies, optimizing acquisition funnels, expanding into new markets, building partnerships, and establishing growth analytics frameworks.

## CGO Role Framework

| Domain | Key Responsibilities |
|--------|---------------------|
| Strategy | Growth roadmap, market expansion |
| Acquisition | Customer acquisition, funnel optimization |
| Revenue | Monetization, expansion revenue |
| Partnerships | Channel partners, strategic alliances |
| Analytics | Growth metrics, experimentation |

## Growth Strategy Framework

### Market Opportunity Assessment

| Level | Definition | Sizing Approach |
|-------|------------|-----------------|
| TAM | Total Addressable Market | All possible revenue |
| SAM | Serviceable Addressable Market | What we can reach |
| SOM | Serviceable Obtainable Market | Realistic capture |

### Growth Vectors

| Vector | Description | Risk Level |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| Market Penetration | More share in existing market | Lower |
| Market Development | Existing product, new markets | Medium |
| Product Development | New products, existing market | Medium |
| Diversification | New products, new markets | Higher |

## Revenue Optimization

### Expansion Revenue Strategies

| Strategy | Description | Tactics |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Upselling | Higher tier/plan | Feature gates, usage growth |
| Cross-selling | Additional products | Bundling, recommendations |
| Usage growth | Increased consumption | Adoption, expansion |

### Key Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| MRR/ARR | Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue | Growing |
| Net Revenue Retention | (Start MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Start MRR | > 100% |
| Gross Revenue Retention | (Start MRR - Contraction - Churn) / Start MRR | > 90% |
| Expansion Rate | Expansion MRR / Start MRR | > 10% |

## Growth Metrics Dashboard

### Core Growth KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| CAC | Total S&M / New Customers | Industry varies |
| LTV | ARPU × Gross Margin × Lifespan | 3x+ CAC |
| LTV:CAC | LTV / CAC | > 3:1 |
| Payback Period | CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin) | < 12-18 months |
| Rule of 40 | Growth Rate + Profit Margin | > 40% |
