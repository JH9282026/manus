# Variance Analysis and Management Reporting

## Monthly Close and Variance Process

### Close Timeline (Target: 5 Business Days)

| Day | Activity |
|---|---|
| Day 1-2 | Sub-ledger close, journal entries, accruals |
| Day 3 | Consolidation, preliminary P&L |
| Day 4 | Variance analysis, commentary |
| Day 5 | Management reporting package distribution |

### Variance Analysis Workflow
1. Generate actual vs budget and actual vs forecast reports
2. Identify material variances (>threshold)
3. Decompose into volume, price, mix, timing components
4. Gather explanations from business owners
5. Assess impact on full-year forecast
6. Prepare variance commentary with actionable insights
7. Present to leadership with recommendations

---

## Variance Commentary Best Practices

### Structure for Each Material Variance
1. **What**: State the variance amount and direction
2. **Why**: Explain the root cause (not just "over/under budget")
3. **Impact**: Full-year impact if trend continues
4. **Action**: What is being done to address (if unfavorable)

### Example Commentary
"Revenue was $2.1M unfavorable to budget (-7%) driven by lower enterprise deal volume. Three large deals slipped from Q1 to Q2 due to extended procurement cycles. These deals remain in pipeline with updated close dates. Forecast reflects $1.5M recovery in Q2 with $600K permanent miss."

## KPI Dashboard Design

### Financial KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target Setting |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue growth | YoY or QoQ growth rate | Strategic plan target |
| Gross margin | Gross profit / Revenue | Prior year + improvement |
| Operating margin | Operating income / Revenue | Budget target |
| EBITDA | Earnings before interest, taxes, D&A | Budget target |
| Free cash flow | Operating CF - CapEx | Positive, growing |
| Cash conversion | FCF / Net Income | >80% strong |
| Revenue per employee | Revenue / FTE | Efficiency benchmark |
| CAC payback | CAC / (MRR × Gross Margin) | <18 months |

### Operational KPIs (by Function)

| Function | Key KPIs |
|---|---|
| Sales | Pipeline, win rate, ASP, sales cycle length |
| Marketing | MQLs, CAC, ROAS, website conversion |
| Product | DAU/MAU, feature adoption, NPS |
| Customer Success | Net revenue retention, churn, CSAT |
| Engineering | Sprint velocity, deployment frequency, uptime |
| HR | Headcount, attrition, time-to-hire, engagement |

## Board Reporting

### Board Package Structure
1. **Executive summary** (1 page): Key takeaways, strategic highlights
2. **Financial overview** (2-3 pages): P&L, cash flow, key metrics
3. **Business review** (3-5 pages): Sales pipeline, product updates, customer metrics
4. **Strategic initiatives** (1-2 pages): Progress on key projects
5. **Risk and opportunities** (1 page): Key risks with mitigation
6. **Appendix**: Detailed financials, supplementary data

### Board-Ready Visualization Standards
- Clean, uncluttered charts with clear titles
- Consistent color scheme across all materials
- Data labels on key values (don't make board members guess)
- Trend lines with 3-5 periods of history
- Red/yellow/green status indicators for KPIs
- Budget/forecast comparison in every financial chart
