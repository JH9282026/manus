# Creative Color Grading

## Cinematic Looks

### Teal and Orange
Most popular blockbuster color scheme.

**Implementation**
1. Push shadows toward teal/cyan
2. Push highlights toward orange/amber
3. Protect skin tones in orange range
4. Balance saturation between colors

### Film Emulation
Recreate characteristics of film stocks.

| Film Stock | Characteristics |
|------------|----------------|
| Kodak Portra | Warm, soft skin tones |
| Kodak Vision3 | Neutral, versatile |
| Fujifilm | Cool, green shadows |
| Kodachrome | Saturated, warm |

### High Contrast Cinematic
- Crushed blacks, bright highlights
- Strong visual impact
- Action and thriller genres
- S-curve with strong rolloff

## Vintage Looks

### 70s Film Look
- Warm orange/brown cast
- Lifted/faded blacks
- Reduced contrast
- Grain and softness

### 80s Neon
- Heavy cyan and magenta
- High saturation
- Strong contrast
- Synthetic, vivid colors

### 90s Video
- Slightly desaturated
- Greenish tint
- Lower contrast
- VHS artifacts optional

### Faded/Matte
- Lifted blacks
- Desaturated colors
- Soft, dreamlike
- Indie film aesthetic

## Color Palette Design

### Complementary Colors
- Opposite on color wheel
- Creates contrast and tension
- Example: Orange/teal, purple/yellow

### Analogous Colors
- Adjacent on color wheel
- Creates harmony and cohesion
- Example: Blue/cyan/green

### Triadic Colors
- Three equidistant colors
- Balanced, dynamic
- Example: Red/yellow/blue

## LUT Workflows

### Creative LUT Application
1. Complete primary correction first
2. Apply creative LUT at reduced intensity (50-80%)
3. Fine-tune with additional corrections
4. Protect problem areas (skin, sky)

### Building Custom LUTs
1. Develop grade on reference footage
2. Export as .cube LUT
3. Test on various footage types
4. Adjust for different lighting conditions
5. Include intensity control

## Mood and Emotion

### Color Psychology

| Color | Emotion | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Warm (orange/yellow) | Comfort, energy, nostalgia | Flashbacks, happy scenes |
| Cool (blue/cyan) | Sadness, isolation, mystery | Night, tension |
| Green | Nature, sickness, envy | Horror, environmental |
| Desaturated | Bleakness, past, serious | Drama, documentary |
| High saturation | Fantasy, energy, hyperreal | Commercials, music |
