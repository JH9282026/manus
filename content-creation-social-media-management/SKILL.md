# Content Creation & Social Media Management

Orchestrate brand presence across digital platforms through planned content development, community cultivation, and data-driven optimization.

## Overview

This skill integrates creative content production with analytical performance tracking to build engaged audiences and achieve measurable business objectives. Social media reaches 4.9 billion users globally with average daily usage of 2.5 hours.

## Platform Quick Reference

| Platform | Best For | Frequency | Content Types |
|----------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, thought leadership | 5/week | Articles, carousels, videos |
| Instagram | Visual storytelling | 7/week | Feed, Stories, Reels |
| Twitter/X | Real-time conversation | 3-5/day | Tweets, threads, polls |
| TikTok | Entertainment, Gen Z | 5/week | Short-form video |
| Facebook | Community, 35+ audience | 5/week | Video, Groups, Live |

## Content Pillar Distribution

- **Educational**: 40% - How-tos, tips, industry insights
- **Entertaining**: 25% - Trends, humor, relatable content
- **Inspirational**: 20% - Stories, quotes, motivation
- **Promotional**: 15% - Products, offers, CTAs

## Strategic Framework

### Content Strategy
1. Define content pillars aligned with brand positioning
2. Map content to customer journey stages
3. Develop editorial calendar with themes and timing
4. Create platform-specific adaptations

### Community Management
1. Establish response time standards (< 1 hour ideal)
2. Develop tone guidelines and escalation procedures
3. Cultivate engagement through questions and UGC
4. Monitor for crisis signals

### Analytics & Optimization
1. Track engagement rates by platform
2. Conduct A/B tests on formats and timing
3. Report weekly, analyze monthly, review quarterly
