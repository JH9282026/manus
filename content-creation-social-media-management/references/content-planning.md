# Content Planning

Editorial calendar development and content strategy frameworks.

---

## Content Pillar Development

### Framework
1. Align with brand positioning
2. Address audience pain points
3. Demonstrate expertise areas
4. Support business objectives

### Example Pillars
- **Education**: Industry trends, how-tos, tips
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Team, culture, process
- **Customer stories**: Testimonials, case studies
- **Product/Service**: Features, benefits, launches
- **Community**: UGC, engagement, recognition

---

## Editorial Calendar

### Components
- Date and time
- Platform(s)
- Content pillar
- Content type/format
- Caption/copy
- Visual assets
- Hashtags
- Owner/approver
- Status

### Planning Cadence
- Monthly: Theme and campaign planning
- Weekly: Content batching and scheduling
- Daily: Engagement and real-time content

---

## Content Mapping to Journey

| Stage | Goal | Content Types |
|-------|------|---------------|
| Awareness | Reach, discovery | Educational, entertaining |
| Consideration | Engagement, trust | Case studies, testimonials |
| Decision | Conversion | Product demos, offers |
| Retention | Loyalty, advocacy | Exclusive content, community |

---

## Content Batching

### Process
1. Set theme for the batch
2. Create all visuals together
3. Write all captions together
4. Schedule in batches
5. Review and adjust

### Efficiency Tips
- Repurpose across platforms
- Create multiple cuts from video
- Carousel to thread conversion
- Quote graphics from blog posts
