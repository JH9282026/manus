# Analytics & Metrics

Performance measurement and reporting frameworks.

---

## Key Metrics by Category

### Engagement Metrics
| Metric | Calculation | Benchmarks |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| Engagement Rate | (Likes+Comments+Shares)/Followers × 100 | LinkedIn: 2-3%, IG: 3-6%, Twitter: 0.5-1% |
| Click-Through Rate | Clicks/Impressions × 100 | 1-3% typical |
| Share/Save Rate | Shares or Saves/Impressions | Higher = quality content |

### Growth Metrics
- Follower growth rate: Monthly net new / Starting × 100
- Reach growth: MoM reach comparison
- Share of voice: Brand mentions / (Brand + Competitor)

### Conversion Metrics
- Link click-through rate
- Conversion rate from social
- Cost per engagement (paid)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
