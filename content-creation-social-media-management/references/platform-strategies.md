# Platform-Specific Strategies

Optimization approaches for each social media platform.

---

## LinkedIn

### Algorithm Priorities
- Native content (no external links initially)
- Early engagement (first hour critical)
- Meaningful comments over reactions
- Dwell time on content

### Best Practices
- Post Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM and 12-2 PM
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Lead with a hook (first 2 lines visible)
- Ask questions to drive comments
- Carousel posts get 3x engagement

### Content Types
- Long-form posts (1300 characters optimal)
- Document carousels (PDF format)
- Native video (under 2 minutes)
- Polls for engagement
- Newsletters for subscribers

---

## Instagram

### Algorithm Priorities
- Interest (predicted engagement)
- Timeliness (recency)
- Relationship (interaction history)
- Reels get 10x reach vs. static posts

### Best Practices
- Post at 9 AM, 12 PM, 7 PM
- Use 3-5 targeted hashtags
- Stories daily for engagement
- Reels for discovery and growth
- Carousel for saves and shares

### Content Types
- Feed posts (evergreen content)
- Stories (daily behind-the-scenes)
- Reels (trending audio, hooks in 3 sec)
- Guides (curated collections)
- Live (real-time engagement)

---

## Twitter/X

### Algorithm Priorities
- Engagement velocity
- Reply and retweet activity
- Topic relevance
- Account authority

### Best Practices
- Post 3-5 times daily minimum
- Thread format increases engagement 3x
- Use 1-2 hashtags maximum
- Participate in trending conversations
- Quote tweet > retweet

---

## TikTok

### Algorithm Priorities
- Completion rate (most important)
- Shares and comments
- Sound usage
- Hashtag relevance

### Best Practices
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds
- Post during audience active hours
- Vertical format only
- Respond to comments with video
