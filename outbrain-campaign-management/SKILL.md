# Outbrain Campaign Management

Manage native advertising campaigns on Outbrain's content discovery platform.

## Overview

Outbrain is a native advertising platform specializing in content discovery on premium publisher sites. This skill covers campaign structure (Marketer, Budget, Campaign, PromotedLink), content optimization, targeting, and performance tracking.

## Campaign Structure

Outbrain uses a four-level hierarchy:
- **Marketer**: Customer account
- **Budget**: Money allocated for campaigns
- **Campaign**: Collection of promoted links
- **PromotedLink**: Individual promoted content

## Content Types

- **Promoted Links**: Native ads with headline + thumbnail
- **Video Recommendations**: Video content promotion
- **Organic Recommendations**: Non-paid content

## Targeting Options

- **Geographic**: Country-level targeting
- **Device**: Desktop, mobile, tablet
- **Publisher**: Premium publisher network
- **Audience**: Behavioral and contextual targeting

## Bidding Strategies

- Conversion Bid Strategy
- Engagement Bid Strategy
- Target CPA
- Target ROAS
- Max Conversions
- Semi Manual
