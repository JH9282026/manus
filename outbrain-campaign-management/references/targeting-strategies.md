# Outbrain Targeting Strategies

Targeting options and strategies for Outbrain campaigns.

## Geographic Targeting

**Country-Level:**
- Single or multiple countries
- Tier 1: US, UK, CA, AU (higher CPCs)
- Tier 2: Western Europe (moderate CPCs)
- Tier 3: Other regions (lower CPCs)

**Strategy:**
- Start with top-performing countries
- Expand based on performance
- Use separate campaigns for different tiers

## Device Targeting

**Desktop:**
- Higher engagement
- Longer content consumption
- Better for in-depth content

**Mobile:**
- Higher volume
- Shorter sessions
- Better for quick-read content

**Tablet:**
- Middle ground
- Good for visual content

**Strategy:**
- Test all devices initially
- Separate campaigns if performance differs significantly
- Optimize content for each device type

## Publisher Targeting

**Premium Publishers:**
- Major news sites
- High-quality content sites
- Better engagement, higher CPCs

**Strategy:**
- Leverage Outbrain's premium network
- Monitor performance by publisher
- Exclude poor performers
- Bid higher on top publishers

## Audience Targeting

**Behavioral:**
- Interest-based segments
- In-market audiences
- Purchase intent signals

**Contextual:**
- Content category alignment
- Keyword contextual targeting
- Brand safety categories

**Strategy:**
- Start broad
- Layer in audience segments based on performance
- Use exclusions to refine targeting
