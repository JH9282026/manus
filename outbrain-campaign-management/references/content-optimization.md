# Outbrain Content Optimization

Best practices for promoted links and content.

## Headline Best Practices

**Length:**
- Optimal: 60-80 characters
- Maximum: 100 characters

**Writing Techniques:**
- Use curiosity gap
- Include numbers (listicles)
- Ask questions
- Create urgency
- Promise value

**Examples:**
- "7 Mistakes That Are Costing You Money"
- "How to Save $500 on Your Next Vacation"
- "The One Thing Successful People Do Every Morning"

## Image Guidelines

**Technical Specs:**
- Size: 1200x800px recommended
- Format: JPG, PNG
- File size: Under 5MB
- Aspect ratio: 3:2 or 16:9

**Image Content:**
- High quality, professional
- Show people when possible
- Use bright, eye-catching colors
- Avoid text overlays
- Relevant to content

## Landing Page Optimization

- Fast loading speed
- Mobile-friendly design
- Clear headline matching ad
- Easy navigation
- Strong call-to-action
- Relevant, valuable content

## Performance Benchmarks

- Average CTR: 0.3-0.6%
- Good CTR: 0.6-1.0%
- Excellent CTR: >1.0%

## A/B Testing

Test variations of:
- Headlines
- Images
- Landing pages
- Call-to-action text
