# Donor Cultivation & Campaigns

## Annual Fund Campaigns
- Segment donor database by giving history and engagement level
- Develop personalized appeals per segment (lapsed, current, monthly prospects)
- Create email series with compelling stories and impact data
- Design direct mail for offline donors
- Coordinate matching gift challenges for urgency

## Major Gift Strategy
- Identify top 20-50 prospects with transformational gift capacity
- Develop individualized cultivation plans with relationship-building touchpoints
- Prepare personalized proposals with naming/recognition opportunities
- Brief executive leadership before donor meetings
- Follow up with stewardship reports showing gift impact

## Capital Campaign Planning
- Develop case for support articulating need and vision
- Create gift table: number of gifts needed at each level
- Design naming opportunity inventory with recognition levels
- Phase campaign: quiet phase (leadership gifts) → public phase
- Track pledges, payments, and recognition commitments

## Crowdfunding & Peer-to-Peer
- Create compelling campaign page with story, images, and goal
- Develop social media content calendar
- Recruit peer fundraisers with toolkits (templates, images, talking points)
- Send regular progress updates to build momentum
- Thank donors immediately with automated acknowledgments

## Event Fundraising
- Design sponsorship packages at multiple levels
- Create auction catalog with item descriptions and values
- Develop event program with mission moments and impact stories
- Track revenue vs. costs for ROI analysis

## Donor Stewardship Best Practices
- Thank within 48 hours of every gift
- Send personalized impact reports showing how gifts created change
- Invite donors to events, site visits, and program observations
- Recognize appropriately per donor preferences
- Communicate proactively about organizational progress and challenges

## Key Fundraising Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Donor retention rate | 45-60% |
| Major donor retention | 70-80% |
| Cost to raise $1 | $0.10-$0.25 |
| Average gift growth | 5-10% annually |
| New donor acquisition cost | Varies by channel |
