# Grant Development & Corporate Funding

## Grant Proposal Components
- **Executive Summary**: Organization, project, request, outcomes (1 page)
- **Needs Statement**: Data-supported problem with local evidence
- **Project Description**: SMART objectives, methods, timeline, staffing
- **Evaluation Plan**: Indicators, data collection, analysis, reporting
- **Budget & Justification**: Line items with cost reasonableness
- **Sustainability**: Continuation plan beyond grant period
- **Organizational Capacity**: Track record, leadership, governance

## Grant Tracking System
- Track all active grants: deadlines, deliverables, reporting requirements
- Set reminders 30/14/7 days before each deadline
- Maintain compliance documentation and expenditure records
- Flag budget modifications or no-cost extensions needed

## Grant Reporting
- Compile programmatic data from staff
- Prepare financial reports: expenditures vs. budget
- Write narrative: activities completed, outcomes achieved, challenges
- Include compelling beneficiary stories with permission
- Submit on time; flag concerns proactively

## Corporate Sponsorship Development
- Research corporate CSR priorities and giving patterns
- Develop tiered sponsorship packages with visibility benefits
- Highlight employee engagement and community impact opportunities
- Create measurable impact reports for corporate partners

## Prospect Research
- Use wealth screening services (WealthEngine, DonorSearch, iWave)
- Review foundation databases (Foundation Directory Online, Instrumentl)
- Analyze 990 forms for giving patterns and board connections
- Score prospects on capacity, affinity, and propensity to give
