# Market Sizing & Industry Analysis

## TAM/SAM/SOM Framework

### Definitions
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)** — Total revenue opportunity if 100% market share achieved
- **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)** — Portion of TAM reachable with current business model
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)** — Realistic capture in 3-5 year timeframe

### Calculation Approaches

#### Top-Down
1. Start with industry-level data (global market size)
2. Apply geographic filters
3. Apply segment filters
4. Apply pricing model adjustments
5. Result: TAM estimate

#### Bottom-Up
1. Count potential customers in target segment
2. Estimate average revenue per customer
3. Multiply for market size
4. Validate against top-down estimate

#### Value Theory
1. Estimate value your solution creates for customers
2. Calculate willingness to pay (typically 10-20% of value created)
3. Multiply by number of potential customers

## Industry Life Cycle Analysis

| Stage | Characteristics | Strategy Implications |
|-------|----------------|---------------------|
| Introduction | Low sales, few competitors, high costs | Innovation, market education |
| Growth | Rising sales, new competitors entering | Market share, brand building |
| Maturity | Stable sales, consolidation, price competition | Efficiency, differentiation |
| Decline | Falling sales, exits, commoditization | Harvest, pivot, or exit |
