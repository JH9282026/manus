# Market Research & Analysis

Conduct systematic market research to inform strategic business decisions with data-driven insights.

## Overview

Analyze industry dynamics, market size, growth trajectories, competitive landscapes, and consumer behavior patterns. Transform raw market data into actionable intelligence for business strategy.

## Quick Start: Research Type Selection

| Need | Methodology | Reference |
|------|------------|-----------|
| Market sizing | TAM/SAM/SOM analysis | `/references/market-sizing.md` |
| Competitive landscape | Porter's Five Forces, benchmarking | `/references/competitive-analysis.md` |
| Consumer insights | Surveys, interviews, segmentation | `/references/competitive-analysis.md` |
| Industry trends | PESTEL, trend analysis | `/references/market-sizing.md` |
| Market entry | Opportunity scoring, barrier analysis | `/references/market-sizing.md` |

## Market Research Process

1. **Define objectives** — Specific questions the research must answer
2. **Design methodology** — Primary/secondary research mix
3. **Collect data** — Industry reports, surveys, interviews, public data
4. **Analyze** — Statistical analysis, pattern identification, segmentation
5. **Synthesize** — Draw conclusions, identify implications
6. **Present** — Executive summary, detailed findings, recommendations

## Key Frameworks

| Framework | Purpose | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| TAM/SAM/SOM | Market sizing | Investment cases, business plans |
| Porter's Five Forces | Industry attractiveness | Market entry decisions |
| PESTEL | Macro-environment | Strategic planning |
| SWOT | Internal + external | Competitive positioning |
| BCG Matrix | Portfolio analysis | Product strategy |
| Ansoff Matrix | Growth strategy | Expansion planning |

## Data Source Hierarchy

| Source Type | Examples | Reliability |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| Government data | Census, BLS, SEC filings | High |
| Industry associations | Trade publications, reports | High |
| Market research firms | Gartner, IBISWorld, Statista | Medium-High |
| Company filings | 10-K, annual reports | High |
| Surveys/interviews | Primary research | Variable |
| News and media | Trade press, business news | Medium |
