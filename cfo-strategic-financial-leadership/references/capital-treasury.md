## Capital Structure

### Optimal Capital Structure Factors
| Factor | Consideration |
|--------|---------------|
| Cost of capital | Minimize WACC |
| Financial flexibility | Access to capital |
| Risk tolerance | Volatility, covenants |
| Industry norms | Peer benchmarking |
| Growth needs | Future investment |
| Tax efficiency | Interest deductibility |

### Capital Structure Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Debt/Equity | Total Debt / Equity | Industry dependent |
| Debt/EBITDA | Total Debt / EBITDA | 2-4x typical |
| Interest Coverage | EBITDA / Interest | > 3x minimum |
| Net Debt/EBITDA | (Debt - Cash) / EBITDA | Lower is safer |

### Financing Options
| Source | Characteristics |
|--------|----------------|
| Bank debt | Flexible, relationship-based |
| Bonds | Fixed rate, longer term |
| Private placement | Negotiated, fewer investors |
| Equity | No repayment, dilutive |
| Convertible | Hybrid debt/equity |

## Cost of Capital

### WACC Formula
```
WACC = (E/V × Re) + (D/V × Rd × (1-T))

Where:
E = Market value of equity
D = Market value of debt
V = E + D
Re = Cost of equity
Rd = Cost of debt
T = Tax rate
```

### Cost of Equity (CAPM)
```
Re = Rf + β × (Rm - Rf)

Where:
Rf = Risk-free rate
β = Beta (systematic risk)
Rm = Expected market return
(Rm - Rf) = Equity risk premium
```

## Capital Allocation

### Allocation Process
1. **Assess** capital available
2. **Prioritize** opportunities
3. **Evaluate** returns and risks
4. **Allocate** across priorities
5. **Monitor** performance

### Investment Criteria
| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|----------|
| IRR | > WACC + premium |
| Payback | < X years |
| NPV | > $X minimum |
| Strategic fit | Required |

### Capital Rationing
- Rank projects by IRR or NPV/Investment
- Select projects within capital constraint
- Consider strategic importance
- Maintain balance across portfolio
