
### Real Estate Videography

**Shot List for Properties**:
- Exterior approach (dronie or flyover)
- Orbit around property
- Neighborhood context
- Backyard and outdoor spaces
- Roof and property line overview

**Techniques**:
- Golden hour lighting preferred
- Reveal shots approaching property
- Smooth, slow movements
- Include surrounding amenities
- Ground-to-air transitions

**Legal Considerations**:
- Property owner permission
- Neighbor privacy consideration
- Part 107 certification required (commercial use)
- Local ordinance compliance

### Construction and Development

**Applications**:
- Progress documentation
- Site surveys
- Marketing materials
- Safety inspections
- Mapping and surveying

**Techniques**:
- Consistent angles for progress tracking
- Orthomosaic mapping
- 3D modeling with photogrammetry
- Time-lapse over construction periods

### Events and Weddings

**Common Shots**:
- Venue establishing shots
- Ceremony overview
- Guest perspective
- Romantic couple portraits
- Group photos from above

**Considerations**:
- Noise during ceremonies
- Battery changes during events
- Weather backup plans
- Guest safety and privacy
- Venue restrictions

### Film and Television Production

**Professional Requirements**:
- Part 107 with waivers as needed
- Production insurance
- Coordination with film permits
- Safety protocols for larger sets
- Integration with ground units

**Specialized Techniques**:
- Coordinated moves with camera department
- Matching director's visual style
- Multiple takes for coverage
- Live monitoring for director
