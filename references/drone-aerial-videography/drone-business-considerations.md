
### Starting a Drone Business

**Legal Requirements**:
- Part 107 certification
- Business registration
- Liability insurance (typically $1M minimum)
- Client contracts
- Property/aviation waivers

**Equipment Investment**:
- Reliable drone platform
- Backup equipment
- Memory cards and storage
- ND filter kit
- Carrying cases
- Maintenance tools

**Pricing Strategies**:
- Hourly rates: $150-500/hour
- Day rates: $1000-3000/day
- Project-based pricing
- Package deals for recurring clients
- Include editing time and post-production

### Building a Portfolio

**Essential Portfolio Elements**:
- Variety of shot types
- Different locations and conditions
- Commercial applications demonstrated
- Technical quality showcase
- Before/after grading examples

**Growing Your Reel**:
- Personal projects
- Spec work for local businesses
- Collaboration with videographers
- Contest entries
- Social media content

### Client Relations

**Pre-Production Communication**:
- Detailed shot lists
- Location scouting
- Weather contingency plans
- Permit and permission requirements
- Timeline and deliverables

**On-Site Professionalism**:
- Punctuality and preparation
- Safety briefings
- Clear communication
- Flexibility within reason
- Professional appearance

**Delivery and Follow-Up**:
- Timely delivery of materials
- Multiple format options
- Archival copies
- Follow-up communication
- Request testimonials/referrals
