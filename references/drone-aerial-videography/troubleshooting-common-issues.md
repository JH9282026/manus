
### Technical Problems

**Gimbal Issues**:
- Calibrate before each session
- Check for obstructions
- Update firmware
- Protect during transport

**Signal Loss**:
- Maintain line of sight
- Avoid metal structures
- Check antenna orientation
- Reduce distance to drone

**Battery Problems**:
- Warm batteries in cold weather
- Store at proper charge levels
- Replace swollen batteries immediately
- Calibrate periodically

### Footage Quality Issues

**Jello Effect** (Rolling Shutter):
- Reduce propeller vibration
- Balance propellers
- Use ND filters
- Lower shutter speed

**Overexposure**:
- Use stronger ND filters
- Reduce ISO
- Increase shutter speed (last resort)
- Shoot during lower light

**Soft Footage**:
- Clean lens and filters
- Check focus settings
- Ensure stable flight
- Verify sharpening settings
