
### Direct Response Template (60 seconds)

```
[HOOK - 5 seconds]
(Provocative question or startling statement)

[PROBLEM - 10 seconds]
(Relatable scenario that creates emotional connection)

[SOLUTION - 15 seconds]
(Introduce product/service and key benefits)

[PROOF - 10 seconds]
(Credibility: testimonials, statistics, years in business)

[OFFER - 10 seconds]
(Special offer or incentive to act now)

[CTA - 7 seconds]
(Clear instructions: phone, website, location)

[TAG - 3 seconds]
(Brand name and tagline)
```

### Branding Template (60 seconds)

```
[SCENE SETTING - 8 seconds]
(Sound effects and brief description to place listener)

[NARRATIVE - 35 seconds]
(Story that illustrates brand values or customer experience)

[BRAND MESSAGE - 12 seconds]
(What the brand stands for, key differentiator)

[CTA & TAG - 5 seconds]
(Website/location and brand tagline)
```

### Promotional Template (30 seconds)

```
[ATTENTION - 3 seconds]
(Quick hook relevant to promotion)

[OFFER - 12 seconds]
(Clear description of the promotion)

[DETAILS - 7 seconds]
(Dates, locations, conditions)

[CTA - 5 seconds]
(How to take advantage)

[TAG - 3 seconds]
(Brand name and tagline)
```
