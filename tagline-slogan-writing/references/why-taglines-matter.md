## Why Taglines Matter



### Business Impact of Effective Taglines

**Brand Value Creation**: Strong taglines contribute significantly to brand equity. Research suggests brands with memorable taglines enjoy 23% higher recognition and 18% greater consumer preference compared to competitors without distinctive taglines.

**Marketing Efficiency**: A clear tagline provides direction for all marketing communications, ensuring consistency and reducing creative development time across campaigns.

**Consumer Decision-Making**: In moments of purchase consideration, taglines serve as mental shortcuts helping consumers choose between options quickly.

**Word-of-Mouth Amplification**: Memorable taglines make brands easier to discuss and recommend, naturally spreading through conversations and social sharing.



### Strategic Functions of Taglines

**1. Positioning Anchor**
A tagline anchors brand positioning in consumers' minds, providing a consistent reference point across all touchpoints. BMW's "The Ultimate Driving Machine" has anchored its performance positioning for over 45 years.

**2. Promise Articulation**
Taglines express what customers can expect. FedEx's "When It Absolutely, Positively Has to Be There Overnight" makes a clear delivery promise.

**3. Competitive Differentiation**
In parity categories, taglines create differentiation. Apple's "Think Different" distinguished the brand during a period when computer specifications were largely similar.

**4. Emotional Trigger**
Taglines can evoke specific emotions. Hallmark's "When You Care Enough to Send the Very Best" triggers feelings of caring and quality.

**5. Memory Device**
The brevity and rhythm of taglines make brands memorable. Bounty's "The Quicker Picker Upper" uses alliteration and rhythm to stick in memory.
