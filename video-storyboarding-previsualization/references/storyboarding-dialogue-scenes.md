### Storyboarding Dialogue Scenes

**Coverage Planning:**
Dialogue scenes require coverage thinking:

- Master shots for geography
- Two-shots for relationship
- Singles for emphasis
- Close-ups for reaction
- Inserts for detail

**Cutting Patterns:**
Plan editing rhythm through boards:

- Shot/reverse shot conventions
- Reaction shot placement
- Character focus moments
- Listening shots importance
- Transitional compositions
