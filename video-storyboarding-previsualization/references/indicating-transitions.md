### Indicating Transitions

**Cut:**
- Default, no notation needed
- Simple panel progression
- Direct change

**Dissolve:**
- Overlapping images
- "DISSOLVE" label
- Duration notation
- Purpose indication

**Fade:**
- Fade to/from black panels
- "FADE IN/OUT" label
- Duration
- Position in sequence

**Wipe/Push:**
- Direction arrows
- Style indication
- Motion path
- Timing notes

**Match Cut:**
- Matched compositions
- "MATCH CUT" notation
- Connection clarity
- Visual similarity
