### Hand-Drawn Storyboards

**Advantages:**
- Immediate and intuitive
- No software learning curve
- Portable (paper and pencil)
- Tactile creative process
- Quick rough sketches

**Challenges:**
- Revision requires redrawing
- Harder to share digitally
- Scanning required
- Quality varies with skill
- Difficult to standardize

**Best For:**
- Individual creators
- Rapid concepting
- Traditional workflows
- On-set adjustments

**Tools:**
- Pencils and markers
- Storyboard paper/pads
- Templates
- Scanner for digitization
