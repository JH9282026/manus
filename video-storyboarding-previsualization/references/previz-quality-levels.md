### Previz Quality Levels

**Pitchvis:**
- Quick concept visualization
- Rough but communicative
- Pitch and funding stage
- Lower fidelity acceptable

**Technical Previz:**
- Accurate measurements
- Real camera data
- Equipment planning
- Department coordination
- Production-ready data

**Postvis:**
- Post-production visualization
- Temp VFX placement
- Edit testing
- Bridge to final effects

**Postviz:**
- After principal photography
- Testing final edits
- VFX placement verification
- Color and mood testing
