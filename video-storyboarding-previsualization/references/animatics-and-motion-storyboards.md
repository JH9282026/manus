### Animatics and Motion Storyboards

**Definition:**
Storyboards edited together with timing and audio.

**Characteristics:**
- Timed to actual duration
- Include temp audio/music
- Show transitions
- Test pacing

**Purpose:**
- Pacing verification
- Edit preview
- Client approval
- Music synchronization

**Format:**
- Video timeline
- Audio tracks
- Transition effects
- Duration markers
