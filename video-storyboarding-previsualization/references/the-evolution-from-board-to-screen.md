### The Evolution from Board to Screen

The storyboarding process typically follows this progression:

1. **Script/Concept** → Written ideas
2. **Thumbnail Boards** → Quick visual exploration
3. **Rough Storyboards** → Basic shot sequence
4. **Detailed Storyboards** → Full information panels
5. **Animatic** → Timed storyboard with audio
6. **Previz** → 3D animated visualization
7. **Production** → Actual filming
