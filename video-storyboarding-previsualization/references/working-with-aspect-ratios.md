### Working with Aspect Ratios

**Standard Ratios:**
- 16:9 (1.78:1): HD/UHD standard
- 2.39:1: Anamorphic cinema
- 1.85:1: Academy flat
- 4:3 (1.33:1): Classic TV
- 9:16: Vertical video
- 1:1: Square format

**Composition Implications:**
Different ratios affect composition:

- Wide ratios: Horizontal emphasis, landscapes, epic scale
- Standard 16:9: Versatile, balanced compositions
- Square: Symmetry, centered subjects
- Vertical: Portrait emphasis, mobile-first

**Multi-Ratio Planning:**
If delivering to multiple ratios:

- Design safe zones for reframing
- Note critical elements
- Plan primary and alternative compositions
- Consider cropping strategies
