### Translating Cinematic Language

**Shot Sizes:**
Indicate standard shot sizes clearly:
- Extreme Wide Shot (EWS)
- Wide Shot (WS)
- Medium Wide Shot (MWS)
- Medium Shot (MS)
- Medium Close-Up (MCU)
- Close-Up (CU)
- Extreme Close-Up (ECU)

**Camera Angles:**
Show perspective through:
- Eye level (straight on)
- High angle (looking down)
- Low angle (looking up)
- Dutch angle (tilted frame)
- Bird's eye (overhead)
- Worm's eye (ground up)
