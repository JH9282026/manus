### When Previz is Essential

**High-Cost Sequences:**
- Stunts and practical effects
- Large-scale crowd scenes
- Expensive location shoots
- Complex equipment needs
- Coordinated logistics

**Technical Complexity:**
- Visual effects heavy sequences
- Virtual production elements
- Multi-unit filming
- Complex choreography
- Unusual camera setups

**Stakeholder Communication:**
- Client approvals needed
- Complex creative concepts
- Risk mitigation
- Budget justification
