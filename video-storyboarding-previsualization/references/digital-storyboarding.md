### Digital Storyboarding

**Advantages:**
- Easy revisions
- Consistent style possible
- Easy sharing
- Templates and assets
- Integration with animatics

**Challenges:**
- Software learning curve
- Equipment required
- Can feel slower initially
- Potential over-polishing
- Technical issues possible

**Best For:**
- Team projects
- Client-facing work
- Complex productions
- Long-term projects

**Tools:**
- Storyboard software (Storyboard Pro)
- Drawing software (Photoshop, Procreate)
- Specialized apps
- Template systems
