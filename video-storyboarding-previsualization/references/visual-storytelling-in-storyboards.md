### Visual Storytelling in Storyboards

**Communicating Emotion:**
Storyboards should convey emotional content beyond action:

- **Expression:** Capture character emotions through facial details
- **Body Language:** Show posture, gesture, and movement
- **Lighting Indication:** Suggest mood through shadow placement
- **Composition:** Use framing to create feelings
- **Scale:** Relationship between characters and environment

**Creating Visual Rhythm:**
Consider the visual flow across panels:

- Vary shot sizes deliberately
- Alternate static and dynamic compositions
- Build visual tension through progressive tightening
- Release tension through wider, calmer frames
- Match visual rhythm to emotional arc
