### The Business Case

**Cost Reduction:**
- Cheaper to redraw than reshoot
- Identifies equipment needs in advance
- Reduces overtime and overages
- Prevents scope creep

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Tests complex sequences virtually
- Identifies safety concerns
- Ensures coverage planning
- Validates creative choices

**Stakeholder Alignment:**
- Provides approval documentation
- Reduces revision cycles
- Creates shared vision
- Professional presentation
