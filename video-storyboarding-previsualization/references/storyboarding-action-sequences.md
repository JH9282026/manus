### Storyboarding Action Sequences

**Breaking Down Action:**
Action sequences require careful panel planning:

1. Establish the space and participants
2. Show key action beats clearly
3. Include reaction moments
4. Vary shot sizes for impact
5. Consider pacing through panel density

**Communicating Movement:**
Use visual conventions for movement:

- Speed lines indicate direction and velocity
- Multiple positions show movement path
- Blurring suggests rapid motion
- Ghost images show trajectories
- Impact stars indicate contact points

**Action Continuity:**
Maintain spatial clarity through action:

- Consistent screen direction
- Clear geography
- Logical progression
- Orientation markers
- Eyeline consistency
