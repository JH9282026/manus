### Creating Shot Lists from Storyboards

**Shot List Elements:**
- Scene number
- Shot number
- Shot description
- Shot type/size
- Camera movement
- Equipment needed
- Cast involved
- Special requirements
- Estimated duration

**Format Example:**
```
Scene | Shot | Description | Type | Movement | Notes
------|------|-------------|------|----------|------
3     | 1    | Est. kitchen | WS  | Static   | Tripod
3     | 2    | Sarah enters | MS  | Track L  | Dolly
3     | 3    | Sarah's face | CU  | Static   | 85mm
```
