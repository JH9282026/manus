# CTV Platform Buying Guides

Detailed guides for buying advertising on major Connected TV platforms.

---

## Roku Advertising

### Platform Overview

- **Reach**: 70+ million US households
- **Market Share**: ~40% of US CTV market
- **Ad Products**: Roku OneView (self-serve), Roku Ads Manager, programmatic via DSPs
- **Minimum Spend**: $5,000 for self-serve; $50,000+ for managed campaigns

### Buying Options

**Roku OneView (Self-Serve)**
- Minimum $5,000 campaign budget
- Access to Roku Channel and third-party apps
- Self-service campaign management
- Basic targeting and reporting

**Roku Ads Manager (Managed)**
- Minimum $50,000 campaign budget
- Dedicated account management
- Premium inventory access
- Advanced targeting and measurement
- Custom audience creation

**Programmatic Access**
- Available through The Trade Desk, Amazon DSP, Google DV360
- Access to Roku inventory via programmatic exchanges
- Real-time bidding capabilities

### Targeting Capabilities

**Demographic Targeting**
- Age (18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55-64, 65+)
- Gender
- Household income ($0-50K, $50-75K, $75-100K, $100K+)
- Parental status
- Geographic (DMA, state, zip code)

**Behavioral Targeting**
- Content genre preferences (sports, news, entertainment, etc.)
- Viewing frequency (light, medium, heavy)
- Device type (Roku TV, Roku Stick, Roku Ultra, etc.)
- App usage patterns

**ACR Targeting**
- Target based on linear TV viewing behavior
- Competitive conquesting (target competitor's TV viewers)
- Program-level targeting
- Network-level targeting

**First-Party Data**
- Customer list matching (email, address)
- Lookalike audience creation
- Retargeting website visitors

### Creative Specifications

**Video Ad Specs**
- Duration: 15s, 30s (standard); 6s, 60s (available)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (required); 3840x2160 (recommended)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- File Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Frame Rate: 29.97 fps
- Audio: Stereo, AAC, -24 LUFS
- Bitrate: 8-20 Mbps
- Max File Size: 2GB

**Interactive Ad Formats**
- QR codes (must be visible for minimum 5 seconds)
- Shoppable ads (product integration)
- Voice-activated ads ("Hey Roku" integration)

### Measurement & Reporting

**Standard Metrics**
- Impressions delivered
- Reach (unique households)
- Frequency
- Completion rate
- CPM

**Advanced Measurement**
- Roku OneView attribution (website visits, conversions)
- Brand lift studies
- Foot traffic attribution
- Cross-device attribution

### Pricing

- **CPM Range**: $25-$50 (standard inventory); $40-$65 (premium)
- **Seasonal Variation**: Higher CPMs in Q4, lower in Q1
- **Inventory Type**: Roku Channel (premium pricing), third-party apps (lower CPMs)

### Best Practices

1. **Start with Roku Channel**: Highest completion rates and engagement
2. **Use ACR data**: Target based on actual TV viewing behavior
3. **Implement frequency caps**: 3-5 impressions per household per week
4. **Test interactive formats**: QR codes drive strong engagement
5. **Leverage first-party data**: Upload customer lists for better targeting
6. **Monitor completion rates**: Should be 85%+ for quality placements

## Amazon Fire TV Advertising

### Platform Overview

- **Reach**: 50+ million US households
- **Market Share**: ~30% of US CTV market
- **Ad Products**: Amazon DSP, Fire TV direct buys
- **Minimum Spend**: $35,000 for managed campaigns; $10,000 for self-serve DSP

### Buying Options

**Amazon DSP (Self-Serve)**
- Minimum $10,000 campaign budget
- Access to Fire TV and third-party CTV inventory
- Programmatic buying interface
- Amazon audience data integration

**Amazon DSP (Managed)**
- Minimum $35,000 campaign budget
- Dedicated Amazon account team
- Custom audience strategies
- Advanced reporting and optimization

**Fire TV Direct Buys**
- Premium inventory on Fire TV home screen
- Sponsorship opportunities
- Custom pricing (typically $100K+ campaigns)

### Targeting Capabilities

**Amazon Shopping Data**
- Purchase history targeting
- Product category interests
- Brand affinity
- Shopping behavior (frequency, recency, value)
- Prime membership status

**Demographic Targeting**
- Age, gender, household income
- Parental status
- Geographic targeting

**Behavioral Targeting**
- Streaming content preferences
- Device usage patterns
- App engagement

**First-Party Data**
- Customer list matching
- Pixel-based retargeting
- Lookalike audiences

### Creative Specifications

**Video Ad Specs**
- Duration: 15s, 30s (standard); 6s, 60s (available)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum; 3840x2160 preferred
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- File Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Frame Rate: 29.97 fps or 23.976 fps
- Audio: Stereo, AAC, -24 LUFS
- Bitrate: 8-20 Mbps
- Max File Size: 500MB

**Interactive Formats**
- Shoppable video ads (direct to Amazon product pages)
- QR codes
- Voice commands ("Alexa, add to cart")

### Measurement & Reporting

**Standard Metrics**
- Impressions, reach, frequency
- Completion rate
- CPM, total spend

**Amazon Attribution**
- Amazon product page visits
- Add-to-cart events
- Purchase conversions
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
- New-to-brand customers

**Cross-Device Measurement**
- Fire TV exposure to mobile/desktop conversion
- Multi-touch attribution

### Pricing

- **CPM Range**: $20-$45 (standard); $35-$60 (premium)
- **E-commerce Advertisers**: Often see lower CPMs due to Amazon's prioritization
- **Seasonal Variation**: Highest CPMs during Prime Day, Black Friday, Q4

### Best Practices

1. **Leverage shopping data**: Use Amazon purchase data for precise targeting
2. **Create shoppable ads**: Direct viewers to product pages for immediate purchase
3. **Target Prime members**: Higher engagement and conversion rates
4. **Use Amazon Attribution**: Track full-funnel impact from awareness to purchase
5. **Coordinate with Amazon campaigns**: Align CTV with sponsored products/brands
6. **Test voice integration**: "Alexa" commands drive strong engagement

## Samsung Ads

### Platform Overview

- **Reach**: 45+ million US households with Samsung smart TVs
- **Market Share**: ~25% of US smart TV market
- **Ad Products**: Samsung Ads Platform, programmatic access
- **Minimum Spend**: $25,000 for direct campaigns

### Buying Options

**Samsung Ads Platform (Direct)**
- Minimum $25,000 campaign budget
- Access to Samsung TV Plus (FAST channels)
- ACR data targeting
- Managed service with Samsung team

**Programmatic Access**
- Available through major DSPs (The Trade Desk, DV360, Xandr)
- Real-time bidding on Samsung inventory
- Self-serve campaign management

### Targeting Capabilities

**ACR Data (Automatic Content Recognition)**
- Target based on linear TV viewing
- Program-level targeting
- Network-level targeting
- Competitive conquesting
- Daypart-based targeting

**Demographic Targeting**
- Age, gender, household income
- Geographic (DMA, zip code)
- Household composition

**Behavioral Targeting**
- Smart TV app usage
- Viewing frequency and recency
- Content genre preferences
- Device usage patterns

**Cross-Device Targeting**
- Samsung mobile device data
- Multi-screen behavior
- App usage across devices

### Creative Specifications

**Video Ad Specs**
- Duration: 15s, 30s (standard)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum; 3840x2160 (4K) recommended
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- File Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Frame Rate: 29.97 fps
- Audio: Stereo, AAC, -24 LUFS
- Bitrate: 10-20 Mbps
- Max File Size: 1GB

### Measurement & Reporting

**Standard Metrics**
- Impressions, reach, frequency
- Completion rate (typically 90%+ on Samsung TV Plus)
- CPM, total spend

**Advanced Measurement**
- Brand lift studies
- Website visit attribution
- Foot traffic attribution
- Cross-device conversion tracking

### Pricing

- **CPM Range**: $25-$50 (Samsung TV Plus); $30-$55 (programmatic)
- **Premium Content**: Higher CPMs for sports and live events

### Best Practices

1. **Leverage ACR data**: Target based on actual TV viewing behavior
2. **Focus on Samsung TV Plus**: Free ad-supported channels with high engagement
3. **Use competitive targeting**: Reach viewers of competitor TV ads
4. **Implement cross-device strategies**: Coordinate Samsung TV and mobile campaigns
5. **Test 4K creative**: Samsung users have high-quality displays

## YouTube TV Advertising

### Platform Overview

- **Reach**: 8+ million subscribers
- **Market Share**: Leading live TV streaming service
- **Ad Products**: Google Ads (Video campaigns), Google DV360
- **Minimum Spend**: No minimum for Google Ads; $10,000+ for DV360

### Buying Options

**Google Ads (Self-Serve)**
- No minimum budget
- Access to YouTube TV inventory
- Self-service campaign management
- Google audience targeting

**Google DV360 (Programmatic)**
- Minimum ~$10,000 campaign budget
- Advanced programmatic capabilities
- Cross-platform targeting (YouTube + YouTube TV + GVP)
- Custom audience strategies

### Targeting Capabilities

**Google Audience Data**
- Affinity audiences (interests)
- In-market audiences (purchase intent)
- Life events (moving, graduating, etc.)
- Custom intent audiences

**Demographic Targeting**
- Age, gender, parental status
- Household income
- Geographic targeting

**Behavioral Targeting**
- YouTube viewing behavior
- Search behavior
- Website visit behavior

**First-Party Data**
- Customer Match (email lists)
- Website retargeting
- Lookalike audiences (Similar Audiences)

### Creative Specifications

**Video Ad Specs**
- Duration: 15s, 30s (standard); 6s, 60s (available)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum; 3840x2160 preferred
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- File Format: MP4, MOV, AVI (H.264)
- Frame Rate: 30 fps or 24 fps
- Audio: Stereo, AAC or MP3
- Max File Size: 1GB

### Measurement & Reporting

**Standard Metrics**
- Impressions, reach, frequency
- View rate (completion rate)
- CPM, CPV (cost per view)

**Google Measurement**
- Brand lift studies (free for qualifying campaigns)
- Google Analytics integration
- Conversion tracking
- Cross-device attribution

### Pricing

- **CPM Range**: $25-$50 (standard); $40-$70 (live sports/events)
- **Live Content Premium**: Significantly higher CPMs for live sports and news

### Best Practices

1. **Target live content**: Sports and news drive high engagement
2. **Use Google audiences**: Leverage search and YouTube behavior data
3. **Implement Brand Lift**: Free measurement for campaigns over $10K
4. **Coordinate with YouTube**: Align YouTube TV with standard YouTube campaigns
5. **Test bumper ads**: 6-second ads work well for awareness
