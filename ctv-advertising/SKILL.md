# CTV Advertising

Create and optimize advertising campaigns for Connected TV and streaming platforms.

## Overview

This skill provides frameworks for planning, executing, and optimizing CTV advertising campaigns across major streaming platforms including Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Samsung TV Plus, Hulu, Peacock, and programmatic CTV exchanges. Covers audience targeting, creative specifications, measurement strategies, and platform-specific best practices.

## Platform Selection Guide

| Platform Type | Best For | Reach | Targeting Capabilities |
|--------------|----------|-------|------------------------|
| Roku Ads | Broad streaming reach | 70M+ US households | Demographic, behavioral, ACR data |
| Amazon Fire TV | E-commerce integration | 50M+ US households | Purchase data, Prime behavior |
| Samsung Ads | Smart TV native | 45M+ US households | ACR data, viewing behavior |
| Hulu Ad Manager | Premium content | 48M+ subscribers | Demographic, interest-based |
| YouTube TV | Live + on-demand | 8M+ subscribers | Google audience data |
| Programmatic CTV | Multi-platform scale | Aggregated inventory | Advanced programmatic targeting |

## Campaign Planning Framework

### Audience Strategy

1. **Define target segments** — use streaming behavior data, not just demographics
2. **Leverage first-party data** — upload customer lists for matching
3. **Apply ACR targeting** — target based on actual viewing behavior (Automatic Content Recognition)
4. **Consider dayparting** — streaming viewing patterns differ from linear TV
5. **Plan for frequency management** — coordinate across platforms to avoid oversaturation

### Budget Allocation

| Budget Level | Recommended Approach | Platform Mix |
|--------------|---------------------|-------------|
| Under $25K | Single platform test | 1 major platform |
| $25K-$100K | Multi-platform | 2-3 platforms |
| $100K-$500K | Programmatic + direct | Programmatic base + 2 direct platforms |
| $500K+ | Full ecosystem | Programmatic + all major platforms |

## Creative Specifications

### Standard CTV Video Specs

- **Duration**: 15s, 30s (most common); 6s, 60s (platform-dependent)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum; 3840x2160 (4K) preferred
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (required)
- **File Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Frame Rate**: 29.97 fps or 23.976 fps
- **Audio**: Stereo, AAC codec, -24 LUFS loudness
- **Bitrate**: 8-20 Mbps for 1080p
- **Max File Size**: Varies by platform (typically 500MB-2GB)

### Creative Best Practices

1. **Design for the big screen** — use bold visuals, large text (minimum 36pt)
2. **Front-load branding** — viewers may skip after 5 seconds on some platforms
3. **Optimize for sound-on** — CTV viewers typically have audio enabled
4. **Include clear CTA** — use QR codes, voice commands, or simple URLs
5. **Test multiple versions** — A/B test creative variations within campaigns
6. **Avoid small text** — ensure readability from 10 feet away

## Targeting Capabilities

### Demographic Targeting
- Age ranges
- Gender
- Household income
- Parental status
- Geographic (DMA, zip code, city)

### Behavioral Targeting
- Content genre preferences
- Viewing frequency
- Device usage patterns
- Time of day preferences
- Binge-watching behavior

### Advanced Targeting
- **ACR Data**: Target based on linear TV viewing
- **Purchase Data**: Amazon shopping behavior, credit card data
- **First-Party Match**: Upload customer emails/addresses
- **Lookalike Audiences**: Expand from existing customers
- **Retargeting**: Re-engage website visitors on CTV

## Measurement & Attribution

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Benchmark |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Completion Rate | % who watched entire ad | 70-95% |
| VCR (Video Completion Rate) | Completed views / impressions | 85%+ |
| Reach | Unique households reached | Campaign-dependent |
| Frequency | Avg impressions per household | 3-5 optimal |
| CPM | Cost per 1,000 impressions | $20-$65 |
| CPA | Cost per acquisition | Varies by vertical |

### Attribution Methods

1. **Pixel-Based**: Track website visits post-exposure
2. **QR Code Tracking**: Measure direct response from CTV ads
3. **Promo Code Attribution**: Unique codes for CTV campaigns
4. **Brand Lift Studies**: Measure awareness and consideration changes
5. **Foot Traffic Attribution**: Mobile location data for store visits
6. **Cross-Device Matching**: Connect CTV exposure to mobile/desktop conversions

- Strong ACR data capabilities

### Amazon Fire TV
- Deep integration with Amazon shopping data

### Samsung Ads
- ACR data from Samsung smart TVs
- Cross-device targeting capabilities
- Premium inventory access
- Strong in sports and news content

### Hulu
- Premium streaming content
- Binge ad selector (viewer choice)
- Pause ads available
- Strong demographic targeting

## Programmatic CTV Buying

### DSP Options
- The Trade Desk (most comprehensive CTV inventory)
- Amazon DSP (Fire TV + third-party inventory)
- Google DV360 (YouTube TV + programmatic)
- Xandr (premium CTV inventory)
- Viant (household-level targeting)

### Programmatic Best Practices

1. **Use private marketplaces (PMPs)** — better inventory quality
2. **Apply brand safety filters** — exclude inappropriate content
3. **Implement frequency caps** — 3-5 impressions per household per week
4. **Optimize by daypart** — test different viewing times
5. **Monitor completion rates** — indicator of ad quality and placement
6. **Leverage household graphs** — target at household level, not device

## Campaign Optimization

### Week 1-2: Learning Phase
- Monitor completion rates by creative
- Analyze performance by daypart
- Review frequency distribution
- Check for technical issues (buffering, playback errors)

### Week 3-4: Optimization Phase
- Shift budget to top-performing creatives
- Adjust daypart targeting based on performance
- Refine audience segments
- Implement frequency caps if needed

### Ongoing Optimization
- Refresh creative every 4-6 weeks
- Test new audience segments
- Expand to additional platforms
- Coordinate with other marketing channels

## Common Challenges

### Challenge: High CPMs
**Solution**: Use programmatic buying for better rates, negotiate direct deals for volume, test less competitive dayparts

### Challenge: Attribution Difficulty
**Solution**: Implement multi-touch attribution, use brand lift studies, track QR code scans and promo codes

### Challenge: Frequency Management
**Solution**: Use household-level frequency caps, coordinate across platforms, monitor reach/frequency reports

### Challenge: Creative Fatigue
**Solution**: Rotate multiple creative versions, refresh creative monthly, test different messaging approaches

### Challenge: Platform Fragmentation
**Solution**: Use programmatic for aggregated reach, focus on top 2-3 platforms, leverage unified measurement tools

## Industry Trends

- **Programmatic Growth**: 80%+ of CTV ads now bought programmatically
- **Shoppable CTV**: Interactive ads with direct purchase capabilities
- **FAST Channels**: Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV growing rapidly
- **Advanced Targeting**: Increased use of first-party data and ACR
- **Cross-Screen Campaigns**: Coordinated CTV + mobile + desktop strategies
- **Measurement Standardization**: Industry moving toward unified metrics
- **Addressable TV**: Household-level targeting becoming standard
