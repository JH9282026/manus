# Social and Governance Strategy

## Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)

### DEI Strategy Framework
1. **Assess**: Demographic data analysis, inclusion survey, pay equity audit
2. **Set goals**: Representation targets, inclusion index targets, pay gap closure timeline
3. **Recruit**: Diverse pipeline development, inclusive hiring practices, bias training
4. **Retain**: ERGs, mentoring, sponsorship programs, flexible work
5. **Advance**: Leadership development, succession planning, promotion equity
6. **Measure**: Track metrics quarterly, report annually, adjust strategy

### Key DEI Metrics

| Category | Metric | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Representation | Gender/ethnicity by level | Quarterly |
| Hiring | Diverse candidate pipeline %, offer acceptance rates | Monthly |
| Retention | Turnover by demographic group | Quarterly |
| Advancement | Promotion rates by demographic | Annually |
| Pay equity | Adjusted gender/race pay gap | Annually |
| Inclusion | Employee inclusion survey score | Annually |

## Health and Safety

### Safety Management System
- Leadership commitment and safety culture
- Hazard identification and risk assessment
- Incident reporting and investigation
- Training and competency development
- Emergency preparedness
- Continuous improvement

### Key Safety Metrics
- **TRIR**: Total Recordable Incident Rate
- **LTIR**: Lost Time Incident Rate
- **Near miss reporting rate**: Leading indicator of safety culture
- **Safety training hours**: Compliance and awareness
- **Fatality rate**: Critical lagging indicator

## Corporate Governance

### Board Effectiveness

| Element | Best Practice |
|---|---|
| Independence | Majority independent directors (>50%) |
| Diversity | Gender, ethnic, skill diversity targets |
| Expertise | ESG competency on board |
| Committees | Sustainability/ESG committee at board level |
| Evaluation | Annual board performance assessment |
| Succession | Documented succession planning process |

### ESG Governance Structure
- **Board level**: ESG/sustainability committee with charter
- **Executive level**: Chief Sustainability Officer or equivalent
- **Operational level**: Cross-functional ESG working groups
- **Incentives**: ESG metrics linked to executive compensation

## Stakeholder Engagement

### Engagement Matrix

| Stakeholder | Priority Topics | Engagement Methods | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Investors | Climate risk, governance, financial ESG metrics | ESG roadshows, proxy engagement | Quarterly |
| Employees | DEI, safety, wellbeing, purpose | Town halls, surveys, ERGs | Ongoing |
| Customers | Sustainable products, supply chain | Sustainability reports, product labels | Annually |
| Communities | Local impact, jobs, environment | Community advisory panels | Semi-annually |
| Regulators | Compliance, disclosure | Policy engagement, reporting | As required |
| NGOs | Environmental/social issues | Partnerships, dialogues | Project-based |

### Engagement Best Practices
- Identify and prioritize stakeholders by influence and impact
- Establish regular two-way communication channels
- Integrate stakeholder feedback into strategy
- Report back on how feedback influenced decisions
- Document engagement activities and outcomes
