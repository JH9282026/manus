# ESG Strategy & Sustainability

Develop and execute comprehensive Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) strategies that integrate sustainability into core business operations and create long-term value.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for building holistic ESG programs covering environmental strategy (climate, biodiversity, circular economy), social initiatives (DEI, labor practices, community), and governance (board structure, ethics, transparency). Cover reporting standards, target-setting, and stakeholder communication.

## ESG Strategy Framework

### Step 1: Assess Current State
- Conduct ESG materiality assessment
- Benchmark against industry peers
- Review current ESG ratings and gaps
- Audit existing policies and programs

### Step 2: Set Strategy and Targets
- Define ESG vision and priorities aligned to business strategy
- Set measurable targets (science-based where applicable)
- Establish governance structure for ESG oversight
- Allocate budget and resources

### Step 3: Implement Programs
- Launch environmental initiatives (energy, waste, water, emissions)
- Develop social programs (DEI, health & safety, community)
- Strengthen governance (board diversity, ethics, transparency)
- Integrate ESG into procurement and supply chain

### Step 4: Report and Communicate
- Publish sustainability report (GRI, TCFD, ISSB aligned)
- Engage with ESG rating agencies proactively
- Communicate to stakeholders (investors, employees, customers)
- Respond to CDP and other disclosure questionnaires

### Step 5: Monitor and Improve
- Track KPIs quarterly against targets
- Conduct annual materiality refresh
- Adjust strategy based on regulatory and market changes
- Pursue continuous improvement and innovation

## ESG Pillar Overview

| Pillar | Key Topics | Example Metrics |
|---|---|---|
| Environmental | Climate, energy, water, waste, biodiversity | GHG emissions (tCO2e), renewable energy %, water intensity |
| Social | DEI, labor, health & safety, community | Gender diversity %, TRIR, training hours, living wage % |
| Governance | Board, ethics, risk, transparency | Board independence %, ethics violations, ESG-linked comp |

## Reporting Framework Selection

| Framework | Audience | Focus | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| GRI | All stakeholders | Impact-focused, comprehensive | Widely used globally |
| ISSB (IFRS S1/S2) | Investors | Financial materiality | New global baseline |
| TCFD | Investors, regulators | Climate risk | Increasingly mandatory |
| CDP | Investors, customers | Environmental disclosure | Scoring-based |
| SASB | Investors | Industry-specific metrics | Merged into ISSB |
