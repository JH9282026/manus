# Prospecting Frameworks

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Definition

### Firmographic Attributes
- **Industry**: Define primary and secondary SIC/NAICS codes
- **Company size**: Employee count ranges and revenue bands
- **Geography**: Countries, states, metro areas
- **Company type**: Private, VC-backed, public, PE-owned
- **Growth stage**: Startup, scale-up, established, enterprise

### Technographic Signals
- **Required technologies**: Must-have tools indicating fit
- **Preferred technologies**: Nice-to-have indicating sophistication
- **Excluded technologies**: Competitor products or incompatible stacks

### Organizational Indicators
- **Decision-maker titles**: VP Sales, CRO, Head of Ops
- **Department size**: Minimum team headcount
- **Growth indicators**: Recent funding, hiring patterns, expansion signals

## Data Source Strategy

### Company Intelligence Sources
| Source | Data Type | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| LinkedIn Company Pages | Firmographics, employees | Company overview, org charts |
| Crunchbase/PitchBook | Funding, investors | Startup and growth-stage research |
| Company websites | Products, team, culture | ICP validation |
| G2/Capterra | Technology usage | Technographic signals |
| Job boards | Hiring patterns | Growth and intent signals |
| SEC filings | Financials | Public company research |
| News aggregators | Events, partnerships | Trigger event identification |

### Contact Intelligence Sources
| Source | Data Type | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| LinkedIn profiles | Title, tenure, background | Decision-maker identification |
| Company team pages | Org structure | Reporting relationships |
| Conference speaker lists | Expertise, visibility | Warm outreach hooks |
| Published articles | Thought leadership | Personalization hooks |
| Professional associations | Industry engagement | Credibility signals |

## Prospecting Workflows

### New Market Entry
1. Define ICP based on best-fit current customers
2. Build initial list of 200+ companies matching firmographics
3. Validate technographic fit using BuiltWith or SimilarTech
4. Identify 2-3 decision-maker contacts per account
5. Enrich with funding, hiring, and news data
6. Score and tier for outreach prioritization

### Event-Based Prospecting
1. Obtain attendee/speaker list
2. Match against ICP criteria
3. Research top 100 priority contacts
4. Identify warm introduction paths
5. Prepare personalized meeting request templates
6. Create day-of engagement plan

### Competitive Displacement
1. Identify competitor customers via reviews, case studies, news
2. Verify current vendor relationship status
3. Research specific pain points and contract timing
4. Score displacement opportunity
5. Develop targeted messaging addressing competitor weaknesses

### Channel Partner Recruitment
1. Map complementary technology ecosystem
2. Identify consultancies, MSPs, and integrators
3. Assess capability overlap and customer base fit
4. Score partnership potential
5. Develop co-selling value propositions
