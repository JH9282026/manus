# Qualification Scoring

## Scoring Model Design

### Multi-Dimensional Scoring Framework

Build qualification scores across these dimensions:

#### Fit Score (45% weight)
Measures how closely a prospect matches the ICP.

| Criteria | Weight | Scoring |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Industry match | 20% | Primary industry=100, Adjacent=60, Peripheral=20 |
| Company size | 15% | Sweet spot=100, Adjacent range=70, Edge range=40 |
| Geography | 10% | Target market=100, Expansion market=60 |

#### Intent Score (25% weight)
Measures buying readiness signals.

| Signal | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Active RFP/evaluation | 100 |
| Recent relevant search activity | 80 |
| Content engagement (downloads, webinars) | 60 |
| Hiring for relevant roles | 50 |
| Recent funding round | 40 |
| Technology stack changes | 30 |

#### Engagement Score (15% weight)
Measures accessibility and relationship warmth.

| Factor | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Existing relationship/referral | 100 |
| Verified direct contact info | 80 |
| LinkedIn connection | 60 |
| Company contact only | 40 |
| No contact information | 10 |

#### Timing Score (15% weight)
Measures urgency and contract cycle alignment.

| Indicator | Score |
|-----------|-------|
| Known contract renewal approaching | 100 |
| Budget cycle alignment | 80 |
| Recent trigger event | 60 |
| General market timing | 40 |

---

## Tier Classification

| Tier | Score Range | Action |
|------|------------|--------|
| Hot | 80-100 | Immediate outreach with personalized approach |
| Warm | 60-79 | Scheduled outreach within 2 weeks |
| Nurture | 40-59 | Add to nurture campaign, monitor for signals |
| Archive | Below 40 | Deprioritize, revisit quarterly |

---

## Account Intelligence Brief Template

For top-tier accounts, produce:

1. **Company Overview** — Business model, market position, recent developments
2. **Pain Point Hypothesis** — Likely challenges based on industry, size, and signals
3. **Competitive Landscape** — Current vendor relationships, satisfaction signals
4. **Engagement Strategy** — Recommended channels, messaging angles, timing
5. **Personalization Hooks** — Specific references for outreach (news, content, connections)
6. **Risk Factors** — Potential obstacles to engagement or conversion

## Compliance Requirements

### GDPR (EU Contacts)
- Document lawful basis for processing (legitimate interest)
- Provide opt-out mechanism in all communications
- Maintain records of processing activities
- Honor data subject access requests within 30 days

### CCPA (California Contacts)
- Disclose data collection practices
- Provide right to opt-out of data sale
- Honor deletion requests
- Maintain 12-month lookback records

### CAN-SPAM (Email Outreach)
- Include physical mailing address
- Provide clear unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor opt-out requests within 10 business days
- Accurate sender and subject line information
