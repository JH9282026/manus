# Lead Generation & Qualification

Identify and qualify potential business prospects through systematic research, scoring, and pipeline building.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for transforming ideal customer profile (ICP) definitions into actionable, prioritized prospect lists enriched with firmographic, technographic, and intent data. Bridge the gap between marketing strategy and sales execution.

## Core Process

1. **Define ICP** — Specify firmographics (industry, size, revenue, geography), technographics (required/preferred tech stack), and organizational attributes (decision-maker titles, department size)
2. **Source prospects** — Use LinkedIn, Crunchbase, job boards, conference lists, review sites, and company websites
3. **Enrich data** — Gather contact details, funding history, hiring signals, technology usage, and recent news
4. **Score and qualify** — Apply weighted scoring across fit, intent, accessibility, and timing dimensions
5. **Prioritize and tier** — Segment into hot/warm/nurture tiers based on qualification threshold
6. **Deliver pipeline** — Output as CRM-ready format with account intelligence briefs

## Qualification Scoring Model

| Attribute | Weight | Scoring Logic |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Company size fit | 25% | Exact match=100, Adjacent=70, Partial=40 |
| Industry fit | 20% | Primary=100, Secondary=60 |
| Technology fit | 20% | All required=100, Partial=50 |
| Intent signals | 15% | Strong=100, Moderate=60, Weak=20 |
| Accessibility | 10% | Direct contact=100, LinkedIn only=60 |
| Timing indicators | 10% | Active buying=100, Planning=60 |

Set qualification threshold at 70+ for pipeline inclusion.

## Key Deliverables

- **Qualified Lead List** — Company and contact data with scores and tiers
- **Account Intelligence Briefs** — Pain points, engagement hooks, competitive landscape for top-tier accounts
- **Qualification Scorecard** — Score breakdown, confidence levels, recommended actions
- **Pipeline Analytics** — Distribution by geography, industry, size, and score
