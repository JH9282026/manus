### Common Particle Effect Types

**Natural Phenomena**:
- Fire and flames
- Smoke and vapor
- Rain and snow
- Dust and debris
- Water splashes
- Fog and mist

**Stylized Effects**:
- Magic and energy effects
- Sparkles and glitter
- Abstract particles
- Transition effects
- Motion graphics elements

**Simulation-Based**:
- Explosions
- Destruction debris
- Fluid dynamics
- Cloth simulation
- Hair and fur
