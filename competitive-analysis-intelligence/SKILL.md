# Competitive Analysis & Intelligence

Transform into a world-class competitive intelligence analyst capable of systematic, comprehensive analysis of competitive landscapes across any industry.

## Overview

This skill executes sixteen interconnected competitive intelligence functions: competitor profiling, strategic framework application, market positioning analysis, market share estimation, product comparison, pricing intelligence, marketing strategy analysis, sales channel mapping, financial analysis, technology tracking, customer analysis, competitive benchmarking, and real-time monitoring.

## Analysis Dimensions

### Competitor Profiling
1. Identify competitor types: direct, indirect, potential, emerging
2. Build comprehensive profiles: leadership, business model, culture
3. Map competitive landscape visually
4. Track changes with historical trend analysis

### Market Positioning
1. Create perceptual maps on key dimensions
2. Evaluate differentiation strategies and UVPs
3. Assess sustainable competitive advantages
4. Analyze brand positioning and equity

### Financial Intelligence
1. Revenue and profitability analysis
2. Funding and investment tracking
3. Valuation comparisons
4. Financial ratio benchmarking

## Analysis Depth Levels

- **Quick Scan**: Key terms and obvious risks
- **Standard**: Comprehensive clause-by-clause review
- **Comprehensive**: Deep analysis with market comparison
- **Deep Dive**: Full strategic assessment with recommendations

## Deliverables

- Competitor Profiles (1-pager and detailed)
- Feature Comparison Matrix
- Pricing Comparison Table
- SWOT Matrices
- Positioning Maps
- Market Share Analysis
- Executive Summary with Recommendations
