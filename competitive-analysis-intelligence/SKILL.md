# Competitive Analysis & Intelligence

Conduct systematic, comprehensive analysis of competitive landscapes across any industry.

## Overview

Transform raw market data into actionable competitive intelligence through strategic framework application, deep competitor profiling, and continuous monitoring. Deliver insights that inform market entry decisions, product strategy, pricing optimization, and strategic planning.

## Core Intelligence Functions

| Function | Purpose | Key Outputs |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Competitor Profiling | Map competitive landscape | Company profiles, org structures |
| Strategic Frameworks | Apply analytical models | SWOT, Porter's 5 Forces, PESTEL |
| Market Positioning | Analyze market positions | Perceptual maps, positioning statements |
| Market Share | Estimate share metrics | TAM/SAM/SOM, revenue share |
| Product Comparison | Compare offerings | Feature matrices, quality benchmarks |
| Pricing Intelligence | Decode pricing strategies | Price positioning, model comparison |
| Marketing Analysis | Evaluate marketing approaches | Channel mix, content strategy |
| Financial Analysis | Assess financial health | Revenue metrics, funding history |

## Strategic Frameworks

### SWOT Analysis
Identify Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats for target company and each competitor.

### Porter's Five Forces
| Force | Assessment Focus |
|-------|------------------|
| Competitive Rivalry | Number and strength of competitors |
| Supplier Power | Concentration, switching costs |
| Buyer Power | Customer concentration, price sensitivity |
| Threat of Substitutes | Alternative solutions available |
| Threat of New Entrants | Barriers to entry, capital requirements |

### PESTEL Analysis
Analyze Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal factors affecting the industry.

## Competitor Profile Components

- Company overview and history
- Leadership and organizational structure
- Business model and revenue streams
- Product/service portfolio
- Market positioning and messaging
- Financial performance indicators
- Technology stack and capabilities
- Customer base and segments
- Strategic direction and recent moves

## Analysis Depth Options

| Depth | Scope | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Quick Scan | Key highlights only | 1-2 hours |
| Standard | Full framework analysis | 1-2 days |
| Comprehensive | Deep-dive all dimensions | 3-5 days |
| Deep Dive | Exhaustive investigation | 1-2 weeks |
