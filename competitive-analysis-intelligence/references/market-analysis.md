# Market Analysis

Market sizing, share analysis, and positioning assessment.

---

## Market Sizing

### TAM/SAM/SOM
- **TAM** (Total Addressable Market): Total market demand
- **SAM** (Serviceable Addressable Market): Segment you can reach
- **SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market): Realistic capture

### Sizing Methods

**Top-Down**
1. Start with industry reports
2. Apply relevant filters
3. Estimate penetration rates

**Bottom-Up**
1. Count potential customers
2. Estimate average deal size
3. Calculate total opportunity

---

## Market Share Analysis

### Calculation Methods
- Revenue share: Company revenue / Total market revenue
- Unit share: Company units / Total market units
- Segment share: Share within specific segments

### Data Sources
- Industry analyst reports (Gartner, IDC, Forrester)
- Company disclosures
- Survey data
- Traffic/usage proxies

### Visualization
- Pie charts for point-in-time
- Stacked area for trends
- Waterfall for changes

---

## Positioning Analysis

### Perceptual Mapping
1. Select two key dimensions (e.g., price vs. quality)
2. Plot competitors on 2D grid
3. Identify clusters and white space
4. Assess differentiation opportunities

### Positioning Statement Template
```
For [target customer] who [need/want],
[Product] is a [category]
that [key benefit].
Unlike [competitor],
[product] [key differentiator].
```

## Market Concentration

### Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI)
```
HHI = Σ(Market Share %)²
```
- < 1,500: Unconcentrated
- 1,500-2,500: Moderately concentrated
- > 2,500: Highly concentrated

### Concentration Ratios
- CR4: Top 4 firms' combined share
- CR8: Top 8 firms' combined share
