# Competitor Profiling

Methodology for building comprehensive competitor profiles.

---

## Profile Components

### Company Overview
- Founding date and history
- Headquarters and locations
- Employee count and growth
- Mission and vision statements

### Leadership Team
- Executive backgrounds
- Board composition
- Key hires and departures
- Leadership style and culture

### Business Model
- Revenue streams
- Customer segments
- Value proposition
- Key partnerships

### Products/Services
- Portfolio overview
- Feature comparison
- Pricing structure
- Roadmap indicators

---

## Data Sources

### Primary Sources
- Company website and press releases
- SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q, S-1)
- Earnings calls and presentations
- Patent filings

### Secondary Sources
- Industry analyst reports
- News and media coverage
- Social media presence
- Employee reviews (Glassdoor)
- Customer reviews (G2, Capterra)

---

## Monitoring System

### Automated Tracking
- Website change detection
- News alerts (Google Alerts)
- Social media monitoring
- Job posting analysis
- Patent filing alerts

### Periodic Reviews
- Weekly intelligence brief
- Monthly competitive update
- Quarterly deep dive
- Annual strategic assessment

---

## Profile Template

```markdown
# [Competitor Name]

## Overview
- Founded: [Year]
- Employees: [Count]
- Revenue: [Amount/Estimate]
- Funding: [Amount/Stage]

## Strengths
- [Key strength 1]
- [Key strength 2]

## Weaknesses
- [Key weakness 1]
- [Key weakness 2]

## Strategic Direction
[Summary of strategic priorities]

## Competitive Threat Level: [High/Medium/Low]
```
