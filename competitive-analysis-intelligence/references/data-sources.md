# Competitive Intelligence Data Sources

Research methodology and recommended data sources.

---

## Public Information Sources

### Financial Data
- SEC EDGAR (10-K, 10-Q, S-1 filings)
- Earnings call transcripts
- Investor presentations
- Annual reports
- Crunchbase/PitchBook (private companies)

### Market Research
- Industry analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester, IDC)
- Market sizing data (Statista, IBISWorld)
- Trade publications
- Academic research

### Company Information
- Official websites
- Press releases
- Blog posts and content
- Social media profiles
- Job postings (strategic signals)

### Review Platforms
- G2, Capterra, TrustRadius (B2B software)
- Glassdoor, Indeed (employee reviews)
- App stores (mobile apps)
- Industry-specific review sites

## Research Best Practices

### Source Validation
- Verify data currency (within 12 months preferred)
- Cross-reference multiple sources
- Distinguish facts from estimates
- Note confidence levels

### Ethical Guidelines
- Use only public information
- No misrepresentation
- Respect terms of service
- Document all sources

### Data Organization
- Create source bibliography
- Note access dates
- Flag estimates vs. facts
- Maintain audit trail
