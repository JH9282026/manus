## Negotiation Preparation Framework

1. **Define objectives** — Must-haves, nice-to-haves, walk-away point
2. **Research** — Market data, counterpart's interests, constraints, BATNA
3. **Identify ZOPA** — Zone of Possible Agreement between parties
4. **Plan strategy** — Opening position, concession strategy, tactics
5. **Rehearse** — Practice key arguments, anticipate objections
6. **Set environment** — Timing, location, agenda control

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Definition |
|---------|-----------|
| BATNA | Best Alternative To Negotiated Agreement |
| ZOPA | Zone of Possible Agreement |
| Reservation price | Walk-away point |
| Anchor | First number/offer on the table |
| Logrolling | Trading concessions on different issues |
| WATNA | Worst Alternative To Negotiated Agreement |

## Negotiation Styles

| Style | When to Use | Risk |
|-------|-------------|------|
| Competitive | One-time deals, clear value claims | Damages relationships |
| Collaborative | Ongoing relationships, complex deals | Time-consuming |
| Accommodating | Relationship more important than outcome | May be exploited |
| Avoiding | Low-stakes, need more information | Issues unresolved |
| Compromising | Time pressure, moderate importance | Suboptimal outcomes |
