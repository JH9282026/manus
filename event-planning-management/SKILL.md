# Event Planning & Management

Design, coordinate, and execute outstanding corporate events, conferences, and experiential programs from concept through post-event analysis.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for end-to-end event management covering strategic planning, budget development, venue selection, vendor coordination, attendee experience design, logistics execution, and ROI measurement. Cover in-person, virtual, and hybrid event formats.

## Event Planning Timeline

| Phase | Timing | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|
| Strategy & Concept | 12-6 months | Goals, budget, format, theme |
| Planning | 6-3 months | Venue, vendors, speakers, marketing |
| Preparation | 3-1 month | Registration, logistics, rehearsals |
| Execution | Event week | Setup, run of show, real-time management |
| Post-Event | 1-4 weeks after | Debrief, ROI analysis, follow-up |

## Budget Framework

| Category | % of Budget | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| Venue & catering | 30-40% | Rental, F&B, A/V |
| Production | 15-25% | Stage, lighting, sound, video |
| Marketing | 10-15% | Promotion, materials, signage |
| Speakers/entertainment | 5-15% | Fees, travel, hospitality |
| Technology | 5-10% | Event app, streaming, Wi-Fi |
| Logistics | 5-10% | Shipping, transportation, insurance |
| Staffing | 5-10% | Event team, security, volunteers |
| Contingency | 5-10% | Unexpected expenses |

## Event Type Selection

| Type | Audience Size | Duration | Primary Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conference | 200-5,000+ | 1-3 days | Education, networking |
| Summit | 50-500 | 1-2 days | Strategy, executive alignment |
| Workshop | 15-50 | Half-day to 2 days | Skill building, hands-on |
| Product launch | 50-1,000+ | 2-4 hours | Awareness, excitement |
| Retreat | 20-200 | 2-4 days | Team building, planning |
| Gala | 100-1,000 | 3-5 hours | Fundraising, recognition |
| Trade show | 500-50,000+ | 2-4 days | Lead generation |
| Webinar | 50-5,000+ | 45-90 min | Education, lead gen |
