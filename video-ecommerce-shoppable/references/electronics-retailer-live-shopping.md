### Electronics Retailer Live Shopping

**Challenge:** Launching new product lines with limited physical retail presence.

**Solution:** Weekly live shopping events featuring:
- Product demonstrations by experts
- Real-time Q&A sessions
- Limited-time live-only offers
- Interactive audience polling

**Results:**
- 3x average conversion rate compared to standard product pages
- Growing loyal live shopping community
- Significant social media engagement
- Successful new product launches
