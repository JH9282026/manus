### Unboxing Videos

**Purpose:**
Reveal product presentation, packaging, and first impressions.

**Psychology:**
- Builds anticipation
- Shows actual product (vs. photoshopped images)
- Creates transparency
- Triggers "new product" excitement

**Best Practices:**
- Film in real-time
- Show genuine reactions
- Highlight packaging quality
- Display all included items
- Note any surprises (positive or negative)

**Authenticity Balance:**
- Professional production quality
- Genuine reactions
- Honest observations
- Brand personality
