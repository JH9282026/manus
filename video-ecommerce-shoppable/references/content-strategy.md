### Content Strategy

**Answer Key Questions:**
- What does it look like?
- How big/small is it?
- How does it work?
- What materials/quality?
- Why should I buy it?

**Address Objections:**
- Durability concerns
- Fit/size questions
- Compatibility issues
- Value justification
- Quality assurance

**Create Desire:**
- Emotional connection
- Aspiration alignment
- Social proof integration
- Urgency creation
- Exclusive feeling
