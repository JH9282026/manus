### Comparison Videos

**Purpose:**
Help customers choose by comparing options or differentiating from competitors.

**Approaches:**
- Product vs. product (own lineup)
- Feature comparisons
- Use case matching
- Problem-solution matching
- Price-value demonstration

**Best Practices:**
- Be fair and accurate
- Focus on genuine differences
- Help with decision, not manipulation
- Respect competitor products
- Provide clear recommendations
