### How-To and Tutorial Videos

**Purpose:**
Educate customers on product use while showcasing capabilities.

**Benefits:**
- Demonstrates value
- Reduces returns
- Builds expertise perception
- Creates supportive community
- SEO value

**Structure:**
1. Problem statement
2. Tool/product introduction
3. Step-by-step demonstration
4. Result showcase
5. Tips and variations
6. Product/purchase CTA
