### Product Category Strategies

**Fashion and Apparel:**
- Fit demonstrations
- Styling options
- Material close-ups
- Size comparison
- Movement and drape

**Beauty and Cosmetics:**
- Application tutorials
- Color matching
- Before/after transformations
- Ingredient education
- Routine building

**Electronics and Tech:**
- Feature demonstrations
- Unboxing experiences
- Setup tutorials
- Comparison testing
- Real-world usage

**Home and Furniture:**
- Scale and proportion
- Room placement
- Assembly instructions
- Material quality
- Lifestyle context

**Food and Beverage:**
- Preparation demonstrations
- Taste reactions
- Ingredient showcases
- Pairing suggestions
- Behind-the-scenes sourcing
