### Types of Interactivity

**Click-to-Buy:**
- Product tags
- Instant add-to-cart
- Checkout links
- Wishlist additions

**Product Exploration:**
- Hotspots with details
- Feature explanations
- Specification reveals
- Color/size variants

**Navigation:**
- Chapter markers
- Skip to products
- Category filters
- Search within video

**Engagement:**
- Polls and quizzes
- Size finders
- Style recommendations
- Personalized paths
