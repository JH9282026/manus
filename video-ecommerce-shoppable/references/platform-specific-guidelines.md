### Platform-Specific Guidelines

**Product Pages:**
- 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- Hook in first 5 seconds
- Get to product quickly
- Clear and concise

**Social Media:**
- Platform specific (see chart below)
- Front-load key content
- Assume no sound
- Mobile-first design

**Email:**
- GIF: 3-5 seconds
- Landing page video: 30-60 seconds
- Compelling thumbnail
