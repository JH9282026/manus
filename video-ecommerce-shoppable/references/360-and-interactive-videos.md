### 360° and Interactive Videos

**Purpose:**
Enable viewer-controlled product exploration from all angles.

**Technology Options:**
- 360° video playback
- Interactive hotspots
- Spin views
- Zoom capabilities
- AR integration

**Best Applications:**
- Fashion (see all angles)
- Electronics (inspect details)
- Furniture (scale and proportion)
- Vehicles
- Real estate

**Production Considerations:**
- Specialized equipment
- Post-production requirements
- Platform compatibility
- File size management
- User experience design
