### Quality Standards by Platform

**Website Product Pages:**
- 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Optimized file sizes (under 50MB)
- Fast-loading formats
- Caption inclusion

**Social Media:**
- Platform-specific specs
- Square or vertical formats
- Sound-off design
- Attention-grabbing openings
- Native upload

**Marketplaces (Amazon, etc.):**
- Platform-specific requirements
- A+ content video specs
- Brand story guidelines
- Technical compliance
