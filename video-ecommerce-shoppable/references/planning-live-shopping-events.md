### Planning Live Shopping Events

**Event Strategy:**
- Theme and focus
- Product selection
- Promotional planning
- Influencer/host selection
- Technical preparation

**Production Checklist:**
- Stream testing
- Backup systems
- Inventory allocation
- Team coordination
- Customer service readiness

**Promotion Timeline:**
- 2 weeks: Initial announcement
- 1 week: Reminder and teasers
- 3 days: Countdown content
- Day of: Final reminders
- Post-event: Replay promotion
