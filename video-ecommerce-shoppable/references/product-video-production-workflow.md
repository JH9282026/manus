### Product Video Production Workflow

**Pre-Production:**
1. Product analysis (features, USPs)
2. Competitive video review
3. Script/shot list development
4. Production planning
5. Talent/model coordination

**Production:**
1. Studio/location setup
2. Lighting optimization
3. Multiple angle capture
4. B-roll collection
5. Sound recording

**Post-Production:**
1. Footage organization
2. Edit assembly
3. Color correction
4. Graphics and text
5. Audio mixing
6. Format exports
