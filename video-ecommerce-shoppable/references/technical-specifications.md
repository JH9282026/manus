### Technical Specifications

**Video Formats:**
- MP4 (H.264) for broad compatibility
- WebM for additional web support
- Platform-specific requirements

**File Sizes:**
- Optimize for page load speed
- Mobile-first consideration
- CDN delivery
- Adaptive bitrate when possible

**Aspect Ratios:**
- 16:9 for product pages (desktop)
- 1:1 for social feeds
- 9:16 for Stories/Reels
- 4:5 for Instagram feed
