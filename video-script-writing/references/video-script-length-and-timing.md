## Video Script Length and Timing


### Words Per Minute for Voiceover
Standard speaking rates:

| Pace | Words Per Minute | Use Case |
|------|------------------|----------|
| Slow | 100-120 WPM | Emotional, documentary |
| Medium | 130-150 WPM | Corporate, explainer |
| Fast | 160-180 WPM | Energetic, promotional |
| Very Fast | 180+ WPM | Disclaimers, certain ads |


### Calculating Video Length
**Formula:** Total words ÷ WPM = Minutes of dialogue

**Example:**
- 450 words at 150 WPM = 3 minutes of voiceover
- Add time for visuals without VO, transitions, music moments
- Final video might be 3:30-4:00


### Pacing Adjustments
Timing changes based on content:

- Complex information: Slow down, add pauses
- Emotional moments: Allow visual breathing room
- Action sequences: Tighter timing
- Demonstrations: Match natural duration of action
