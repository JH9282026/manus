## Music and Sound Effects


### Music Cues in Scripts
Indicate music throughout:

```
MUSIC: Upbeat corporate track begins (builds through intro)

[Main content]

MUSIC: Transitions to emotional, piano-driven underscore

[Testimonial section]

MUSIC: Returns to upbeat, crescendos to conclusion

MUSIC: Fades under final VO, ends with logo
```


### Sound Effect Notes
Include key sound effects:

```
SFX: Keyboard typing (establishing work environment)
SFX: Email "send" whoosh
SFX: Notification chime
SFX: Subtle "success" sting on transformation moment
```


### Audio Atmosphere
Set the sonic environment:

```
AMBIENT: Busy office background (low, underneath VO)
AMBIENT: Quiet, peaceful—birds, gentle wind
AMBIENT: Urban—traffic, city sounds, energy
```
