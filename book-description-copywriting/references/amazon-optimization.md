# Amazon Optimization

Maximize discoverability and conversion on Amazon.

---

## Amazon Algorithm Factors

### Where Keywords Matter

| Location | Weight | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Title/Subtitle | Highest | Include primary keyword |
| Description | High | Natural integration |
| Backend Keywords | Medium | Hidden in KDP dashboard |
| Categories | High | Browse discoverability |

### Keyword Research Process

1. List all genre/topic keywords
2. Research search volume (Publisher Rocket)
3. Analyze competitor keywords
4. Identify long-tail opportunities
5. Test and refine

## HTML Formatting

### Allowed Tags

```html
<b>Bold text</b>
<i>Italic text</i>
<br> (line breaks via blank lines)
```

### Best Practices

- Use bold sparingly for emphasis
- Create visual breaks with spacing
- Consider bold tagline at end
- Test on mobile devices

### Example Formatted Description

```html
<b>A mother's worst nightmare. A detective's impossible case.</b>

When Sarah Chen's daughter vanishes from their quiet suburban home, the police have no leads—and Sarah has no answers.

But as she digs into the investigation, Sarah uncovers secrets that threaten to destroy everything she thought she knew about her family.

<i>"Unputdownable. The twists will leave you breathless."</i> —New York Times Bestselling Author

<b>If you love domestic thrillers with shocking endings, download now.</b>
```

## Character Limits

- **Total limit**: ~4,000 characters (including HTML)
- **Above fold**: ~150 words visible
- **Optimal length**: 200-400 words

---

## Conversion Elements

### Social Proof

- Bestseller status
- Awards and recognition
- Review quotes
- Sales numbers

### Comparison Titles

**Effective Formulas:**
- "For fans of [Author A] and [Author B]"
- "[Book A] meets [Book B]"
- "If you loved [Book], you'll devour this"

**Caution:**
- Don't over-compare (1-2 is enough)
- Ensure accuracy
- Choose widely known books
