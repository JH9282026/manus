# Book Description Copywriting

Craft compelling text that sells books on Amazon, back covers, and promotional materials.

## Overview

Book description copywriting achieves a delicate balance: hook readers emotionally, convey the book's essence, and create purchase urgency—without giving away too much. The description is often the deciding factor between purchase and pass.

## Essential Structure

1. **Hook**: Attention-grabbing opening line
2. **Setup**: World, situation, or problem introduction
3. **Character(s)**: Who readers will follow
4. **Conflict/Stakes**: What's at risk
5. **Intrigue**: Questions raised, mysteries hinted
6. **Call to Action**: Implicit invitation to read

## Amazon Optimization

- **Above the fold**: Only ~150 words show before "Read more"
- **Front-load**: Most compelling content first
- **Scannable**: Short paragraphs, white space
- **Keywords**: Integrate naturally for search

## Hook Types

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Question | "What would you do if your husband was a serial killer?" |
| Statement | "She thought she'd escaped her past. She was wrong." |
| Contrast | "By day, kindergarten teacher. By night, hunting her daughter's killer." |
| Promise | "A love story that stays with you after the final page." |
| Scenario | "It's 3 AM. Your child is gone. The ransom note says don't call police." |

## Genre Conventions

| Genre | Focus | Avoid |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Romance | Both leads, chemistry, conflict | Spoiling HEA |
| Thriller | Immediate tension, stakes, twists | Revealing villain |
| Mystery | Crime/puzzle, sleuth, atmosphere | Solution hints |
| Fantasy | World, magic, quest | Over-explaining rules |
| Self-Help | Reader's problem, transformation | Being vague |
| Memoir | Compelling story element, journey | Chronological summary |

## Comparison Titles

Use sparingly and accurately:
- "For fans of Sally Rooney and Taylor Jenkins Reid"
- "Bridgerton meets The Witcher"
- "If you loved *Where the Crawdads Sing*..."

## Optimal Length

- **Amazon**: 200-400 words total (150 above fold)
- **Back cover**: 150-250 words
- **Promotional**: 50-100 words
