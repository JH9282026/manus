# Retention Analytics and Metrics

Measuring and analyzing retention effectiveness.

## Key Metrics

### Retention Rate
**Formula:** (Employees at end / Employees at start) × 100
**Target:** >85%
**Frequency:** Monthly, quarterly, annually

### Turnover Rate
**Formula:** (Departures / Average headcount) × 100
**Target:** <15%
**Types:** Voluntary, involuntary, regrettable

### Time-Based Retention
- 90-day retention (new hires)
- 1-year retention
- 3-year retention
- 5-year retention

### Cost of Turnover
**Components:**
- Recruitment costs
- Onboarding expenses
- Training investment
- Lost productivity
- Knowledge loss

**Formula:** Total costs / Number of departures

## Predictive Analytics

### Attrition Risk Modeling
**Factors:**
- Tenure
- Performance ratings
- Engagement scores
- Compensation relative to market
- Promotion history
- Manager effectiveness

### Early Warning Indicators
- Declining engagement scores
- Increased absenteeism
- Reduced productivity
- Withdrawal from team activities
- LinkedIn profile updates
