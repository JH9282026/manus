# Regulatory Advocacy & Compliance

## Regulatory Comment Letters
- Analyze proposed rule against current operations
- Quantify compliance costs and competitive impacts
- Identify specific provisions requiring modification
- Propose alternative approaches achieving regulatory objectives with reduced burden
- Cite legal authority and precedent
- Submit before comment period deadline

## Agency Meeting Strategy
- Request meetings with program staff and decision-makers
- Prepare meeting objectives, topics, and participant list
- Follow ex parte communication rules where applicable
- Document all communications per agency requirements
- Follow up with written summary and supporting materials

## PAC Management
- Analyze contribution strategy against business priorities and political landscape
- Identify candidates aligned with organizational interests
- Develop contribution recommendations with supporting rationale
- Ensure compliance with federal and state campaign finance laws
- Prepare FEC reports and state equivalents
- Track contribution impact on legislative relationships

## Lobbying Compliance Framework
- Maintain federal and state lobbying registrations
- File LD-2 (activities/expenditures) and LD-203 (contributions) reports quarterly
- Track all lobbying contacts with covered officials
- Train staff on gift, travel, and interaction rules
- Document government relations activities for compliance and institutional knowledge
- Monitor revolving door restrictions for former government employees

## Regulatory Impact Analysis
- Assess operational, financial, and competitive impacts of proposed rules
- Model compliance costs across different scenarios
- Identify unintended consequences
- Develop alternatives that achieve policy objectives efficiently
