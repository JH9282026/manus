# Assessment Methodology

## Assessment Depth Levels

### Surface Scan
- Policy document review only
- Gap identification against key requirements
- High-level risk rating
- Typical duration: 1-3 days
- Best for: Initial baseline, quick health check

### Standard Assessment
- Policy and procedure review
- Sample evidence verification
- Interview-based control validation
- Detailed gap analysis with citations
- Typical duration: 1-2 weeks
- Best for: Annual compliance audits

### Deep Dive Audit
- Comprehensive documentation review
- Full evidence testing and verification
- Process observation and walkthrough
- Technical control testing
- Historical compliance trend analysis
- Typical duration: 3-6 weeks
- Best for: Pre-IPO, M&A due diligence, regulatory exam prep

## Compliance Scoring Methodology

### Overall Score Calculation (0-100)
- **Control Implementation** (40%): Are required controls in place?
- **Documentation Quality** (20%): Are policies current, complete, and approved?
- **Evidence Availability** (20%): Can compliance be demonstrated?
- **Operational Effectiveness** (20%): Do controls work in practice?

### Benchmark Scoring
| Score Range | Rating | Interpretation |
|-------------|--------|---------------|
| 90-100 | Excellent | Minimal gaps, best practice alignment |
| 80-89 | Good | Minor gaps, manageable risk |
| 70-79 | Satisfactory | Notable gaps requiring attention |
| 60-69 | Needs Improvement | Significant gaps, elevated risk |
| Below 60 | Critical | Material non-compliance, urgent action needed |

## Vendor Risk Assessment Process

### Batch Assessment Workflow
1. Distribute standardized security questionnaire (SIG, CAIQ)
2. Collect SOC 2 Type II reports and certifications
3. Review Business Associate Agreements or DPAs
4. Score each vendor against risk criteria
5. Generate ranked vendor compliance scorecard
6. Identify contractual remediation requirements

### Vendor Risk Scoring
| Category | Weight | Factors |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Data access level | 30% | Type and volume of data accessed |
| Security posture | 25% | SOC 2, ISO 27001, penetration testing |
| Compliance certifications | 20% | Relevant regulatory certifications |
| Incident history | 15% | Past breaches, response quality |
| Contractual protections | 10% | Indemnification, liability caps, insurance |

## Remediation Roadmap Template

### Priority Classification
| Priority | Criteria | Timeline |
|----------|----------|----------|
| P1 - Critical | Active violation, imminent enforcement | 0-30 days |
| P2 - High | Material gap, significant penalty risk | 30-90 days |
| P3 - Medium | Partial compliance, moderate risk | 90-180 days |
| P4 - Low | Best practice gap, minimal risk | 180-365 days |

### Action Item Format
- **Finding**: Description of compliance gap
- **Regulation**: Specific article/section reference
- **Risk Rating**: P1-P4 with justification
- **Remediation**: Specific corrective action
- **Owner**: Responsible individual/team
- **Resources**: Estimated effort and cost
- **Deadline**: Target completion date
- **Verification**: How compliance will be confirmed
