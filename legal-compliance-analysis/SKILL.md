# Legal Compliance Analysis

Evaluate organizational compliance against legal requirements and generate actionable remediation recommendations.

## Overview

Perform multi-jurisdictional compliance assessments across regulatory frameworks including GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOX, AML/KYC, environmental regulations, labor laws, and industry-specific standards. Produce risk-rated gap analyses and prioritized remediation roadmaps.

## Core Assessment Process

1. **Scope definition** — Identify applicable regulations based on industry, geography, and data types
2. **Documentation collection** — Gather policies, procedures, contracts, training records
3. **Gap analysis** — Map current state against regulatory requirements control-by-control
4. **Risk rating** — Classify findings as Critical/High/Medium/Low based on penalty exposure
5. **Remediation planning** — Prioritize actions by risk severity and effort
6. **Reporting** — Produce executive summary, detailed findings, and evidence matrix

## Risk Rating Framework

| Rating | Definition | Response Timeline |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Critical | Active violation with imminent enforcement risk | Immediate (0-30 days) |
| High | Significant gap with material penalty exposure | Urgent (30-90 days) |
| Medium | Partial compliance requiring documented improvement | Planned (90-180 days) |
| Low | Minor gap or best practice recommendation | Scheduled (180-365 days) |

## Key Deliverables

- **Compliance Assessment Report** — Executive summary, compliance score (0-100), jurisdiction-by-jurisdiction analysis, penalty exposure calculations
- **Remediation Roadmap** — Prioritized actions with effort estimates, cost projections, and timelines
- **Compliance Evidence Matrix** — Control-by-control mapping to documentation with sufficiency ratings

## Important Disclaimer

This skill provides compliance analysis, not legal advice. Findings should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before regulatory submission.
