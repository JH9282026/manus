
### Working with Studios and Producers

**Pre-production:**
- Provide clear, finalized scripts
- Include pronunciation guides and emphasis notes
- Share reference spots you like
- Communicate brand voice and tone
- Discuss music and SFX preferences

**During the session:**
- Be present (or available by phone) for direction
- Get multiple takes with different approaches
- Ask for variations you might need later
- Trust the professionals—they know audio

**Post-production:**
- Review drafts promptly and provide specific feedback
- Ensure all versions needed (different lengths, different endings)
- Get properly formatted files for station submission
- Request stems/elements for future use

### Technical Requirements

**Standard radio ad formats:**
- MP3 (320 kbps) or WAV
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz
- Bit depth: 16-bit
- Mono or stereo depending on station requirements

**Timing considerations:**
- 60-second spots: exactly 59-60 seconds
- 30-second spots: exactly 29-30 seconds
- 15-second spots: exactly 14-15 seconds
- Include slight cushion for station bumpers

**Volume standards:**
- Follow broadcast standards (varies by market/station)
- Avoid excessive compression or loudness
- Don't exceed peak levels
