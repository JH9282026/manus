# Real Estate Market Trends and Demographic Analysis

## Key Economic Indicators

### Housing Affordability Index
Measures whether typical family can qualify for mortgage on typical home
**Components:** Income levels, home prices, interest rates
**Source:** National Association of REALTORS®

### Home Sales Volume
**Existing Home Sales:** Indicator of market activity
**New Home Sales:** Indicator of construction and future supply
**Trend Analysis:** Year-over-year comparisons reveal market direction

### Inventory Levels
**Months of Supply:** Current inventory / monthly sales rate
**Balanced Market:** 5-6 months of supply
**Seller's Market:** <4 months
**Buyer's Market:** >6 months

## Demographic Factors

### Population Trends
**Growth Areas:** Job creation, migration patterns
**Age Demographics:** Baby boomers, millennials, Gen Z
**Household Formation:** Marriage rates, family size

### Employment and Income
**Job Growth:** Primary driver of housing demand
**Wage Growth:** Affects affordability and purchasing power
**Industry Diversity:** Economic stability indicator

### Urbanization Trends
**Urban Core:** Revitalization and gentrification
**Suburbs:** Family-oriented growth
**Exurbs:** Remote work enabling longer commutes

## Conclusion
Analyzing market trends and demographics provides insight into future demand, pricing, and investment opportunities.
