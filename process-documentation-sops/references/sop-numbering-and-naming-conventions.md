### SOP Numbering and Naming Conventions

**Numbering Systems:**

*Sequential:* SOP-001, SOP-002, SOP-003
*Department-Based:* SOP-HR-001, SOP-FIN-001, SOP-OPS-001
*Function-Based:* SOP-PAY-001, SOP-REC-001, SOP-INV-001
*Hierarchical:* SOP-FIN-AP-001 (Finance > Accounts Payable > First SOP)

**Naming Conventions:**
- Use descriptive titles (not codes alone)
- Be consistent across all documents
- Include version in filename: `SOP-FIN-001_Accounts_Payable_v2.3.pdf`
- Avoid spaces (use underscores or hyphens)
