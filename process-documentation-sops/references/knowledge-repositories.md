### Knowledge Repositories

**Centralized knowledge bases** provide single-source-of-truth for process documentation:
- Structured hierarchy of documents
- Search functionality
- Access controls
- Version management
- Usage analytics

**Repository Structure Example:**
```
📁 Process Documentation
├── 📁 Operations
│   ├── 📁 Manufacturing
│   ├── 📁 Quality Control
│   └── 📁 Logistics
├── 📁 Finance
│   ├── 📁 Accounts Payable
│   ├── 📁 Accounts Receivable
│   └── 📁 Reporting
├── 📁 Human Resources
│   ├── 📁 Recruitment
│   ├── 📁 Onboarding
│   └── 📁 Performance
└── 📁 IT
    ├── 📁 Security
    ├── 📁 Infrastructure
    └── 📁 Support
```
