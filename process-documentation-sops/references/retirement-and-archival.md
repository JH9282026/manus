### Retirement and Archival

**When to retire:**
- Process eliminated
- Superseded by new document
- Consolidation with other documents
- Organizational changes

**Retirement process:**
1. Identify replacement document (if applicable)
2. Notify stakeholders
3. Update cross-references in other documents
4. Move to archive
5. Apply "Superseded" watermark
6. Retain per retention policy
