### Document Approval Workflow

**Typical Workflow Stages:**
1. **Draft**: Author creates initial version
2. **Review**: Subject matter experts verify accuracy
3. **Revision**: Author incorporates feedback
4. **Approval**: Designated approver authorizes
5. **Publication**: Document controller releases
6. **Communication**: Stakeholders notified of changes

**Approval Matrix Example:**

| Document Type | Reviewer | Approver |
|---------------|----------|----------|
| Department SOP | Department Manager | Director |
| Cross-functional SOP | All affected managers | VP Operations |
| Compliance SOP | Quality Manager + Legal | Compliance Officer |
