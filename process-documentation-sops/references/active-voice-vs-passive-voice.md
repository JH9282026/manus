### Active Voice vs. Passive Voice

**Active voice** (preferred for procedures):
- Subject performs the action
- Clearer and more direct
- "The operator starts the machine"

**Passive voice** (occasional use):
- Action is done to the subject
- Can obscure responsibility
- "The machine is started by the operator"

**When passive is acceptable:**
- Emphasis on the action, not the actor: "Reports are generated daily"
- Unknown or irrelevant actor: "The system was updated"
