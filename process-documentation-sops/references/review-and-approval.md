### Review and Approval

**Review types:**
- **Technical review**: Accuracy of procedure
- **Operational review**: Feasibility and practicality
- **Compliance review**: Regulatory alignment
- **Editorial review**: Clarity and consistency

**Approval checklist:**
- [ ] Accurate and complete
- [ ] Follows template standards
- [ ] Clear and understandable
- [ ] Properly formatted
- [ ] Visuals current and clear
- [ ] Cross-references verified
- [ ] Metadata complete
