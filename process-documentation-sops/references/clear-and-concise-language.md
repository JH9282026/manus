### Clear and Concise Language

**Be direct:**
- ✓ "Click Save"
- ✗ "You should proceed to click on the Save button"

**Eliminate filler words:**
- ✓ "Enter the customer ID"
- ✗ "Basically, what you need to do is enter the customer ID"

**Use concrete terms:**
- ✓ "Wait 30 seconds"
- ✗ "Wait a short time"
