#### Standard SOP Sections

**1. Header Information**
```
Document Number: SOP-FIN-001
Title: Accounts Payable Invoice Processing
Version: 2.3
Effective Date: March 1, 2026
Department: Finance
Author: [Name]
Approver: [Name]
Next Review Date: March 1, 2027
```

**2. Purpose**
States why the SOP exists and what it aims to achieve.

*Example:* "This SOP establishes standardized procedures for processing vendor invoices to ensure timely payment, accurate record-keeping, and appropriate approval controls."

**3. Scope**
Defines what the SOP covers and what it doesn't cover, including applicability.

*Example:* "This procedure applies to all vendor invoices processed by the Accounts Payable team. It excludes employee expense reimbursements (see SOP-FIN-003) and credit card reconciliation (see SOP-FIN-005)."

**4. Definitions**
Clarifies terms, acronyms, and jargon used in the document.

*Example:*
- **PO**: Purchase Order
- **Three-Way Match**: Verification of invoice against PO and receiving documentation
- **Vendor Master**: Centralized database of approved supplier information

**5. Responsibilities**
Lists roles involved and their specific responsibilities.

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| AP Clerk | Receives, codes, and enters invoices |
| AP Supervisor | Reviews exceptions, approves payments |
| Department Manager | Approves invoices over $5,000 |
| Controller | Monthly reconciliation review |

**6. Procedure**
The step-by-step instructions, numbered sequentially.

**7. References**
Related documents, forms, systems, or regulations.

**8. Revision History**
Tracks all changes made to the document.

**9. Attachments/Appendices**
Forms, checklists, screenshots, or flowcharts supporting the procedure.
