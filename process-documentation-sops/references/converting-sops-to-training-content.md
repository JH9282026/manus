### Converting SOPs to Training Content

**From SOP to Training:**

| SOP Element | Training Adaptation |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Purpose | Learning objectives |
| Definitions | Glossary/pre-requisite knowledge |
| Procedure | Guided exercises |
| References | Additional resources |
| Troubleshooting | Practice scenarios |

**Enhancement techniques:**
- Add context and rationale ("why we do this")
- Include knowledge checks
- Provide practice opportunities
- Add real-world examples
