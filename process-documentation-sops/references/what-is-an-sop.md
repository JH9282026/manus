### What is an SOP?

A Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is a documented set of step-by-step instructions that describe how to perform a routine activity. SOPs ensure that operations are carried out consistently, efficiently, and in compliance with applicable standards or regulations.

SOPs differ from informal documentation in their:
- **Authority**: Officially approved by management
- **Scope**: Organization-wide or department-wide applicability
- **Structure**: Standardized format and sections
- **Control**: Formal version management and change processes
