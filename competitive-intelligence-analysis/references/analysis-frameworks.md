# Intelligence Analysis Frameworks

Methodologies for synthesizing competitive intelligence.

---

## Competitor Strategy Reverse-Engineering

### Approach
1. Collect observable actions (products, pricing, marketing)
2. Identify patterns across actions
3. Infer strategic priorities
4. Validate against public statements
5. Project future moves

### Strategic Indicators
- Investment priorities (M&A, hiring, R&D)
- Market entry/exit decisions
- Pricing and positioning changes
- Partnership and alliance patterns

---

## Win/Loss Analysis

### Data Collection
- Sales team debriefs
- Customer interviews
- CRM opportunity data
- Competitive mention tracking

### Analysis Dimensions
- Win/loss rates by competitor
- Common win themes
- Loss reason categorization
- Objection patterns

### Output
- Competitive positioning recommendations
- Sales training priorities
- Product development insights

---

## Scenario Planning

### Competitor Move Analysis
1. Identify potential competitor actions
2. Assess probability and timing
3. Model impact on market
4. Develop response options
5. Prepare contingency plans

### Scenario Types
- Pricing war initiation
- New product launch
- Market entry/expansion
- Acquisition activity
- Leadership change

---

## Pattern Recognition

### Behavioral Patterns
- Competitive response tendencies
- Innovation cycles
- Pricing discipline
- Marketing approaches

### Early Warning Signals
- Unusual hiring activity
- Patent filing clusters
- Executive departures
- Partnership announcements
