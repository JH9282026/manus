# Intelligence Deliverable Templates

Standard formats for competitive intelligence outputs.

---

## Sales Battlecard

### Structure
```markdown
# [Competitor] Battlecard

## Quick Facts
- Founded: [Year]
- Employees: [Count]
- Key Products: [List]
- Target Market: [Description]

## Strengths (Watch Out For)
- [Strength 1]: How they'll position it
- [Strength 2]: How they'll position it

## Weaknesses (Attack Here)
- [Weakness 1]: Your counter-positioning
- [Weakness 2]: Your counter-positioning

## Common Objections & Responses
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| [Their claim] | [Your counter] |

## Trap Questions
- "Ask them about [topic]" - Why it matters

## Proof Points
- [Customer win against them]
- [Feature advantage]
```

---

## Executive Brief

### Structure (2-3 pages)
1. **Key Findings**: Top 3-5 insights
2. **Strategic Implications**: What it means
3. **Recommended Actions**: What to do
4. **Monitoring Triggers**: What to watch

## Competitive Dashboard

### Metrics to Track
- Market share estimates
- Product release velocity
- Pricing changes
- Social media sentiment
- Job posting trends
- Website traffic
- Customer review scores

### Update Cadence
- Daily: News alerts
- Weekly: Social and job metrics
- Monthly: Market share and performance
- Quarterly: Strategic assessment
