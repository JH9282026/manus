# Catalog Structure

Guidance on catalog organization, pagination, and navigation.

---

## Catalog Organization

### Organization Approaches

| Approach | Best For | Structure |
|----------|----------|----------|
| By Room | Home furnishings | Living, Bedroom, Dining, etc. |
| By Category | Mixed products | Furniture, Textiles, Lighting |
| By Collection | Themed assortments | Coastal, Modern, Rustic |
| By Price | Budget-conscious buyers | Budget, Mid-range, Premium |
| By Use | Activity-focused | Work from Home, Entertaining |

## Pagination Strategy

### High-Value Positions

| Position | Content | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| Cover | Brand statement + compelling offer | First impression |
| Pages 2-3 | Welcome + what's new | Sets tone |
| Center Spread | Feature story or collection | High attention |
| Back Cover | Final CTA or signature product | Last impression |

### Content Placement

| Section | Position | Rationale |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Hero Products | Front of book | Captures attention |
| New Arrivals | Pages 4-10 | Fresh and exciting |
| Core Categories | Middle | Depth of offering |
| Clearance/Sale | Back of book | Secondary priority |
| Ordering Info | Final pages | Reference material |

## Page Layouts

### Product Density Options

| Layout Type | Products/Page | Best For |
|-------------|---------------|----------|
| Feature | 1-2 | Hero products, lifestyle |
| Standard | 3-6 | Primary assortment |
| Grid | 6-12 | Browsing, variety |
| Reference | 12+ | Accessories, components |

## Navigation Elements

### Page Headers

- Category identification
- Consistent placement
- Clear typography

### Page Numbers

- Clear, consistent placement
- Referenced in TOC
- Index references

### Cross-References

| Type | Format |
|------|--------|
| See Also | "See also: Matching Accessories, p. 48" |
| Related Items | "Coordinates with our Coastal Collection, p. 12" |
| More Options | "More colors available at company.com" |

## Seasonal Themes

### Seasonal Framing

| Season | Theme | Copy Approach |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Spring | Renewal, freshness | "Fresh Start," "Refresh Your Space" |
| Summer | Outdoor, entertaining | "Summer Living," "Al Fresco" |
| Fall | Coziness, warmth | "Cozy Up," "Harvest Home" |
| Winter/Holiday | Gifting, celebration | "The Art of Giving," "Gather" |

### Seasonal Copy Example

```
**FRESH START**

Shake off winter and invite spring inside. Our new arrivals in soft pastels and natural textures bring the optimism of the season to every room. From breezy linens to garden-ready furniture, everything you need to refresh your home.
```

## Catalog Length Guidelines

| Type | Pages | Products | Use Case |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Mini/Magalog | 8-16 | 20-50 | Focused promotion |
| Standard | 32-64 | 100-300 | Seasonal catalog |
| Comprehensive | 100+ | 500+ | Full assortment |

### Length Factors

- Product count
- Product complexity
- Category depth
- Editorial content needs
- Budget constraints
- Distribution method
