# Instagram Creative Specifications

## Feed Ads
- Image: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1) or 1080 x 1350 px (4:5)
- Video: 1080 x 1080 px, up to 60 seconds
- Carousel: 1080 x 1080 px per card, 2-10 cards

## Stories Ads
- Image/Video: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- Duration: Up to 60 seconds
- Safe zone: Center 1080 x 1420 px

## Reels Ads
- Video: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- Duration: 9-30 seconds (15 seconds optimal)
- Sound required
- Looping recommended

## Explore Ads
- Same as Feed specs
- Appears in Explore grid
