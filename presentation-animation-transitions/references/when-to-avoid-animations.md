## When to Avoid Animations



### Inappropriate Contexts

**Legal/Compliance Presentations**: Professionalism paramount, no distractions
**Crisis Communications**: Seriousness undermined by effects
**Dense Data Reviews**: Animations slow information access
**Time-Constrained Settings**: Animations consume precious seconds
**Technical Documentation**: Efficiency over style
**Print-Destined Content**: Animations don't translate
**Conservative Audiences**: Some industries/cultures expect restraint
**Accessibility-Critical Settings**: Motion sensitivity concerns



### The "Would This Work as a Cut?" Test

Before adding any transition or animation, ask: "Would an instant cut/appear work just as well here?" If yes, the animation may be unnecessary. Only add motion when it genuinely enhances understanding or engagement.
