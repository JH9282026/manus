# Portrait Photography

Create compelling portrait photography through lighting mastery, posing direction, and technical excellence.

## Overview

Portrait photography captures the personality, mood, and character of subjects through controlled lighting, thoughtful posing, and technical skill. This skill covers lighting patterns, posing techniques, camera settings, and creating professional portraits across various styles and environments.

## Lighting Patterns

| Pattern | Description | Effect | Setup |
|---------|-------------|--------|-------|
| **Rembrandt** | Triangle of light under eye on shadow side | Dramatic, artistic | Light at 45\u00b0, slightly above, creates nose shadow connecting to cheek |
| **Loop** | Small nose shadow pointing down | Flattering, versatile | Light 30-45\u00b0 from subject, slightly above eye level |
| **Split** | Half face lit, half in shadow | Dramatic, moody | Light 90\u00b0 to side, divides face |
| **Butterfly** | Butterfly shadow under nose | Glamorous, beauty | Light directly in front, above subject |
| **Broad** | Side of face toward camera is lit | Widens face | Subject turned away from light |
| **Short** | Side of face away from camera is lit | Narrows face, sculpts | Subject turned toward light |

## Lighting Quality

**Hard Light:**
- Small, direct light source
- Defined shadows, high contrast
- Emphasizes texture and features
- Dramatic, edgy look

**Soft Light:**
- Large, diffused light source
- Gentle shadows, low contrast
- Flattering, smooths skin
- Natural, approachable look

## Posing Techniques

**Body:**
- Angle shoulders (not square to camera)
- Weight on back foot
- Create triangles with arms
- Avoid symmetry

**Face:**
- Slight head tilt
- Jaw forward and down (define jawline)
- Eyes toward light
- Genuine expression

**Hands:**
- Relaxed, natural position
- Avoid flat palms toward camera
- Create shape and flow
- Interact with environment or clothing

## Camera Settings

**Aperture:**
- f/1.4-f/2.8: Shallow depth of field, background blur
- f/4-f/5.6: Moderate depth, some background detail
- f/8+: Deep depth, environmental context

**Shutter Speed:**
- 1/125s minimum (avoid motion blur)
- 1/200s+ for active subjects

**ISO:**
- 100-400 (natural light)
- 400-1600 (indoor/low light)
- Keep as low as possible for quality

**Focal Length:**
- 50mm: Natural perspective
- 85mm: Flattering compression, popular for portraits
- 135mm: Strong compression, beautiful bokeh

## Natural Light Portraits

**Golden Hour:**
- First/last hour of sunlight
- Warm, soft, flattering light
- Long shadows, dimension

**Overcast:**
- Soft, even light
- No harsh shadows
- Flattering for skin tones

**Window Light:**
- Large, soft source
- Position subject near window
- Use reflector for fill light
