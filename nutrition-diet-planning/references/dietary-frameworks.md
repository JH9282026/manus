# Dietary Frameworks

## Popular Diet Frameworks

### Mediterranean Diet
- **Focus**: Whole grains, fruits, vegetables, olive oil, fish, nuts
- **Evidence**: Strong for cardiovascular health, longevity
- **Macros**: Moderate carb, moderate fat, moderate protein
- **Best for**: General health, heart disease prevention

### Ketogenic Diet
- **Focus**: Very low carb (<50g/day), high fat, moderate protein
- **Evidence**: Effective for short-term weight loss, epilepsy
- **Macros**: 70-75% fat, 20% protein, 5-10% carbs
- **Considerations**: Difficult to sustain, potential nutrient gaps

### Plant-Based/Vegan
- **Focus**: Whole plant foods, no animal products
- **Evidence**: Reduces chronic disease risk, environmental benefits
- **Key nutrients to monitor**: B12, iron, calcium, omega-3, zinc
- **Best for**: Ethical/environmental preferences, chronic disease prevention

### Intermittent Fasting
| Protocol | Eating Window | Fasting Window |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| 16:8 | 8 hours | 16 hours |
| 5:2 | 5 normal days | 2 low-calorie days |
| OMAD | 1 hour | 23 hours |
| Eat-Stop-Eat | 24-hour fast 1-2x/week | — |

### Paleo Diet
- **Focus**: Meat, fish, vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds
- **Excludes**: Grains, legumes, dairy, processed foods
- **Evidence**: Mixed; effective for weight loss, debated long-term
- **Best for**: Whole food emphasis, food sensitivity exploration

## Therapeutic Diets

### Diabetes Management
- Consistent carbohydrate intake per meal
- Low glycemic index foods preferred
- Monitor portions of starchy foods
- Pair carbs with protein and fat
- Limit added sugars and refined carbs

### Heart Health (DASH Diet)
- Sodium: <2,300mg (ideal <1,500mg)
- Increase potassium (fruits, vegetables)
- Limit saturated fat
- Emphasize whole grains, lean protein
- 4-5 servings each fruits and vegetables daily

### Anti-Inflammatory
- Emphasize: fatty fish, berries, leafy greens, turmeric, olive oil
- Limit: refined carbs, processed meat, trans fats, excess sugar
- Evidence-based for autoimmune conditions, chronic pain

### Food Allergies/Intolerances
| Condition | Avoid | Alternatives |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Celiac disease | Gluten (wheat, barley, rye) | Rice, quinoa, corn, GF oats |
| Lactose intolerance | Dairy (or use lactase) | Plant milks, lactose-free dairy |
| Nut allergy | Tree nuts, peanuts | Seeds (sunflower, pumpkin) |

## Sports Nutrition

### Pre-Competition Fueling
| Timing | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| 3-4 days before | Carb loading (8-12 g/kg/day) |
| Night before | Familiar, carb-rich dinner |
| 3-4 hours before | Substantial meal (2-4 g/kg carbs) |
| 1 hour before | Light snack (1 g/kg carbs) |
| 15 min before | Small simple carb if needed |

### Recovery Nutrition
- Within 30-60 minutes post-exercise
- 1.0-1.2 g/kg carbohydrate
- 0.3-0.4 g/kg protein
- Rehydrate: 1.5L per kg lost
- Continue carb-rich meals for 24 hours after intense exercise

### Supplement Evidence

| Supplement | Evidence Level | Benefit |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Creatine | Strong | Strength, power, lean mass |
| Caffeine | Strong | Endurance, focus, power |
| Protein powder | Strong | Convenience for meeting targets |

| Fish oil | Moderate | Anti-inflammatory, recovery |
