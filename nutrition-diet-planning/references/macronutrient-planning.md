# Macronutrient Planning

## Protein Planning

### Protein Requirements by Goal
| Population | Protein (g/kg/day) |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Sedentary adult | 0.8-1.0 |
| Recreational exerciser | 1.0-1.4 |
| Endurance athlete | 1.2-1.6 |
| Strength/power athlete | 1.6-2.2 |
| Weight loss (caloric deficit) | 1.8-2.4 |
| Elderly (sarcopenia prevention) | 1.2-1.6 |

### Protein Distribution
- Distribute evenly across 3-5 meals
- 20-40g per meal for optimal muscle protein synthesis
- Include protein at every meal
- Post-workout: 20-40g within 2 hours

### Complete Protein Sources
| Source | Protein per 100g | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| Chicken breast | 31g | Lean, versatile |
| Salmon | 25g | Omega-3 rich |
| Greek yogurt | 10g | Casein protein |
| Eggs | 13g | Complete amino acids |
| Tofu | 8g | Plant-based complete |
| Lentils | 9g | Fiber-rich, combine with grains |

## Carbohydrate Planning

### Carbohydrate Quality
| Type | Examples | Use |
|------|---------|-----|
| Complex/whole | Oats, brown rice, sweet potato | Base of carb intake |
| Fibrous | Vegetables, legumes | Every meal |
| Fruit | Berries, banana, apple | 2-4 servings daily |
| Simple/refined | White bread, sugar | Limit; peri-workout only |

### Carbohydrate Timing
| Timing | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| Pre-workout (1-3hrs) | Moderate carbs, low fat |
| During workout (>60min) | 30-60g/hr simple carbs |
| Post-workout (0-2hrs) | High glycemic for replenishment |
| Rest of day | Complex carbs, fiber-rich |

## Fat Planning

### Fat Type Guidelines
| Fat Type | Target | Sources |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Monounsaturated | Primary fat source | Olive oil, avocado, nuts |
| Polyunsaturated (omega-3) | 250-500mg EPA+DHA daily | Fish, flaxseed, walnuts |
| Saturated | <10% of calories | Limit red meat, dairy fat |
| Trans fat | Avoid completely | Processed foods, hydrogenated oils |
