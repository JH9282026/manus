# Nutrition & Diet Planning

Create personalized nutrition plans across dietary frameworks for health, performance, and body composition goals.

## Overview

Design evidence-based nutrition programs integrating macronutrient optimization, micronutrient sufficiency, meal timing, and lifestyle-specific considerations. Support goals from weight management to athletic performance to therapeutic diets.

## Calorie Estimation

### Basal Metabolic Rate (Mifflin-St Jeor)
- **Males**: BMR = 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) - 5 × age - 5
- **Females**: BMR = 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) - 5 × age - 161

### Activity Multipliers (TDEE)
| Level | Multiplier | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Sedentary | 1.2 | Desk job, minimal exercise |
| Lightly active | 1.375 | Light exercise 1-3 days/week |
| Moderately active | 1.55 | Moderate exercise 3-5 days/week |
| Very active | 1.725 | Hard exercise 6-7 days/week |
| Extremely active | 1.9 | Athlete, physical job + training |

## Macronutrient Guidelines

| Goal | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|------|---------|-------|-----|
| Weight loss | 1.6-2.2 g/kg | 2-4 g/kg | 0.8-1.2 g/kg |
| Maintenance | 1.2-1.6 g/kg | 3-5 g/kg | 0.8-1.2 g/kg |
| Muscle gain | 1.6-2.2 g/kg | 4-7 g/kg | 0.8-1.5 g/kg |
| Endurance athlete | 1.2-1.6 g/kg | 6-10 g/kg | 1.0-1.5 g/kg |

## Essential Micronutrients

| Nutrient | Key Sources | Daily Target |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| Vitamin D | Sunlight, fish, fortified foods | 600-2000 IU |
| Iron | Red meat, spinach, legumes | 8-18 mg |
| Calcium | Dairy, leafy greens, fortified | 1000-1200 mg |
| Omega-3 | Fatty fish, flaxseed, walnuts | 250-500 mg EPA+DHA |
| Magnesium | Nuts, seeds, whole grains | 310-420 mg |
| B12 | Animal products, fortified foods | 2.4 mcg |
