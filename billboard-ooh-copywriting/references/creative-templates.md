# OOH Creative Templates

Proven copy structures and creative frameworks.

---

## Billboard Copy Templates

### Standard Billboard

```
[VISUAL: Single striking image]
[HEADLINE: 3-7 words that complete visual meaning]
[LOGO: Brand identifier + optional tagline]
```

### Product Launch

```
[VISUAL: Product hero shot]
[HEADLINE: New + benefit]
Example: "New. Wireless. Unstoppable."
[LOGO + availability: "Now at [Retailer]"]
```

### Location-Based

```
[VISUAL: Appetizing product shot]
[HEADLINE: Direction + benefit]
Example: "Exit 14B for the best coffee."
[LOGO + distance indicator]
```

## Headline Formulas

### The Imperative
Direct commands that tell viewers what to do.
- "Think small" (VW)
- "Just do it" (Nike)
- "Eat fresh" (Subway)

### The Question
Questions that demand an answer.
- "Got milk?"
- "Where's the beef?"
- "What happens in Vegas...?"

### The Statement
Bold declarations that stake a position.
- "Different is better"
- "The ultimate driving machine"
- "Because you're worth it"

### The Twist
Sets up expectation, then subverts.
- "We're #2. We try harder." (Avis)

## Visual-Verbal Relationships

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Complementary | Word and image say same thing | Coffee cup + "Wake Up" |
| Additive | Word adds info image can't | Burger + "Now $5" |
| Completion | Each needs the other | Car on road + "Escape" |
| Contradiction | Creates intrigue | Luxury car + "Affordable" |
| Transformation | Word changes image meaning | Commuters + "Future leaders" |

## Transit Interior Card

```
[HEADLINE: Problem statement or attention-getter]
[BODY: 2-4 short sentences explaining solution]
[CTA: Phone, website, or location]
[LOGO + tagline]
```

### Example:

> **Injured at work?**
> You may be entitled to compensation.
> Free consultation. No fee unless you win.
> Call 555-HELP or visit HelpForWorkers.com

## Digital Billboard Sequence

### Build Sequence (3 frames)
1. Visual setup
2. Add headline
3. Add punchline/CTA/logo

### Narrative Sequence
1. Problem
2. Tension
3. Solution (your brand)

### Teaser Campaign
- Week 1: Mysterious image only
- Week 2: Add partial headline
- Week 3: Full reveal
