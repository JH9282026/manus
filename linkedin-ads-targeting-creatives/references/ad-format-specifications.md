# LinkedIn Ad Format Specifications

This comprehensive reference guide provides detailed technical specifications for all LinkedIn advertising creative formats, ensuring optimal ad delivery and performance across desktop and mobile devices.

## Sponsored Content Formats

Sponsored Content ads appear directly in the LinkedIn feed, blending with organic posts to engage professionals.

### Single Image Ads

Single Image Ads feature a static image, introductory text, a headline, and a call-to-action (CTA).

**Image Specifications**
- **File Type**: JPG, PNG, or GIF (non-animated)
- **File Size**: Up to 5 MB
- **Image Ratios**:
  - **Horizontal**: 1.91:1 (recommended 1200 x 628 pixels)
  - **Square**: 1:1 (recommended 1200 x 1200 pixels)
  - **Vertical**: 1:1.91 (mobile only, recommended 628 x 1200 pixels) or 4:5 (recommended 720 x 900 pixels)
- **Maximum Dimensions**: 7680 x 4320 pixels (horizontal), 4320 x 4320 pixels (square), 1254 x 2400 pixels (vertical)
- **Minimum Dimensions**: 640 x 360 pixels (horizontal), 360 x 360 pixels (square), 360 x 640 pixels (vertical)

**Text Specifications**
- **Ad Name** (optional): 255 characters
- **Headline**: 70 characters recommended (200 characters maximum)
- **Introductory Text**: 150 characters recommended (3000 characters maximum, but truncates at ~150 on desktop)
- **Description** (LinkedIn Audience Network only): 70 characters

**CTA Options**
- Apply
- Download
- View Quote
- Learn More
- Sign Up
- Subscribe
- Register
- Join
- Attend
- Request Demo

**URL Requirements**
- Required: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 2000 characters

**Best Practices**
- Use bright colors to stand out against LinkedIn's neutral background
- Larger visuals can lead to 38% higher CTR
- Keep headline under 70 characters to avoid truncation
- Test different aspect ratios for optimal performance

### Video Ads

Video Ads use motion, sound, and narrative to tell a richer story.

**Video Specifications**
- **File Type**: MP4 (or MOV)
- **Audio Format**: AAC or MPEG4
- **File Size**: 75 KB minimum, 500 MB maximum (200 MB max if using Campaign Manager)
- **Duration**: 3 seconds to 30 minutes (15-30 seconds recommended)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 frames per second recommended

**Aspect Ratios and Dimensions**
- **Vertical**: 
  - 4:5 (360 x 450 to 1080 x 1350 pixels)
  - 9:16 (360 x 640 to 1080 x 1920 pixels)
- **Landscape**: 16:9 (640 x 360 to 1920 x 1080 pixels)
- **Square**: 1:1 (360 x 360 to 1920 x 1920 pixels)

**Text Specifications**
- **Ad Name** (optional): 255 characters
- **Headline**: 70 characters recommended (200 maximum)
- **Introductory Text**: 150 characters recommended (600 maximum)

**Thumbnail Specifications**
- **File Type**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Maximum 2 MB
- **Dimensions**: Must match video aspect ratio

**URL Requirements**
- Required: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 2000 characters

**Best Practices**
- Keep videos 15-30 seconds for highest completion rates
- Include captions (79% of LinkedIn videos watched muted)
- Design for silent viewing with strong visuals
- Use custom thumbnail for better engagement
- Test vertical formats for mobile optimization

### Carousel Ads

Carousel Ads feature a swipeable series of 2-10 image cards.

**Card Specifications**
- **Number of Cards**: 2-10
- **File Type**: JPG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (Square only)
- **Recommended Resolution**: 1080 x 1080 pixels
- **Maximum Dimensions**: 4320 x 4320 pixels
- **File Size**: Maximum 10 MB per card

**Text Specifications**
- **Ad Name** (optional): 255 characters
- **Introductory Text**: 255 characters
- **Card Headline**: 45 characters (30 characters for Lead Gen Form CTA)

**URL Requirements**
- Required per card: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 2000 characters per card
- Each card can have its own unique URL

**Best Practices**
- Use 3-5 cards for optimal engagement
- Tell a sequential story across cards
- Each card should work independently
- Use consistent visual style across cards
- Test different card orders

### Document Ads

Document Ads allow sharing of content like eBooks or guides directly in the feed.

**Document Specifications**
- **File Types**: PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX
- **File Size**: Up to 100 MB
- **Page Limits**: Up to 300 pages or 1 million words
- **Aspect Ratios**: Vertical, Horizontal, Square (auto-detected)

**Text Specifications**
- **Ad Name** (optional): 255 characters
- **Headline**: 70 characters recommended
- **Introductory Text**: 150 characters recommended

**URL Requirements**
- No specific URL requirements
- Secure hyperlinks within document recommended

**Best Practices**
- Aim for under 10 pages for in-feed browsing
- Design for mobile legibility
- Use high-quality, professional formatting
- Include clear value proposition on first page
- Can be ungated or gated with Lead Gen Form

### Event Ads

Event Ads promote upcoming LinkedIn or third-party events.

**Image Specifications**
- **Image Ratio**: 4:1 (pulled from Event page banner)
- **Source**: Automatically pulled from LinkedIn Event page

**Text Specifications**
- **Event Name** (optional): 255 characters
- **Introductory Text**: 600 characters

**URL Requirements**
- Required: Must link to LinkedIn Event page
- http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 2000 characters

**Best Practices**
- Optimize Event page banner for 4:1 ratio
- Include clear event details in intro text
- Highlight key speakers or topics
- Use urgency in messaging (limited seats, early bird)

### Thought Leader Ads

Thought Leader Ads promote content from executives or subject matter experts.

**Source Requirements**
- Existing organic post from employee or thought leader
- Must be from a LinkedIn member profile (not Company Page)

**Eligible Formats**
- Single Image
- Video
- Event
- Article/Newsletter

**Text and Creative**
- Intro text, headline, and URLs pulled from original post
- Non-editable (must edit original post)
- Labeled "Promoted by [Your Company]"

**CTA Options**
- None (unless Event promotion, which includes RSVP)
- Links in original post remain clickable

**Best Practices**
- Choose high-performing organic posts
- Ensure thought leader profile is complete and professional
- Coordinate with thought leader on content strategy
- Monitor engagement on original post

### Article and Newsletter Ads

Article and Newsletter Ads sponsor long-form content directly in the feed.

**Source Requirements**
- Published LinkedIn article or newsletter post
- Must be native LinkedIn content

**Creative Specifications**
- Image/Intro Text/Headline pulled from original content
- Non-editable (must edit original article/newsletter)

**Destination**
- Opens LinkedIn article or newsletter directly
- Keeps users on LinkedIn platform

**CTA Options**
- "Unlock Article" (Lead Gen campaigns only)
- No CTA button for other objectives

**Best Practices**
- Optimize article headline and hero image
- Use compelling intro paragraph
- Promote high-value, evergreen content
- Track article engagement metrics

### Connected TV (CTV) Ads

CTV Ads extend brand messages to premium streaming environments.

**Video Specifications**
- **File Type**: MP4
- **Dimensions**: 1920 x 1080 pixels or 1280 x 720 pixels recommended
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **File Size**: Maximum 500 MB
- **Duration**: 6-60 seconds (recommended: 6, 15, 30, 45, or 60 seconds)

**Audio Specifications**
- **Channels**: 2-channel
- **Loudness**: -23 integrated LUFS
- **Codec**: PCM (16 or 24 bit, preferred) or AAC
- **Bitrate**: 192 Kbps minimum
- **Sample Rate**: 48 kHz

**Technical Specifications**
- **Frame Rate**: 23.98, 24, 25, 29.97, or 30 fps
- **Codec**: H.264

**Best Practices**
- Design for large screen viewing
- Ensure audio quality is broadcast-ready
- Use standard TV commercial durations
- Test on actual CTV devices

### Job Ads

Job Ads promote open roles to job seekers.

**Specifications**
- **Source**: Uses existing LinkedIn job post
- **Introductory Text**: Up to 150 characters

**Best Practices**
- Optimize job post title and description
- Include key benefits and requirements
- Use compelling company branding
- Target relevant professional audiences

## Sponsored Messaging Formats

Sponsored Messaging engages audiences directly in LinkedIn Messaging.

### Conversation Ads

Conversation Ads allow prospects to explore content paths tailored to their interests.

**Text Specifications**
- **Ad Name** (optional): 255 characters
- **Message Text**: Up to 8,000 characters
- **Custom Footer**: Up to 20,000 characters
- **CTA Buttons**: Up to 25 characters each (up to 5 CTAs per message)
- **Total Buttons**: Up to 50 buttons in entire conversation tree

**Banner Specifications** (optional, desktop only)
- **File Type**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 300 x 250 pixels
- **File Size**: Maximum 2 MB

**Sender Image**
- LinkedIn profile image of the sender
- Automatically pulled from sender profile

**URL Requirements**
- Required: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 2000 characters

**Best Practices**
- Create choose-your-own-path experiences
- Keep initial message concise
- Offer 2-3 clear path options
- Use personalization tokens
- Test conversation flows before launch

### Message Ads

Message Ads send a direct, one-to-one message to a targeted prospect's LinkedIn inbox.

**Text Specifications**
- **Ad Name** (optional): 50 characters
- **Message Subject**: 60 characters
- **Message Text**: Up to 1,500 characters
- **Custom Footer**: Up to 2,500 characters
- **CTA Button**: 20 characters

**Banner Specifications** (optional, desktop only)
- **File Type**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 300 x 250 pixels
- **File Size**: Maximum 2 MB

**Sender Image**
- LinkedIn profile image of the sender
- Automatically pulled from sender profile

**URL Requirements**
- Required: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 1024 characters

**Best Practices**
- Launch on Tuesday or Wednesday for best performance
- Personalize subject line and message
- Single, clear call-to-action
- Keep message concise and valuable
- Test different sender profiles

## Lead Gen Forms

Lead Gen Forms capture leads instantly using pre-filled data from LinkedIn profiles.

**Form Specifications**
- **Form Fields**: Up to 12 fields
- **Custom Questions**: Up to 3

**Text Specifications**
- **Form Name**: 256 characters
- **Landing Page URL**: 2000 characters
- **Offer Headline**: 60 characters
- **Offer Detail** (optional): 160 characters
- **Privacy Policy**: 2000 characters
- **CTA Button**: 20 characters
- **Confirmation Message**: 300 characters

**Available Fields**
- First Name
- Last Name
- Email Address
- Company Name
- Job Title
- Seniority
- Phone Number
- Country
- State/Province
- City
- Postal Code
- Work Email

**Best Practices**
- Request only essential information
- Use 3-5 fields for optimal conversion
- Include clear privacy policy
- Customize confirmation message
- Set up lead sync with CRM

## Text and Dynamic Ads

These ads run in the LinkedIn right rail on desktop.

### Text Ads

Text Ads feature a compelling headline, brief description, and optional image.

**Text Specifications**
- **Headline**: 25 characters
- **Description**: 75 characters

**Logo Specifications**
- **File Type**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 100 x 100 pixels
- **File Size**: Maximum 2 MB

**URL Requirements**
- Required: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 2000 characters

**Best Practices**
- Use concise, action-oriented language
- Include company logo for brand recognition
- Test multiple variations
- Cost-effective for website traffic

### Spotlight Ads

Spotlight Ads drive attention to offers with personalized ads featuring viewer's profile info.

**Text Specifications**
- **Headline**: 50 characters
- **Description**: 70 characters
- **Company Name**: 25 characters
- **CTA Button**: 18 characters

**Image Specifications**
- **Company Logo**: 100 x 100 pixels, JPG or PNG, maximum 2 MB
- **Background Image** (optional): 300 x 250 pixels, JPG or PNG, maximum 2 MB

**URL Requirements**
- Required: http:// or https:// prefix
- Maximum length: 500 characters

**Best Practices**
- Leverage personalization for engagement
- Use compelling background image
- Clear, benefit-focused headline
- Test different CTA variations

### Follower Ads

Follower Ads grow LinkedIn Page audience by inviting professionals to follow the brand.

**Text Specifications**
- **Headline**: 50 characters
- **Description**: 70 characters
- **Company Name**: 25 characters

**Image Specifications**
- **Company Logo**: 100 x 100 pixels, JPG or PNG, maximum 2 MB

**CTA Options**
- Visit careers
- Visit company
- Visit jobs
- Visit life
- "Follow" (for non-followers)

**Best Practices**
- Highlight company culture and values
- Use for employer branding
- Target relevant professional audiences
- Monitor follower growth metrics

## Ad Review and Approval Guidelines

### Content Compliance

**Required Standards**
- Comply with LinkedIn Advertising Policies
- Comply with User Agreement
- Meet Professional Community Policies

**Text and Grammar**
- Use standard text, spelling, and grammar
- Avoid excessive capitalization (except valid acronyms/names)
- Limit emoji usage
- No contact information in ad creative

**Prohibited Elements**
- Pop-ups or pop-unders
- Animated GIFs
- Unrelated hashtags
- Misleading claims

**Brand Affiliation**
- Do not use "LinkedIn" in ad content
- Do not imply affiliation with or endorsement by LinkedIn
- Exception: "find me on LinkedIn" is allowed

**Truthfulness**
- All claims must be truthful
- Pricing and offers must match landing page
- Discounts must be accurate

**Language Consistency**
- Ad creative language should match landing page
- Match audience language selected in ad set settings

**Landing Page Requirements**
- Working links required
- Functioning back button
- Consistent display and landing page URLs
- Clear billing practices where applicable
- Privacy policy where applicable

### Review Process

**Submission**
- Ads automatically submitted upon launch or editing
- Review typically completed within 24 hours

**Best Practices**
- Create ad sets at least 48 hours in advance
- Allow time for potential edits
- Review all content before submission
- Ensure compliance with all policies

## Technical Considerations

### File Optimization

**Images**
- Compress images without quality loss
- Use appropriate file format (JPG for photos, PNG for graphics)
- Optimize for fast loading
- Test on multiple devices

**Videos**
- Use H.264 codec for compatibility
- Optimize bitrate for quality and file size
- Include audio even if designing for silent viewing
- Test playback on multiple devices

### Mobile Optimization

**Design Considerations**
- Majority of LinkedIn users access via mobile
- Test all formats on mobile devices
- Use mobile-friendly aspect ratios (1:1, 4:5, 9:16)
- Ensure text is readable on small screens
- Optimize file sizes for mobile data

### Accessibility

**Best Practices**
- Include captions for video content
- Use alt text for images (where supported)
- Ensure sufficient color contrast
- Use readable fonts and sizes
- Test with accessibility tools

## Conclusion

Adhering to LinkedIn's ad format specifications ensures optimal ad delivery, performance, and user experience across all devices and placements. By following these technical requirements and best practices, advertisers can create professional, engaging ads that resonate with LinkedIn's professional audience and achieve campaign objectives. Regular testing and optimization of different formats and specifications will help identify the most effective combinations for specific campaign goals.
