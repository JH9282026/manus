# Customer Service Strategy & Operations

Design, implement, and optimize customer service operations across all channels for exceptional experience delivery and cost efficiency.

## Overview

Provide comprehensive service design, operational management, workforce optimization, quality assurance, and performance analytics frameworks. Enable organizations to deliver consistent, high-quality service while managing costs and scaling with business growth.

## Service Strategy Framework

### Channel Strategy Selection

| Channel | Best For | Target Response Time | Cost per Contact |
|---------|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Self-service/FAQ | Simple, repetitive inquiries | Instant | $0.10 |
| Chatbot/AI | Routine questions, triage | <30 seconds | $0.50-1.00 |
| Live chat | Moderate complexity, multitasking | <60 seconds | $5-8 |
| Email/ticket | Complex, non-urgent issues | <4 hours | $5-10 |
| Phone | High complexity, emotional issues | <60 seconds wait | $8-15 |
| Social media | Public visibility, brand issues | <1 hour | $6-12 |
| Video | Technical troubleshooting | Scheduled | $15-25 |

### Tiered Support Model

| Tier | Scope | Skills Required | Resolution Target |
|------|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| Tier 0 | Self-service, knowledge base | None (automated) | 60% of all inquiries |
| Tier 1 | Standard troubleshooting, FAQ | Product knowledge, soft skills | 70-80% of contacts |
| Tier 2 | Complex issues, escalations | Deep product/technical expertise | 90% of escalations |
| Tier 3 | Engineering/specialist | Domain expertise, development | 100% (final tier) |

## Operational Metrics Dashboard

### Key Performance Indicators

| Metric | Definition | Target | Warning |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| First Contact Resolution (FCR) | % resolved on first contact | >75% | <65% |
| Average Handle Time (AHT) | Avg time per interaction | Channel-specific | >120% of target |
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | Post-interaction satisfaction | >85% | <75% |
| Service Level | % answered within target time | >80% in 20 sec (phone) | <70% |
| Agent Utilization | % of time handling contacts | 75-85% | >90% (burnout risk) |
| Abandonment Rate | % who hang up/leave queue | <5% | >8% |
| Transfer Rate | % transferred to another agent | <15% | >25% |
| Quality Score | QA evaluation average | >85% | <75% |

## Workforce Management Essentials

### Forecasting Contact Volume

1. **Gather historical data** — Minimum 12 months of contact volume by interval
2. **Identify patterns** — Day-of-week, time-of-day, seasonal trends
3. **Apply growth factors** — Business growth, product launches, marketing campaigns
4. **Calculate staffing needs** — Use Erlang C formula for phone, adjusted models for async
5. **Build schedules** — Match staffing to forecasted demand curves
6. **Monitor and adjust** — Real-time adherence monitoring and intraday management
