# Lifecycle Marketing

Engage customers at every stage of their journey to maximize lifetime value.

## Overview

Lifecycle marketing involves creating targeted strategies for each stage of the customer journey, from initial awareness through advocacy, to build lasting relationships and maximize customer lifetime value.

## Customer Lifecycle Stages

### Five Core Stages

| Stage | Goal | Key Activities | Metrics |
|-------|------|---------------|---------|
| **Awareness** | Attract potential customers | SEO, ads, content, social, PR | Traffic, reach, impressions |
| **Consideration** | Build interest and trust | Webinars, case studies, comparisons | Engagement, leads, MQLs |
| **Conversion** | Drive purchase decision | Trials, demos, offers, testimonials | Conversion rate, CAC, sales |
| **Retention** | Keep customers engaged | Onboarding, support, education | Churn rate, NRR, usage |
| **Advocacy** | Turn customers into promoters | Referrals, reviews, community | NPS, referral rate, reviews |

## Awareness Stage

### Objectives
- Build brand awareness
- Attract target audience
- Generate initial interest
- Establish credibility

### Tactics
- SEO-optimized content marketing
- Paid advertising (search, social, display)
- Social media presence
- PR and media coverage
- Influencer partnerships
- Brand awareness campaigns

### Content Types
- Blog posts and articles
- Infographics and visual content
- Social media posts
- Videos and podcasts
- Industry reports
- Educational resources

## Consideration Stage

### Objectives
- Educate prospects
- Build trust and authority
- Differentiate from competitors
- Capture leads

### Tactics
- Gated content (whitepapers, ebooks)
- Webinars and virtual events
- Email nurture sequences
- Retargeting campaigns
- Product comparison content
- Case studies and testimonials

### Content Types
- Detailed guides and ebooks
- Comparison charts
- Product demos and videos
- Customer success stories
- Expert webinars
- ROI calculators

## Conversion Stage

### Objectives
- Remove purchase barriers
- Create urgency
- Provide social proof
- Facilitate decision-making

### Tactics
- Free trials or demos
- Limited-time offers
- Cart abandonment recovery
- Personalized recommendations
- Live chat support
- Money-back guarantees

### Optimization Strategies
- Streamline checkout process
- Reduce form fields
- Add trust signals
- Offer multiple payment options
- Provide clear pricing
- Use urgency and scarcity

## Retention Stage

### Objectives
- Onboard successfully
- Drive product adoption
- Prevent churn
- Increase engagement

### Tactics
- Welcome and onboarding sequences
- Product education and training
- Regular communication
- Feature adoption campaigns
- Proactive support
- Usage monitoring and intervention

### Onboarding Best Practices
- Set clear expectations
- Provide quick wins
- Offer guided tours
- Send timely tips and resources
- Monitor activation metrics
- Personalize based on use case

### Churn Prevention
- Identify at-risk customers
- Proactive outreach
- Address pain points
- Offer incentives to stay
- Gather feedback
- Improve product based on insights

## Advocacy Stage

### Objectives
- Generate referrals
- Collect reviews and testimonials
- Build community
- Create brand ambassadors

### Tactics
- Referral programs with incentives
- Review request campaigns
- Customer advisory boards
- User-generated content campaigns
- Exclusive VIP programs
- Community building

### Referral Program Elements
- Clear value proposition
- Easy sharing mechanism
- Rewards for both parties
- Tracking and attribution
- Promotion and awareness
- Ongoing optimization

## Lifecycle Campaign Examples

### B2B SaaS Lifecycle

**Awareness**: SEO content, LinkedIn ads, industry reports
**Consideration**: Product comparison guides, webinars, case studies
**Conversion**: Free trial, personalized demos, ROI calculator
**Retention**: Onboarding emails, feature adoption, success check-ins
**Advocacy**: Referral program, review requests, customer stories

### Ecommerce Lifecycle

**Awareness**: Social ads, influencer partnerships, content marketing
**Consideration**: Product reviews, comparison shopping, retargeting
**Conversion**: Abandoned cart emails, limited-time discounts, free shipping
**Retention**: Post-purchase emails, loyalty program, personalized recommendations
**Advocacy**: Referral discounts, review incentives, UGC campaigns

## Lifecycle Metrics

### Acquisition Metrics
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Traffic sources
- Lead generation rate
- Marketing qualified leads (MQLs)

### Activation Metrics
- Activation rate
- Time to first value
- Onboarding completion rate
- Feature adoption rate

### Retention Metrics
- Customer retention rate
- Churn rate
- Net revenue retention (NRR)
- Product usage metrics
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT, NPS)

### Revenue Metrics
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Repeat purchase rate
- Upsell/cross-sell rate

### Advocacy Metrics
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Referral rate
- Review volume and ratings
- Social mentions and shares

- Event attendee nurture

**Consideration to Conversion**:
- Trial/demo follow-up
- Abandoned cart recovery

**Conversion to Retention**:
- Welcome series
- Onboarding sequences
- Product education

**Retention to Advocacy**:
- Milestone celebrations
- Review requests
- Referral invitations

## Customer Journey Mapping

### Mapping Process

1. **Define Personas**: Identify key customer segments
2. **Identify Touchpoints**: Map all interactions across channels
3. **Understand Emotions**: Capture feelings at each stage
4. **Identify Pain Points**: Find friction and obstacles
5. **Optimize Experience**: Improve weak points
6. **Measure and Iterate**: Track metrics and refine

### Journey Map Components
- Customer actions
- Touchpoints and channels
- Emotions and motivations
- Pain points and obstacles
- Opportunities for improvement
- Responsible teams
