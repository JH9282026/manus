# Influencer Marketing

Leverage influencer partnerships to build trust, reach new audiences, and drive conversions.

## Overview

Influencer marketing leverages individuals with dedicated social followings to promote products or services through authentic endorsements and content creation.

## Influencer Tiers

### Classification by Reach

| Tier | Followers | Engagement Rate | Cost | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------------|------|----------|
| **Nano** | 1K-10K | 5-10% | $10-$100/post | Niche audiences, local businesses |
| **Micro** | 10K-100K | 3-7% | $100-$1K/post | Targeted campaigns, authenticity |
| **Macro** | 100K-1M | 1-3% | $1K-$10K/post | Brand awareness, reach |
| **Mega** | 1M+ | <1% | $10K-$1M+/post | Mass awareness, celebrity appeal |

### Tier Characteristics

**Nano-Influencers**:
- Highly engaged, tight-knit communities
- Authentic, personal connections
- Cost-effective
- Niche expertise
- High trust factor

**Micro-Influencers**:
- Strong engagement rates
- Specific niche focus
- Affordable and scalable
- Authentic content
- Growing preference (44% of brands in 2024)

**Macro-Influencers**:
- Established authority
- Professional content quality
- Broader reach
- Higher production value
- More expensive

**Mega-Influencers/Celebrities**:
- Maximum reach
- Brand prestige
- High costs
- Lower engagement rates
- Broad, less targeted audience

## Influencer Discovery

### Finding the Right Influencers

**Criteria**:
- **Audience Alignment**: Demographics match target market
- **Engagement Rate**: >3% for micro, >1% for macro
- **Content Quality**: Professional, on-brand aesthetic
- **Authenticity**: Genuine connection with audience
- **Brand Fit**: Values and style alignment
- **Past Collaborations**: Track record with brands

### Discovery Methods

**Manual Research**:
- Platform search and hashtags
- Competitor analysis
- Industry publications
- Social listening
- Customer recommendations

**Influencer Platforms**:
- Upfluence
- AspireIQ (Aspire.io)
- Creator.co
- Modash
- Influencity
- GRIN

**Platform-Specific Tools**:
- Instagram Creator Marketplace
- TikTok Creator Marketplace
- YouTube BrandConnect
- Amazon Influencer Program

### Vetting Process

**Audience Analysis**:
- Follower demographics
- Engagement authenticity
- Comment quality
- Follower growth patterns
- Audience location

**Red Flags**:
- Sudden follower spikes
- Low engagement despite high followers
- Generic, bot-like comments
- Inconsistent posting
- Controversial content
- Fake followers (use detection tools)

**Tools for Vetting**:
- HypeAuditor
- Social Blade
- IG Audit
- Modash

## Campaign Strategy

### Campaign Types

**Sponsored Posts**:
- Single post featuring product
- Instagram feed, Stories, Reels
- TikTok videos
- YouTube videos
- Blog posts

**Product Reviews**:
- Honest product evaluation
- Unboxing videos
- Tutorial content
- Before/after demonstrations

**Brand Ambassadorships**:
- Long-term partnerships
- Multiple posts over time
- Exclusive representation
- Deeper brand integration

**Affiliate Partnerships**:
- Commission-based compensation
- Unique discount codes
- Trackable links
- Performance-driven

**Takeovers**:
- Influencer controls brand account
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Live Q&As
- Limited duration (day/week)

**Contests and Giveaways**:
- Influencer hosts giveaway
- Brand provides prizes
- Engagement and reach boost
- Follower growth

**Engagement**:

**Conversion**:

**Content Creation**:

- Repurpose for ads

## Outreach and Negotiation

### Outreach Best Practices

**Initial Contact**:
- Personalize message
- Explain why they're a good fit
- Be clear about expectations
- Provide campaign details
- Professional but friendly tone

**Outreach Channels**:
- Direct message on platform
- Email (if available)
- Influencer platform
- Management agency (for larger influencers)

**Pitch Template**:
```
Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Brand]. We love your content on [specific topic] and think you'd be a great fit for our [product/campaign].

We're looking to [campaign objective] and would love to collaborate with you on [deliverables].

Are you interested in learning more?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Compensation Models

**Payment Types**:
- Flat fee per post
- Commission/affiliate
- Free product (gifting)
- Hybrid (fee + commission)
- Performance bonuses

**Pricing Factors**:
- Follower count
- Engagement rate
- Platform
- Content type (photo vs. video)
- Usage rights
- Exclusivity
- Production complexity

**Negotiation Tips**:
- Research market rates
- Start with budget range
- Be flexible on deliverables
- Offer long-term partnerships
- Provide creative freedom
- Clarify usage rights upfront

### Contracts and Agreements

**Key Terms**:
- Deliverables (number, type, timing)
- Compensation and payment terms
- Content approval process
- Usage rights and duration
- Exclusivity clauses
- FTC disclosure requirements
- Performance expectations
- Termination conditions

## Content and Creative

### Creative Brief

**Include**:
- Campaign objectives
- Key messages and talking points
- Product information
- Brand guidelines
- Hashtags and mentions
- Do's and don'ts
- Deadlines
- Approval process

**Balance**:
- Provide guidance, not scripts
- Allow creative freedom
- Trust influencer's voice
- Maintain authenticity
- Ensure brand safety

### Content Types by Platform

**Instagram**:
- Feed posts (photos, carousels)
- Stories (24-hour, swipe-ups)
- Reels (short-form video)
- IGTV (long-form video)
- Live streams

**TikTok**:
- Short-form videos (15-60 seconds)
- Trending sounds and challenges
- Duets and stitches
- Live streams

**YouTube**:
- Dedicated videos
- Product integrations
- Sponsored segments
- Shorts

**Blog/Website**:
- Sponsored posts
- Product reviews
- Tutorials and guides
- Roundups and lists

### FTC Compliance

**Disclosure Requirements**:
- Clear and conspicuous
- Before "more" button
- In video (verbal + text)
- Use #ad, #sponsored, #partner
- Not just #collab or #sp

**Platform-Specific**:
- Instagram: "Paid partnership with [Brand]" tag
- YouTube: Check "includes paid promotion"
- TikTok: Toggle "branded content"

**Penalties**:
- FTC fines
- Brand reputation damage
- Platform penalties
- Legal liability

**Conversions**:

- Leads
- Sign-ups
- App downloads
- Promo code usage

### ROI Calculation

**Earned Media Value (EMV)**:
```
EMV = (Impressions × CPM) + (Engagements × Cost per Engagement)
```

**Return on Investment**:
```
ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
```

**Cost Per Acquisition**:
```
CPA = Total Campaign Cost / Number of Conversions
```
