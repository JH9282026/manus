### Title Slide Layout

The opening slide sets presentation tone and brand impression.

**Essential Elements:**
- Presentation title (prominent placement)
- Subtitle or tagline
- Presenter name and title
- Date or event name
- Company logo (prominent)
- Brand imagery or background

**Design Considerations:**
- Strong visual impact
- Clear hierarchy: title dominates
- Adequate space for long titles
- Professional, polished appearance
- Sets expectation for presentation quality

**Variations:**
- Standard corporate title
- Event-specific (conferences)
- Client-branded co-presentations
- Internal vs. external versions
