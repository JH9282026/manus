### Design Best Practices

1. **Start with strategy**: Understand needs before designing
2. **Keep it simple**: Don't over-design; focus on usability
3. **Design for flexibility**: Accommodate various content types
4. **Maintain hierarchy**: Clear visual organization
5. **Ensure readability**: Appropriate contrast and sizing
6. **Test extensively**: Real content, real users, real scenarios
7. **Plan for scale**: Consider printing and screen projection
8. **Build in guidance**: Help users make good choices
9. **Create sufficient layouts**: Cover common needs
10. **Document everything**: Make guidelines accessible
