### Template Architecture

**Single Template Approach:**
- One comprehensive template for all needs
- Maximum layout variety
- Simpler distribution and maintenance
- Risk of overwhelming users

**Multiple Template Approach:**
- Specialized templates for different purposes
- Tailored layouts for specific use cases
- Easier user selection
- More complex maintenance

**Hybrid Approach:**
- Core template with essential layouts
- Supplementary templates for specific needs
- Balance of flexibility and specificity
- Recommended for most organizations
