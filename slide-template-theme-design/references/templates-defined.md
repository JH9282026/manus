### Templates Defined

Templates are complete presentation frameworks that include themes plus additional elements:

**Template Components:**
- Themes (all color, font, and effect settings)
- Master slides with predefined layouts
- Placeholder positions and formatting
- Sample content and structure examples
- Custom slide layouts for specific purposes
- Default slide arrangements and organization

Templates provide both visual styling AND structural organization. They tell users not just how slides should look, but how they should be organized and what content goes where.
