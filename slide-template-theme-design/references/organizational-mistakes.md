### Organizational Mistakes

1. **No governance**: Nobody owns templates
2. **Poor distribution**: Hard to find or access
3. **Missing documentation**: Users don't know how to use properly
4. **No training**: Users struggle with templates
5. **Ignoring feedback**: Not improving based on user input
6. **Too many templates**: Overwhelming options
7. **Inconsistent enforcement**: Some use, some don't
8. **Version confusion**: Multiple versions circulating
9. **No maintenance**: Templates drift out of brand compliance
10. **Silos**: Departments create competing templates
