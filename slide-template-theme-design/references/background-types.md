### Background Types

**Solid Colors:**
- Clean, professional appearance
- Maximum content flexibility
- Easy to maintain consistency
- Best for text-heavy slides

**Gradients:**
- Modern, dynamic feel
- Subtle or dramatic options
- Linear, radial, or custom
- Can guide visual flow

**Patterns:**
- Brand personality expression
- Subtle texture addition
- Can use brand elements
- Risk of visual competition

**Images:**
- High visual impact
- Requires careful text treatment
- Full-bleed or partial coverage
- Must maintain readability
