### Needs Assessment

Before designing templates, understand requirements:

**Presentation Types:**
- Sales/pitch decks
- Internal reports
- Training materials
- Executive briefings
- Client deliverables
- Conference presentations
- Webinar slides

**User Analysis:**
- Who creates presentations?
- What's their design skill level?
- What software do they use?
- How much guidance do they need?
- What's their workflow?

**Content Analysis:**
- Common content types (text, data, images)
- Typical slide counts
- Required elements (logos, disclaimers)
- Regulatory/compliance needs
