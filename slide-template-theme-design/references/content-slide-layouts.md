### Content Slide Layouts

**Title + Body Text:**
- Clear title area
- Large content zone for text
- Consistent margins
- Readable text sizing

**Title + Bullets:**
- Title placeholder
- Bulleted list area
- Proper indentation levels
- Spacing between bullets

**Two-Column Layout:**
- Balanced column widths
- Clear visual separation
- Independent content areas
- Flexible for various content

**Title + Image + Text:**
- Image placeholder (sized appropriately)
- Text area adjacent to image
- Clear content relationship
- Multiple image/text ratios (50/50, 60/40, 70/30)
