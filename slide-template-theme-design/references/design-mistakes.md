### Design Mistakes

1. **Over-designing**: Too many elements, decoration over function
2. **Insufficient layouts**: Forcing content into wrong layouts
3. **Poor color choices**: Low contrast, inaccessible combinations
4. **Font overload**: Too many typefaces
5. **Ignoring hierarchy**: Everything looks equally important
6. **Cramped spacing**: Not enough breathing room
7. **Inconsistent elements**: Varying styles across layouts
8. **Weak title slides**: Missing opportunity for impact
9. **Forgotten variations**: No dark mode, no alternatives
10. **Static thinking**: Not considering animation or transitions
