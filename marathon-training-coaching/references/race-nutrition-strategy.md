# Race Day Nutrition & Strategy

## Fueling Guidelines

### Daily Nutrition During Training
| Phase | Carbs (g/kg) | Protein (g/kg) | Fat |
|-------|-------------|---------------|-----|
| Base building | 5-7 | 1.4-1.6 | 20-25% |
| Peak training | 7-10 | 1.6-1.8 | 20-25% |
| Taper | 8-10 | 1.4-1.6 | 20% |
| Race week carb load | 10-12 | 1.2-1.4 | <15% |

### Race Day Fueling
- **Pre-race meal** (3-4 hours before): 1-4 g/kg carbs, low fat/fiber
- **During race**: 30-60g carbs per hour after first 45 minutes
- **Hydration**: 400-800ml per hour (practice in training)
- **Electrolytes**: Sodium 300-600mg per hour

### Fueling Products
| Product Type | Carbs | Best For |
|-------------|-------|----------|
| Energy gel | 20-25g | Quick energy, easy to carry |
| Chews/blocks | 25-30g | Sustained energy, chewing preference |
| Sports drink | 15-20g per 8oz | Hydration + fuel combined |
| Real food (dates, banana) | Varies | Ultra runners, sensitive stomachs |

### Practice Rule
Never try anything new on race day. Test all nutrition in training runs.

## Race Execution Strategy

### Pacing Strategy
- **Negative split** (recommended): Run second half slightly faster
- First 5K: 10-15 seconds per mile SLOWER than goal pace
- Miles 5-20: Settle into goal pace
- Miles 20-26.2: Effort-based, maintain or push

### Common Pacing Mistakes
- Starting too fast (adrenaline-driven)
- Surging on hills
- Racing other runners early
- Ignoring heart rate data
- Not adjusting for weather

### Weather Adjustments
| Temperature | Pace Adjustment |
|------------|----------------|
| 40-55°F (4-13°C) | Ideal — no adjustment |
| 55-65°F (13-18°C) | +1-2% slower |
| 65-75°F (18-24°C) | +3-5% slower |
| 75-85°F (24-29°C) | +5-10% slower |
| >85°F (>29°C) | +10%+ or shift to effort-based |

### Mental Strategies
- Break race into segments (5K blocks)
- Have mantras for difficult miles (20-23)
- Dedicate miles to people
- Focus on process cues (posture, cadence, breathing)
- Use crowd energy at strategic points

## Post-Race Recovery

| Timeframe | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Immediately | Walk, hydrate, light carbs + protein |
| First 24 hours | Compression, elevation, gentle walking |
| Week 1 | No running, light cross-training only |
| Week 2 | Easy 20-30 min runs, 3-4 days |
| Week 3-4 | Gradual return to normal training |
| General rule | 1 easy day per mile raced (26 days) |
