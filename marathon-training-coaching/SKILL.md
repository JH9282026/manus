# Marathon Training & Coaching

Design personalized, evidence-based marathon training programs for runners of all levels.

## Overview

Create structured training plans integrating periodization principles, heart rate and pace zones, nutrition strategies, tapering protocols, and race-day execution plans. Adapt to individual fitness levels, goals, and time constraints.

## Training Zones

| Zone | % Max HR | % Threshold Pace | Purpose |
|------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 1 - Recovery | 60-65% | Easy | Active recovery |
| 2 - Aerobic | 65-75% | Easy-moderate | Base building, fat oxidation |
| 3 - Tempo | 80-88% | Marathon-HM pace | Lactate threshold |
| 4 - Threshold | 88-92% | 10K-HM pace | VO2max stimulus |
| 5 - Interval | 92-100% | 5K pace or faster | Speed, VO2max |

## Periodization Structure (16-20 Week Plan)

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | Key Workouts |
|-------|-------|-------|-------------|
| Base Building | 1-4 | Aerobic foundation | Easy runs, strides |
| Build Phase 1 | 5-8 | Endurance + speed | Long runs, tempo |
| Build Phase 2 | 9-12 | Race-specific fitness | Marathon pace runs |
| Peak | 13-16 | Maximum fitness | Long runs with MP segments |
| Taper | 17-19 | Recovery + sharpening | Reduced volume, maintained intensity |
| Race Week | 20 | Execution | Light jogs, race day |
