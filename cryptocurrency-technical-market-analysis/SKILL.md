# Cryptocurrency Technical & Market Analysis

Evaluate cryptocurrency price action, market structure, and trading opportunities through technical analysis, crypto-native indicators, and macro-contextual integration.

## Overview

Combine traditional chart analysis techniques with specialized tools for cryptocurrency's unique market microstructure—including 24/7 trading, high volatility, and on-chain transparency.

## Key Technical Indicators

### Trend Indicators
| Indicator | Interpretation |
|-----------|----------------|
| 50/200 MA | Golden cross (bullish) / Death cross (bearish) |
| EMA Ribbon | Trend direction and strength |
| ADX | Trend strength (>25 = trending) |

### Momentum Indicators
| Indicator | Levels |
|-----------|--------|
| RSI | >70 overbought, <30 oversold |
| MACD | Signal line crossovers, histogram divergence |
| Stochastic | >80 overbought, <20 oversold |

### Volatility Indicators
| Indicator | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| Bollinger Bands | Squeeze = pending breakout |
| ATR | Position sizing, stop placement |

## Crypto-Specific Indicators

### Bitcoin Heat Map
- Based on 200-week moving average
- Historical cycle floor indicator
- Green = accumulation zone
- Red = distribution zone

### Rainbow Chart
- Logarithmic regression bands
- Blue/green = buy zones
- Orange/red = sell zones

### Fear & Greed Index
| Score | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------|
| 0-25 | Extreme fear (contrarian buy) |
| 25-45 | Fear |
| 45-55 | Neutral |
| 55-75 | Greed |
| 75-100 | Extreme greed (contrarian sell) |
