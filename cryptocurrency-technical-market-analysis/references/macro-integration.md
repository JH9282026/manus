# Macro Integration

Incorporating macroeconomic factors into crypto technical analysis.

---

## Market Correlations

### Current Correlation Regime
| Asset | BTC Correlation | Notes |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| Nasdaq | 0.5-0.8 | Tech stock proxy |
| S&P 500 | 0.4-0.7 | Broad risk |
| Gold | Variable | Sometimes negative |
| DXY (Dollar) | -0.3 to -0.6 | Inverse |

### Correlation Shifts
- Risk-on: Crypto follows risk assets
- Risk-off: Correlation increases
- Crypto-specific events: Correlation decreases

## Economic Calendar Trading

### High-Impact Events
| Event | Frequency | Impact |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| FOMC Decision | 8x/year | Very high |
| CPI Release | Monthly | High |
| Jobs Report | Monthly | High |
| GDP | Quarterly | Medium |

### Trading Approach
**Before Event**
- Reduce position size
- Widen stops
- Avoid new entries

**After Event**
- Wait for volatility settlement
- Trade the reaction, not the expectation
- Monitor correlation behavior

## Regime Identification

### Bull Regime
- Price above 200 MA
- Higher highs, higher lows
- Volume on advances
- Fear & Greed > 50

### Bear Regime
- Price below 200 MA
- Lower highs, lower lows
- Volume on declines
- Fear & Greed < 50

### Accumulation Regime
- Low volatility
- Tight range
- Declining volume
- Mixed sentiment

---

## Risk Management

### Position Sizing
```
Position Size = Account Risk / (Entry - Stop)
Account Risk = Account Size × Risk % (1-2%)
```

### Volatility Adjustment
- High volatility: Smaller positions, wider stops
- Low volatility: Normal positions, tighter stops

### Correlation Risk
- Don't overweight correlated positions
- Monitor portfolio beta to BTC
- Diversify across crypto sectors
