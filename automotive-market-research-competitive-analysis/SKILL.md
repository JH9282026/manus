## Competitive Intelligence Framework

### Analysis Components

1. **Market Position**: Share, growth, segment strength
2. **Strategy**: Differentiation approach, target market
3. **Innovation**: R&D investment, patent activity, partnerships
4. **Financial Health**: Revenue, profitability, investment capacity
5. **Operational Excellence**: Manufacturing, supply chain, quality
