# Environmental & Sustainability Research

Conduct rigorous environmental and sustainability analysis using carbon accounting, life cycle assessment, ESG frameworks, and climate risk methodologies.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for comprehensive sustainability research covering greenhouse gas accounting, environmental impact assessment, ESG data analysis, and climate risk evaluation. Cover standards (GHG Protocol, ISO 14040, TCFD) and practical implementation for corporate sustainability programs.

## Carbon Accounting Framework (GHG Protocol)

| Scope | Coverage | Examples | Data Quality |
|---|---|---|---|
| Scope 1 | Direct emissions | Fuel combustion, company vehicles, process emissions | High (measured) |
| Scope 2 | Purchased energy | Electricity, steam, heating, cooling | High (utility data) |
| Scope 3 | Value chain | Suppliers, transportation, employee commute, product use, disposal | Lower (estimated) |

## Key Sustainability Frameworks

| Framework | Focus | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| GHG Protocol | Carbon accounting | Emission measurement and reporting |
| TCFD | Climate risk disclosure | Financial risk from climate change |
| GRI Standards | Sustainability reporting | Comprehensive ESG disclosure |
| SASB/ISSB | Industry-specific ESG metrics | Material ESG factors by sector |
| CDP | Environmental disclosure | Climate, water, forest questionnaires |
| Science Based Targets (SBTi) | Emission reduction goals | Paris-aligned target setting |
| ISO 14001 | Environmental management | EMS certification |
| ISO 14040/44 | Life Cycle Assessment | Product environmental impact |

## Research Process

1. **Define scope** — Organizational boundaries, time period, metrics
2. **Collect data** — Activity data, emission factors, operational data
3. **Calculate impacts** — Apply methodologies and conversion factors
4. **Benchmark** — Compare against industry peers and standards
5. **Analyze gaps** — Identify improvement opportunities
6. **Report** — Present findings per applicable framework

## Climate Risk Categories (TCFD)

| Risk Type | Category | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Transition | Policy/Legal | Carbon pricing, emission regulations |
| Transition | Technology | Clean energy shift, stranded assets |
| Transition | Market | Changing consumer preferences |
| Transition | Reputation | Stakeholder concerns, greenwashing |
| Physical | Acute | Extreme weather events, floods |
| Physical | Chronic | Sea level rise, temperature shifts |
