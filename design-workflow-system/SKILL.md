# Design Workflow Skills for Manus.AI

## Overview
This comprehensive skill system enables AI agents to create visually stunning, user-centered designs for websites, landing pages, web apps, and mobile applications. The workflow follows an iterative process, continuously refining designs until they achieve **9/10 quality or higher**.

## Philosophy
- **Iterative Excellence**: Designs evolve through systematic review and refinement cycles
- **Research-Driven**: All design decisions are grounded in research and best practices
- **User-Centered**: UX principles guide every visual decision
- **Quality Threshold**: No design ships below 9/10 quality score
