# Video Content Calendar And Planning

Detailed reference content for the Video Marketing Distribution skill.

---

## Video Content Calendar and Planning


### Content Calendar Components

**Strategic Planning** (Quarterly/Annually):
- Campaign calendars
- Major content initiatives
- Seasonal planning
- Resource allocation

**Tactical Planning** (Monthly/Weekly):
- Specific video topics
- Platform assignments
- Production schedules
- Publication dates

**Day-to-Day** (Daily):
- Publishing execution
- Community engagement
- Trend response
- Performance monitoring


### Calendar Template Elements

For each video, document:
- **Title/Topic**: Video subject
- **Type**: Content category (educational, promotional, etc.)
- **Platform(s)**: Where it will be published
- **Target Audience**: Specific segment
- **Goal**: What this video should achieve
- **Status**: Idea → Scripted → Produced → Scheduled → Published
- **Publish Date**: When to go live
- **Owner**: Who's responsible
- **Assets Needed**: Thumbnail, captions, etc.


### Production Scheduling

**Batching Strategy**:
- Batch similar content types
- Batch by location/set
- Batch by talent availability
- Build buffer inventory

**Production Workflow**:
1. Ideation and planning sprint
2. Script writing block
3. Production day(s)
4. Editing window
5. Review and approval
6. Publishing and promotion
