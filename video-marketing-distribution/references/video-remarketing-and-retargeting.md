## Video Remarketing and Retargeting


### Video Viewer Retargeting

Target ads to people who have watched your videos:

**Audience Segments**:
- Viewed any video
- Viewed specific video
- Watched 25%, 50%, 75%, 95% of video
- Engaged (liked, commented, shared)
- Multiple video viewers

**Retargeting Strategies**:
- Show bottom-of-funnel content to video viewers
- Move awareness viewers to consideration content
- Re-engage video viewers who didn't convert
- Cross-sell to existing customers via video


### Building Video Remarketing Funnels

**Stage 1**: Brand awareness video (broad targeting)
**Stage 2**: Product/solution video (retarget Stage 1 viewers)
**Stage 3**: Testimonial/case study (retarget Stage 2 viewers)
**Stage 4**: Conversion video/offer (retarget Stage 3 viewers)


### Platform-Specific Retargeting

**YouTube**: Video engagement audiences
**Facebook/Instagram**: Video custom audiences
**LinkedIn**: Video engagement retargeting
**Twitter**: Video viewer audiences
