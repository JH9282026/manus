## Community Building Through Video


### Video Community Tactics

**Engagement Content**:
- Q&A videos responding to community questions
- Comment highlights and shoutouts
- Community challenge participation
- User content features and reactions

**Community Platforms**:
- YouTube Community tab
- Discord channels
- Facebook Groups
- Membership platforms (Patreon, Memberful)
- Branded community platforms

**Live Community Events**:
- Live Q&As
- Watch parties
- Community showcases
- Behind-the-scenes streams


### Building Community Loyalty

- Consistent content schedule
- Direct community communication
- Exclusive content for community members
- Recognition of active community members
- Community input on content decisions
- Authentic personality and connection
