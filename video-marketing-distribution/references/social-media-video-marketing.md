## Social Media Video Marketing



### Facebook Video Strategy

**Video Types That Work**:
- Native uploads (not YouTube links)
- Square and vertical formats
- Live video (high organic reach)
- Short-form (Facebook Reels)

**Best Practices**:
- First 3 seconds crucial (autoplay environment)
- Design for sound-off viewing (captions essential)
- Optimize for mobile viewing
- Use Facebook Premiere for launches
- Leverage Groups for community video

**Distribution Tactics**:
- Organic page posts
- Facebook ads (video views, conversions)
- Groups strategy
- Live streaming
- Stories and Reels



### Instagram Video Strategy

**Video Formats**:
- Reels (primary focus, maximum reach)
- Stories (ephemeral, engagement)
- Feed videos (declining, but still relevant)
- Instagram Live (real-time connection)
- IGTV (long-form, reduced priority)

**Best Practices**:
- Vertical video priority
- Hook in first second
- Trending audio for Reels
- Aesthetic consistency with brand
- CTAs to profile or link in bio

**Distribution Tactics**:
- Organic Reels for reach
- Stories for engagement
- Paid advertising
- Influencer partnerships
- Collaboration features



### LinkedIn Video Strategy

**Content That Works**:
- Professional insights and expertise
- Industry news and commentary
- Behind-the-scenes professional content
- Event highlights
- Employee advocacy content
- Educational content

**Best Practices**:
- Native uploads strongly preferred
- Professional but authentic tone
- Square format performs well
- Captions essential (office viewing)
- Personal accounts outperform company pages

**Distribution Tactics**:
- Personal account posts
- Company page content
- LinkedIn Live (thought leadership)
- Sponsored content
- Employee amplification programs



### Twitter/X Video Strategy

**Video Characteristics**:
- Short, punchy content
- News and timely content
- Conversational tone
- Quick takes and reactions

**Best Practices**:
- Under 45 seconds optimal
- Hook immediately
- Design for autoplay
- Use native upload
- Engage in conversations around video
