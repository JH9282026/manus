## Video Embeds and Syndication


### Website Embedding Strategy

**Embed Locations**:
- Homepage
- Product pages
- Blog posts
- About/team pages
- Resource libraries
- Help centers

**Technical Considerations**:
- Player choice (YouTube, Vimeo, self-hosted)
- Loading performance
- Mobile responsiveness
- Accessibility features
- Analytics tracking


### Content Syndication

**Syndication Options**:
- Third-party video platforms (YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion)
- Content discovery networks
- Partner website embedding
- Media outlet distribution
- Podcast video distribution

**Syndication Considerations**:
- Canonical content designation
- Attribution and linking
- Exclusivity agreements
- Rights management
- Performance tracking
