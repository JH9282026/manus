## Video Email Marketing


### Why Video in Email Works

- **Increases open rates**: "Video" in subject line boosts opens 19%
- **Increases click-through rates**: Video thumbnails drive 65% more clicks
- **Improves conversion**: Video emails can increase conversions 21%
- **Reduces unsubscribes**: Engaging content keeps subscribers


### Email Video Best Practices

**Technical Considerations**:
- Most email clients don't support embedded video
- Use animated GIF thumbnails linked to video
- Host video on reliable platform
- Test across email clients

**Content Recommendations**:
- Keep videos short (under 2 minutes for email-linked content)
- Place video early in email
- Use compelling thumbnail
- Include clear CTA
- Provide text summary for accessibility

**Types of Video Emails**:
- Welcome series with introduction video
- Product launch announcements
- Personalized sales outreach
- Customer testimonial features
- Educational series
- Event promotions and recaps


### Personalized Video Email

**Use Cases**:
- Sales prospecting
- Customer success check-ins
- Account management
- Customer onboarding
- Special occasions (anniversaries, milestones)

**Tools**: Vidyard, Loom, BombBomb, Hippo Video
