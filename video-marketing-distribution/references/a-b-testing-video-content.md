## A/B Testing Video Content


### Elements to Test

**Pre-Click Elements**:
- Thumbnails
- Titles
- Descriptions
- Posting time

**Content Elements**:
- Opening hooks (first 5 seconds)
- Video length
- CTA placement and language
- Presenters/talent
- Visual style and production value

**Post-Video Elements**:
- End screens
- Cards
- Follow-up content


### Testing Methodology

1. **Hypothesis**: Form clear prediction
2. **Isolation**: Test one variable at a time
3. **Sample Size**: Ensure statistical significance
4. **Duration**: Run long enough for meaningful data
5. **Analysis**: Compare performance on key metrics
6. **Implementation**: Apply learnings to future content


### What to Do with Results

- Document all tests and results
- Share learnings across team
- Update best practices based on findings
- Continue testing—audience preferences evolve
- Scale winning variations
- Kill underperforming approaches
