## Influencer and Partnership Distribution


### Influencer Video Strategies

**Sponsored Content**:
- Influencer creates branded content
- Posted on influencer's channels
- Authentic voice and style
- May include usage rights for brand

**Co-Created Content**:
- Collaborative creation process
- Shared on both brand and influencer channels
- Leverages both audiences

**Affiliate/Ambassador Programs**:
- Ongoing partnerships
- Multiple videos over time
- Authentic, long-term relationships


### Finding the Right Partners

**Audience Alignment**: Does their audience match yours?
**Content Quality**: Does their content meet your standards?
**Engagement Rate**: Are followers actively engaged?
**Brand Fit**: Does their persona align with your brand?
**Authenticity**: Will the partnership feel genuine?


### Partnership Best Practices

- Clear contracts and expectations
- Creative freedom within guidelines
- Proper disclosure compliance
- Usage rights clarity
- Performance metrics and reporting
- Long-term relationship building
