# Video Promotion Tactics

Detailed reference content for the Video Marketing Distribution skill.

---

## Video Promotion Tactics


### Pre-Launch Promotion

- Tease content on social media
- Email subscribers about upcoming content
- Build anticipation in community
- Coordinate with partners/influencers
- Create countdown content


### Launch Promotion

- Publish across all platforms strategically
- Send email announcement
- Share across social channels
- Engage in relevant communities
- Notify partners and collaborators
- Respond to early engagement actively


### Ongoing Promotion

- Repurpose into clips and highlights
- Include in relevant blog posts
- Add to email signatures
- Share in webinars and presentations
- Use in sales conversations
- Feature in newsletters
- Promote older evergreen content


### Amplification Tactics

- **Employee advocacy**: Team shares content
- **Influencer partnerships**: Creator amplification
- **Community seeding**: Share in relevant groups
- **Paid amplification**: Boost top performers
- **Cross-promotion**: Partner brand sharing
- **PR/media outreach**: Earned media coverage
