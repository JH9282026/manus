# Best Practices

Detailed reference content for the Video Marketing Distribution skill.

---

## Best Practices


### Strategic Best Practices

1. **Align video to business goals**: Every video should serve a purpose
2. **Know your audience deeply**: Create for specific people, not everyone
3. **Plan before producing**: Strategy determines execution
4. **Balance quality and quantity**: Find sustainable level
5. **Integrate with broader marketing**: Video supports all channels
6. **Build for long-term**: Compound value over time
7. **Stay agile**: Adapt to platform and audience changes


### Tactical Best Practices

1. **Hook immediately**: First 3 seconds determine success
2. **Design for sound-off**: Captions and visual storytelling
3. **Mobile-first design**: Most consumption is mobile
4. **Optimize for each platform**: Native content performs best
5. **Include clear CTAs**: Tell viewers what to do next
6. **Engage with audience**: Respond to comments and feedback
7. **Repurpose effectively**: One video, multiple formats
8. **Test continuously**: Data-driven improvement


### Team and Process Best Practices

1. **Define clear ownership**: Who's responsible for what
2. **Create templates and standards**: Ensure consistency
3. **Build review processes**: Quality without bottlenecks
4. **Document learnings**: Share knowledge across team
5. **Invest in skills development**: Keep team capabilities growing
6. **Use right tools**: Efficiency through technology
7. **Measure what matters**: Focus on impactful metrics
