# Common Mistakes

Detailed reference content for the Video Marketing Distribution skill.

---

## Common Mistakes


### Strategy Mistakes

1. **No clear objectives**: Creating video without defined goals
2. **Ignoring the funnel**: Same content for all funnel stages
3. **Wrong platform focus**: Investing in platforms where audience isn't
4. **Inconsistent presence**: Sporadic posting and engagement
5. **No distribution plan**: Publish and pray approach
6. **Siloed video strategy**: Video disconnected from overall marketing


### Content Mistakes

1. **Too promotional**: All sales, no value
2. **Inconsistent quality**: Varying standards hurt brand
3. **Too long**: Not matching content to context
4. **Weak openings**: Losing viewers in first seconds
5. **No clear CTA**: Missing opportunity to direct action
6. **Ignoring accessibility**: No captions, poor audio


### Measurement Mistakes

1. **Vanity metrics focus**: Views without business impact
2. **No attribution**: Can't connect video to outcomes
3. **Missing benchmarks**: No baseline for comparison
4. **Short-term thinking**: Not tracking long-term value
5. **Platform silos**: Not aggregating cross-platform performance
