# Video On Landing Pages And Websites

Detailed reference content for the Video Marketing Distribution skill.

---

## Video on Landing Pages and Websites


### Homepage Video Strategy

**Types of Homepage Videos**:
- Brand story/overview
- Explainer videos
- Customer testimonials reel
- Background videos (visual only)

**Best Practices**:
- Keep above-the-fold video concise
- Autoplay only without sound
- Include play controls
- Optimize for fast loading
- Consider user intent


### Product Page Videos

**Content to Include**:
- Product demonstrations
- Feature explanations
- Customer reviews/testimonials
- Use case examples
- Comparison information

**Placement Recommendations**:
- Near product images
- In product image carousel
- Supporting product descriptions
- FAQ sections


### Landing Page Video

**Purpose**: Increase conversion rates

**Best Practices**:
- Place video prominently
- Keep landing page focused
- Video should match page goal
- Include transcript or summary
- Test with and without video

**Video Types for Landing Pages**:
- Explainer videos (complex offerings)
- Testimonials (trust building)
- Demo videos (product understanding)
- VSLs (direct response)


### Video Background and Ambient Video

**When to Use**:
- Creating atmosphere and mood
- Showing product in context
- Adding visual interest
- Conveying brand personality

**Best Practices**:
- No audio (or muted by default)
- Short, seamless loops
- Optimize for performance
- Don't distract from content
- Provide fallback for low-bandwidth
