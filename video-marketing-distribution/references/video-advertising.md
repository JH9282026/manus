## Video Advertising


### YouTube Advertising

**Ad Formats**:

**Skippable In-Stream Ads**:
- Viewers can skip after 5 seconds
- Pay when viewers watch 30 seconds or complete
- Best for longer brand stories

**Non-Skippable In-Stream Ads**:
- 15-20 seconds, cannot skip
- Higher completion but potential negative sentiment
- Best for concise, high-impact messages

**Bumper Ads**:
- 6 seconds, non-skippable
- Great for reinforcement and reach
- Brand awareness focus

**Video Discovery Ads**:
- Appear in search results and related videos
- Thumbnail and text, click to watch
- Intent-based discovery

**Best Practices**:
- Hook immediately (especially skippable)
- Branding within first 5 seconds
- Clear CTA
- Test multiple creative variations
- Use audience targeting effectively


### Facebook/Instagram Video Ads

**Ad Formats**:
- In-feed video ads
- Stories/Reels ads
- In-stream video ads
- Carousel video ads

**Best Practices**:
- Design for sound-off
- Capture attention in first second
- Vertical or square formats
- Keep ads concise
- Strong visual branding
- Clear action-driving CTA

**Targeting Capabilities**:
- Demographic targeting
- Interest and behavior targeting
- Custom audiences (website, email)
- Lookalike audiences
- Retargeting video viewers


### TikTok Advertising

**Ad Formats**:
- In-Feed Ads
- TopView
- Branded Hashtag Challenge
- Branded Effects
- Spark Ads (boost organic content)

**Best Practices**:
- Create TikTok-native content
- Less polished, more authentic
- Use trending formats and sounds
- Embrace creator collaboration
- Test quickly and iterate


### LinkedIn Video Advertising

**Best For**:
- B2B marketing
- Professional services
- High-value targeting
- Thought leadership

**Ad Formats**:
- Sponsored Content (video)
- Video ads

**Best Practices**:
- Professional but human
- Focus on business outcomes
- Captions essential
- Target by professional attributes
