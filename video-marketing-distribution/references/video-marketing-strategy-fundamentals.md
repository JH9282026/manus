## Video Marketing Strategy Fundamentals



### Defining Video Marketing Objectives

Every video marketing strategy must begin with clear objectives aligned to business goals:

**Awareness Objectives**:
- Increase brand recognition
- Reach new audiences
- Generate initial interest in products/services
- Build thought leadership position

**Consideration Objectives**:
- Educate prospects about solutions
- Demonstrate product capabilities
- Build trust through expertise
- Nurture leads through the funnel

**Conversion Objectives**:
- Drive sales and signups
- Generate qualified leads
- Encourage specific actions
- Support customer onboarding

**Retention Objectives**:
- Improve customer experience
- Reduce churn
- Build community
- Create brand advocates



### Understanding Your Video Audience

Effective video marketing requires deep audience understanding:

**Demographic Analysis**:
- Age, gender, location, income
- Platform preferences
- Device usage patterns
- Video consumption habits

**Psychographic Analysis**:
- Values and beliefs
- Content preferences
- Pain points and aspirations
- Communication style preferences

**Behavioral Analysis**:
- Where they consume video
- When they watch video
- Video lengths they prefer
- Actions they take after watching



### Building a Video Strategy Framework

**The 3H Video Strategy** (developed by Google):

**Hero Content**: Tent-pole content designed for broad reach
- Big campaigns and launches
- Highly produced brand films
- Major announcements
- Goal: Massive awareness and reach

**Hub Content**: Regularly scheduled content for your audience
- Series and recurring formats
- Educational content
- Product-focused content
- Goal: Build ongoing engagement

**Help Content**: Always-on content addressing audience needs
- Tutorials and how-tos
- FAQ videos
- Support content
- Goal: Search discovery and utility
