## YouTube Marketing Strategy



### Why YouTube Matters

- Second largest search engine after Google
- 2+ billion logged-in monthly users
- Average session duration of 40+ minutes
- Videos appear in Google search results
- Long-term discoverability through search



### YouTube Content Strategy

**Search-Optimized Content**:
- Answer common questions
- Create how-to content
- Target high-volume keywords
- Optimize for YouTube SEO

**Browse/Suggested Content**:
- Create binge-worthy series
- Optimize for session time
- Use end screens and cards
- Build strong audience retention

**Shorts Content**:
- Capture short-form trends
- Drive channel discovery
- Repurpose long-form content
- Experiment with new formats



### YouTube Monetization

**Ad Revenue**: YouTube Partner Program (1,000 subs, 4,000 watch hours)
**Channel Memberships**: Monthly supporter subscriptions
**Super Chat/Thanks**: Live stream and video donations
**Merchandise**: YouTube's merchandise shelf
**Sponsored Content**: Brand partnerships
**Lead Generation**: Drive to owned products/services
