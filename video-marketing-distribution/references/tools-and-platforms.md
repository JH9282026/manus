# Tools And Platforms

Detailed reference content for the Video Marketing Distribution skill.

---

## Tools and Platforms


### Video Hosting Platforms

- **YouTube**: Largest video platform, great for discovery
- **Vimeo**: Professional hosting, privacy features
- **Wistia**: Marketing-focused analytics and lead capture
- **Vidyard**: Sales and marketing video platform
- **JW Player**: Enterprise video streaming


### Social Media Platforms

- YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter


### Video Advertising Platforms

- Google Ads (YouTube)
- Meta Ads Manager (Facebook/Instagram)
- TikTok Ads
- LinkedIn Campaign Manager
- Programmatic platforms (The Trade Desk, DV360)


### Analytics and Measurement

- Platform native analytics
- Google Analytics (website video tracking)
- VidIQ, TubeBuddy (YouTube analytics enhancement)
- Sprout Social, Hootsuite (social video analytics)


### Video Creation and Editing

- **Professional**: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve
- **Mid-range**: Filmora, Camtasia, ScreenFlow
- **Easy**: InVideo, Lumen5, Canva Video
- **AI-powered**: Synthesia, Pictory, Runway


### Distribution and Management

- Sprinklr, Sprout Social, Hootsuite, Buffer, Later
- Tubular Labs (intelligence)
- Dash Hudson (visual marketing platform)
