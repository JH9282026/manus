## Video Analytics and KPIs


### Universal Video Metrics

**View Metrics**:
- Total views
- Unique viewers
- View velocity (views over time)
- View sources

**Engagement Metrics**:
- Watch time (total and average)
- Audience retention curve
- Completion rate
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)

**Action Metrics**:
- Click-through rate
- Conversion rate
- Subscription/follow rate
- Save rate


### Platform-Specific Analytics

**YouTube Analytics**:
- Watch time and average view duration
- Audience retention
- Traffic sources
- Impression click-through rate
- Revenue (if monetized)

**Facebook/Instagram Insights**:
- Reach vs. impressions
- 3-second and 10-second views
- ThruPlays
- Reactions, comments, shares, saves
- Audience demographics

**LinkedIn Analytics**:
- Views and viewers
- Engagement metrics
- Follower demographics
- Company page analytics


### Analytics Best Practices

- Establish benchmarks before optimization
- Compare to industry and historical performance
- Focus on metrics aligned to goals
- Track trends over time, not just snapshots
- Combine quantitative with qualitative feedback
- Report consistently and actionably
