## Video Marketing Automation


### Automation Opportunities

**Production Automation**:
- AI video generation tools
- Template-based video creation
- Automated captioning
- Auto-formatting for platforms

**Distribution Automation**:
- Scheduled publishing
- Cross-platform posting
- Automated social sharing
- RSS-triggered distribution

**Engagement Automation**:
- Comment response templates
- Chatbot integration
- Automated email sequences
- Retargeting pixel triggers

**Reporting Automation**:
- Automated performance reports
- Dashboard integration
- Alert notifications
- Competitive monitoring


### Tools for Automation

**Production**: Synthesia, Lumen5, InVideo, Pictory
**Distribution**: Buffer, Hootsuite, Later, Sprout Social
**Engagement**: ManyChat, HubSpot, Intercom
**Analytics**: Databox, Google Data Studio, Tableau
