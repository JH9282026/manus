## Video Marketing Funnel



### Awareness Stage (Top of Funnel)

**Goal**: Introduce your brand to new audiences

**Video Types**:
- Brand awareness videos
- Entertaining social content
- Viral-worthy content
- Educational content addressing common questions
- Thought leadership pieces

**Metrics to Track**:
- Views and impressions
- Reach and unique viewers
- Brand lift studies
- Social shares
- Time on brand

**Distribution Channels**:
- Social media platforms
- YouTube ads
- Display video advertising
- Influencer partnerships
- Public relations/earned media



### Consideration Stage (Middle of Funnel)

**Goal**: Educate and nurture interested prospects

**Video Types**:
- Detailed product demonstrations
- Comparison videos
- Testimonials and case studies
- Educational webinars
- FAQ videos
- Expert interviews

**Metrics to Track**:
- Watch time and completion rates
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Email signups
- Content downloads
- Return visits

**Distribution Channels**:
- Email marketing
- Retargeting campaigns
- Website embedded videos
- YouTube (owned channel)
- Webinar platforms



### Conversion Stage (Bottom of Funnel)

**Goal**: Drive purchase or desired action

**Video Types**:
- Sales videos and VSLs
- Pricing explanations
- Feature demonstrations
- Customer success stories
- Personalized video outreach
- Trial/demo invitation videos

**Metrics to Track**:
- Conversion rates
- Cost per acquisition
- Revenue attribution
- Pipeline influence
- Close rates

**Distribution Channels**:
- Sales pages and landing pages
- Email sequences
- Sales team outreach
- Remarketing campaigns
- Checkout pages



### Retention Stage (Post-Purchase)

**Goal**: Maximize customer value and create advocates

**Video Types**:
- Onboarding videos
- Training and tutorial content
- Customer success highlights
- Product update announcements
- Community content
- Loyalty program content

**Metrics to Track**:
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Retention and churn rates
- Product adoption metrics
- Support ticket reduction
- Referral rates
- Upsell/cross-sell success

**Distribution Channels**:
- Customer success platforms
- In-app messaging
- Email sequences
- Customer community
- Help center
