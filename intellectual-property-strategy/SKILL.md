# Intellectual Property Strategy

Develop, protect, and monetize innovation assets through systematic IP portfolio analysis and strategic planning.

## Overview

This skill transforms patent data, trademark registrations, trade secrets, and copyright holdings into actionable business intelligence for landscape analysis, FTO assessment, valuation, and monetization.

## Analysis Type Selection

| Objective | Type | Output | Reference |
|-----------|------|--------|-----------|
| Understand tech space | Landscape Mapping | Trends, players, white spaces | `/references/patent-analysis-frameworks.md` |
| Check product clearance | Freedom-to-Operate | Risk assessment, design-arounds | `/references/patent-analysis-frameworks.md` |
| Monitor competitors | Competitive Intel | Strategy map, threat alerts | `/references/patent-analysis-frameworks.md` |
| Value for deal | Valuation | Portfolio value, royalty potential | `/references/ip-monetization-protection.md` |
| Revenue from IP | Monetization | Licensing targets, cross-license | `/references/ip-monetization-protection.md` |
| Guide R&D | White Space ID | Innovation opportunity map | `/references/patent-analysis-frameworks.md` |
