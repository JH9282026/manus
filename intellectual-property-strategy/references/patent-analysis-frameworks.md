# Patent Analysis Frameworks

## Landscape Analysis
1. Define technology with CPC/IPC codes and keywords
2. Search across target jurisdictions
3. Analyze filing trends and growth rates
4. Identify top assignees by portfolio size
5. Technology clustering and heat maps
6. Citation network analysis
7. White space identification

## Freedom-to-Operate
1. Define product technical features
2. Search for blocking patents
3. Analyze claim scope vs. product
4. Assess patent validity
5. Evaluate infringement risk
6. Recommend: proceed, design around, license, challenge

## Competitive Intelligence
- Monitor competitor filings
- Track inventor movements
- Analyze prosecution strategies
- Map technology direction
- Identify collaborative research

## Classification Systems
- CPC: joint USPTO/EPO, most granular
- IPC: WIPO, broader categories
- Combine with keywords for precision
