# IP Monetization & Protection

## Valuation Methods
| Method | Best For | Approach |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Cost-based | Early-stage | Development cost |
| Market-based | Comparable deals | Similar transactions |
| Income-based | Revenue-generating | DCF of royalties |
| Relief-from-royalty | Licensing | Saved payments |

## Licensing Strategy
- Identify licensees through market/patent analysis
- Model: exclusive, non-exclusive, field-of-use
- Benchmark royalties from comparable transactions
- Consider cross-licensing for mutual FTO

## M&A Due Diligence
1. Inventory: patents, trademarks, trade secrets, copyrights
2. Verify ownership chain
3. Assess validity and enforceability
4. Check licenses, encumbrances, litigation
5. FTO for target products
6. Value as deal component

## Portfolio Optimization
- Categorize: core, strategic, non-core
- Assess maintenance cost vs. value
- Identify for licensing, sale, or abandonment
- File strategy for gaps in core areas

## IP Types
- Patents: technical novelty; 20 years
- Trademarks: brands; indefinite if maintained
- Trade secrets: confidential info; no expiration
- Copyrights: creative works; life + 70 years
- Design patents: ornamental; 15 years
