### Types of Packaging Taglines

**Brand taglines:**
Appear across all products in a brand
- "Just Do It" (Nike)
- "Because You're Worth It" (L'Oréal)

**Product taglines:**
Specific to a single product or line
- "Melts in your mouth, not in your hands" (M&Ms)

**Campaign taglines:**
Temporary taglines tied to marketing campaigns
- Seasonal or promotional messaging
