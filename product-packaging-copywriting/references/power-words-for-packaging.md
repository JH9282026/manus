### Power Words for Packaging

Certain words have proven effectiveness on packaging:

**Quality indicators:**
Premium, Artisan, Handcrafted, Authentic, Genuine, Professional-grade, Award-winning, Best-selling

**Benefit triggers:**
Transform, Discover, Unlock, Reveal, Boost, Restore, Protect, Nourish

**Value signals:**
Free, Bonus, Extra, More, Now, New, Improved, Enhanced

**Trust builders:**
Certified, Tested, Proven, Guaranteed, Trusted, Recommended, Doctor-approved

**Urgency creators:**
Limited, Exclusive, Special, Seasonal, While supplies last
