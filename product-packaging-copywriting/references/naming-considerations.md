### Naming Considerations

**Descriptive names:**
Clearly communicate what the product is or does
- Example: "Daily Moisturizing Lotion"
- Advantage: Immediate clarity
- Disadvantage: Less distinctive

**Evocative names:**
Create emotional associations without literal description
- Example: "Olay Regenerist"
- Advantage: Distinctive and memorable
- Disadvantage: May require more supporting copy

**Invented names:**
Completely new words or unusual combinations
- Example: "Swiffer" or "Febreze"
- Advantage: Highly distinctive, trademarkable
- Disadvantage: Requires significant brand building
