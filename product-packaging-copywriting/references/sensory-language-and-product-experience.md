### Sensory Language and Product Experience

Effective packaging copy engages multiple senses to create a vivid product experience before purchase.

**Visual language:**
- "Rich, golden honey"
- "Crystal-clear formula"
- "Vibrant, eye-catching colors"

**Texture/touch language:**
- "Silky smooth finish"
- "Crisp, crunchy texture"
- "Soft, luxurious feel"

**Scent language:**
- "Fresh citrus burst"
- "Warm vanilla notes"
- "Clean, ocean-fresh scent"

**Taste language (food):**
- "Bold, robust flavor"
- "Delicate sweetness"
- "Tangy with a hint of spice"

**Sound language:**
- "Satisfying crunch"
- "Whisper-quiet operation"
- "Click that confirms secure closure"
