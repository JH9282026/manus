### Brand Architecture on Packaging

**Master brand approach:**
Brand name is dominant, product name secondary
- Example: "Dove | Men+Care | Clean Comfort Body Wash"

**Product brand approach:**
Product name is dominant, company name secondary
- Example: "Tide | Procter & Gamble"

**Sub-brand approach:**
Equal emphasis on both brand and sub-brand
- Example: "Nike Air Max"
