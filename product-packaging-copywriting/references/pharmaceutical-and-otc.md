### Pharmaceutical and OTC

**Regulatory environment:**
- FDA-mandated Drug Facts panel
- Specific claim substantiation requirements
- Warning hierarchy requirements
- Controlled terminology

**Copy considerations:**
- Clarity and accuracy paramount
- Trust and professionalism essential
- Side effect and interaction warnings
- Dosage and administration precision

**Example structure:**
```
BRAND NAME
[Active Ingredient] [Strength]
[Primary Indication]

Uses: [FDA-approved indications]
Warnings: [Required warnings]
Directions: [Dosing instructions]
Drug Facts: [Standardized panel]
```
