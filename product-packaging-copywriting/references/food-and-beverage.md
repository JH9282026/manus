### Food and Beverage

**Key elements:**
- Flavor/variety identification
- Nutritional highlights
- Ingredient transparency
- Serving suggestions
- Storage instructions

**Trend considerations:**
- Clean labeling
- Allergen clarity
- Sustainability sourcing
- Health claims

**Example - Snack Bar:**

*Front:* "PROTEIN BAR | Chocolate Peanut Butter | 20g Protein | Gluten Free"
*Back:* Story about ingredients, full nutrition facts, detailed ingredient list
