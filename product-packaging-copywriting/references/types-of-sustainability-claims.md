### Types of Sustainability Claims

**Material claims:**
- "Made from recycled materials"
- "Biodegradable packaging"
- "Plastic-free"
- "FSC-certified paper"

**Process claims:**
- "Carbon neutral manufacturing"
- "Ethically sourced"
- "Cruelty-free"
- "Fair trade certified"

**End-of-life claims:**
- "Recyclable"
- "Compostable"
- "Refillable"
- "Zero waste"
