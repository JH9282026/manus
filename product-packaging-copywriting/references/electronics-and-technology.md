### Electronics and Technology

**Key elements:**
- Key specifications
- Compatibility information
- What's in the box
- Quick start guidance
- Warranty information

**Trend considerations:**
- Minimal packaging aesthetic
- Sustainability
- Digital manual integration
- Unboxing experience

**Example - Wireless Earbuds:**

*Front:* "TRUE WIRELESS | 24-Hour Battery | Active Noise Cancellation"
*Back:* Key specs, what's included, compatibility, QR code for setup
