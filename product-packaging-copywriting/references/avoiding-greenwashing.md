### Avoiding Greenwashing

**Best practices:**
- Make specific, substantiated claims
- Provide certification details
- Avoid vague terms like "eco-friendly"
- Be transparent about limitations
- Follow FTC Green Guides

**Example:**
*Greenwashing:* "Eco-friendly packaging"
*Specific:* "Bottle made from 50% post-consumer recycled plastic. Recyclable in most communities."
