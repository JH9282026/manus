### Key Metrics to Test

**Attention:**
- Does the package stand out?
- What do consumers notice first?
- How quickly do they identify the product?

**Communication:**
- Do consumers understand key claims?
- Are benefits clear?
- Any confusion or misinterpretation?

**Motivation:**
- Does the copy drive purchase intent?
- Does it differentiate from alternatives?
- What's most compelling?

**Comprehension:**
- Are instructions clear?
- Do warnings register?
- Can consumers find key information?
