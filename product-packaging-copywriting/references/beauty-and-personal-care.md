### Beauty and Personal Care

**Key elements:**
- Key ingredient callouts
- Skin/hair type targeting
- Usage instructions
- Results claims (with substantiation)

**Trend considerations:**
- Clean beauty/ingredient transparency
- Sustainability
- Inclusivity
- Cruelty-free certification

**Example - Face Cream:**

*Front:* "GLOW RESTORE | Vitamin C Night Cream | Brightens & Firms"
*Back:* Key ingredients with benefits, how to use, full ingredient list, sustainability info
