### Emerging Trends

**Smart packaging:**
- NFC tags for interactive experiences
- Freshness indicators with copy integration
- Connected packaging for reordering

**Personalization:**
- Mass customization possibilities
- Name personalization (like Coca-Cola campaign)
- Localized messaging at scale

**Transparency:**
- Blockchain-verified sourcing information
- Real-time supply chain data
- Ingredient origin tracking

**Accessibility:**
- Braille and tactile elements
- Audio descriptions via technology
- Inclusive design principles

**Minimalism:**
- Less is more philosophy
- Focus on essential information
- Digital extension for details
