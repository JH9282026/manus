### Writing Effective Instructions

**Principles:**
- Use numbered steps for sequential processes
- Use bullet points for tips or options
- Start each step with an action verb
- Keep steps short (10 words or fewer)
- Include quantity or timing where relevant
- Test with actual users

**Example - Face Wash:**

*How to Use:*
1. Wet face with warm water
2. Apply a small amount to fingertips
3. Massage gently in circular motions
4. Rinse thoroughly
5. Use morning and evening for best results
