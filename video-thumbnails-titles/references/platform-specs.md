## Platform-Specific Strategies

### YouTube Thumbnails and Titles

YouTube is the primary platform for thumbnail optimization:

#### YouTube-Specific Considerations
- Thumbnails appear in search results, suggested videos, homepage, and subscriptions
- Watch page thumbnails are larger than browse thumbnails
- Mobile displays prioritize larger text and clearer images
- YouTube Shorts require vertical (9:16) cover images

#### YouTube Title Best Practices
- Include primary keyword early for SEO
- Maximum 100 characters (display varies)
- Avoid excessive punctuation
- Update underperforming titles

### Facebook and Instagram

Social media platforms have unique requirements:

#### Facebook Video Thumbnails
- Auto-generated options available
- Custom uploads recommended
- Consider news feed display context
- Text overlay limits for ads (20% rule relaxed but still relevant)

#### Instagram Reels and Posts
- Cover images should work in grid view (1:1 crop)
- Vertical format (9:16) for Reels
- Feed posts display at 1:1 or 4:5
- Stories and Reels have full-bleed display

### LinkedIn and Professional Platforms

Business context changes thumbnail strategy:

- More conservative color choices
- Professional photography preferred
- Clear value proposition in title
- Industry-specific visual language
- Less exaggerated expressions
- Data and results in titles perform well

### TikTok and Short-Form

Short-form platforms have distinct needs:

- Cover frame selection from video
- Text overlays must work on mobile
- Trending elements may boost discovery
- Hook-focused titles critical
- Hashtag strategy in titles/captions
