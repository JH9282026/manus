# Growth Hacking

Drive rapid, scalable growth through systematic experimentation and creative tactics.

## Overview

Growth hacking is a data-driven, experimental approach to rapidly scale businesses by identifying and leveraging the most effective growth strategies through continuous testing and optimization.

## Growth Hacking Fundamentals

### Core Principles

**Data-Driven Decision Making**:
- Base decisions on evidence, not assumptions
- Track metrics rigorously
- Use analytics to guide experiments
- Measure everything

**Rapid Experimentation**:
- Fast cycles of testing
- Generate ideas quickly
- Prioritize systematically
- Learn from failures
- Scale winners

**Cross-Functional Collaboration**:
- Product, marketing, engineering alignment
- Shared growth goals
- Collaborative ideation
- Integrated execution

**Focus on Retention**:
- Retention drives sustainable growth
- Fix leaky bucket before filling it
- Optimize onboarding and activation
- Reduce churn

**Scalable Mechanisms**:
- Build systems that compound
- Viral loops and referrals
- Automated growth engines
- Network effects

## AARRR Framework (Pirate Metrics)

### Acquisition
**Goal**: Get users to your product

**Tactics**:
- Content marketing and SEO
- Paid advertising
- Social media marketing
- Influencer partnerships
- PR and media coverage
- Viral content
- Partnerships and integrations

**Metrics**:
- Traffic sources
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Conversion rate by channel
- Viral coefficient

### Activation
**Goal**: Deliver great first experience

**Tactics**:
- Streamlined onboarding
- Quick wins and "aha moments"
- Interactive tutorials
- Personalized experiences
- Gamification
- Progress indicators

**Metrics**:
- Activation rate
- Time to first value
- Onboarding completion rate
- Feature adoption

### Retention
**Goal**: Keep users coming back

**Tactics**:
- Email and push notifications
- Feature updates and improvements
- Personalized content
- Habit-forming design
- Community building
- Customer success programs

**Metrics**:
- Retention rate (Day 1, 7, 30)
- Churn rate
- Daily/Monthly active users (DAU/MAU)
- Cohort retention curves

### Referral
**Goal**: Turn users into advocates

**Tactics**:
- Referral programs with incentives
- Social sharing features
- Viral loops
- User-generated content
- Ambassador programs
- Built-in sharing mechanisms

**Metrics**:
- Viral coefficient (K-factor)
- Referral rate
- Viral cycle time
- Shares and invites

### Revenue
**Goal**: Monetize users effectively

**Tactics**:
- Pricing optimization
- Upsell and cross-sell
- Conversion rate optimization
- Payment friction reduction
- Value-based pricing
- Freemium to paid conversion

**Metrics**:
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Conversion to paid rate
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)

## Growth Experimentation Process

### G.R.O.W.S. Framework

**Gather Ideas**:
- Brainstorm growth hypotheses
- Research competitors
- Analyze user behavior
- Survey customers
- Review analytics
- Study successful companies

**Rank Ideas**:
Use ICE scoring:
- **Impact**: Potential effect on goal (1-10)
- **Confidence**: Likelihood of success (1-10)
- **Ease**: Resources required (1-10)
- **ICE Score**: (Impact + Confidence + Ease) / 3

**Outline Experiments**:
- Define hypothesis
- Set success criteria
- Determine sample size
- Plan implementation
- Estimate timeline
- Assign ownership

**Work (Execute)**:
- Build minimum viable test
- Launch experiment
- Monitor in real-time
- Collect data
- Document process

**Study Results**:
- Analyze data
- Determine statistical significance
- Extract learnings
- Decide: Scale, iterate, or kill
- Document insights

## Growth Hacking Tactics

### Viral Growth Tactics

**Referral Programs**:
- Dropbox: Free storage for referrals
- Airbnb: Travel credits for both parties
- Uber: Ride credits for referrals
- PayPal: Cash incentives (early days)

**Built-In Virality**:
- Hotmail: "Get your free email at Hotmail"
- Instagram: Watermarks on shared photos
- Zoom: Free users in paid meetings
- Calendly: Branding on scheduling pages

**Social Sharing**:
- Easy one-click sharing
- Pre-populated messages
- Visual content for sharing
- Incentives for sharing

### Product-Led Growth

**Freemium Model**:
- Free tier with core value
- Premium features for paid users
- Easy upgrade path
- Usage-based limits

**Free Trial**:
- Time-limited full access
- Credit card optional
- Automated onboarding
- Conversion campaigns

**Self-Service Onboarding**:
- No sales required
- Interactive tutorials
- Quick time-to-value
- In-app guidance
