### Risk Mitigation Strategies

**Mitigation Options:**
1. **Avoid**: Eliminate risk by not performing activity
2. **Reduce**: Implement controls to lower likelihood/impact
3. **Transfer**: Shift risk to third party (insurance)
4. **Accept**: Acknowledge and monitor risk

**Control Implementation:**
- Prioritize based on risk level
- Balance cost vs. benefit
- Design preventive and detective controls
- Document mitigation decisions
