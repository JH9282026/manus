### Change Management Policies

**Change Categories:**
| Category | Description | Approval |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Emergency | Critical fixes | Fast-track |
| Standard | Pre-approved types | Delegated |
| Normal | Typical changes | CAB review |
| Major | Significant impact | Executive approval |

**Change Freeze Periods:**
- End of financial quarters
- Peak business periods
- Major system updates
- Regulatory reporting windows
