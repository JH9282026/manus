### Approval Workflows

**Approval Design Principles:**
1. Clear criteria for approval/rejection
2. Appropriate authority levels
3. Escalation paths for exceptions
4. Time limits to prevent delays
5. Documentation of decisions

**Approval Patterns:**
- **Sequential**: One approver after another
- **Parallel**: Multiple approvers simultaneously
- **Conditional**: Based on criteria (amount, type)
- **Hierarchical**: Based on organizational level
- **Percentage**: Required approval percentage
