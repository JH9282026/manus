### Compliance Checkpoints

**Checkpoint Types:**
1. **Entry Checkpoints**: Validate before process starts
2. **In-Process Checkpoints**: Verify during execution
3. **Exit Checkpoints**: Confirm before completion
4. **Post-Process Checkpoints**: Review after completion

**Checkpoint Design:**
- Define trigger conditions
- Specify verification criteria
- Establish escalation procedures
- Document outcomes
- Enable monitoring
