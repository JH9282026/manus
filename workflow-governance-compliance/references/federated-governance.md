### Federated Governance

**Characteristics:**
- Central standards with local implementation
- Shared governance framework
- Balanced authority
- Coordinated but flexible

**Advantages:**
- Balance of control and flexibility
- Leverages local knowledge
- Maintains enterprise standards
- Enables collaboration

**Disadvantages:**
- Complex coordination
- Potential for conflict
- Requires mature processes
- Higher governance overhead

**Best For:**
- Large enterprises
- Multi-national organizations
- Diverse but related businesses
- Mature process organizations
