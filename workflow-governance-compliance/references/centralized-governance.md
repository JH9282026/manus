### Centralized Governance

**Characteristics:**
- Single governance body controls all processes
- Uniform standards across organization
- Centralized decision-making
- Consistent enforcement

**Advantages:**
- Strong consistency and control
- Efficient use of specialized expertise
- Clear accountability
- Easier compliance management

**Disadvantages:**
- Slower response to local needs
- May not accommodate business unit differences
- Can create bottlenecks
- Risk of disconnection from operations

**Best For:**
- Highly regulated industries
- Organizations requiring strict control
- Smaller organizations
- Critical, high-risk processes
