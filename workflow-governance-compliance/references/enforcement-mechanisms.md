### Enforcement Mechanisms

**Enforcement Approaches:**
- **System-Enforced**: Technical controls prevent violations
- **Policy-Enforced**: Policies define consequences
- **Culture-Enforced**: Organizational values drive compliance
- **Audit-Enforced**: Review and accountability

**Escalation Procedures:**
- Define escalation triggers
- Establish escalation paths
- Set response timelines
- Document escalation outcomes
