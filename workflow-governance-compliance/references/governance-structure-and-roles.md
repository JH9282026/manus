### Governance Structure and Roles

**Executive Sponsor:**
- Provides strategic direction and resources
- Champions governance initiatives
- Resolves escalated conflicts
- Ensures alignment with business strategy

**Process Owner:**
- Accountable for end-to-end process performance
- Defines process requirements and standards
- Approves process changes
- Reports on process metrics

**Process Steward:**
- Day-to-day process management
- Monitors process execution
- Identifies improvement opportunities
- Coordinates with stakeholders

**Compliance Officer:**
- Interprets regulatory requirements
- Develops compliance policies
- Monitors compliance status
- Manages regulatory relationships

**Process Analyst:**
- Documents and models processes
- Analyzes process performance
- Recommends improvements
- Supports change implementation
