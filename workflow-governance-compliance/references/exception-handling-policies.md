### Exception Handling Policies

**Exception Types:**
1. **Process Exceptions**: Deviations from standard flow
2. **Policy Exceptions**: Departures from policy
3. **Control Exceptions**: Bypassing controls
4. **Compliance Exceptions**: Regulatory waivers

**Exception Management:**
- Document all exceptions
- Require appropriate approval
- Time-limit exceptions
- Track and report patterns
- Review for permanent resolution
