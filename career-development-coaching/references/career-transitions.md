# Career Transitions

Strategies for navigating career changes and pivots.

---

## Transition Types

### Industry Change
- Same function, different industry
- Leverage transferable skills
- Build industry knowledge

### Function Change
- Different function, same industry
- Leverage industry expertise
- Develop new competencies

### Full Pivot
- New function and industry
- Highest difficulty
- Requires significant preparation

### Level Change
- Moving up or down
- Individual contributor to management
- Management to executive

---

## Transferable Skills Framework

### Core Categories
| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Communication | Presentation, writing, negotiation |
| Leadership | Team management, mentoring, decision-making |
| Analytical | Problem-solving, data analysis, research |
| Project | Planning, execution, stakeholder management |
| Technical | Industry-agnostic tools, methodologies |
| Interpersonal | Relationship building, collaboration |

### Skill Translation
1. List all current skills
2. Research target role requirements
3. Map overlapping skills
4. Identify gaps to address
5. Reframe experience using target language

## Transition Strategies

### Lateral Move
- Take related role at current employer
- Build new skills on the job
- Reduce risk of external change

### Step Back to Move Forward
- Accept lower title/pay initially
- Gain entry to new field
- Accelerate once established

### Bridge Role
- Role combining old and new skills
- Gradual transition
- Build credibility in new area

### Entrepreneurial Path
- Freelance in new area
- Build portfolio/experience
- Lower barriers to entry

### Education/Certification
- Formal credential in new field
- Signals commitment
- Builds foundation

---

## Rebranding Yourself

### Narrative Development
1. Craft your "why" story
2. Connect past to future
3. Highlight transferable value
4. Address obvious questions
5. Practice until natural

### Materials Update
- Resume focused on transferable skills
- LinkedIn with new positioning
- Portfolio demonstrating new capabilities
- Cover letter addressing transition

### Network Strategy
- Identify connectors in target field
- Conduct informational interviews
- Join relevant professional groups
- Attend industry events
- Build visibility in new space

---

## Timeline Expectations

| Transition Type | Typical Timeline |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Industry (same function) | 3-6 months |
| Function (same industry) | 6-12 months |
| Full pivot | 12-24 months |
| Level up | 6-18 months |

### Phases
1. **Exploration** (1-3 months): Research, informational interviews
2. **Preparation** (3-6 months): Skill building, networking
3. **Search** (3-6 months): Active job search
4. **Onboarding** (3-6 months): Proving yourself in new role
