# Interview Guide

Comprehensive preparation for interview success.

---

## Interview Types

### Phone Screen (15-30 min)
**Purpose:** Basic qualification check
**Focus:** Background, interest, fit
**Preparation:** Company overview, your story

### Hiring Manager (45-60 min)
**Purpose:** Role fit and capability
**Focus:** Experience, skills, team fit
**Preparation:** STAR stories, role research

### Technical/Skills (60-90 min)
**Purpose:** Competency validation
**Focus:** Problem-solving, technical depth
**Preparation:** Practice, portfolio, case studies

### Panel (60-90 min)
**Purpose:** Multiple perspectives
**Focus:** Cross-functional fit
**Preparation:** Research all interviewers

### Executive/Final (30-45 min)
**Purpose:** Culture and leadership fit
**Focus:** Vision, values, strategic thinking
**Preparation:** Company strategy, big picture

---

## STAR Method Deep Dive

### Situation (10-20%)
- Set context briefly
- Time, place, scope
- Keep it concise

### Task (10-20%)
- Your specific responsibility
- What was at stake
- Clear objective

### Action (60%)
- What YOU did (use "I")
- Step by step
- Decision rationale
- Skills demonstrated

### Result (20%)
- Quantified outcome
- What you learned
- Broader impact

## Common Question Categories

### Tell Me About Yourself
- 2-minute professional story
- Past → Present → Future
- Connect to this role

### Strengths/Weaknesses
**Strengths:** 
- Relevant to role
- Supported by examples

**Weaknesses:**
- Genuine but not disqualifying
- Show self-awareness
- Describe improvement actions

### Behavioral Questions
| Topic | Example Questions |
|-------|-------------------|
| Leadership | Tell me about leading a team through change |
| Conflict | Describe a disagreement with a colleague |
| Failure | Tell me about a time you failed |
| Achievement | What's your proudest accomplishment? |
| Pressure | How do you handle tight deadlines? |

### Why This Company/Role
- Specific company reasons
- Connect to your goals
- Show research and enthusiasm

### Questions to Ask
**About the Role:**
- What does success look like in 90 days?
- What are the biggest challenges?

**About the Team:**
- How would you describe the team culture?
- What's the team's biggest priority right now?

**About Growth:**
- What development opportunities exist?
- How do people grow in this organization?

## Interview Day Checklist

### Before
- [ ] Research company, interviewers, role
- [ ] Prepare STAR stories
- [ ] Prepare questions to ask
- [ ] Plan logistics (route, login, tech check)
- [ ] Prepare professional attire

### During
- [ ] Arrive 10-15 minutes early
- [ ] Bring copies of resume
- [ ] Take notes (ask permission)
- [ ] Ask clarifying questions
- [ ] Show enthusiasm

### After
- [ ] Send thank you within 24 hours
- [ ] Personalize each note
- [ ] Reference specific conversation points
- [ ] Reaffirm interest
