# Resume Writing

Detailed guidance for creating effective, ATS-optimized resumes.

---

## ATS Optimization

### How ATS Works

Applicant Tracking Systems scan resumes for:
- Keyword matches to job descriptions
- Standard section headers
- Clean formatting
- Contact information extraction

### ATS-Friendly Formatting

| Element | Do | Don't |
|---------|----|---------|
| File Format | .docx or .pdf | Images of text |
| Fonts | Standard (Arial, Calibri, Times) | Creative fonts |
| Headers | Standard names (Experience, Education) | Creative headers |
| Layout | Single column | Multi-column, tables |
| Graphics | None or minimal | Icons, images, charts |
| Margins | 0.5-1 inch | Unusual margins |

### Keyword Integration

1. **Analyze job descriptions** — Identify repeated terms
2. **Match language** — Use exact phrasing when accurate
3. **Include variations** — Both acronyms and spelled out
4. **Distribute naturally** — Throughout resume, not stuffed

## Resume Structure

### Contact Information

```
Full Name
City, State | Phone | Email | LinkedIn URL
```

**Tips**:
- No full address needed
- Professional email address
- LinkedIn vanity URL

### Professional Summary

**Length**: 3-4 sentences

**Template**:
"[Role] with [X years] experience in [industry/function]. [Key achievement or expertise]. [Relevant skills/specializations]. [What you're looking for or value you bring]."

**Example**:
"Results-driven Product Manager with 8+ years of experience in B2B SaaS. Led cross-functional teams to launch 5 products generating $12M in annual revenue. Expert in agile methodologies, user research, and data-driven decision making. Seeking to drive product strategy at a growth-stage technology company."

### Experience Section

**Format**:
```
Job Title | Company Name
Location | Dates (Month Year - Month Year)

• [Achievement using CAR format]
• [Achievement using CAR format]
• [Achievement using CAR format]
```

**Guidelines**:
- 3-6 bullet points per role
- Most recent roles get more bullets
- Start with action verbs
- Quantify everything possible

### Skills Section

**Organization Options**:

```
Technical Skills: Python, SQL, Tableau, AWS, Salesforce
Certifications: PMP, AWS Solutions Architect, Scrum Master
Languages: English (Native), Spanish (Fluent), Mandarin (Conversational)
```

Or:

```
Skills: Product Management | Agile/Scrum | SQL | User Research | A/B Testing | Roadmapping
```

### Education Section

**Format**:
```
Degree, Major | University Name | Year
- Relevant honors, activities, or coursework (if recent graduate)
```

## Achievement Writing

### CAR Format

| Component | Content | Length |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Challenge | Context/situation | Brief |
| Action | What YOU did | Specific |
| Result | Quantified outcome | Impactful |

### Action Verbs by Function

| Function | Strong Verbs |
|----------|-------------|
| Leadership | Led, Directed, Managed, Oversaw, Spearheaded |
| Achievement | Achieved, Exceeded, Delivered, Accomplished |
| Growth | Increased, Grew, Expanded, Doubled, Tripled |
| Efficiency | Reduced, Streamlined, Optimized, Automated |
| Creation | Built, Developed, Created, Launched, Established |
| Analysis | Analyzed, Identified, Assessed, Evaluated |

### Quantification Examples

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Improved sales | Increased sales 45% ($2.3M) in 12 months |
| Managed team | Led 15-person team across 3 time zones |
| Reduced costs | Cut operational costs 30% ($500K annually) |
| Launched product | Launched product serving 50K users in 6 months |

## Resume by Career Level

### Entry Level (0-3 years)

**Length**: 1 page
**Focus**: Education, internships, projects, skills
**Tips**: Include relevant coursework, academic projects, volunteer work

### Mid-Level (3-10 years)

**Length**: 1-2 pages
**Focus**: Professional achievements, career progression
**Tips**: Lead with summary, emphasize impact and scope

### Senior/Executive (10+ years)

**Length**: 2 pages
**Focus**: Leadership impact, strategic contributions
**Tips**: Emphasize scope (P&L, team size), board/executive experience

## Industry-Specific Considerations

| Industry | Emphasis | Special Sections |
|----------|----------|------------------|
| Technology | Technical skills, projects | GitHub, Portfolio |
| Finance | Certifications, quantified results | Series licenses |
| Healthcare | Licenses, compliance | Certifications |
| Creative | Portfolio, tools | Work samples link |
| Academia | Publications, research | CV format often required |
| Sales | Quota achievement, rankings | % to goal metrics |

## Common Resume Mistakes

| Mistake | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| No quantification | Weak impact | Add numbers everywhere |
| Responsibilities vs achievements | Boring, undifferentiated | Focus on results |
| Too long | Overwhelming | Ruthlessly edit |
| Typos/errors | Unprofessional | Multiple proofreads |
| Generic objective | Wasted space | Use targeted summary |
| Outdated format | Dated impression | Modern, clean design |
| Missing keywords | ATS rejection | Mirror job description |
