# Resume Templates

Role-specific guidance for resume writing and optimization.

---

## Resume Formats

### Chronological (Most Common)
**Best For:** Traditional career progression, staying in same field
**Structure:** Experience in reverse chronological order

### Functional
**Best For:** Career changers, gaps in employment
**Structure:** Skills and achievements grouped by competency

### Combination
**Best For:** Senior professionals, specialized roles
**Structure:** Skills summary + chronological experience

---

## Section Guidelines

### Contact Information
- Full name (larger font)
- Phone number (one line)
- Professional email
- LinkedIn URL (customized)
- Location (city, state)
- Portfolio/website (if relevant)

### Professional Summary
- 3-4 sentences maximum
- Lead with years of experience and expertise
- Include 2-3 key achievements or skills
- Target specific role/industry

**Formula:**
```
[Title/Role] with [X years] experience in [industry/function]. 
[Key achievement or expertise]. [Unique value proposition]. 
[What you're seeking].
```

### Experience Section
**For Each Role:**
- Company name, location
- Title, dates (month/year)
- 3-6 bullet points
- Achievement-focused, not task-focused

**Bullet Format:**
```
[Action verb] [what you did] [resulting in/by] [quantified impact]
```

### Skills Section
- Technical skills relevant to target role
- Group by category (Languages, Tools, Methodologies)
- Proficiency levels if appropriate
- Include ATS keywords

### Education
- Degree, Major, University, Year
- Relevant coursework (entry level only)
- GPA if >3.5 and recent graduate
- Honors and awards

## Industry-Specific Tips

### Technology Roles
- Technical skills prominently displayed
- Include programming languages, frameworks
- Quantify system scale, performance
- Link to GitHub, portfolio

### Business/Finance
- Financial metrics and impact
- Revenue, cost savings, efficiency gains
- Client relationship metrics
- Professional certifications prominent

### Marketing
- Campaign performance metrics
- Growth percentages
- Channel expertise
- Creative portfolio links

### Operations
- Process improvement percentages
- Cost reduction figures
- Team size and scope
- Efficiency metrics

### Creation
Created, Developed, Designed, Built, Launched, Established

### Improvement
Improved, Enhanced, Optimized, Streamlined, Transformed

### Analysis
Analyzed, Evaluated, Assessed, Identified, Researched

### Collaboration
Collaborated, Partnered, Coordinated, Facilitated, Aligned
