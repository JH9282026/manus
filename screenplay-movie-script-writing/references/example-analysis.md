### Example Analysis

*The Silence of the Lambs* - Quid Pro Quo Scene:

- **Clarice's objective:** Get information about Buffalo Bill
- **Lecter's objective:** Gain personal information about Clarice
- **Obstacle:** Each wants what the other won't freely give
- **Tactics:** Negotiation, manipulation, vulnerability
- **Stakes:** Lives hang in balance; Clarice's trauma exposed
- **Turn:** Power shifts as Clarice reveals childhood trauma
- **Button:** "Quid pro quo" established as ongoing dynamic
