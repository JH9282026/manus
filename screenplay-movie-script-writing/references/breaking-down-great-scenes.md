### Breaking Down Great Scenes

Analyze produced scenes for:

1. **Objective:** What does each character want?
2. **Obstacle:** What prevents them getting it?
3. **Tactic:** How do they try to overcome obstacles?
4. **Stakes:** What happens if they fail?
5. **Turn:** How does the scene shift power or information?
6. **Button:** What's the final moment that punctuates the scene?
