### Voice-Over Narration

Use voice-over purposefully, not as exposition crutch:

**Effective voice-over:**
```
                         MARCUS (V.O.)
          They say you can't choose your family.
          But you can choose how hard you fight
          for them.

EXT. HOSPITAL - NIGHT

Marcus sits on a bench outside the ER, head in hands.
Rain soaks through his jacket. He doesn't move.
```
