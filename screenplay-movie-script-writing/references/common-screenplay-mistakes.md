## Common Screenplay Mistakes

1. **Over-writing action lines:** Keep them brief and visual
2. **On-the-nose dialogue:** Subtext is essential
3. **Starting too early:** Enter the story as late as possible
4. **Passive protagonist:** Characters must drive action
5. **Weak antagonist:** Opposition must be formidable
6. **Telling instead of showing:** Film is a visual medium
7. **Too many characters:** Focus on essential roles
8. **Unmotivated actions:** Every choice needs reason
9. **Predictable plot:** Surprise within inevitability
10. **Weak ending:** The climax must satisfy
11. **Improper formatting:** Industry standard is non-negotiable
12. **First scene in a car:** Avoid clichéd openings
13. **Dream sequences that cheat:** Audiences feel manipulated
14. **Voice-over as crutch:** Use sparingly and purposefully
15. **Unproduceable scripts:** Consider budget and logistics
