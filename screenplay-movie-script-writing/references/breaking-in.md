### Breaking In

Entry paths to professional screenwriting:

**Spec scripts:** Write original screenplays to demonstrate your voice
**Competitions:** Win or place in prestigious contests
**Coverage services:** Use Black List or similar for exposure
**Networking:** Industry connections through events, classes, organizations
**Short films:** Write produceable shorts to build portfolio
**Web series:** Create original content showcasing abilities
**Assistants:** Work as assistant to learn industry from inside
