### Intercut Sequences

Cutting between two simultaneous scenes:

```
INT. INTERROGATION ROOM - NIGHT

Detective Chen leans across the table.

                         DETECTIVE CHEN
          Where were you at midnight?

INTERCUT WITH:

INT. BASEMENT - NIGHT

Marcus works frantically, dismantling equipment.

                         SUSPECT (V.O.)
          Home. Sleeping.

Back to the interrogation room:

                         DETECTIVE CHEN
          Funny. Your car says otherwise.
```
