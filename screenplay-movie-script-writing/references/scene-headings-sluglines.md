### Scene Headings (Sluglines)

Scene headings establish location and time. Format:

```
INT. COFFEE SHOP - DAY
```

**Components:**
- **INT./EXT.:** Interior or Exterior
- **LOCATION:** Specific place name
- **TIME:** DAY, NIGHT, DAWN, DUSK, CONTINUOUS, LATER

**Examples:**
```
EXT. CENTRAL PARK - DAWN
INT. SARAH'S APARTMENT - KITCHEN - NIGHT
INT. MOVING CAR - DAY
EXT. BEACH - SUNSET
```
