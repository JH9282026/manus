### Montage Sequences

Montages compress time and show progression:

```
MONTAGE - SARAH'S TRAINING

-- Sarah struggles through pushups, collapses after ten.

-- Sarah runs, gasping, falls behind the group.

-- Sarah at the shooting range, misses every target.

-- WEEKS LATER: Sarah knocks out fifty pushups, barely winded.

-- Sarah leads the running pack.

-- Sarah's grouping: perfect center mass.

END MONTAGE
```
