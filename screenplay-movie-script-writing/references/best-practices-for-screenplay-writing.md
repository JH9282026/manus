## Best Practices for Screenplay Writing

1. **Read screenplays:** Study produced scripts in your genre
2. **Write regularly:** Consistency beats inspiration
3. **Outline first:** Structure before drafting
4. **Get feedback early:** Don't polish first drafts
5. **Cut ruthlessly:** Every word must earn its place
6. **Know your ending:** Write toward a destination
7. **Rewrite extensively:** First drafts are never final
8. **Study film:** Watch movies as a writer
9. **Learn the market:** Know what's selling and why
10. **Network:** Relationships drive the industry
11. **Stay current:** Industry standards evolve
12. **Be professional:** Presentation matters
13. **Develop multiple projects:** Don't stake everything on one script
14. **Accept rejection:** It's part of the process
15. **Keep improving:** Craft develops over time
