# Newsletter Content Strategy

## Content Pillar Framework

### Define 3-5 Content Pillars
Each pillar represents a consistent topic category:

Example for a marketing newsletter:
1. **Strategy insights** — Frameworks and strategic thinking
2. **Tool reviews** — Software and platform evaluations
3. **Case studies** — Real-world examples and results
4. **Trends** — Industry news and predictions
5. **Quick tips** — Actionable tactical advice

### Content Mix
| Pillar | Frequency | Audience Value |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Primary pillar | Every issue | Core value proposition |
| Secondary pillars | Rotating | Variety and breadth |
| Promotional | 1 in 5 issues max | Revenue/conversion |

---

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Newsletter Template
- **Monday**: Research and curate content
- **Tuesday**: Write draft
- **Wednesday**: Edit and design
- **Thursday**: Schedule and test
- **Friday/Saturday**: Send (test optimal day)

### Monthly Planning
- Theme for each month aligned with business goals
- Mix of evergreen and timely content
- Plan promotional emails in advance
- Schedule breaks for holidays

## Subscriber Growth Strategies

### Acquisition Channels
| Channel | Tactic | Expected Conversion |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Website | Pop-up, inline forms, exit intent | 1-5% of visitors |
| Social media | Lead magnets, pinned posts | 2-8% of clicks |
| Content upgrades | Bonus content for email | 5-15% |
| Cross-promotion | Newsletter swaps, shoutouts | 1-3% |
| Paid ads | Facebook/Google lead gen | Varies by cost |
| Referral program | Incentivized sharing | 5-15% of subs |

### Lead Magnets
- Checklists and templates
- Free tools or calculators
- Exclusive reports or data
- Mini-courses (email sequence)
- Resource libraries
- Free consultations

## Segmentation Strategy

### Segmentation Criteria
| Criterion | Use Case |
|-----------|----------|
| Engagement level | Re-engagement for inactive, rewards for active |
| Interest/topic | Personalized content recommendations |
| Signup source | Tailored onboarding |
| Purchase history | Upsell/cross-sell targeting |
| Geography | Time zone, local content |

### Welcome Sequence
1. **Email 1** (Immediate): Welcome + deliver lead magnet
2. **Email 2** (Day 2): Introduce yourself/brand story
3. **Email 3** (Day 4): Best content/resources
4. **Email 4** (Day 7): Ask about preferences
5. **Email 5** (Day 10): Soft CTA or offer
