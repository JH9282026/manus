# Newsletter Copywriting

Craft compelling email newsletters that build lasting subscriber relationships and drive consistent engagement.

## Overview

Design and write email newsletters covering content strategy, subject line optimization, layout patterns, voice development, and engagement metrics. Build sustainable newsletter programs that deliver value and drive business results.

## Quick Start: Newsletter Type Selection

| Type | Frequency | Focus | Reference |
|------|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Industry roundup | Weekly | Curated news and insights | `/references/content-strategy.md` |
| Educational series | Weekly/biweekly | Teaching and tutorials | `/references/content-strategy.md` |
| Product updates | Monthly | Features and announcements | `/references/writing-techniques.md` |
| Personal/thought leadership | Weekly | Opinion and analysis | `/references/writing-techniques.md` |
| Promotional | As needed | Sales and offers | `/references/writing-techniques.md` |

## Newsletter Anatomy

| Section | Purpose | Length |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Subject line | Drive opens | 6-10 words |
| Preview text | Support subject line | 40-90 characters |
| Header | Brand recognition | Logo + issue number |
| Intro/hook | Set context, build anticipation | 2-4 sentences |
| Main content | Deliver core value | 200-800 words |
| Secondary content | Additional resources | 100-300 words |
| CTA | Drive specific action | 1 clear button/link |
| Footer | Unsubscribe, social links | Standard legal |

## Subject Line Formulas

| Formula | Example | Best For |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Number + Benefit | "5 ways to reduce churn by 40%" | Educational |
| Question | "Are you making this SEO mistake?" | Curiosity |
| How to | "How to write emails that convert" | Tutorial |
| News hook | "Google just changed everything" | Industry news |
| Personal | "I almost quit last week" | Thought leadership |
| Urgency | "Last chance: 50% off expires tonight" | Promotional |

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Open rate | 20-25% | 30%+ |
| Click rate | 2-3% | 5%+ |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.5% | <0.2% |
| List growth rate | 2-5%/month | 5%+/month |
