# Growth Metrics & Analytics

Comprehensive guide to measuring and analyzing growth.

## Key Metrics

**Acquisition:**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- Payback period
- Traffic by source

**Activation:**
- Activation rate
- Time to value
- Onboarding completion

**Retention:**
- Day 1, 7, 30 retention
- DAU/MAU ratio
- Churn rate
- Cohort retention

**Revenue:**
- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV:CAC ratio

**Referral:**
- K-factor (viral coefficient)
- Referral rate
- Viral cycle time

## Cohort Analysis

Track user behavior over time by signup cohort to identify trends and improvements.

## Growth Accounting

```
New Users = Acquired - Churned + Resurrected

Quick Ratio = (New + Resurrected) / Churned
```
