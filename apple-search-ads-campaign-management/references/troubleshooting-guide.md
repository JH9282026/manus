# Apple Search Ads Troubleshooting Guide

Comprehensive troubleshooting guide for diagnosing and resolving common Apple Search Ads campaign issues, from low impressions to ad disapprovals.

---

## Low Impressions

### Symptoms
- Campaign or ad group receiving very few impressions
- Impressions significantly below expectations
- Brand campaign with <100 impressions/day
- Category campaign with <50 impressions/day

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Campaign/Ad Group Status**
- Verify campaign is active (not paused or ended)
- Verify ad group is active
- Check if budget is exhausted

**Check 2: Bid Competitiveness**
- Compare your max CPT bid to Apple's suggested bid
- If your bid is <50% of suggested bid, you're likely being outbid
- Review "Recommendations" page for bid guidance

**Check 3: Budget Availability**
- Check if daily budget is being hit early in the day
- If budget exhausted by noon, increase daily budget
- Review budget pacing throughout the day

**Check 4: Keyword Relevance**
- Search your keywords in App Store
- Check if your app appears in organic results
- If app doesn't appear organically, relevance may be too low for ads to show
- Apple won't show ads if app isn't relevant, regardless of bid

**Check 5: App Store Optimization (ASO)**
- Review app title, subtitle, keyword field
- Ensure keywords are present in app metadata
- Check if app category is appropriate
- Verify app has sufficient ratings/reviews

**Check 6: Targeting Settings**
- Review location targeting (too narrow?)
- Review demographic targeting (too restrictive?)
- Review device targeting (iPhone only when iPad also relevant?)
- Review customer type targeting (too narrow?)

**Check 7: Keyword Volume**
- Some keywords have very low search volume
- Check if keywords appear in App Store autocomplete (indicator of volume)
- Consider adding higher-volume keywords

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Increase Bids**
- Raise max CPT bid to at least Apple's suggested bid
- For brand campaigns, bid aggressively (suggested bid + 20-50%)
- Monitor for 24-48 hours to see impact

**Solution 2: Increase Budget**
- If budget is being exhausted, increase daily budget
- Aim for budget to last full 24 hours
- Monitor budget pacing

**Solution 3: Improve ASO**
- Add target keywords to app title, subtitle, or keyword field
- Improve app quality (ratings, reviews)
- Optimize app category selection
- Wait 24-48 hours after metadata changes for indexing

**Solution 4: Broaden Targeting**
- Expand location targeting
- Remove demographic restrictions (unless critical)
- Target both iPhone and iPad (if app supports both)
- Change customer type to "All Users"

**Solution 5: Add More Keywords**
- Expand keyword list with related terms
- Add broad match keywords
- Enable Search Match for automatic discovery

**Solution 6: Check for Account/Campaign Issues**
- Verify payment method is valid
- Check for any account-level issues or restrictions
- Contact Apple Ads support if technical issue suspected

## Low Tap-Through Rate (TTR)

### Symptoms
- TTR <1% for any campaign
- TTR <3% for brand campaign
- TTR significantly below category benchmarks

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Ad Creative Quality**
- Review app icon (is it compelling?)
- Review first 3 screenshots (are they engaging?)
- Review app rating (is it <4.0?)
- Review app name and subtitle (are they clear and appealing?)

**Check 2: Keyword Relevance**
- Are keywords truly relevant to your app?
- Search keywords in App Store - does your app fit among results?
- Review search terms report for irrelevant traffic

**Check 3: Ad Position**
- Low bids may result in lower ad positions
- Lower positions typically have lower TTR
- Check if you're being outbid

**Check 4: Competition**
- Review competitors appearing for same keywords
- Are competitors' apps more appealing (better icon, ratings, etc.)?
- Is your app differentiated?

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Improve App Icon**
- Test new icon designs
- Ensure icon is clear, recognizable, stands out
- Follow App Store icon design best practices
- Note: Icon changes require app update

**Solution 2: Optimize Screenshots**
- Put most compelling screenshots first
- Use text overlays to highlight key benefits
- Show app in action (not just marketing graphics)
- Create custom product pages with optimized screenshots

**Solution 3: Improve App Rating**
- Encourage satisfied users to leave reviews
- Address issues causing negative reviews
- Respond to reviews professionally
- Target rating of 4.5+ for best performance

**Solution 4: Refine Keywords**
- Pause irrelevant keywords with low TTR
- Focus on keywords where your app is truly competitive
- Add negative keywords to filter irrelevant traffic

**Solution 5: Increase Bids**
- Higher bids may improve ad position
- Better position can improve TTR
- Test bid increases and monitor TTR impact

**Solution 6: Use Custom Product Pages**
- Create custom product pages tailored to specific keyword themes
- Align ad creative with user search intent
- Test different screenshot variations

**Solution 7: Improve App Name/Subtitle**
- Ensure app name clearly communicates value
- Use subtitle to highlight key benefit
- Make it immediately clear what app does
- Note: Requires app update

- Conversion rate <5% for any campaign
- Conversion rate <20% for brand campaign
- High taps but few installs

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Product Page Quality**
- Review full product page (screenshots, description, reviews)
- Is value proposition clear?
- Are screenshots compelling?
- Is description well-written?

- Read recent reviews (are they negative?)
- Check for common complaints

**Check 3: App Pricing**
- Is app paid when competitors are free?
- Are in-app purchases expensive?
- Is pricing clearly communicated?

**Check 4: Keyword-to-App Relevance**
- Are users finding what they expected?
- Review search terms report
- Are keywords misleading or too broad?

**Check 5: Competition**
- Review "You Might Also Like" section on your product page
- Are competitors more appealing?
- Is your app differentiated?

**Check 6: Technical Issues**
- Check if app has recent crashes or bugs (reviews will indicate)
- Verify app is compatible with current iOS version
- Check if app size is very large (may deter downloads)

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Optimize Screenshots**
- Redesign screenshots to better showcase value
- Use text overlays to highlight benefits
- Show key features in first 3 screenshots
- Create custom product pages for different audiences

**Solution 2: Improve App Description**
- Rewrite description to be more compelling
- Lead with key benefits (not features)
- Use clear, concise language
- Include social proof (user count, awards, etc.)

**Solution 3: Improve App Rating**
- Address issues causing negative reviews
- Encourage satisfied users to leave positive reviews
- Respond to negative reviews professionally
- Fix bugs and crashes

**Solution 4: Add App Preview Videos**
- Create compelling app preview videos
- Show app in action
- Demonstrate key features
- Keep videos short (15-30 seconds)

**Solution 5: Adjust Pricing Strategy**
- Consider free with IAP instead of paid upfront
- Offer free trial for subscription apps
- Ensure pricing is competitive
- Clearly communicate value for price

**Solution 6: Refine Keyword Targeting**
- Pause keywords with high taps but low conversions
- Focus on keywords where your app is truly relevant
- Add negative keywords to filter unqualified traffic
- Use exact match for high-intent keywords

**Solution 7: Use Custom Product Pages**
- Create custom product pages aligned with keyword themes
- Show users exactly what they searched for
- Improve relevance between search and landing page
- Test different variations

**Solution 8: Improve App Quality**
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Improve app performance
- Add requested features
- Enhance user experience
- Note: Requires app update

## High Cost Per Tap (CPT)

### Symptoms
- CPT >$3 for most categories (except finance, games)
- CPT significantly above category benchmarks
- Budget depleting quickly with few taps

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Bid Settings**
- Review max CPT bids
- Are you bidding significantly above suggested bid?
- Are you in a bidding war?

**Check 2: Keyword Competition**
- Search keywords in App Store
- Count number of ads shown (high competition = multiple ads)
- Review quality of competing apps

**Check 3: Keyword Relevance**
- Are keywords too broad or generic?
- Are keywords highly competitive?
- Review search terms report for expensive terms

**Check 4: Match Type**
- Are you using broad match for expensive keywords?
- Broad match can trigger for expensive variations

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Lower Bids**
- Reduce max CPT bids to more reasonable levels
- Start with Apple's suggested bid
- Accept lower impression share if necessary
- Monitor impact on impressions and conversions

**Solution 2: Refine Keyword Targeting**
- Pause very expensive keywords (unless converting well)
- Focus on less competitive, more specific keywords
- Use long-tail keywords (lower competition, lower cost)
- Add negative keywords to prevent expensive broad matches

**Solution 3: Change Match Types**
- Switch expensive broad match keywords to exact match
- More control over what triggers ads
- Prevents expensive irrelevant variations

**Solution 4: Improve TTR**
- Higher TTR can improve Quality Score equivalent
- Better relevance can lower CPT
- Optimize app icon, screenshots, rating
- Use custom product pages

**Solution 5: Target Less Competitive Keywords**
- Identify niche, long-tail keywords
- Target specific features or use cases
- Avoid highly competitive generic terms
- Build volume through many smaller keywords

**Solution 6: Geographic Targeting**
- Test less competitive geographic markets
- International markets often have lower CPTs
- Ensure app is localized for target markets

## High Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)

### Symptoms
- CPA >50% of user LTV
- CPA significantly above category benchmarks
- Unprofitable campaigns

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Conversion Rate**
- Is conversion rate low? (See "Low Conversion Rate" section)
- Low conversion rate is often primary driver of high CPA

**Check 2: Cost Per Tap**
- Is CPT high? (See "High Cost Per Tap" section)
- High CPT contributes to high CPA

**Check 3: Keyword Quality**
- Review search terms report
- Identify keywords with high spend, low conversions
- Are keywords truly relevant?

**Check 4: Campaign Type**
- Competitor campaigns typically have higher CPAs
- Discovery campaigns may have higher CPAs initially
- Is high CPA expected for this campaign type?

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Improve Conversion Rate**
- Optimize product page (screenshots, description, videos)
- Improve app rating and reviews
- Use custom product pages
- See "Low Conversion Rate" section for detailed solutions

**Solution 2: Reduce Cost Per Tap**
- Lower bids
- Refine keyword targeting
- Improve TTR
- See "High Cost Per Tap" section for detailed solutions

**Solution 3: Pause Underperforming Keywords**
- Export search terms report
- Filter for: High spend, low/zero conversions
- Pause or add as negative keywords
- Reallocate budget to better-performing keywords

**Solution 4: Focus on High-Performing Segments**
- Identify best-performing:
  - Keywords
  - Ad groups
  - Campaigns
  - Geographies
  - Devices
- Allocate more budget to winners
- Pause or reduce budget for losers

**Solution 5: Improve Keyword Relevance**
- Focus on keywords where your app is truly competitive
- Use exact match for high-intent keywords
- Add negative keywords liberally
- Align keywords with app's core value proposition

**Solution 6: Use CPA Goal Bidding**
- Set target CPA in ad group settings
- Apple's algorithm will optimize bids to meet target
- Requires sufficient conversion data (20+ conversions)

**Solution 7: Test Custom Product Pages**
- Create custom product pages for different keyword themes
- Improve relevance between search and landing page
- Can significantly improve conversion rates
- Monitor performance in App Analytics

## Ad Disapprovals

### Symptoms
- Ad status shows "Rejected" or "Disapproved"
- Today Tab ad not approved
- Custom product page rejected

### Common Rejection Reasons

**1. Prohibited Content**
- Violent, offensive, or sexually explicit content
- Misleading claims or false information
- Content that violates App Store Review Guidelines

**2. Today Tab Specific Issues**
- Pricing, offers, or ranking claims in screenshots or promotional text
- Inappropriate content
- Not localized in target region's default language
- Missing iPhone assets

**3. Custom Product Page Issues**
- Screenshots not from actual app (mockups)
- Misleading screenshots
- Poor quality assets
- Non-compliance with guidelines

**4. Metadata Issues**
- App name, subtitle, or description violates policies
- Inappropriate keywords

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Review Rejection Reason**
- Check email from Apple Ads
- Review rejection details in Apple Ads dashboard
- Understand specific issue cited

**Solution 2: Review Apple Advertising Policies**
- Read Apple Advertising Policies thoroughly
- Ensure all assets comply
- Pay special attention to Today Tab guidelines if applicable

**Solution 3: Revise Assets**
- Remove prohibited content
- Replace misleading screenshots
- Update promotional text to remove pricing/ranking claims
- Ensure localization is correct

**Solution 4: Resubmit**
- Make necessary changes
- Resubmit ad or custom product page for review
- Wait for approval (typically 24-48 hours)

**Solution 5: Contact Apple Ads Support**
- If rejection reason is unclear, contact support
- Provide details and ask for clarification
- Follow guidance provided

### Prevention

**Before Submitting:**
- Review all assets against Apple Advertising Policies
- Ensure screenshots are from actual app
- Remove any pricing, offers, or ranking claims (for Today Tab)
- Verify localization is correct
- Check that all assets are high quality
- Test deep links thoroughly (if using)

## Budget Pacing Issues

### Symptoms
- Daily budget exhausted early in the day
- Inconsistent daily spend
- Budget not being spent

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Budget Exhaustion Time**
- Review when daily budget is typically exhausted
- If exhausted before noon, budget is too low for bid levels

**Check 2: Bid Levels**
- Are bids very high relative to budget?
- High bids can deplete budget quickly

**Check 3: Impression Volume**
- Is campaign receiving very high impressions?
- High volume can deplete budget quickly

**Check 4: Budget Not Spending**
- Are impressions very low?
- Are bids too low?
- Is targeting too narrow?

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Increase Daily Budget (If Exhausting Early)**
- Raise daily budget to last full 24 hours
- Aim for budget to last at least 18-20 hours
- Monitor pacing over several days

**Solution 2: Lower Bids (If Exhausting Early)**
- Reduce max CPT bids
- Slower budget depletion
- May reduce impression share but improve pacing

**Solution 3: Increase Bids (If Budget Not Spending)**
- Raise max CPT bids to be more competitive
- Should increase impressions and spend
- Monitor for 24-48 hours

**Solution 4: Adjust Targeting (If Budget Not Spending)**
- Broaden targeting (location, demographics, device)
- Add more keywords
- Enable Search Match

**Solution 5: Use Campaign Budget Optimization**
- Let Apple optimize budget allocation across ad groups
- Can improve overall efficiency
- Requires sufficient budget and conversion data

## Search Match Not Performing

### Symptoms
- Search Match ad group has very low impressions
- Search Match generating irrelevant traffic
- Search Match CPA much higher than other ad groups

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: App Metadata Quality**
- Search Match relies on app metadata (title, subtitle, keywords)
- Is metadata optimized?
- Are keywords in metadata relevant?

**Check 2: Bid Competitiveness**
- Is Search Match bid competitive?
- Compare to suggested bid

**Check 3: Negative Keywords**
- Are there too many negative keywords?
- Overly aggressive negatives can limit Search Match

**Check 4: Search Terms Report**
- Review what search terms are triggering Search Match
- Are they relevant?
- Are they converting?

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Improve App Metadata**
- Optimize app title, subtitle, keyword field
- Include relevant keywords
- Ensure metadata accurately describes app
- Wait 24-48 hours after changes for re-indexing

**Solution 2: Increase Search Match Bid**
- Raise max CPT bid for Search Match ad group
- Should increase impressions
- Monitor for 24-48 hours

**Solution 3: Review and Adjust Negative Keywords**
- Remove overly broad negative keywords
- Keep only truly irrelevant negatives
- Allow Search Match more room to discover

**Solution 4: Mine Search Terms Report**
- Export search terms for Search Match ad group
- Add high-performing terms as exact match keywords in other ad groups
- Add irrelevant terms as negative keywords
- Continuous optimization process

**Solution 5: Give It Time**
- Search Match improves over time as Apple's algorithm learns
- Requires at least 2-4 weeks of data
- Don't judge performance too early

## Conversion Tracking Issues

### Symptoms
- Conversions not being reported
- Conversion numbers seem incorrect
- Discrepancy between Apple Ads and MMP (Mobile Measurement Partner)

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Attribution Window**
- Apple Ads uses 30-day click attribution by default
- MMPs may use different windows
- Discrepancies are normal

**Check 2: View-Through Conversions**
- Apple Ads includes view-through conversions (users who saw ad but didn't tap)
- MMPs may not track these
- Can cause discrepancies

**Check 3: Redownloads**
- Apple Ads counts redownloads (users who previously had app)
- Some MMPs may not count these as conversions
- Can cause discrepancies

**Check 4: Time Lag**
- Conversions may be reported with delay
- Check if data is recent (last 24-48 hours)
- Wait for data to stabilize

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Understand Attribution Differences**
- Accept that Apple Ads and MMP numbers will differ
- Use Apple Ads data for campaign optimization
- Use MMP data for cross-channel attribution
- Don't expect perfect match

**Solution 2: Align Attribution Windows**
- If possible, set MMP to use same attribution window as Apple Ads
- Reduces discrepancies
- Check MMP documentation

**Solution 3: Focus on Trends, Not Absolute Numbers**
- Use conversion data to identify trends
- Which campaigns are improving?
- Which keywords are performing best?
- Relative performance is more important than absolute numbers

**Solution 4: Contact Apple Ads Support**
- If conversions are completely missing or clearly incorrect, contact support
- Provide details and examples
- May be technical issue

## Custom Product Page Not Showing

### Symptoms
- Selected custom product page in ad group but default page is showing
- Custom product page not appearing in ads

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Approval Status**
- Is custom product page approved in App Store Connect?
- Check status in App Store Connect > Custom Product Pages

**Check 2: Localization**
- Is custom product page localized for target region?
- Custom product page must be available in campaign's target language

**Check 3: Ad Group Configuration**
- Verify custom product page is selected in ad group settings
- Check that selection was saved

**Check 4: Today Tab Requirements**
- Today Tab ads REQUIRE custom product page
- Verify custom product page is selected
- Verify it has iPhone assets

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Wait for Approval**
- If custom product page is pending approval, wait
- Typically 24-48 hours
- Cannot use until approved

**Solution 2: Add Localization**
- Add localization for target region in App Store Connect
- Submit for approval
- Wait for approval

**Solution 3: Reselect Custom Product Page**
- Edit ad group
- Reselect custom product page from dropdown
- Save changes
- Wait 1-2 hours for changes to take effect

**Solution 4: Create New Custom Product Page**
- If existing custom product page has issues, create new one
- Submit for approval
- Select in ad group once approved

## Keyword Not Showing Ads

### Symptoms
- Specific keyword has zero impressions
- Keyword shows "Low Search Volume" or similar status
- Keyword not triggering ads despite active campaign

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Keyword Status**
- Check keyword status in Apple Ads dashboard
- Look for warnings or errors

**Check 2: Search Volume**
- Does keyword appear in App Store autocomplete?
- If not, search volume may be too low

**Check 3: Relevance**
- Search keyword in App Store
- Does your app appear in organic results?
- If not, relevance may be too low for ads to show

**Check 4: Bid Competitiveness**
- Is bid competitive for this keyword?
- Check suggested bid

**Check 5: Negative Keywords**
- Is keyword accidentally added as negative keyword?
- Check campaign and ad group negative keyword lists

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Improve Relevance**
- Add keyword to app metadata (title, subtitle, keyword field)
- Improve ASO for this keyword
- Wait 24-48 hours for re-indexing

**Solution 2: Increase Bid**
- Raise max CPT bid for this keyword
- Try bidding above suggested bid
- Monitor for 24-48 hours

**Solution 3: Remove from Negative Keywords**
- Check if keyword is in negative keyword list
- Remove if accidentally added

**Solution 4: Accept Low Volume**
- Some keywords have very low search volume
- May not be worth targeting
- Focus on higher-volume alternatives

**Solution 5: Use Broad Match or Search Match**
- Instead of exact match, try broad match
- Or rely on Search Match to capture variations
- May get impressions for related searches

## Competitor Keywords Not Showing

### Symptoms
- Competitor keyword has zero impressions
- Can't seem to win competitor keyword auctions

### Diagnosis

**Check 1: Relevance**
- Apple has strict relevance requirements for competitor keywords
- Your app must be truly relevant to competitor's category
- Search competitor keyword in App Store - does your app appear organically?

**Check 2: Bid Competitiveness**
- Competitor keywords are highly competitive
- Competitor likely bidding aggressively on their own brand
- Your bid may not be competitive

**Check 3: App Quality**
- Is your app quality (rating, reviews) comparable to competitor?
- Lower quality apps may not win competitor auctions

### Solutions

**Solution 1: Ensure Relevance**
- Your app must be in same category as competitor
- Must offer similar functionality
- If not truly relevant, Apple won't show your ads
- Focus on competitors you're truly comparable to

**Solution 2: Bid Aggressively**
- Competitor keywords require very high bids
- Try bidding 2-3x suggested bid
- Monitor CPA closely - may not be profitable

**Solution 3: Improve App Quality**
- Improve app rating to 4.5+
- Encourage positive reviews
- Fix issues causing negative reviews
- Better quality improves chances of winning auctions

**Solution 4: Accept Limitations**
- Some competitor keywords may not be winnable
- Competitor has advantage on their own brand
- Focus on category keywords instead
- Or target smaller competitors

**Solution 5: Use Product Page Ads**
- Instead of search results ads, try product page ads
- Appear on competitor's app page
- May be easier to win than search results

## General Troubleshooting Process

### Step 1: Identify the Problem
- What specific metric is underperforming?
- How far from benchmark or target?
- Is it affecting all campaigns or specific ones?

### Step 2: Gather Data
- Export relevant reports (campaign, ad group, keyword, search terms)
- Review metrics over time (not just one day)
- Look for patterns and trends

### Step 3: Diagnose Root Cause
- Use diagnostic checklists above
- Identify most likely cause(s)
- Prioritize issues to address

### Step 4: Implement Solution
- Make one change at a time (for clear attribution)
- Document what you changed and when
- Set expectations for how long to wait for results

### Step 5: Monitor Results
- Wait appropriate time for data (usually 24-48 hours minimum)
- Compare before and after metrics
- Assess if solution worked

### Step 6: Iterate
- If solution worked, scale it
- If solution didn't work, try next solution
- Continuous optimization process

### When to Contact Apple Ads Support

- Technical issues (ads not serving despite correct setup)
- Billing issues
- Account access issues
- Unclear rejection reasons
- Suspected bugs or platform issues
- After exhausting self-service troubleshooting

**How to Contact:**
- Apple Ads Help Center: https://searchads.apple.com/help
- Email support through Apple Ads dashboard
- Provide detailed information: account ID, campaign ID, specific issue, steps taken
