# Brand Strategy & Positioning

Develop the long-term strategic foundation for successful brand building.

## Overview

Brand strategy defines how your brand will be perceived in the marketplace—what it stands for, how it differentiates, and how it connects with customers. It encompasses purpose, values, personality, positioning, and the overall experience delivered across touchpoints.

## Strategy vs Identity vs Marketing

| Element | Definition | Timeframe |
|---------|------------|-----------|
| Strategy | Why we exist, who we serve, how we're different | Long-term (years) |
| Identity | Visual and verbal expression of strategy | Medium-term |
| Marketing | Tactical execution and communications | Short-term |

## Brand Strategy Components

### Purpose, Vision, Mission, Values

| Component | Question | Example |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Purpose | Why do we exist? | Patagonia: "Save our home planet" |
| Vision | Where are we going? | Amazon: "Earth's most customer-centric company" |
| Mission | What do we do? | Google: "Organize world's information" |
| Values | What do we believe? | 3-5 core principles |

## Target Audience Framework

### Research Methods

| Method | Type | Best For |
|--------|------|----------|
| Surveys | Quantitative | Broad patterns |
| Interviews | Qualitative | Deep motivation |
| Focus Groups | Qualitative | Group dynamics |
| Observation | Behavioral | Real actions |
| Social Listening | Ongoing | Sentiment, language |

### Persona Components

- Demographics (age, income, location)
- Psychographics (values, attitudes, lifestyle)
- Goals and aspirations
- Pain points and frustrations
- Behaviors and decision-making

## Competitive Analysis

### SWOT Framework

- **Strengths**: Internal advantages
- **Weaknesses**: Internal limitations
- **Opportunities**: External possibilities
- **Threats**: External risks

### Competitor Categories

- **Direct**: Same product, same audience
- **Indirect**: Same need, different solution
- **Aspirational**: Excellence standards to learn from
