### Pre-Production Planning

**Timeline:**
| Phase | Typical Duration |
|-------|------------------|
| Planning | 1-2 weeks |
| Customer recruitment | 2-4 weeks |
| Scheduling | 1-2 weeks |
| Production | 1-2 days |
| Post-production | 2-4 weeks |

**Budget Considerations:**
- Professional production: $3,000-$15,000+ per video
- Mid-level production: $1,000-$3,000 per video
- DIY with quality equipment: $100-$500 per video
- Incentives/compensation for participants
- Travel if filming on location
- Post-production and editing

**Resource Requirements:**
- Video production team or equipment
- Location(s) for filming
- Release forms and legal review
- Customer relationship management
- Project management
