### Question Framework

**Introduction and Context:**
- Tell us about yourself and your role
- Describe your company/situation
- What were you trying to accomplish?

**The Problem:**
- What challenges were you facing?
- What was the impact of this problem?
- What had you tried before?
- Why was solving this important?

**The Decision:**
- How did you find out about [product/service]?
- What made you choose us over alternatives?
- What were your initial expectations?

**The Experience:**
- Describe the implementation/onboarding
- What was working with [company] like?
- Were there any surprises?
- How did your team respond?

**The Results:**
- What results have you achieved?
- Can you share specific numbers or metrics?
- How has this impacted your work/life?
- What benefits were unexpected?

**The Recommendation:**
- What would you say to someone considering this?
- Would you recommend us? Why?
- What advice would you give to others in your situation?
