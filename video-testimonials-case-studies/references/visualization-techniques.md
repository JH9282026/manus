### Visualization Techniques

**Animated Statistics:**
- Numbers that count up
- Progress bars
- Pie charts building
- Line graphs drawing

**Infographic Style:**
- Icon-based representations
- Simplified comparisons
- Visual metaphors
- Clean, branded design

**Integration with Interview:**
- Show data as customer discusses it
- Use graphics to emphasize key points
- Don't overwhelm with data
- Keep visuals simple and clear
