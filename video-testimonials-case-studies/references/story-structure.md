### Story Structure

**Opening Hook (0-15 seconds):**
- Compelling result or statement
- Question that creates curiosity
- Visual that captures attention
- Establishes value proposition

**Context and Problem (15-60 seconds):**
- Customer introduction
- Situation before solution
- Pain points and challenges
- Stakes and importance

**Solution (30-60 seconds):**
- Decision to choose your product/service
- Implementation experience
- Working relationship
- Key features/benefits experienced

**Results (30-90 seconds):**
- Measurable outcomes
- Unexpected benefits
- Emotional impact
- Specific examples

**Conclusion (15-30 seconds):**
- Recommendation
- Call to action
- Final compelling statement
