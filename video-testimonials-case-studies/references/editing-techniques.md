### Editing Techniques

**Cutting for Impact:**
- Remove filler words and pauses
- Tighten pacing without losing meaning
- Use reaction shots to cover edits
- J and L cuts for smooth transitions

**Using B-Roll:**
- Cover edits in interview
- Illustrate points being made
- Break up talking head
- Add visual interest

**Graphics and Text:**
- Name/title lower thirds
- Key statistics highlighted
- Pull quotes for emphasis
- Subtle branding elements

**Music:**
- Underscore emotion appropriately
- Don't overpower dialogue
- License properly
- Match energy to content
