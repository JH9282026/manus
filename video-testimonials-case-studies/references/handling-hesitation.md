### Handling Hesitation

**Common Objections:**

**"I'm not comfortable on camera":**
- Emphasize coaching and support provided
- Offer to show examples of other testimonials
- Explain editing makes everyone look good
- Offer practice session before filming

**"I need approval from my company":**
- Provide template language for internal approval
- Offer flexibility on what can/cannot be said
- Assure review process before publication
- Consider anonymization if needed

**"I don't have time":**
- Emphasize minimal time commitment (typically 2-4 hours)
- Offer flexible scheduling
- Provide remote options if appropriate
- Handle logistics (location, travel, etc.)

**"What do I get in return?":**
- Exposure and promotion of their brand
- Professional video for their own use
- Strengthened partnership relationship
- Consider appropriate incentives
