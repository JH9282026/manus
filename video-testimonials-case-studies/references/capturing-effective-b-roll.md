### Capturing Effective B-Roll

**Technical Approach:**
- Shoot in same quality/settings as interview
- Capture multiple angles of key items
- Include wide, medium, and close-up shots
- Movement adds production value

**Practical Tips:**
- Over-capture (edit from plenty)
- 10-15 seconds per shot minimum
- Stabilization essential
- Natural actions, not posed

**What to Avoid:**
- Staged, awkward actions
- Empty environments
- Unrelated generic footage
- Shots that won't edit with interview
