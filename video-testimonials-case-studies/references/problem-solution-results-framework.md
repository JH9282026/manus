### Problem-Solution-Results Framework

**The Problem:**
- What challenge did they face?
- What was the impact?
- What was at stake?
- Why was this urgent/important?

**The Solution:**
- What did they choose and why?
- How was it implemented?
- What was the experience?
- What made it work?

**The Results:**
- What measurable outcomes occurred?
- What additional benefits emerged?
- How has it changed their situation?
- What does the future look like?
