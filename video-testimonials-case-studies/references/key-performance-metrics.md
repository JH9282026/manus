### Key Performance Metrics

**Engagement Metrics:**
- View count (total and unique)
- Watch time / Average view duration
- Drop-off points in video
- Engagement rate (likes, shares, comments)
- Click-through rate (CTR) on CTAs

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Form submissions after viewing
- Demo requests influenced by video
- Proposal acceptance rates when video included
- Sales cycle acceleration
- Deal win rates with testimonial usage

**Attribution Metrics:**
- Assisted conversions in path
- First-touch vs. last-touch attribution
- Multi-touch attribution modeling
- Survey responses ("How did you hear about us?")
