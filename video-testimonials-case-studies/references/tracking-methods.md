### Tracking Methods

**Video Platform Analytics:**
- Wistia, Vimeo, YouTube analytics
- Heat maps showing viewing patterns
- Viewer-level engagement data
- Integration with marketing platforms

**Website Analytics:**
- Google Analytics events
- Page-specific behavior after video view
- Goal completions attributed to video pages
- Behavior flow analysis

**Sales Attribution:**
- CRM tracking of video views
- Deal-level video engagement
- Win/loss analysis with video usage
- Sales rep feedback
