### Interview Techniques

**Open-Ended Questions:**
- Start questions with "Tell me about..." or "Describe..."
- Avoid yes/no questions
- Follow up with "Can you elaborate on that?"
- Let silence encourage more detail

**Active Listening:**
- Don't interrupt valuable tangents
- Note topics for follow-up
- React naturally to build connection
- Circle back to important points

**Getting Specific Details:**
- "Can you give me an example?"
- "What specifically made that different?"
- "Can you put a number on that?"
- "What did that look like day-to-day?"

**Emotional Content:**
- "How did that make you feel?"
- "What was the moment you knew it was working?"
- "What would it have meant if this hadn't worked?"
- "What are you most proud of?"
