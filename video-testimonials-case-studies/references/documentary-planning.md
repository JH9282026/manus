### Documentary Planning

**Story Arc Development:**
1. What was the situation before?
2. What problem needed solving?
3. Why did they choose you?
4. What was the implementation like?
5. What results were achieved?
6. What advice would they give others?

**Shot Planning:**
- Interview setup and locations
- B-roll requirements
- Data/results visualization
- Product/service demonstration
- Environmental context shots
