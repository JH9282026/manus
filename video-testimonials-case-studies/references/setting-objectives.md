### Setting Objectives

**Define Success:**
- What do you want viewers to do after watching?
- What objections or concerns should be addressed?
- What differentiators should be highlighted?
- How will this video be used (website, sales, ads)?

**Identify Target Audience:**
- Who needs to be convinced?
- What are their concerns and priorities?
- What language and references resonate with them?
- What type of customer story will they relate to?
