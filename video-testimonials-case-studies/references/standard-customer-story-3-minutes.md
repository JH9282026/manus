### Standard Customer Story (3 minutes)

**Structure:**
```
0-15 sec: Opening hook
15-45 sec: Customer introduction and context
45-90 sec: Problem and challenge
90-120 sec: Solution selection and implementation
120-150 sec: Results achieved
150-180 sec: Recommendation and conclusion
```

**Question Flow:**
1. Tell us about yourself and your company
2. What challenges were you facing?
3. Why did you choose [company]?
4. What results have you seen?
5. What would you tell others?
