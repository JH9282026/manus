# Operational and Commercial Due Diligence

## Commercial Due Diligence

### Market Assessment
- Market size and growth rate (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Industry lifecycle stage (growth, mature, declining)
- Competitive landscape and market share dynamics
- Barrier to entry analysis
- Regulatory environment and trends

### Customer Analysis
- Top customer interviews (satisfaction, switching risk)
- Revenue concentration and dependency
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV)
- Churn rate analysis and trending
- Net revenue retention metrics
- Contract terms and renewal patterns

### Competitive Position
- Market share relative to competitors
- Competitive advantages (sustainable vs temporary)
- Pricing power assessment
- Win/loss analysis for recent deals
- Differentiation clarity and defensibility

## Operational Due Diligence

### Process Assessment
- Document core operational workflows
- Identify manual processes ripe for automation
- Assess capacity utilization and scalability
- Review quality control and error rates
- Evaluate supply chain resilience

### Technology Assessment
- Architecture review (modern vs legacy)
- Technical debt quantification
- Security audit and vulnerability assessment
- Scalability testing results
- IP ownership and protection
- Development team capabilities and practices
- Disaster recovery and business continuity

### Human Capital Assessment
- Organization structure and reporting lines
- Key person identification and dependency
- Employee turnover rates by department
- Compensation benchmarking
- Employment agreements and non-competes
- Cultural assessment and integration risks
- Pending HR issues or litigation

---

## Legal Due Diligence

### Contract Review Priorities
1. Customer contracts (terms, renewals, change-of-control)
2. Supplier agreements (dependencies, exclusivity)
3. Employment agreements (key persons, non-competes)
4. Lease agreements (terms, renewal options, transfer provisions)
5. IP licenses (restrictions, expiration, sub-licensing rights)
6. Partnership/JV agreements

### Compliance Assessment
- Regulatory compliance history
- Data privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA)
- Environmental compliance
- Industry-specific regulations
- Pending or threatened litigation
- Government investigations or audits

## Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Scoring Matrix

| Probability / Impact | Low Impact | Medium Impact | High Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| High probability | Medium risk | High risk | Critical risk |
| Medium probability | Low risk | Medium risk | High risk |
| Low probability | Minimal risk | Low risk | Medium risk |

### Risk Mitigation in Deal Structure
- **Representations and warranties**: Seller confirms specific facts
- **Indemnification**: Seller covers specific identified risks
- **Escrow/holdback**: Portion of price held for claims
- **Earnout**: Tie portion of price to future performance
- **Purchase price adjustment**: Adjust for working capital or identified items
- **Insurance**: Representations and warranties insurance (RWI)
