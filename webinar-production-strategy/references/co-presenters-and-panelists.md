Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

## Co-Presenters and Panelists


### Roles in Multi-Presenter Webinars


#### Host/Moderator
- Welcomes audience
- Introduces speakers
- Manages time
- Facilitates Q&A
- Handles technical issues


#### Presenter/Speaker
- Delivers main content
- Answers relevant questions
- Stays on time
- Engages with audience


#### Technical Producer
- Manages platform
- Handles slides
- Monitors chat
- Troubleshoots issues
- Supports speakers


### Coordinating Multiple Speakers


#### Pre-Webinar
- Assign clear roles
- Define transitions
- Rehearse together
- Align on messaging
- Prepare backup plans


#### During Webinar
- Clear hand-off signals
- Support each other visually
- Monitor each other's audio
- Cover for technical issues


#### Panel Management
- Brief panelists on format
- Prepare questions in advance
- Balance speaking time
- Moderate effectively
- Keep on topic and time
