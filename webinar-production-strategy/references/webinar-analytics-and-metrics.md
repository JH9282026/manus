## Webinar Analytics and Metrics


### Key Performance Indicators


#### Registration Metrics
- Total registrations
- Registration conversion rate
- Cost per registration
- Source attribution


#### Attendance Metrics
- Show-up rate (registrations to attendees)
- Live vs. replay attendance
- Average watch time
- Drop-off points


#### Engagement Metrics
- Poll participation rate
- Chat activity
- Questions submitted
- Resource downloads


#### Conversion Metrics
- CTA click-through rate
- Conversion rate
- Revenue generated
- ROI calculation


### Analytics Tools

- Platform built-in analytics
- UTM tracking for registration sources
- CRM integration for lead tracking
- Post-webinar surveys


### Optimization Through Data

Review after each webinar:
- What topics drove registration?
- Where did attendees drop off?
- What questions were asked?
- What content resonated?
- What objections emerged?
