Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

## Webinar Promotion and Registration


### Landing Page Optimization


#### Essential Elements
- Compelling headline
- Clear value proposition
- Speaker credentials
- Date, time, and duration
- Registration form
- Social proof


#### Registration Form Best Practices
- Minimum required fields
- Email essential, phone optional
- Qualification questions if needed
- Clear privacy statement
- Confirmation message


### Promotion Channels


#### Email Marketing
- Announcement email
- Reminder sequence (1 week, 1 day, 1 hour)
- Segmented outreach
- Partner promotions


#### Social Media
- Organic posts
- Paid promotion
- Speaker amplification
- Event countdowns


#### Website
- Banner or pop-up
- Blog posts
- Resource page listing
- Homepage feature


#### Paid Advertising
- Facebook/Instagram ads
- LinkedIn ads (B2B)
- Google search ads
- Retargeting campaigns


### Promotion Timeline

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 3-4 weeks before | Announcement, landing page live |
| 2-3 weeks before | Email sequence begins |
| 1-2 weeks before | Social media ramp-up |
| 1 week before | Reminder email |
| 1 day before | Final reminder |
| 1 hour before | Last-chance reminder |
