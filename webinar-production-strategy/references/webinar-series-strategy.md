## Webinar Series Strategy


### Building a Webinar Series

Series webinars build momentum and audience:


#### Series Benefits
- Recurring audience anticipation
- Compound learning for attendees
- Easier promotion over time
- Community building
- Predictable content calendar


#### Series Formats

**Educational Series**:
- Progressive curriculum
- Weekly or bi-weekly sessions
- Builds on previous content
- Certificate or completion tracking

**Interview/Guest Series**:
- Different expert each session
- Consistent format and questions
- Theme connecting episodes
- Cross-promotional opportunities

**Update/News Series**:
- Regular industry updates
- Recurring schedule (monthly)
- Timely, current content
- Community discussion


### Series Planning

1. Define series theme and scope
2. Plan episode topics in advance
3. Schedule consistent timing
4. Create series branding
5. Build email sequences
6. Coordinate promotion across episodes
