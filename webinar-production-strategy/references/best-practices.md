# Best Practices

Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

---

## Best Practices

1. **Audio first**: Invest in quality microphone before camera
2. **Rehearse fully**: Technical and content run-through
3. **Engage every 10 minutes**: Polls, questions, interaction
4. **Start and end on time**: Respect attendee schedules
5. **Record everything**: Create replay and repurposing assets
6. **Follow up immediately**: While attention is high
7. **Collect feedback**: Improve continuously
8. **Repurpose content**: Maximize webinar investment
9. **Build series**: Recurring webinars build audience
10. **Track metrics**: Measure and optimize performance
