# Live Vs Automated Webinars

Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

---

## Live vs. Automated Webinars


### Live Webinars


#### Advantages
- Real interaction and Q&A
- Authentic energy and engagement
- Timely, current content
- Builds real relationships


#### Challenges
- Scheduling constraints
- Technical risk
- Energy requirement
- Limited scalability


### Automated/Evergreen Webinars


#### Advantages
- 24/7 availability
- Global timezone coverage
- Consistent performance
- Scalable without effort


#### Challenges
- Perceived as less authentic
- Limited true interaction
- May feel dated over time
- Ethical considerations


### Hybrid Approaches

- Live webinar recorded for replay
- Live Q&A periods within automated presentation
- Chatbot interaction during automated sessions
- Regular live sessions + automated replays


### Automation Platforms

- WebinarJam/EverWebinar
- Demio (automated features)
- EasyWebinar
- WebinarNinja
