## Repurposing Webinar Content


### The Repurposing Framework

From one webinar, create multiple content pieces:


#### Video Content
- Full recording on YouTube
- Short clips for social media
- Highlight reel
- Key moment snippets


#### Audio Content
- Podcast episode
- Audio clips
- Audio-only replay option


#### Written Content
- Full transcription
- Blog post summary
- Key takeaways article
- Social media posts


#### Visual Content
- Quote graphics
- Data visualizations
- Infographics
- Slide deck as download


### Repurposing Workflow

1. **Archive**: Save all assets (recording, slides, chat, Q&A)
2. **Transcribe**: Get full text transcription
3. **Identify highlights**: Mark best moments
4. **Create derivatives**: Produce each content type
5. **Distribute**: Schedule across channels
6. **Track**: Monitor performance
