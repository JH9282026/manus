## Why Webinars Work



### The Business Case for Webinars

Webinars deliver measurable business results:

| Metric | Industry Average |
|--------|-----------------|
| Registration to attendance rate | 40-50% |
| Attendee engagement rate | 32-44% |
| Lead generation cost | Lower than most digital channels |
| Post-webinar conversion rate | 10-20% for qualified offers |
| Content repurposing value | High (multiple content pieces) |



### Strategic Benefits



#### Lead Generation
- Capture contact information at registration
- Pre-qualify through registration questions
- Identify high-intent prospects through attendance behavior
- Build email lists with engaged subscribers



#### Education and Value Delivery
- Demonstrate expertise and thought leadership
- Train customers on product/service use
- Provide continuing education
- Build trust through generous value



#### Sales and Conversion
- Move prospects through sales funnel
- Demonstrate products in action
- Overcome objections in real-time
- Create urgency through live offers



#### Community Building
- Regular touchpoint with audience
- Interactive relationship building
- Networking opportunities (especially panels)
- Brand affinity development
