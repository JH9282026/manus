# Common Mistakes

Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

---

## Common Mistakes

1. **Poor audio quality**: Undermines all other efforts
2. **No rehearsal**: Technical issues during live event
3. **Too much content**: Cramming rather than teaching
4. **No engagement**: Lecture without interaction
5. **Weak opening**: Loses audience early
6. **Unclear CTA**: Confused about next steps
7. **No follow-up**: Wasted leads and opportunity
8. **Ignoring no-shows**: 50%+ of registrants, still valuable
9. **No repurposing**: One-and-done content
10. **Inconsistent scheduling**: Unpredictable for audience
