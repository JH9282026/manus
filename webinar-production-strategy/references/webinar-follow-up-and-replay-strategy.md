## Webinar Follow-Up and Replay Strategy


### Immediate Follow-Up


#### Within 24 Hours
- Recording sent to all registrants
- Thank you message to attendees
- "Sorry we missed you" to no-shows
- Call-to-action reminder


#### Within 48-72 Hours
- Resources and slides
- Additional value content
- Testimonials from attendees
- Second CTA opportunity


### Replay Strategy


#### Replay Access
- Limited time availability creates urgency
- Gated vs. ungated access
- Speed up option for replay
- Chapter markers for navigation


#### Replay Promotion
- Email sequence for replays
- Social media promotion
- Retargeting to non-attendees
- Ongoing availability decisions


### Recording Repurposing

From one webinar, create:
- YouTube video
- Podcast episode (audio extraction)
- Blog post (transcription)
- Social media clips
- Email content
- Lead magnet
- Course content
