## International and Global Webinars


### Time Zone Considerations


#### Optimal Timing
- Consider where majority of audience is located
- Offer multiple sessions if global audience
- Provide replay access for missed sessions
- Include time zone in all communications


#### Multi-Region Strategy
- Live session for primary region
- Replay or second live for other regions
- Consider automated webinars for 24/7 coverage


### Language and Localization

- Subtitles or closed captions
- Translated materials and slides
- Local moderators for questions
- Cultural considerations in content
