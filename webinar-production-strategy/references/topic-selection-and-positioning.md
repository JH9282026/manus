## Topic Selection and Positioning



### Finding Winning Topics



#### Audience Research
- Survey existing audience about interests
- Analyze frequently asked questions
- Review content performance data
- Monitor industry conversations



#### Competitive Analysis
- What topics do competitors cover?
- What gaps exist in coverage?
- What unique angles can you offer?
- What's being overlooked?



#### Trend Alignment
- Current industry developments
- Seasonal relevance
- News and event tie-ins
- Emerging challenges or opportunities



### Topic Testing

Before committing to major promotion:
- Test topics in social media posts
- A/B test email subject lines
- Review search volume for topic keywords
- Consider polling audience directly



### Positioning for Registrations

Strong webinar positioning includes:



#### Compelling Title
- Specific outcome or benefit
- Curiosity or intrigue element
- Numbers or timeframes when relevant
- Clear topic indication

**Examples**:
- "5 LinkedIn Strategies That Generated $500K in Sales"
- "The Future of [Industry]: Predictions from 4 Leading Experts"
- "How to [Achieve Result] Without [Common Obstacle]"



#### Value Proposition
Clearly communicate:
- What attendees will learn
- Why it matters to them
- What they'll be able to do afterward
- Why now is the right time
