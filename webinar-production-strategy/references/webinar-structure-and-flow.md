## Webinar Structure and Flow



### Standard Webinar Structure



#### Opening (5-10 minutes)
- Welcome and introduction
- Housekeeping (how to use platform features)
- Presenter credibility establishment
- Agenda preview



#### Content Section (25-40 minutes)
- Main teaching or demonstration
- 3-5 key points or sections
- Stories and examples
- Visual support



#### Transition (2-3 minutes)
- Summary of key learnings
- Preview of offer (if applicable)
- Bridge to next section



#### Call-to-Action (5-10 minutes)
- Present offer or next steps
- Explain benefits and features
- Address objections
- Create urgency



#### Q&A (10-15 minutes)
- Live questions from audience
- Prepared questions as backup
- Final call-to-action reminder
- Closing and thank you



### Pacing Considerations

- **Attention spans**: Change visuals every 30-60 seconds
- **Engagement dips**: Expect engagement drop at 20-30 minutes
- **Interactive breaks**: Poll or question every 10-15 minutes
- **Energy management**: Start and end strong



#### 30-Minute Quick Session
- Opening: 3 minutes
- Content: 20 minutes
- Q&A: 7 minutes



#### 45-Minute Standard Webinar
- Opening: 5 minutes
- Content: 30 minutes
- Q&A: 10 minutes



#### 60-Minute Educational Webinar
- Opening: 5 minutes
- Content: 35 minutes
- Q&A/Discussion: 15 minutes
- Close: 5 minutes



#### 90-Minute Workshop
- Opening: 5 minutes
- Content Section 1: 25 minutes
- Interactive Exercise: 10 minutes
- Content Section 2: 25 minutes
- Q&A: 20 minutes
- Close: 5 minutes
