# Advanced Webinar Formats

Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

---

## Advanced Webinar Formats


### Workshop-Style Webinars

Interactive, hands-on sessions:


#### Workshop Elements
- Pre-work and preparation
- Live exercises and activities
- Breakout rooms (if platform supports)
- Workbooks and templates
- Post-session implementation


#### Workshop Pacing
- Introduction and setup (10%)
- Instruction (30%)
- Activity/exercise (40%)
- Discussion and Q&A (20%)


### Masterclass Webinars

Premium, in-depth education:

- Extended duration (90-180 minutes)
- Higher production value
- Expert instructor positioning
- Comprehensive coverage
- Premium pricing or positioning


### Summit/Multi-Speaker Events

Large-scale online events:

- Multiple presenters across days
- Conference-style scheduling
- Sponsor integration
- Networking components
- Higher production complexity
