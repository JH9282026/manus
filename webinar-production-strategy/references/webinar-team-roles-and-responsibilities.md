Detailed reference content for the Webinar Production Strategy skill.

---

## Webinar Team Roles and Responsibilities


### Core Webinar Team


#### Host/Presenter
- Deliver main content
- Engage audience
- Answer questions
- Maintain energy


#### Moderator
- Manage chat and Q&A
- Filter questions
- Troubleshoot attendee issues
- Handle housekeeping


#### Technical Producer
- Manage platform
- Control slides and screens
- Monitor audio/video quality
- Record and backup


### Extended Team

**Pre-Event**:
- Content creator (slides, materials)
- Copywriter (emails, landing page)
- Designer (graphics, thumbnails)
- Marketing (promotion)

**Post-Event**:
- Video editor (replay, clips)
- Sales team (follow-up)
- Analytics (reporting)


### Team Communication

- Pre-event briefing
- Live communication channel (Slack, text)
- Clear escalation procedures
- Post-event debrief
