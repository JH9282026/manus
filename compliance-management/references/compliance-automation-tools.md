# Compliance Automation Tools

Leveraging technology for efficient compliance management.

## Leading Platforms

### Vanta
**Features:**
- Automated evidence collection
- SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA
- Integration with 50+ services
- Continuous monitoring
- Audit management

**Best For:** Startups and mid-market SaaS

### Drata
**Features:**
- Real-time compliance monitoring
- Automated control testing
- Multi-framework support
- Personnel management
- Vendor risk management

**Best For:** Fast-growing tech companies

### Secureframe
**Features:**
- Compliance automation
- Risk management
- Audit workflow
- Employee training
- Policy management

**Best For:** Security-first organizations

### OneTrust
**Features:**
- Privacy management
- Cookie consent
- Data mapping
- DSAR automation
- Vendor assessment

**Best For:** Large enterprises, GDPR focus
