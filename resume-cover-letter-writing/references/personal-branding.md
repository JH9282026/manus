## Personal Branding


### Defining Your Professional Brand

Your personal brand is the unique combination of skills, experience, and personality that you want employers to associate with you.

**Brand development questions:**
- What are you known for professionally?
- What unique value do you bring?
- What problems do you solve?
- What makes you different from others in your field?
- What do you want to be known for?

**Brand consistency:**
Ensure consistency across all career documents and platforms:
- Resume
- Cover letters
- LinkedIn profile
- Portfolio/website
- Interview talking points


### Communicating Your Brand

**Through language:**
Develop and use consistent terminology to describe your expertise and value proposition across all materials.

**Through visuals:**
Consider consistent design elements across resume, LinkedIn, and portfolio.

**Through content:**
For senior professionals, thought leadership content can reinforce brand positioning.
