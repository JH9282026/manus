# Best Practices Summary

Detailed reference content for the Resume Cover Letter Writing skill.

---

## Best Practices Summary


### Resume Best Practices

1. **Lead with impact:** Most impressive, relevant content first.

2. **Quantify everything possible:** Numbers create credibility.

3. **Tailor for each application:** Match language and emphasis to job requirements.

4. **Use strong action verbs:** Start bullets with powerful, specific verbs.

5. **Optimize for ATS:** Include keywords, use standard formatting.

6. **Maintain clean design:** Clear hierarchy, plenty of white space.

7. **Proofread obsessively:** Multiple reviews, multiple readers.

8. **Keep it focused:** Length should match career stage and relevance.

9. **Tell achievement stories:** CAR/STAR format for compelling bullets.

10. **Update regularly:** Keep document current even when not job searching.


### Cover Letter Best Practices

1. **Customize every letter:** Research company and position.

2. **Hook them early:** Strong opening that creates interest.

3. **Provide evidence:** Specific examples that prove your value.

4. **Show genuine interest:** Explain why THIS company and role.

5. **Keep it concise:** One page, 3-4 paragraphs.

6. **Match your brand:** Consistent with resume in style and claims.

7. **Close with action:** Request the interview explicitly.

8. **Proofread carefully:** Errors here are especially damaging.
