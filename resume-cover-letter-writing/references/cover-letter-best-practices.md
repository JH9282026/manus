## Cover Letter Best Practices


### Customization Is Essential

Generic cover letters are worse than no cover letter. Every letter should be customized:

**Company-specific customization:**
- Reference their products, services, or recent news
- Mention company values or culture elements
- Show understanding of their challenges or goals

**Position-specific customization:**
- Address specific requirements from the job posting
- Explain why THIS role interests you
- Connect your experience to their needs


### Length and Format

**Length:**
- 3-4 paragraphs
- One page maximum
- 250-400 words typically
- Long enough to add value, short enough to be read

**Format:**
- Professional business letter format
- Same header/font as your resume for consistency
- Clear paragraph breaks
- Plenty of white space


### Tone and Voice

**Professional but personable:**
- Avoid overly formal or stiff language
- Show enthusiasm without desperation
- Be confident without arrogance
- Let your personality come through

**Active voice:**
- "I led" not "The team was led by me"
- "I achieved" not "Results were achieved"
