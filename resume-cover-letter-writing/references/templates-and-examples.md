Detailed reference content for the Resume Cover Letter Writing skill.

## Templates and Examples


### Professional Summary Templates

**Template 1 (Achievement-led):**
"[Achievement]-driven [Title] with [X] years of experience in [area]. Expertise in [skill 1], [skill 2], and [skill 3]. [Specific accomplishment with metrics]. Seeking to leverage [key skills] to [contribute to goal] at [target type of company]."

**Template 2 (Skills-led):**
"[Title] with expertise in [skill area]. [X] years of experience [doing what] for [type of organizations]. Track record of [achievement type], including [specific example]. Passionate about [aspect of work]."


### Cover Letter Templates

**Opening template:**
"[Referral/Connection/Achievement hook]. I'm excited to apply for the [Position] at [Company] because [specific reason showing research and genuine interest]."

**Body template:**
"In my current role at [Company], I [achievement with metrics]. This experience directly relates to your need for [requirement from job posting]. Additionally, my background in [relevant area] positions me to [add specific value]."

**Closing template:**
"I'm enthusiastic about the opportunity to [specific contribution] at [Company]. Thank you for considering my application. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background in [area] can contribute to [company goal or team]."
