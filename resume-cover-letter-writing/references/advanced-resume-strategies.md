## Advanced Resume Strategies


### Formatting for Executive Presence

Senior professionals should consider:

**Executive summary:**
- Lead with board-level achievements
- Emphasize P&L responsibility and scope
- Include global and organizational impact

**Board and advisory roles:**
- List relevant board positions
- Include advisory and investment activities
- Note speaking engagements and thought leadership

**Selective detail:**
- Focus depth on most recent/relevant roles
- Summarize earlier career concisely
- Emphasize career trajectory


### Addressing Sensitive Situations

**Termination:**
- Don't volunteer reasons for leaving
- Be prepared to discuss honestly in interviews
- Focus resume on accomplishments, not departures

**Multiple short tenures:**
- Group short-term consulting/contract work
- Consider combining related short stints
- Be prepared to explain pattern

**Overqualification:**
- Tailor aggressively to target role
- Consider omitting advanced credentials if they raise concerns
- Address in cover letter why you want this level


### Resume for Internal Mobility

Internal candidates have unique considerations:

**Highlight cross-functional impact:**
- Projects with other departments
- Company-wide initiatives
- Relationships with hiring area

**Demonstrate growth:**
- Show progression within company
- Include promotions and expanded responsibilities
- Note professional development completed

**Balance confidentiality:**
- Share what's appropriate publicly
- Reference achievements without revealing competitive info
