## Industry-Specific Resume Guidance


### Technology Resumes

Technology roles require specific considerations:

**Technical skills presentation:**
- List programming languages with proficiency levels
- Include frameworks, tools, and platforms
- Mention databases, cloud services, and methodologies (Agile, Scrum)
- Consider a separate "Tech Stack" section for engineers

**Project emphasis:**
- Highlight technical projects with specific technologies used
- Quantify impact (performance improvements, scale handled)
- Include GitHub profile or portfolio links
- Open source contributions add value

**Example tech resume bullet:**
"Architected microservices migration from monolithic PHP application to Go/Kubernetes stack, reducing deployment time from 2 hours to 15 minutes and improving system reliability to 99.95% uptime."


### Healthcare Resumes

Healthcare professionals need to emphasize:

**Credentials and certifications:**
- List all relevant licenses with state and expiration
- Include certifications (BLS, ACLS, specialty certs)
- Note any teaching appointments or hospital privileges

**Clinical experience:**
- Specify patient populations served
- Include volume metrics (patients seen, procedures performed)
- Highlight specialized skills and equipment

**Example healthcare bullet:**
"Managed care for 15-20 ICU patients per shift across 200-bed Level I trauma center, maintaining 98% medication administration accuracy and leading code blue responses averaging 3 per week."


### Finance Resumes

Finance positions value specific achievements:

**Deal and transaction experience:**
- Include deal sizes and your specific role
- Highlight industries or product types covered
- Note any publications or research produced

**Technical and analytical skills:**
- Financial modeling and valuation methods
- Bloomberg, CapIQ, FactSet proficiency
- Programming languages for quantitative roles

**Example finance bullet:**
"Executed $2.3B in M&A advisory transactions across healthcare and technology sectors, including lead associate role on $450M acquisition that closed 30% above initial bid."


### Sales Resumes

Sales professionals should quantify performance:

**Revenue metrics:**
- Quota attainment percentages
- Revenue generated
- Deal sizes and sales cycles
- Rankings among peers

**Activity metrics:**
- Territory size
- Accounts managed
- New business vs. account management

**Example sales bullet:**
"Achieved 145% of $2.5M annual quota across 75-account territory, generating $3.6M in revenue and earning Top Performer recognition (top 5% of 200-person sales organization)."


### Creative Resumes

Creative professionals balance portfolio with resume:

**Portfolio integration:**
- Always include portfolio URL
- May reference specific projects with metrics
- Describe creative process and collaboration

**Technical skills:**
- Software proficiency (Adobe Creative Suite, Figma)
- Specialized skills (motion graphics, UX research)
- Production knowledge

**Example creative bullet:**
"Led visual identity redesign for Fortune 500 client, delivering comprehensive brand system adopted across 50+ global markets with 95% stakeholder satisfaction scores."
