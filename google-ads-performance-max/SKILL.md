# Google Ads Performance Max Campaigns

Create goal-based campaigns that automatically optimize across all Google advertising channels using AI and machine learning.

## Overview

This skill covers Performance Max (PMax) campaign setup, asset group creation, audience signals, bidding strategies, and cross-channel optimization. Use it for maximizing conversions or conversion value across Search, Display, YouTube, Discover, Gmail, and Maps simultaneously.

## When to Use Performance Max

| Use Case | Why PMax | Alternative |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Maximize conversions across all channels | Automated placement, AI optimization | Multiple campaign types |
| Limited time/resources | Single campaign manages all channels | Manual campaign management |
| E-commerce with product feeds | Dynamic ads across all networks | Shopping + Display campaigns |
| Lead generation (multi-channel) | Reach users across entire journey | Search + Display campaigns |
| New product launches | Maximum reach quickly | Multiple campaigns |

## Campaign Setup Requirements

### Minimum Requirements
- **Conversion tracking** - At least 1 conversion action
- **Budget** - Recommended $50+/day for learning
- **Assets** - Minimum 1 of each required asset type
- **Goal** - Conversions or Conversion Value

### Conversion Goals

| Goal Type | Best For | Bidding Strategy |
|-----------|----------|------------------|
| **Conversions** | Lead generation, sign-ups | Maximize Conversions or Target CPA |
| **Conversion Value** | E-commerce, variable order values | Maximize Conversion Value or Target ROAS |
| **Store Visits** | Local businesses | Maximize store visits |
| **New Customer Acquisition** | Customer growth | New customer value optimization |

## Asset Groups

### Asset Requirements

| Asset Type | Minimum | Maximum | Specifications |
|------------|---------|---------|----------------|
| **Images** | 1 | 20 | Landscape (1.91:1), Square (1:1), Portrait (4:5) |
| **Logos** | 1 | 5 | Square (1:1), min 128x128px |
| **Videos** | 0 | 5 | YouTube URLs, 10s-3min |
| **Headlines** | 3 | 5 | Max 30 characters |
| **Long Headlines** | 1 | 5 | Max 90 characters |
| **Descriptions** | 2 | 5 | Max 90 characters |
| **Business Name** | 1 | 1 | Max 25 characters |
| **Call-to-Action** | Optional | 1 | Predefined options |

### Image Specifications

| Type | Aspect Ratio | Min Size | Recommended | Use |
|------|--------------|----------|-------------|-----|
| Landscape | 1.91:1 | 600x314 | 1200x628 | Display, Discover |
| Square | 1:1 | 300x300 | 1200x1200 | Display, Gmail |
| Portrait | 4:5 | 480x600 | 960x1200 | Discover, YouTube |

### Asset Best Practices

**Images**:
- Provide 15-20 images (mix of all aspect ratios)
- High quality, clear subject
- Minimal text (<20%)
- Show products, lifestyle, brand

**Headlines**:
- 5 unique headlines (max variety)
- Include keywords, offers, benefits
- Vary length (15-30 characters)
- No repetition

**Descriptions**:
- 5 unique descriptions
- Complete sentences
- Include CTAs
- Highlight value propositions

## Audience Signals

### What Are Audience Signals?

Hints to Google about who your ideal customers are. Not strict targeting - Google expands beyond signals.

### Signal Types

| Signal Type | Examples | Use Case |
|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **Custom Segments** | Keywords, URLs, apps | Define interests/intent |
| **Your Data** | Website visitors, customer lists | Remarketing, lookalikes |
| **Demographics** | Age, gender, income | Demographic preferences |
| **Interests** | Affinity, in-market | Broad interest targeting |

### Audience Signal Strategy

**Start Narrow, Let Google Expand**:
1. **Week 1-2**: Provide strong signals (remarketing, customer lists)
2. **Week 3-4**: Google expands beyond signals
3. **Week 5+**: Monitor performance, adjust signals if needed

**Example Signals**:
```
Product: Running Shoes

Custom Segments:
- Keywords: running shoes, marathon training, trail running
- URLs: runnersworld.com, nike.com/running
- Apps: Strava, Nike Run Club

Your Data:
- Website visitors (last 30 days)
- Product viewers (running shoes category)
- Cart abandoners

Demographics:
- Age: 25-54
- Gender: All
- Household Income: Top 50%

Interests:
- Affinity: Sports & Fitness > Running Enthusiasts
- In-Market: Apparel > Athletic Apparel
```

## Bidding Strategies

| Strategy | Goal | Requirements | Best For |
|----------|------|--------------|----------|
| **Maximize Conversions** | Maximum conversion volume | 30+ conversions/month | Lead gen, flexible CPA |
| **Target CPA** | Cost-efficient conversions | 30+ conversions/month | Specific CPA target |
| **Maximize Conversion Value** | Maximum revenue | 50+ conversions/month | E-commerce, flexible ROAS |
| **Target ROAS** | Revenue efficiency | 50+ conversions/month | Specific ROAS target |

### Learning Period

- **Duration**: 2-6 weeks
- **Conversions needed**: 50+ for full optimization
- **What to expect**: Fluctuating performance, higher CPA initially
- **Best practice**: Don't make major changes during learning

## Performance Max vs. Other Campaign Types

| Feature | Performance Max | Search | Display | Shopping |
|---------|----------------|--------|---------|----------|
| **Networks** | All (Search, Display, YouTube, Discover, Gmail, Maps) | Search only | Display only | Shopping only |
| **Control** | Low (automated) | High | Medium | Medium |
| **Setup Complexity** | Medium | Low | Low | High |
| **Optimization** | AI-driven | Manual or automated | Manual or automated | Manual or automated |
| **Best For** | Multi-channel reach | Search intent | Awareness | E-commerce |

## Asset Group Organization

### Single vs. Multiple Asset Groups

**Single Asset Group**:
- Simple product/service
- One target audience
- Limited assets

**Multiple Asset Groups**:
- Multiple products/services
- Different audiences
- Distinct messaging themes

### Example: Running Shoe Store

```
Campaign: Running Shoes - Performance Max

Asset Group 1: Men's Running Shoes
- Images: Men's shoes, male runners
- Headlines: "Men's Running Shoes", "Shop Men's Runners"
- Audience Signals: Male, 25-54, running enthusiasts

Asset Group 2: Women's Running Shoes
- Images: Women's shoes, female runners
- Headlines: "Women's Running Shoes", "Shop Women's Runners"
- Audience Signals: Female, 25-54, running enthusiasts

Asset Group 3: Trail Running
- Images: Trail shoes, outdoor running
- Headlines: "Trail Running Shoes", "Off-Road Runners"
- Audience Signals: Trail running keywords, outdoor enthusiasts
```

## Performance Reporting

### Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Tells You | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Conversions** | Total conversions | Meets goal |
| **Conversion Value** | Total revenue | Meets ROAS target |
| **CPA** | Cost per conversion | \u2264Target CPA |
| **ROAS** | Return on ad spend | \u2265Target ROAS |
| **Impression Share** | % of available impressions | >80% |

### Channel Performance Breakdown

**Available in Reporting**:
- Search
- Display
- YouTube
- Discover
- Gmail
- Maps

**How to View**:
1. Navigate to Campaigns
2. Select Performance Max campaign
3. Click "Insights" tab
4. View "Asset Group Performance" and "Search Terms"

## Optimization Strategies

### Asset Optimization

**Monthly Review**:
1. Check asset performance ratings (Low, Good, Best)
2. Replace "Low" performing assets
3. Add new asset variations
4. Test different creative themes

### Audience Signal Refinement

**Quarterly Review**:
1. Analyze conversion data by audience
2. Add high-performing audiences as signals
3. Remove low-performing signals
4. Test new audience combinations

### Budget Optimization

**Weekly Monitoring**:
- Check if "Limited by Budget"
- Increase budget if performance is strong
- Decrease if CPA/ROAS not meeting targets

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Low Impressions** | Budget too low, bids too low | Increase budget 20-50% |
| **High CPA** | Learning period, weak signals | Wait 2-4 weeks, improve signals |
| **Low Conversion Volume** | Budget too low, target too aggressive | Increase budget or relax target |
| **"Learning" Status** | Insufficient conversions | Increase budget, wait for 50+ conversions |
