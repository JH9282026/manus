# SaaS, E-commerce, B2B & B2C

## SaaS
- Hero: product category + benefit + screenshot + trial CTA
- Features: 3-5 with benefit descriptions and GIFs
- Pricing tiers with comparison table
- Trust: SOC 2, GDPR, integrations, G2 ratings
- Messaging: problem-solution-results; quantify ROI

## E-commerce
- Product: multi-angle photos, video, variants, pricing
- Purchase: prominent Add to Cart, stock indicator
- Social proof: reviews, user photos, purchase counts
- Trust: payment logos, shipping guarantees, returns

## B2B
- Business impact quantification
- Progressive profiling forms
- Case studies with metrics, ROI calculators
- Multiple paths: demo, trial, content download
- Client logos, certifications, executive testimonials

## B2C
- Emotional hero with lifestyle imagery
- Simplified conversion: one-click, social login
- Heavy social proof: reviews, UGC, influencers
- Benefits over features; identity transformation

## Lead Generation
- Clear lead magnet with visual representation
- Minimal fields (name + email)
- Specific value: "47-page guide" > "comprehensive guide"
- Immediate delivery; thank-you with secondary CTAs
