# Healthcare, Financial & Other Industries

## Healthcare
- Provider credentials, services, insurance accepted
- Online appointment booking
- HIPAA-compliant forms, medical disclaimers, ADA accessibility

## Financial Services
- Fee transparency, comparison tools
- Security: encryption, FDIC/SIPC, fraud prevention
- Compliance: risk disclosures, performance disclaimers

## Real Estate
- Professional photography, virtual tours
- Agent credentials, transaction history
- Search with location, price, property type filters

## Education
- Curriculum breakdown, instructor credentials
- Student success stories, free previews
- Payment plans, money-back guarantee

## Nonprofit
- Impact stories with imagery
- Multiple giving levels with descriptions
- Recurring donation option; charity ratings

## Testing Strategy
1. A/B test headlines first
2. Test CTA variations
3. Experiment with form length
4. Try different trust signals
5. Measure micro-conversions
