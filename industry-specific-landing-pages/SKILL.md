# Industry-Specific Landing Pages

Design landing pages tailored to the unique psychology, expectations, and compliance requirements of different industries.

## Overview

Generic pages fail because they ignore industry-specific buyer behavior. This skill provides frameworks for SaaS, e-commerce, B2B, B2C, healthcare, financial services, real estate, education, nonprofit, and local business landing pages.

## Universal Principles
1. One page, one goal
2. Value proposition clear in 5 seconds
3. Mobile-first (60%+ traffic)
4. Fast load (<3 seconds)
5. Clear visual hierarchy
6. Social proof throughout
