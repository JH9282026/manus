# Industry-Specific Marketing Playbooks

Tailored marketing strategies for key industries and business models.

---

## B2B SaaS Marketing

### Market Characteristics

- Long sales cycles (3-12+ months)
- Multiple decision-makers
- High customer lifetime value
- Emphasis on product-led growth or sales-led
- Recurring revenue model
- Low marginal cost of serving customers

### Core Strategy Elements

**Product-Led Growth (PLG)**:

*Characteristics*:
- Free trial or freemium model
- Self-service onboarding
- Viral growth mechanisms
- Usage-based pricing
- Bottom-up adoption

*Marketing Focus*:
- In-product marketing
- Activation optimization
- User education and onboarding
- Community building
- Referral programs

*Examples*: Slack, Dropbox, Notion, Figma

**Sales-Led Growth**:

*Characteristics*:
- High-touch sales process
- Enterprise focus
- Custom pricing
- Implementation services
- Top-down selling

*Marketing Focus*:
- Lead generation and nurturing
- Sales enablement
- Account-based marketing
- Thought leadership
- ROI calculators and business cases

*Examples*: Salesforce, Workday, ServiceNow

### Channel Strategy

**Primary Channels**:

1. **Content Marketing & SEO** (30-40% of budget):
   - Long-form guides and resources
   - Product comparison pages
   - Use case documentation
   - Customer success stories
   - Industry reports and research

2. **Paid Search** (20-30%):
   - Branded keywords (protect)
   - Competitor keywords
   - High-intent solution keywords
   - Remarketing

3. **LinkedIn** (15-25%):
   - Sponsored content
   - InMail campaigns
   - Account-based targeting
   - Thought leadership

4. **Email Marketing** (10-15%):
   - Nurture sequences
   - Product updates
   - Educational content
   - Event invitations

5. **Webinars & Events** (10-15%):
   - Educational webinars
   - Product demos
   - Industry conferences
   - User conferences

### Funnel Optimization

**Awareness**:
- Thought leadership content
- Industry research and reports
- Podcast sponsorships
- Conference speaking
- PR and media coverage

**Consideration**:
- Product comparison guides
- ROI calculators
- Case studies and testimonials
- Demo videos
- Free tools and templates

**Decision**:
- Free trial or demo
- Personalized proposals
- Security and compliance documentation
- Customer references
- Proof of concept

**Retention & Expansion**:
- Onboarding programs
- Product education
- Feature adoption campaigns
- Upsell and cross-sell
- Customer advisory boards

### Key Metrics

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- CAC Payback Period
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC Ratio (target 3:1+)
- Net Revenue Retention (target 110%+)
- Churn Rate (target <5% annually)
- Product Qualified Leads (PQLs) for PLG

## Ecommerce & DTC Marketing

### Market Characteristics

- Short sales cycles (minutes to days)
- Direct consumer relationships
- High competition
- Emphasis on brand and experience
- Transaction-based revenue
- Inventory and logistics considerations

### Core Strategy Elements

**Brand Building**:
- Authentic brand story
- Consistent visual identity
- Values-driven messaging
- Community engagement
- Influencer partnerships

**Customer Experience**:
- Seamless website UX
- Fast page load times
- Easy checkout process
- Excellent customer service
- Hassle-free returns

**Retention Focus**:
- Email and SMS marketing
- Loyalty programs
- Subscription models
- Personalized recommendations
- Post-purchase engagement

### Channel Strategy

**Primary Channels**:

1. **Paid Social** (30-40% of budget):
   - Facebook and Instagram ads
   - TikTok ads (growing)
   - Pinterest ads (visual products)
   - Influencer partnerships

2. **Email & SMS** (15-25%):
   - Welcome series
   - Abandoned cart recovery
   - Post-purchase sequences
   - Promotional campaigns
   - Win-back campaigns

3. **SEO & Content** (15-20%):
   - Product pages optimization
   - Blog content
   - Buying guides
   - User-generated content

4. **Paid Search** (10-20%):
   - Google Shopping
   - Branded search
   - Product keywords
   - Remarketing

5. **Affiliate & Partnerships** (5-15%):
   - Affiliate networks
   - Influencer affiliates
   - Cashback sites
   - Comparison shopping engines

### Conversion Optimization

**Product Pages**:
- High-quality images and videos
- Detailed descriptions
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Size guides and fit information
- Related products
- Urgency and scarcity indicators

**Cart & Checkout**:
- Guest checkout option
- Multiple payment methods
- Shipping cost transparency
- Progress indicators
- Trust badges and security
- Exit-intent popups

**Post-Purchase**:
- Order confirmation emails
- Shipping updates
- Delivery notifications
- Review requests
- Replenishment reminders
- Cross-sell recommendations

### Retention Strategies

**Email Marketing**:
- Segment by purchase behavior
- Personalized product recommendations
- VIP customer programs
- Birthday and anniversary emails
- Re-engagement campaigns

**Loyalty Programs**:
- Points-based rewards
- Tiered benefits
- Exclusive access
- Referral bonuses
- Gamification elements

**Subscription Models**:
- Subscribe and save discounts
- Flexible subscription management
- Surprise and delight
- Exclusive subscriber perks

### Key Metrics

- Revenue and growth rate
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) - target 4:1+
- Conversion Rate - target 2-3%+
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Repeat Purchase Rate - target 25-30%+
- Cart Abandonment Rate
- Email Revenue (% of total)

## B2B Professional Services

### Market Characteristics

- Relationship-driven sales
- High-value, low-volume
- Expertise and trust critical
- Long sales cycles
- Referral-heavy
- Local or industry-specific

### Core Strategy Elements

**Thought Leadership**:
- Industry expertise demonstration
- Speaking engagements
- Published research
- Media commentary
- Book authorship

**Relationship Building**:
- Networking events
- Industry associations
- Client entertainment
- Executive connections
- Alumni networks

**Referral Generation**:
- Client satisfaction focus
- Referral incentive programs
- Partner networks
- Case study development
- Testimonial collection

### Channel Strategy

**Primary Channels**:

1. **Content Marketing** (30-40%):
   - Industry insights and analysis
   - White papers and research
   - Case studies
   - Blog and newsletter
   - Podcast or video series

2. **LinkedIn** (20-30%):
   - Executive thought leadership
   - Company page content
   - Employee advocacy
   - Targeted advertising
   - Group participation

3. **Events & Networking** (15-25%):
   - Industry conferences
   - Hosted events and dinners
   - Webinars and workshops
   - Sponsorships

4. **Email Marketing** (10-15%):
   - Newsletter
   - Personalized outreach
   - Event invitations
   - Content distribution

5. **Referral Programs** (10-15%):
   - Client referral incentives
   - Partner programs
   - Alumni networks

### Lead Generation

**Inbound**:
- SEO-optimized service pages
- Downloadable resources (gated)
- Webinar registrations
- Newsletter signups
- Contact form inquiries

**Outbound**:
- Targeted LinkedIn outreach
- Email campaigns to ideal clients
- Conference follow-up
- Warm introductions
- Speaking engagement leads

**Referral**:
- Client referral requests
- Partner referrals
- Professional network
- Industry associations

### Key Metrics

- Pipeline value
- Qualified leads
- Conversion rate (lead to client)
- Average project value
- Client acquisition cost
- Client lifetime value
- Referral rate
- Win rate
- Sales cycle length

## Healthcare Marketing

### Market Characteristics

- Highly regulated (HIPAA, FDA)
- Trust and credibility paramount
- Complex decision-making
- Multiple stakeholders
- Long consideration periods
- Privacy-sensitive

### Regulatory Considerations

**HIPAA Compliance**:
- Protected Health Information (PHI) security
- Patient consent for marketing
- Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
- Secure communication channels
- Data encryption and access controls

**FDA Regulations** (for medical devices, pharma):
- Truthful and non-misleading claims
- Substantiation of claims
- Disclosure of risks
- Approval processes for materials

### Core Strategy Elements

**Trust Building**:
- Medical professional endorsements
- Clinical evidence and research
- Patient testimonials (with consent)
- Accreditations and certifications
- Transparent communication

**Education Focus**:
- Patient education resources
- Condition awareness campaigns
- Treatment option information
- Preventive care guidance
- Health and wellness content

**Patient Experience**:
- Easy appointment scheduling
- Telehealth options
- Patient portals
- Follow-up communication
- Reputation management

### Channel Strategy

**Primary Channels**:

1. **SEO & Local Search** (25-35%):
   - Google Business Profile optimization
   - Local SEO for practices
   - Condition and treatment pages
   - Health information content
   - Schema markup for healthcare

2. **Content Marketing** (20-30%):
   - Health blog
   - Patient education videos
   - Condition guides
   - FAQ pages
   - Newsletter

3. **Paid Search** (15-25%):
   - Condition-related keywords
   - Treatment keywords
   - Local service ads
   - Remarketing (HIPAA-compliant)

4. **Social Media** (10-15%):
   - Health tips and education
   - Patient stories (with consent)
   - Behind-the-scenes
   - Community engagement
   - Awareness campaigns

5. **Email Marketing** (10-15%):
   - Appointment reminders
   - Health tips
   - Seasonal campaigns (flu shots, etc.)
   - Patient satisfaction surveys

### Patient Acquisition

**Awareness**:
- Health condition education
- Symptom checkers
- Preventive care information
- Community health events
- PR and media relations

**Consideration**:
- Provider profiles and bios
- Treatment options information
- Insurance and payment information
- Patient testimonials
- Virtual tours

**Conversion**:
- Easy online scheduling
- New patient forms online
- Insurance verification
- Telehealth options
- Clear calls-to-action

### Key Metrics

- New patient acquisitions
- Patient acquisition cost
- Patient lifetime value
- Appointment booking rate
- Patient satisfaction (NPS, reviews)
- Website conversion rate
- Local search rankings
- Online review ratings

## Financial Services Marketing

### Market Characteristics

- Highly regulated (SEC, FINRA, CFPB)
- Trust and security critical
- Complex products
- Long consideration periods
- High customer lifetime value
- Competitive landscape

### Regulatory Considerations

**Compliance Requirements**:
- Truth in advertising
- Disclosure requirements
- Approval processes for materials
- Record-keeping requirements
- Privacy regulations (GLBA)
- Anti-money laundering (AML)

**Marketing Restrictions**:
- Testimonial regulations
- Performance claims substantiation
- Risk disclosures
- Prohibited practices
- Social media compliance

### Core Strategy Elements

**Trust and Security**:
- Security certifications
- Encryption and protection
- Fraud prevention
- Transparent fees
- Regulatory compliance messaging

**Education and Guidance**:
- Financial literacy content
- Product education
- Calculators and tools
- Market insights
- Retirement planning resources

**Personalization**:

- Financial goal alignment
- Customized recommendations

3. **Paid Search** (15-25%):
   - Product keywords

   - Competitor keywords
   - Remarketing

4. **Email Marketing** (15-20%):
   - Nurture campaigns
   - Product education
   - Market updates
   - Personalized offers

5. **Display & Video** (10-15%):
   - Brand awareness
   - Retargeting
   - YouTube education
   - Connected TV

### Customer Acquisition

**Awareness**:
- Brand advertising
- Financial education content
- Sponsorships and partnerships
- PR and thought leadership
- Community involvement

**Consideration**:
- Product comparison tools

**Conversion**:

- Live chat support
- Branch locator
- Mobile app download

**Retention**:
- Account management
- Cross-sell campaigns
- Financial planning services
- Loyalty rewards
- Customer education

- Assets under management (AUM)
- Customer acquisition cost
- Customer lifetime value
- Cross-sell rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Digital adoption rate
- Loan origination volume
- Deposit growth
