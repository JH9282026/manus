Detailed tactics and best practices for major marketing channels.

## Content Marketing & SEO

### Content Strategy Framework

**Content Pillars Approach**:

1. Identify 3-5 core topics aligned with:
   - Customer pain points
   - Product value propositions
   - Search demand
   - Competitive gaps

2. Create content clusters:
   - Pillar page (comprehensive guide)
   - 8-12 supporting articles
   - Internal linking structure

**Content Types by Funnel Stage**:

| Stage | Content Types | Purpose |
|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Awareness** | Blog posts, infographics, videos, podcasts | Attract and educate |
| **Consideration** | Guides, webinars, case studies, comparisons | Build trust and authority |
| **Decision** | Product demos, free trials, testimonials | Drive conversion |
| **Retention** | Tutorials, best practices, community content | Increase engagement and loyalty |

### SEO Best Practices 2026

**Technical SEO**:
- Page speed optimization (Core Web Vitals)
- Mobile-first indexing
- Structured data markup (Schema.org)
- XML sitemaps and robots.txt
- HTTPS security
- Clean URL structure

**On-Page SEO**:
- Keyword research and targeting
- Title tag optimization (50-60 characters)
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- Header tag hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Image optimization (alt text, compression)
- Internal linking strategy

**Content SEO**:
- Long-form content (2,000+ words for pillar pages)
- Natural keyword integration
- Topic clusters and semantic SEO
- Answer search intent
- E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
- Regular content updates

**Link Building**:
- Guest posting on authoritative sites
- Digital PR and media coverage
- Resource page link building
- Broken link building
- Competitor backlink analysis
- Avoid: Link farms, PBNs, paid links

**Local SEO** (for local businesses):
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Local citations (NAP consistency)
- Local content creation
- Customer reviews management
- Local link building

## Paid Advertising

### Google Ads Strategy

**Campaign Structure**:
```
Account
  ↓
Campaigns (by goal/product)
  ↓
Ad Groups (by theme/keyword group)
  ↓
Keywords & Ads
```

**Search Campaign Best Practices**:

*Keyword Strategy*:
- Use all match types strategically:
  - Exact match: High intent, low volume
  - Phrase match: Balance of control and reach
  - Broad match: Maximum reach (use with smart bidding)
- Negative keywords: Exclude irrelevant searches
- Long-tail keywords: Lower cost, higher intent

*Ad Copy Elements*:
- Headline 1: Include primary keyword
- Headline 2: Unique value proposition
- Headline 3: Call-to-action
- Description: Benefits and differentiators
- Extensions: Sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets

*Bidding Strategies*:
- Manual CPC: Full control, requires active management
- Enhanced CPC: Manual with automated adjustments
- Target CPA: Optimize for cost per acquisition
- Target ROAS: Optimize for return on ad spend
- Maximize Conversions: Spend budget to get most conversions

**Display & Video Campaigns**:

*Targeting Options*:
- Demographics: Age, gender, income, parental status
- Interests: Affinity and in-market audiences
- Remarketing: Previous website visitors
- Custom audiences: Based on keywords, URLs, apps
- Placement targeting: Specific websites/videos

*Creative Best Practices*:
- Responsive display ads (multiple assets)
- Video ads: 6-15 seconds for skippable, 6 seconds for bumper
- Clear branding and CTA
- Mobile-optimized creative
- A/B test different messages and visuals

### Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

**Campaign Objectives**:
- Awareness: Brand awareness, reach
- Consideration: Traffic, engagement, app installs, video views, lead generation
- Conversion: Conversions, catalog sales, store traffic

**Audience Targeting**:

*Core Audiences*:
- Demographics: Age, gender, education, job title
- Location: Country, state, city, radius
- Interests: Based on pages, activities, device usage
- Behaviors: Purchase behavior, device usage, travel

*Custom Audiences*:
- Website visitors (Meta Pixel)
- Customer list upload
- App activity
- Engagement (video, lead form, Instagram, Facebook)

*Lookalike Audiences*:
- Based on custom audiences
- 1-10% similarity (1% = most similar)
- Use high-value customer lists for best results

**Creative Strategy**:

*Image Ads*:
- 1080 x 1080 px (square) or 1200 x 628 px (landscape)
- Minimal text on image (avoid text-heavy)
- Eye-catching visuals
- Clear value proposition

*Video Ads*:
- First 3 seconds critical for hook
- Captions for sound-off viewing
- 15-60 seconds optimal length
- Square or vertical format for mobile

*Carousel Ads*:
- 2-10 cards
- Tell a story or showcase multiple products
- Each card can have unique link

*Stories & Reels Ads*:
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Full-screen immersive experience
- Interactive elements (polls, stickers)

**Optimization Tips**:
- Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO)
- Conversion API for accurate tracking
- Broad targeting with algorithm optimization
- Dynamic creative testing
- Frequency capping to avoid ad fatigue

### LinkedIn Ads

**Best for B2B**:
- High-value products/services
- Professional audiences
- Decision-maker targeting

**Targeting Capabilities**:
- Job title, function, seniority
- Company name, industry, size
- Skills and groups
- Education and degrees
- Matched audiences (website, email lists)

**Ad Formats**:

*Sponsored Content*:
- Single image ads
- Video ads
- Carousel ads
- Event ads
- Document ads

*Sponsored Messaging*:
- Message ads (direct to inbox)
- Conversation ads (choose-your-path)

*Text Ads*:
- Small ads in sidebar
- Lower cost, lower engagement

*Dynamic Ads*:
- Personalized ads using profile data
- Follower ads, spotlight ads, content ads

**Cost Considerations**:
- Higher CPC than other platforms ($5-10+ typical)
- Focus on quality over volume
- Optimize for conversions, not clicks
- Use lead gen forms to reduce friction

## Email Marketing

### Email Program Structure

**Email Types**:

*Transactional*:
- Order confirmations
- Shipping notifications
- Password resets
- Account updates

*Promotional*:
- Product launches
- Sales and discounts
- Seasonal campaigns
- Event invitations

*Lifecycle/Nurture*:
- Welcome series
- Onboarding sequences
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Win-back campaigns

*Content/Newsletter*:
- Weekly/monthly newsletters
- Educational content
- Industry news and insights
- Company updates

### Email Campaign Best Practices

**Subject Line Optimization**:
- 40-50 characters optimal
- Personalization (name, company)
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, !!!, ALL CAPS)
- A/B test different approaches
- Emoji usage (test for your audience)

**Email Design**:
- Mobile-responsive (60%+ open on mobile)
- Single column layout
- Clear hierarchy and scannable
- Prominent CTA button
- Alt text for images
- Balance text and images

**Personalization**:
- First name in subject/body
- Dynamic content based on:
  - Past purchases
  - Browsing behavior
  - Demographic data
  - Engagement history
- Behavioral triggers

**Segmentation Strategies**:

| Segment Type | Criteria | Use Case |
|-------------|----------|----------|
| **Demographic** | Age, location, job title | Targeted messaging |
| **Behavioral** | Purchase history, engagement | Product recommendations |
| **Lifecycle** | New subscriber, active, dormant | Appropriate nurture |
| **Engagement** | Opens, clicks, conversions | Re-engagement campaigns |
| **Psychographic** | Interests, values, preferences | Content relevance |

**Automation Workflows**:

*Welcome Series* (3-5 emails):
1. Immediate: Welcome + brand intro
2. Day 2: Value proposition + resources
3. Day 5: Social proof + testimonials
4. Day 8: Product/service overview
5. Day 12: Special offer or CTA

*Abandoned Cart* (3 emails):
1. 1 hour: Reminder with cart contents
2. 24 hours: Add urgency or social proof
3. 3 days: Discount or incentive

*Re-engagement* (2-3 emails):
1. "We miss you" + value reminder
2. Special offer or new features
3. Final chance / preference center

### Deliverability Optimization

**Authentication**:
- SPF record
- DKIM signature
- DMARC policy
- Dedicated sending domain

**List Hygiene**:
- Double opt-in
- Regular list cleaning
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Sunset policy for inactive subscribers
- Easy unsubscribe process

**Engagement Optimization**:
- Send time optimization
- Frequency testing
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Preference center
- Avoid spam traps

## Social Media Marketing

### Platform-Specific Strategies

**LinkedIn** (B2B focus):

*Content Types*:
- Thought leadership articles
- Industry insights and trends
- Company news and culture
- Employee spotlights
- Case studies and success stories

*Best Practices*:
- Post during business hours (Tue-Thu optimal)
- Use native video (higher engagement)
- Encourage employee advocacy
- Engage in relevant groups
- Use hashtags strategically (3-5)

**Instagram** (Visual, lifestyle, DTC):

*Content Mix*:
- Feed posts: High-quality, curated content
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes, timely content
- Reels: Short-form video, trending audio
- IGTV/Long-form: Tutorials, interviews
- Live: Q&As, events, product launches

*Best Practices*:
- Consistent visual aesthetic
- User-generated content
- Influencer partnerships
- Shopping features for ecommerce
- Hashtag strategy (mix of sizes)
- Post 4-7 times per week

**TikTok** (Gen Z, viral, entertainment):

*Content Strategy*:
- Authentic, unpolished content
- Trending sounds and challenges
- Educational/how-to content
- Behind-the-scenes
- User-generated content campaigns

*Best Practices*:
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Vertical video (9:16)
- Captions for accessibility
- Engage with comments
- Post 1-3 times daily
- Use TikTok Creator Marketplace

**Twitter/X** (News, real-time, B2B):

*Content Types*:
- Industry news and commentary
- Quick tips and insights
- Engagement with community
- Live event coverage
- Customer service

*Best Practices*:
- Tweet 3-5 times daily
- Use relevant hashtags (1-2)
- Engage in conversations
- Share visual content
- Utilize threads for longer content

**Facebook** (Community, broad reach):

*Content Mix*:
- Video content (prioritized by algorithm)
- Community engagement
- Events and groups
- Live streaming
- User-generated content

*Best Practices*:
- Focus on engagement over reach
- Use Facebook Groups for community
- Video content performs best
- Post 1-2 times daily
- Respond to comments quickly

### Social Media Management

**Content Calendar**:
- Plan 2-4 weeks ahead
- Mix of content types
- Balance promotional and value content (80/20 rule)
- Align with marketing campaigns
- Leave room for real-time content

**Engagement Strategy**:
- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Monitor brand mentions
- Engage with industry content
- Build relationships with influencers
- User-generated content campaigns

**Analytics and Optimization**:
- Track engagement rate, reach, impressions
- Monitor follower growth
- Analyze top-performing content
- Adjust strategy based on data
- Benchmark against competitors

## Events and Webinars

### Webinar Strategy

**Planning**:
- Choose relevant, timely topics
- Partner with industry experts or customers
- 30-45 minutes optimal length
- Interactive elements (polls, Q&A)
- Promote 2-4 weeks in advance

**Promotion Channels**:
- Email to existing database
- Social media promotion
- Partner promotion
- Paid advertising
- Website and blog

**Follow-up**:
- Send recording within 24 hours
- Nurture sequence for attendees
- Different sequence for no-shows
- Repurpose content (blog, social clips)
- Sales follow-up for high-intent attendees

### In-Person Events

**Event Types**:
- Trade shows and conferences
- User conferences
- Executive dinners
- Workshops and training
- Networking events

**Maximizing ROI**:
- Pre-event outreach to schedule meetings
- Engaging booth experience
- Lead capture system
- Post-event follow-up within 48 hours
- Content creation (photos, videos, quotes)
- Measure: Leads generated, pipeline, brand awareness

## Influencer and Partnership Marketing

### Influencer Collaboration

**Influencer Tiers**:
- Nano (1K-10K): High engagement, niche audiences
- Micro (10K-100K): Engaged communities, affordable
- Macro (100K-1M): Broader reach, established authority
- Mega (1M+): Maximum reach, celebrity status

**Campaign Types**:
- Sponsored posts
- Product reviews
- Unboxing videos
- Takeovers
- Affiliate partnerships
- Brand ambassadorships

**Selection Criteria**:
- Audience alignment
- Engagement rate (>3% good)
- Content quality and authenticity
- Brand fit and values
- Past brand collaborations

### Strategic Partnerships

**Partnership Types**:
- Co-marketing campaigns
- Content collaborations
- Integration partnerships
- Referral partnerships
- Event partnerships

**Success Factors**:
- Complementary audiences
- Aligned values and goals
- Clear value exchange
- Defined roles and responsibilities
- Measurement framework
