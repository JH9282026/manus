### In-Camera Tools

**Zebras:**
- Indicate exposure levels
- Typically set for skin tone or highlight warning
- Help prevent overexposure

**Waveform:**
- Shows exposure distribution
- More accurate than histogram
- Essential for video exposure

**False Color:**
- Maps exposure to colors
- Quick visual reference
- Identifies over/underexposure

**LUT Preview:**
- Shows approximate final look
- Helps exposure decisions
- Don't rely solely on it
