### Interior vs Exterior Shooting

**Interior Challenges:**
- Limited natural light
- Color temperature mixing
- Space constraints
- Reflection management
- Sound considerations

**Interior Solutions:**
- Control all lighting
- Consistent color temperature
- Wide-angle lenses
- Careful composition
- Flag and shape light

**Exterior Challenges:**
- Changing light conditions
- Weather unpredictability
- Background control
- Sound challenges
- Crowd management

**Exterior Solutions:**
- Time shooting for light quality
- Diffusion and bounce tools
- ND filters essential
- Weather contingencies
- Location scouting
