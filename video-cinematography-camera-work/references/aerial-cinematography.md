### Aerial Cinematography

**Platforms:**
- Drones (most common now)
- Helicopter mounts
- Cable systems
- Cranes for low altitude

**Drone Cinematography:**
- Smooth, consistent movements
- Pre-planned flight paths
- Weather considerations
- Legal requirements
- Safety protocols

**Technique:**
- Slow, deliberate movements
- Maintain orientation
- Plan for wind
- Battery management
- Backup shots
