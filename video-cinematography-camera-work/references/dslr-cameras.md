### DSLR Cameras

**Digital Single-Lens Reflex (DSLR) cameras** brought cinematic video to mainstream creators:

**Advantages:**
- Large sensors for shallow depth of field
- Interchangeable lens systems
- Affordable entry point
- Dual photo/video capability
- Established lens ecosystems

**Limitations:**
- Video often secondary to photo features
- Rolling shutter artifacts
- Limited recording times (some models)
- Autofocus inconsistency (older models)
- Ergonomics designed for photography

**Popular Video DSLRs:**
- Canon 5D series (cinema revolution)
- Canon 90D (APS-C video features)
- Nikon D850 (4K capable)
- Nikon D780 (hybrid improvements)
