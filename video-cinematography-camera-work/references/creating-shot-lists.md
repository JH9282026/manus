### Creating Shot Lists

**Elements to Include:**
- Scene/sequence number
- Shot number
- Shot description
- Shot type/size
- Camera movement
- Equipment needed
- Notes

**Example Format:**
```
Scene 3: Kitchen Conversation
Shot 3A: Wide establishing, kitchen from doorway, tripod
Shot 3B: MS over Sarah's shoulder to John, dolly
Shot 3C: MCU Sarah, static
Shot 3D: MCU John, static
Shot 3E: CU hands cooking, insert, slider
```
