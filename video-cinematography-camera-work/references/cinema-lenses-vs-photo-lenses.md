### Cinema Lenses vs Photo Lenses

**Cinema Lens Characteristics:**
- Manual focus with long throw
- Consistent aperture through zoom
- Declicked aperture rings
- Front filter thread consistency
- Focus/iris gears for follow focus
- Calibrated focus marks
- Higher price point

**Photo Lens Considerations:**
- Autofocus capability
- Compact design
- Variable aperture in zooms
- Focus by wire limitations
- More affordable options
- Can work well for video
