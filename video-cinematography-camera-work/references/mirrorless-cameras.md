### Mirrorless Cameras

**Mirrorless cameras** have become the dominant video capture format:

**Advantages:**
- Compact body design
- Electronic viewfinder shows actual exposure
- No mirror mechanism (silent operation)
- Advanced autofocus systems
- Excellent video features
- IBIS (In-Body Image Stabilization)

**Limitations:**
- Battery consumption (EVF always active)
- Heat management for extended recording
- Smaller bodies = smaller batteries
- EVF refresh rate considerations

**Leading Video Mirrorless:**
- Sony A7S III (low-light champion)
- Sony FX3 (cinema-focused)
- Panasonic GH6/S5 II (video-first design)
- Canon R5/R6 (hybrid excellence)
- Fujifilm X-T5 (film simulations)
- Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera
