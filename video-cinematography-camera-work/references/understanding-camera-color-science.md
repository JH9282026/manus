### Understanding Camera Color Science

**What It Is:**
How cameras interpret and render color information varies by manufacturer:

- **ARRI:** Known for pleasing skin tones, natural colors
- **RED:** Saturated, contrasty default look
- **Sony:** Neutral, sometimes criticized for skin tones
- **Blackmagic:** Film-like, Resolve integration
- **Canon:** Warm, pleasing skin tones

**Implications:**
- Choose cameras based on look goals
- Understand grading requirements
- Match cameras in multi-cam
- Learn your camera's characteristics
