### High Speed Cinematography

**Applications:**
- Slow-motion effects
- Sports analysis
- Product impacts
- Nature behavior
- Scientific research

**Technical Requirements:**
- High frame rate cameras (240fps+)
- Significant lighting (less exposure time)
- Fast lenses for light gathering
- High-speed storage
- Specialized knowledge

**Planning:**
- Calculate light requirements
- Plan action timing
- Test before critical shots
- Backup recording plans
