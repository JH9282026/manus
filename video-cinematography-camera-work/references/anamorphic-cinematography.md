### Anamorphic Cinematography

**Characteristics:**
- Horizontal compression creates distinct look
- Oval bokeh
- Lens flares
- Wider aspect ratios (2.39:1 common)
- Cinematic "epic" feel

**Considerations:**
- Specialized (expensive) lenses
- Post-processing to desqueeze
- Unique focus characteristics
- Breathing and distortion
- Character-driven choice
