### Recommended Investment Order

**Phase 1 (Essential):**
- Quality tripod and fluid head
- External monitor
- Basic microphone
- Memory cards and storage

**Phase 2 (Enhancement):**
- Gimbal or stabilizer
- Additional lenses
- ND filters
- Small slider

**Phase 3 (Professional):**
- Professional audio
- Lighting kit
- Advanced monitoring
- Specialty lenses
