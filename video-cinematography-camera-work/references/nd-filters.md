### ND Filters

**Purpose:**
- Reduce light without affecting other settings
- Maintain shallow DOF in bright conditions
- Use proper shutter speed outdoors

**Types:**
- Solid ND (fixed density)
- Variable ND (adjustable)
- ND grads (graduated)
- Internal ND (cinema cameras)

**Common Densities:**
- ND 3 (3-stop)
- ND 6 (6-stop)
- ND 9 (9-stop)
