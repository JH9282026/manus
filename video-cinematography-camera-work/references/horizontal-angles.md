### Horizontal Angles

**Front Angle:**
- Direct confrontation
- Maximum connection
- Breaking fourth wall potential
- Most intense

**Three-Quarter Angle:**
- Most flattering for faces
- Shows dimensionality
- Standard interview angle
- Reduces confrontation

**Profile:**
- Side view
- Classic, dramatic
- Suggests nobility
- Less direct connection

**Over-Shoulder (OTS):**
- Perspective shot
- Establishes relationship
- Conversation framing
- Creates depth
