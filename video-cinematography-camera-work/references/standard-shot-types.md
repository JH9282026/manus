### Standard Shot Types

**Extreme Wide Shot (EWS):**
- Subject tiny in environment
- Establishes grand scale
- Shows location context
- Creates epic feel

**Wide Shot (WS):**
- Full body in frame
- Shows environment and subject
- Establishes scene geography
- Standard establishing shot

**Medium Wide Shot (MWS):**
- Knees up
- Shows body language
- More intimate than wide
- Cowboy shot

**Medium Shot (MS):**
- Waist up
- Conversational framing
- Shows gesture and expression
- Standard interview frame

**Medium Close-Up (MCU):**
- Chest up
- More intimate
- Focus on face
- Reduced context

**Close-Up (CU):**
- Face fills frame
- Maximum intimacy
- Shows subtle expressions
- Emotional emphasis

**Extreme Close-Up (ECU):**
- Eyes, mouth, hands
- Maximum intensity
- Stylistic choice
- Detail emphasis
