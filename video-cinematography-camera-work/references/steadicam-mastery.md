### Steadicam Mastery

**When to Use:**
- Following shots
- Long takes
- Floating through environments
- Intimate character moments
- Location exploration

**Operating Principles:**
- Balance system properly
- Smooth walking technique
- Anticipate movements
- Practice transitions
- Physical conditioning

**Alternatives:**
- Gimbals (electronic)
- Easy Rigs (arm support)
- Hybrid systems
- Each has trade-offs
