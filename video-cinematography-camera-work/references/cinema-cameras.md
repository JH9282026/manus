### Cinema Cameras

**Dedicated cinema cameras** prioritize video capture:

**Advantages:**
- Built for extended video recording
- Professional codec options (RAW, ProRes)
- Robust color science
- Built-in ND filters
- XLR audio inputs
- Superior dynamic range
- No recording limits

**Limitations:**
- Higher cost
- Larger, heavier bodies
- Steeper learning curve
- Accessory requirements
- Power consumption

**Professional Cinema Cameras:**
- Blackmagic URSA Mini Pro
- Canon C70/C300 Mark III
- Sony FX6/FX9
- RED Komodo/V-Raptor
- ARRI Alexa Mini
