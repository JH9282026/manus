### High Dynamic Range (HDR) Shooting

**HDR Fundamentals:**
- Captures wider range of brightness levels
- Requires HDR-capable displays for full effect
- Growing delivery format (streaming services)
- Different workflow from SDR

**HDR Shooting Considerations:**
- Monitor for HDR on set when possible
- Protect highlights and shadows
- Use full dynamic range of camera
- Plan for HDR/SDR delivery
- Color science considerations
