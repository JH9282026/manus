### The Moving Master

**Concept:**
A moving camera that covers an entire scene in one take:

**Benefits:**
- Maintains scene geography
- Creates immersion
- Showcases choreography
- Reduces editing needs
- Impressive achievement

**Challenges:**
- Technical complexity
- Blocking coordination
- Performance pressure
- Lighting challenges
- Limited coverage options

**Planning:**
- Extensive rehearsal
- Clear blocking marks
- Lighting that works throughout
- Backup traditional coverage
