### Pan and Tilt

**Pan (Horizontal Rotation):**
- Camera rotates left/right
- Reveals environment
- Follows horizontal action
- Establishes geography

**Tilt (Vertical Rotation):**
- Camera rotates up/down
- Reveals vertical scale
- Follows vertical action
- Creates reveal moment

**Execution Tips:**
- Smooth, consistent speed
- Start and end on stable compositions
- Follow action naturally
- Use fluid head for smoothness
