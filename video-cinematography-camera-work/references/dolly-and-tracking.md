### Dolly and Tracking

**Dolly (Forward/Backward):**
- Camera moves toward/away from subject
- Changes perspective
- Creates intimacy or distance
- Dolly in = intensification
- Dolly out = contextualization

**Track (Lateral Movement):**
- Camera moves parallel to action
- Follows moving subjects
- Creates dynamic energy
- Requires smooth surface or equipment

**Dolly Zoom (Vertigo Effect):**
- Dolly while zooming opposite direction
- Background changes while subject stays same
- Creates disorienting effect
- Used for psychological moments
