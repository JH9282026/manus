### Vertical Angles

**Eye Level:**
- Neutral perspective
- Most natural feeling
- Standard documentary angle
- No power dynamic implied

**High Angle:**
- Looking down at subject
- Diminishes subject
- Can suggest vulnerability
- Overview perspective

**Low Angle:**
- Looking up at subject
- Empowers subject
- Creates heroic feeling
- Dramatic effect

**Bird's Eye:**
- Directly overhead
- Abstract perspective
- God-like view
- Establishes geography

**Worm's Eye:**
- Ground level looking up
- Extreme empowerment
- Surreal perspective
- Dynamic impact
