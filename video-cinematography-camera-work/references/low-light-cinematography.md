### Low Light Cinematography

**Camera Considerations:**
- Large sensor cameras (full frame or larger)
- High native ISO capability
- Dual native ISO options
- Noise performance at high ISO

**Lens Considerations:**
- Fast aperture lenses (f/1.4 or wider)
- Prime lenses for maximum aperture
- Quality at wide apertures
- Focus challenges

**Lighting Solutions:**
- Add subtle, motivated lighting
- Practical lights in frame
- Embrace existing sources
- Balance ambient with controlled

**Technique:**
- Stabilization crucial
- Focus assistance needed
- Embrace grain as aesthetic
- Test camera limits beforehand
