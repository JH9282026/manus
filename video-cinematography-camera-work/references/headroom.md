### Headroom

Space above subject's head:

**Standard Headroom:**
- Eyes at upper third line
- Small but present gap above head
- Consistent across shot sizes
- Professional appearance

**Excessive Headroom:**
- Feels amateur
- Wastes frame space
- Diminishes subject
- Common beginner mistake

**No Headroom:**
- Intentional pressure/tension
- Cramped feeling
- Used for style
- Can feel uncomfortable
