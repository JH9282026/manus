### Aperture and T-Stops

**Aperture (f-stops):**
- Controls light entering lens
- Affects depth of field
- Lower number = wider opening
- f/1.4 vs f/5.6 = 4 stops difference

**T-stops (for cinema):**
- Measures actual light transmission
- More accurate than f-stops
- Accounts for light loss in lens elements
- Standard on cinema lenses

**Depth of Field Impact:**
- Wide aperture (f/1.4) = shallow depth of field
- Narrow aperture (f/8) = deep depth of field
- Sensor size affects DOF at same aperture
- Subject distance affects DOF
