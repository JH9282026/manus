### Advanced Dolly Techniques

**Parallel Tracking:**
- Camera moves alongside subject
- Maintains consistent distance
- Requires coordinated timing
- Creates floating feeling

**Push-In/Pull-Out:**
- Intensifying (push) or releasing (pull)
- Emotional emphasis
- Subject isolation
- Dramatic punctuation

**Arc Shots:**
- Camera circles subject
- Creates dimensionality
- Suggests importance
- Technical complexity

**Compound Moves:**
- Multiple movements combined
- Dolly while panning/tilting
- Crane with rotation
- Requires coordination
