### Handheld Cinematography

**When to Use:**
- Documentary realism
- Intense action
- Intimate moments
- Run-and-gun situations
- Creating urgency

**Handheld Technique:**
- Brace camera against body
- Keep arms close
- Breathe steadily
- Move from core, not extremities
- Use wide angles for stability

**Styles:**
- Controlled handheld (subtle movement)
- Energetic handheld (dynamic shake)
- Documentary handheld (natural observation)
