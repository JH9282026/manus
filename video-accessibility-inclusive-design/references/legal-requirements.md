
### United States Regulations

**Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)**:
- Applies to places of public accommodation
- Increasingly applied to websites and video
- Requires effective communication
- May require captions and audio descriptions

**Section 508**:
- Federal agencies and contractors
- WCAG 2.0 Level AA standard
- Video accessibility requirements
- Procurement standards

**21st Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act (CVAA)**:
- Broadcast and internet protocol video
- Caption requirements
- Audio description requirements
- Equipment accessibility

**FCC Regulations**:
- Broadcast caption requirements
- IP video caption requirements
- Quality standards for captions
- Audio description quotas

### International Regulations

**European Accessibility Act (EAA)**:
- EU-wide accessibility requirements
- Effective June 2025
- Covers digital services including video
- WCAG-based standards

**Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA)**:
- Ontario, Canada
- WCAG 2.0 Level AA
- Video accessibility requirements
- Phased implementation

**UK Accessibility Regulations**:
- Public sector accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA
- OFCOM broadcasting requirements
- Subtitling and signing quotas

**Australian DDA**:
- Discrimination Act applies to digital content
- WCAG-based expectations
- Broadcasting captioning requirements

### Compliance Strategies

**Risk Assessment**:
- Identify applicable regulations
- Assess current compliance level
- Prioritize remediation efforts
- Document compliance efforts

**Compliance Program**:
1. Establish accessibility policy
2. Define standards and procedures
3. Train content creators
4. Implement quality assurance
5. Document and report
6. Continuously improve
