## Encoding Settings

| Quality | Resolution | Bitrate | Framerate | Use Case |
|---------|-----------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Low | 720p | 2,500 kbps | 30fps | Limited bandwidth |
| Standard | 1080p | 4,500 kbps | 30fps | Most streams |
| High | 1080p | 6,000 kbps | 60fps | Fast motion/gaming |
| Ultra | 4K | 13,000 kbps | 30fps | Premium productions |

## Pre-Stream Checklist

- [ ] Test internet speed (upload ≥ 2x target bitrate)
- [ ] Audio levels check (-12 to -6 dB peaks)
- [ ] Lighting and framing verification
- [ ] Stream key and platform connection test
- [ ] Backup internet connection ready
- [ ] Scene transitions tested
- [ ] Graphics and overlays loaded
- [ ] Chat moderation configured
- [ ] Recording backup enabled
