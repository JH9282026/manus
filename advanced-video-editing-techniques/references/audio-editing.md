# Audio Editing in Video

Refine audio for professional video production.

---

## Audio-Video Synchronization

### Sync Techniques

- Clap/slate for manual sync
- Waveform matching
- Timecode alignment
- Software automatic sync

### Maintaining Sync

- Lock tracks to prevent drift
- Check sync periodically
- Address frame rate mismatches
- Export with matched settings

## Mix Levels

### Standard Levels

| Element | Target Level |
|---------|--------------|
| Dialogue | -12 to -6 dB |
| Music (under dialogue) | -18 to -24 dB |
| Music (featured) | -12 to -6 dB |
| Sound effects | Variable |
| Overall mix | -6 dB peak |

### Broadcast Standards

- LUFS measurements
- Loudness normalization
- True peak limiting
- Platform-specific requirements
