# Speed and Time Manipulation

Control temporal perception through speed effects.

---

## Speed Ramping

### Fundamentals

Smooth transitions between playback speeds:
- Normal speed to slow motion to normal
- Requires high frame rate source footage
- Creates emphasis on key moments

### Technical Requirements

- Source footage: 60fps minimum, 120-240fps ideal
- Sufficient resolution for quality
- Clean, sharp footage
- Plan ramp points during shooting

### Frame Rate Conversions

| Source | At 24fps | Slow Motion |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| 60fps | 40% speed | 2.5x slower |
| 120fps | 20% speed | 5x slower |
| 240fps | 10% speed | 10x slower |

### Applications

- Action highlights
- Sports key moments
- Dance and movement
- Dramatic reveals
- Product reveals

---

## Slow Motion

### Purpose

- Emphasize critical moments
- Create dreamlike quality
- Extend time for emotional impact
- Showcase detail in fast action

### Frame Blending Options

| Method | Quality | Use Case |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Frame sampling | Choppy | Stylistic choice |
| Frame blending | Smoother, artifacts | General use |
| Optical flow | Smoothest | High-quality work |

---

## Fast Motion / Time-Lapse

### Applications

- Compress extended time periods
- Show processes too slow to observe
- Create energetic sequences
- Setup/construction montages

### Techniques

- Shoot at normal speed, speed up in post
- Shoot true time-lapse (interval shooting)
- Hyperlapse with stabilization

---

## Freeze Frames

### Applications

- Stop action for emphasis
- Create punctuation
- Allow voiceover or graphics
- Begin speed ramps

### Implementation

- Export single frame
- Add motion effects if desired
- Match color and grain to sequence

---

## Time Remapping

### Advanced Control

- Variable speed throughout clip
- Complex timing curves
- Keyframe-based control
- Bezier interpolation for smoothness

### Keyframing Tips

- Use ease in/out for natural feel
- Match speed to emotional beats
- Preview at full speed frequently
- Consider audio implications

---

## Reverse Motion

### Applications

- Surreal, impossible effects
- Restoration/healing metaphors
- Magic and wonder
- Comedic effect

### Technical Notes

- Works best with fluid motion
- Audio typically removed or replaced
- Some footage reverses better than others
