### Modifying Outdoor Light

**Scrims and Diffusion:**
- Reduce intensity
- Soften shadows
- Require frames and stands
- Wind-sensitive

**Reflectors:**
- Bounce fill light
- Various surfaces (silver, gold, white)
- Require holders or stands
- Effective and portable

**Negative Fill:**
- Block ambient light
- Add contrast
- Create shadow
- Black fabric or floppy
