### Common Problems and Solutions

**Problem: Harsh Shadows**
- Solution: Use larger source or add diffusion
- Solution: Add fill light
- Solution: Move source closer

**Problem: Mixed Color Temperatures**
- Solution: Gel lights to match
- Solution: Choose dominant source
- Solution: Control environment

**Problem: Insufficient Light**
- Solution: Open aperture
- Solution: Increase ISO
- Solution: Add more fixtures
- Solution: Remove diffusion (carefully)

**Problem: Hot Spots**
- Solution: Move light further
- Solution: Add diffusion
- Solution: Adjust beam angle
- Solution: Bounce instead of direct
