### HMI Lighting

**Characteristics:**
- Extremely powerful output
- Daylight balanced (5600K)
- Efficient power-to-light ratio
- Professional standard for large spaces

**Applications:**
- Exterior daylight simulation
- Large interior spaces
- Through-window daylight effects
- Film and television standard

**Considerations:**
- High cost (purchase and rental)
- Power requirements
- Ballast equipment needed
- Flicker concerns at some frame rates
