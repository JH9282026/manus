### LED Technology Deep Dive

**LED Panel Types:**

**Bi-Color Panels:**
- Adjustable color temperature
- Typically 2700K-6500K range
- Variable intensity
- Most versatile general choice

**RGB/RGBW Panels:**
- Full color spectrum
- Creative effects
- App control common
- Higher price point

**Daylight-Only Panels:**
- Fixed 5600K output
- Often higher output
- Lower cost option
- Less flexibility

**Flexible LED:**
- Wraps around objects
- Fits tight spaces
- Even illumination
- Innovative applications
