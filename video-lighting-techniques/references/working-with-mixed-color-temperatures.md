### Working with Mixed Color Temperatures

**The Challenge:**
Multiple sources at different temperatures create color casts:
- Daylight through windows (5,600K)
- Interior tungsten lights (3,200K)
- Fluorescent overheads (variable)
- LED screens (variable)

**Solutions:**

**Gel Your Lights:**
- CTO (Color Temperature Orange): Warms cool lights
- CTB (Color Temperature Blue): Cools warm lights
- Plus/Minus Green: Corrects fluorescent

**Choose Dominant Source:**
- Balance to most important light
- Accept other sources as accent colors
- Use creatively in composition

**Control the Environment:**
- Black out windows to eliminate daylight
- Replace practical bulbs with matched temperature
- Use only controlled lighting
