### Maximizing Window Light

**Window as Key Light:**
- Position subject beside window
- Face angled toward light
- Distance controls intensity
- Time of day affects quality

**Diffusing Window Light:**
- Sheer curtains soften light
- Diffusion frames for control
- Frosted glass diffuses naturally
- Avoid direct sunlight through windows

**Bouncing Window Light:**
- Use reflector on shadow side
- White foam core as fill
- Reduces contrast ratio
- Maintains natural quality
