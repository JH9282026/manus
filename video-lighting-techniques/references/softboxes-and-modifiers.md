### Softboxes and Modifiers

**Softboxes:**
- Various sizes available
- Create soft, directional light
- Rectangular, octagonal, strip shapes
- Essential modifier

**Umbrellas:**
- Shoot-through (diffusion)
- Reflective (bounce)
- Affordable and portable
- Less control than softboxes

**Other Modifiers:**
- Beauty dishes
- Grids and honeycombs
- Barn doors
- Snoots
- Scrims and silks
