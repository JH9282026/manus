### Choosing Ratios

**Low Ratios (2:1 - 3:1):**
- Corporate and commercial
- Interview and documentary
- Fashion and beauty
- Safe, flattering

**Moderate Ratios (4:1 - 5:1):**
- Narrative filmmaking
- Character development
- Natural appearance
- Slight drama

**High Ratios (8:1+):**
- Film noir
- Psychological drama
- Horror
- Strong stylization
