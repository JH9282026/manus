### Product Lighting Setups

**Hero Product Shot:**
- Main key light
- Fill for shadow detail
- Back/rim light for separation
- Accent lights for highlights

**Tabletop Setup:**
- Sweep/curved background
- Overhead diffused light
- Side accents
- Reflectors for fill

**360 Product Video:**
- Consistent lighting all angles
- Even background illumination
- Multiple key positions or rotating product
- No harsh shadows
