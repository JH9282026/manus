### Corporate and Commercial Lighting

**Professional Standards:**
- Clean, even illumination
- Flattering but not overly dramatic
- Brand color considerations
- Consistent across shots

**Interview Setup:**
1. Position key at 30-45 degrees
2. Soften key with large modifier
3. Add fill to taste (reflector often sufficient)
4. Back light for separation
5. Light background separately

**Product Showcase:**
- Emphasize product features
- Create appealing highlights
- Show true colors
- Consider 360° viewing
