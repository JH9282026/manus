### Technical Considerations

**Camera Settings:**
- Wide aperture lenses (f/2.8 or faster)
- Higher ISO (know your camera's limits)
- Slower shutter speeds (know the consequences)
- Noise reduction settings

**Equipment:**
- Fast lenses essential
- Full-frame sensors preferred
- External monitors for focusing
- Low-light specialized cameras


---

### Technical Considerations

**Exposure:**
- Light meters can misinform
- Use waveforms and scopes
- Expose for face, not incident
- IRE/percentage targets vary

**Light Quality:**
- Larger, softer sources often better
- Reduces contrast on skin
- More forgiving of imperfections
- Creates even illumination
