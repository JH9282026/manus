### Gels and Color

**Color Temperature Correction:**
- CTO (orange): Converts daylight to tungsten
- CTB (blue): Converts tungsten to daylight
- Various strengths: 1/8, 1/4, 1/2, full

**Color Effects:**
- Colored gels for creative looks
- Party gel packs
- Color theory application
- Motivated color (police lights, etc.)
