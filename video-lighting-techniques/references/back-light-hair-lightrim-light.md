### Back Light (Hair Light/Rim Light)

**Purpose:**
- Separates subject from background
- Adds dimension and depth
- Creates highlights on hair/shoulders
- Adds polish to setup

**Placement:**
- Behind and above subject
- Opposite side from key
- Aimed at back of head/shoulders
- Flagged from camera lens

**Intensity:**
- Strong enough to see effect
- Not so strong as to overpower
- Typically similar to fill level
- Depends on hair/clothing color
