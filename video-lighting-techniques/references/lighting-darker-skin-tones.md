### Lighting Darker Skin Tones

**Key Considerations:**
- Use larger, softer key lights
- Consider higher key light placement
- Back light/rim light creates definition
- Separation from background essential

**Common Mistakes:**
- Underexposure
- Excessive contrast
- Insufficient separation
- Generic approaches

**Best Practices:**
- Increase overall light levels
- Reduce lighting ratios
- Add opalescent elements
- Choose complementary backgrounds
