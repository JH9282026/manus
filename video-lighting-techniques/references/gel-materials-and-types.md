### Gel Materials and Types

**Correction Gels:**
- CTO (Color Temperature Orange): 1/8, 1/4, 1/2, Full
- CTB (Color Temperature Blue): 1/8, 1/4, 1/2, Full
- Plus Green/Minus Green: Fluorescent correction

**Color Effect Gels:**
- Primary colors (red, blue, green)
- Secondary colors (cyan, magenta, yellow)
- Special effects colors
- Party gels (variety packs)
