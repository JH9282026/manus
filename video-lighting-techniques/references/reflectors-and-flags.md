### Reflectors and Flags

**Reflectors:**
- 5-in-1 collapsible (silver, gold, white, black, diffusion)
- Foam core boards
- Beadboard
- Shiny boards

**Flags and Cutters:**
- Black fabric on frames
- Control spill
- Create negative fill
- Shape light

**Frames:**
- 4x4, 6x6, 8x8, 12x12 foot frames
- Hold diffusion or bounce
- Hollywood standard
- Require C-stands
