### Weather Conditions

**Overcast:**
- Natural diffusion
- Low contrast
- Cool color cast
- Excellent for faces, flat for landscapes

**Partly Cloudy:**
- Unpredictable
- Light constantly changing
- Plan for continuity issues
- Have quick setups ready

**Rain:**
- Diffused, soft light
- Reflective surfaces
- Moody atmosphere
- Equipment protection essential
