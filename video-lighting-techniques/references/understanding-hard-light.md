### Understanding Hard Light

**Characteristics:**
- Sharp, defined shadows
- High contrast between lit and unlit areas
- Specular highlights
- Created by small or distant sources

**When to Use Hard Light:**
- Creating drama and tension
- Film noir aesthetic
- Horror and thriller genres
- Emphasizing texture
- Simulating direct sunlight
- Creating graphic shadows

**Sources of Hard Light:**
- Direct sunlight
- Bare bulbs
- Small LED panels
- Fresnel lights focused
- Spot fixtures
