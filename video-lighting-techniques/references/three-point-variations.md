### Three-Point Variations

**Broad Lighting:**
- Key lights the wider side of face
- More of face illuminated
- Can broaden appearance
- Standard for most uses

**Short Lighting:**
- Key lights the narrow side of face
- More dramatic
- Slimming effect
- Creates more depth

**Loop Lighting:**
- Small shadow loop from nose
- Key at 30-45 degrees
- Classic portrait lighting
- Flattering for most faces

**Rembrandt Lighting:**
- Triangle of light on shadow side cheek
- Key at 45+ degrees
- More dramatic
- Named after painter's style
