### Lighting Different Surfaces

**Matte Surfaces:**
- Absorb light evenly
- Easiest to light
- Soft light works well
- Direct light acceptable

**Glossy Surfaces:**
- Reflect light sources
- Show reflections of environment
- Require careful positioning
- Tent or gradient lighting

**Metallic Surfaces:**
- Reflect everything
- Need controlled reflections
- Light the reflections, not the object
- Gradients essential

**Translucent Products:**
- Back lighting reveals quality
- Combined front/back lighting
- Colored gels for effect
- Glass, bottles, liquids
