### Lighting the Screen

**Goals:**
- Perfectly even exposure
- No hot spots or dark areas
- Consistent color
- Separate from subject lighting

**Technique:**
- Light screen from both sides
- 45-degree angles
- Soft, diffused sources
- Measure with light meter or waveform

**Common Problems:**
- Center hot spot (lights too close)
- Edge falloff (lights too centered)
- Color variation (mixed sources)
- Shadows from subject (needs distance)
