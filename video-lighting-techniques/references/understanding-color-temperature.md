### Understanding Color Temperature

Color temperature measures the warmth or coolness of light, expressed in Kelvin (K):

**Common Color Temperatures:**
- Candlelight: ~1,800K (very warm)
- Tungsten/incandescent: ~3,200K (warm)
- Halogen: ~3,400K (warm)
- Fluorescent: ~4,000-5,000K (varies)
- Daylight: ~5,600K (neutral)
- Overcast sky: ~6,500K (cool)
- Shade: ~7,000-8,000K (very cool)
- Clear blue sky: ~10,000K+ (extremely cool)
