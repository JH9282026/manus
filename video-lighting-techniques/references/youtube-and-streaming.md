### YouTube and Streaming

**Self-Filming Considerations:**
- Consistent, reproducible setup
- Flattering for face
- Background interest
- Affordable solutions

**Basic YouTube Setup:**
1. Key light 45° off camera
2. Fill light or reflector opposite
3. Back light for separation
4. Ring lights as alternative

**Advanced Streaming:**
- RGB accent lighting
- Animated backgrounds
- Multiple cameras, consistent lighting
- Production value enhancement
