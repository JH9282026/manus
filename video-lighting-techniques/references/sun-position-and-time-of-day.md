### Sun Position and Time of Day

**Early Morning:**
- Low, directional light
- Golden tones
- Long shadows
- Best natural quality

**Mid-Morning/Mid-Afternoon:**
- Higher sun
- Harder shadows
- Less golden
- Manageable with modification

**Midday:**
- Overhead sun
- Harsh eye socket shadows
- High contrast
- Requires intervention

**Late Afternoon:**
- Returning to golden quality
- Lower angle
- Longer shadows
- Excellent shooting time
