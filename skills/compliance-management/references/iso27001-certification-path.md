# ISO 27001 Certification Path

Roadmap to ISO 27001 certification.

## ISMS Framework

### Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA)
- **Plan:** Establish ISMS
- **Do:** Implement and operate
- **Check:** Monitor and review
- **Act:** Maintain and improve

## Annex A Controls (14 Domains)

1. **A.5:** Information security policies
2. **A.6:** Organization of information security
3. **A.7:** Human resource security
4. **A.8:** Asset management
5. **A.9:** Access control
6. **A.10:** Cryptography
7. **A.11:** Physical and environmental security
8. **A.12:** Operations security
9. **A.13:** Communications security
10. **A.14:** System acquisition, development, maintenance
11. **A.15:** Supplier relationships
12. **A.16:** Information security incident management
13. **A.17:** Business continuity
14. **A.18:** Compliance

## Certification Process

### Stage 1: Documentation Review
- ISMS scope
- Policies and procedures
- Risk assessment
- Statement of Applicability (SoA)

### Stage 2: Implementation Audit
- On-site assessment
- Control testing
- Evidence review
- Interviews

### Certification Decision
- Non-conformities addressed
- Certificate issued (3-year validity)
- Annual surveillance audits

## Key Documents

- Information Security Policy
- Risk Assessment and Treatment Plan
- Statement of Applicability
- Internal Audit Reports
- Management Review Minutes
- Incident Response Procedures
- Business Continuity Plan
- Asset Inventory
- Access Control Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
