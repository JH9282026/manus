# AI Integration in HR

Implementing AI while maintaining human-centered approach.

## AI Use Cases

### Recruitment
- Resume screening
- Candidate matching
- Interview scheduling
- Chatbots for candidate engagement

### Performance Management
- Goal tracking automation
- Performance insights
- Development recommendations
- Feedback analysis

### Learning & Development
- Personalized learning paths
- Skills gap identification
- Content recommendations
- Progress tracking

## Governance Framework

### Ethical Considerations
- Algorithmic fairness
- Bias detection and mitigation
- Transparency in AI decisions
- Human oversight requirements

### Implementation Steps
1. Define AI strategy and objectives
2. Assess readiness and capabilities
3. Select appropriate use cases
4. Pilot and iterate
5. Scale with governance
6. Monitor and optimize

## Change Management

- Communicate AI's role clearly
- Provide hands-on training
- Address fears and concerns
- Show value and benefits
- Build AI literacy across organization
