# Workforce Planning Framework

Strategic approach to aligning talent with business needs.

## Planning Process

### 1. Business Strategy Alignment
- Understand organizational goals
- Identify critical capabilities
- Determine workforce implications

### 2. Current State Assessment
- Workforce demographics
- Skills inventory
- Capability gaps
- Succession risks

### 3. Future State Forecasting
- Growth projections
- Technology impact
- Market trends
- Regulatory changes

### 4. Gap Analysis
- Quantitative gaps (headcount)
- Qualitative gaps (skills, capabilities)
- Timing considerations
- Risk assessment

### 5. Action Planning
- Build vs. buy decisions
- Development programs
- Recruitment strategies
- Organizational redesign

## Tools and Techniques

- Scenario planning
- Predictive analytics
- Skills mapping
- Succession planning matrices
