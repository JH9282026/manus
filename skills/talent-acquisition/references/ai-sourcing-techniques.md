## Boolean Search Mastery

### Advanced Operators
- AND, OR, NOT
- Parentheses for grouping
- Quotes for exact phrases
- Wildcards (*)

### Example Searches
```
("software engineer" OR "developer") AND (Python OR Java) AND "machine learning" NOT intern
```
