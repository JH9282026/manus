# Candidate Experience Optimization

Creating positive, engaging recruitment experiences.

## Application Process

### Optimization Checklist
- Mobile-friendly design
- <10 minutes to complete
- Progress indicators
- Save and resume capability
- Clear instructions
- Minimal required fields

## Communication Strategy

### Touchpoints
1. Application confirmation (immediate)
2. Status update (within 1 week)
3. Interview invitation (within 2 weeks)
4. Post-interview follow-up (within 3 days)
5. Decision communication (within 1 week)

### Best Practices
- Personalized messages
- Timely responses
- Transparent timelines
- Constructive feedback
- Respectful rejections

## Interview Experience

### Preparation
- Clear agenda shared in advance
- Interview panel introductions
- Logistics and directions
- What to expect guidance

### During Interview
- Warm welcome
- Structured questions
- Time for candidate questions
- Next steps clarity

### Post-Interview
- Thank you message
- Feedback collection
- Timely decision
- Offer or rejection with reasoning
