# Engagement Survey Strategies

Measuring and improving employee engagement.

## Survey Types

### Annual Engagement Survey
- Comprehensive assessment
- 40-60 questions
- All engagement drivers
- Benchmark comparisons

### Pulse Surveys
- Quick check-ins
- 5-10 questions
- Monthly or quarterly
- Track trends

### Onboarding Surveys
- 30, 60, 90-day check-ins
- New hire experience
- Early intervention

### Exit Surveys
- Departure reasons
- Improvement opportunities
- Pattern identification

## Key Question Areas

### Job Satisfaction
- Role clarity
- Work meaningfulness
- Resource adequacy

### Manager Effectiveness
- Support and guidance
- Communication
- Recognition

### Career Development
- Growth opportunities
- Learning access
- Career path clarity

### Work Environment
- Team collaboration
- Work-life balance
- Company culture

### Compensation & Benefits
- Pay fairness
- Benefits satisfaction
- Total rewards

## Best Practices

### Survey Design
- Keep it concise
- Use validated questions
- Include open-ended questions
- Ensure anonymity
- Mobile-friendly

### Communication
- Explain purpose
- Guarantee confidentiality
- Set expectations
- Share timeline

### Action Planning
- Share results transparently
- Identify priorities
- Create action plans
- Assign ownership
- Track progress
- Communicate actions taken
