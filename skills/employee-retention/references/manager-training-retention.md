# Manager Training for Retention

Developing managers to retain talent.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Active Listening
- Give full attention
- Ask clarifying questions
- Reflect back understanding
- Avoid interrupting
- Show empathy

### 2. Regular One-on-Ones
- Weekly or biweekly meetings
- Employee-driven agenda
- Career discussions
- Feedback exchange
- Problem-solving

### 3. Recognition and Appreciation
- Specific, timely praise
- Public and private recognition
- Celebrate wins
- Acknowledge effort
- Personalize appreciation

### 4. Development Conversations
- Discuss career goals
- Identify growth opportunities
- Create development plans
- Provide stretch assignments
- Support learning

### 5. Feedback Delivery
- Timely and specific
- Balanced (positive and constructive)
- Actionable
- Two-way dialogue
- Follow-up

## Training Program

### Module 1: Manager's Role in Retention
- Impact of managers on turnover
- Cost of losing employees
- Manager responsibilities

### Module 2: Communication Skills
- Active listening
- Effective questioning
- Difficult conversations
- Feedback techniques

### Module 3: Recognition and Motivation
- Importance of recognition
- Recognition best practices
- Understanding motivators
- Personalization

### Module 4: Career Development
- Career conversations
- Development planning
- Growth opportunities
- Succession planning

### Module 5: Work-Life Balance
- Flexibility options
- Boundary setting
- Workload management
- Well-being support

## Ongoing Support

- Manager peer groups
- Coaching and mentoring
- Resources and tools
- Regular refreshers
- Performance accountability
