# Analytics Tools

Comprehensive guide to HR analytics platforms, BI tools, statistical software, and technology stack recommendations.

## Specialized HR Analytics Platforms
- **Visier:** Comprehensive people analytics, predictive models, 17x more accurate than guesswork
- **HRBench:** Predictive analytics for 25+ HR metrics with visual forecasts
- **isolved:** All four analytics types with easy dashboards

## Business Intelligence Platforms
- **Microsoft Power BI:** Data visualization, integrates with Microsoft ecosystem
- **Tableau:** Advanced visualization, Salesforce Einstein AI integration
- **Looker (Google):** Cloud-native, real-time dashboards

## All-in-One HRIS with Analytics
- **Workday:** Prism Analytics, predictive insights, benchmarking
- **SAP SuccessFactors:** Workforce Analytics, People Analytics
- **Oracle HCM Cloud:** Analytics Cloud, AI-powered insights

## Machine Learning Tools
- **Python:** scikit-learn, pandas, TensorFlow for custom models
- **R:** Statistical analysis and modeling
- **Decision Trees, Random Forest, XGBoost:** Common algorithms for HR predictions

## Selection Criteria
- Ease of use for HR team and managers
- Integration with existing systems
- Scalability and performance
- Cost (licensing, implementation, maintenance)
- Vendor support and roadmap
