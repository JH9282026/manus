# CRO Analytics Tools

Comprehensive guide to analytics tools for conversion rate optimization.

## Web Analytics Platforms

### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
- Event-based tracking
- Funnel analysis
- Free tier available
- Best for: General analytics

### Adobe Analytics
- Enterprise solution
- Advanced segmentation
- No data sampling
- Best for: Large organizations

### Matomo
- Privacy-focused
- Self-hosted option
- GDPR compliant
- Best for: Privacy requirements

## Heatmap & Session Recording Tools

### Hotjar
- Heatmaps, session recordings
- Surveys and feedback
- From $39/month
- Best for: Comprehensive insights

### Microsoft Clarity
- Free heatmaps and recordings
- Rage click detection
- Unlimited usage
- Best for: Budget-conscious teams

### Crazy Egg
- Visual analytics
- A/B testing
- From $29/month
- Best for: Visual optimization

## A/B Testing Platforms

### Optimizely
- Enterprise testing
- Advanced statistics
- Full-stack capabilities
- Best for: Large-scale testing

### VWO
- All-in-one CRO platform
- From $199/month
- Visual editor
- Best for: Mid-market

### AB Tasty
- GDPR compliant
- Personalization
- Best for: European market

## Product Analytics Tools

### Mixpanel
- Event tracking
- Funnel analysis
- Free tier available
- Best for: Product analytics

### Amplitude
- Behavioral cohorts
- Predictive analytics
- From $49/month
- Best for: Advanced analysis

### Heap
- Automatic event capture
- Retroactive analysis
- Best for: No-code tracking

## Tool Selection Guide

**Small Business:**
- GA4 (free)
- Microsoft Clarity (free)
- VWO or Convert
- Cost: $0-$300/month

**Mid-Market:**
- GA4 or Mixpanel
- Hotjar
- VWO
- Cost: $500-$2,000/month

**Enterprise:**
- Adobe Analytics
- Optimizely
- Amplitude
- Cost: $5,000-$50,000+/month
