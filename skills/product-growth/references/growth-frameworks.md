# Growth Frameworks

Comprehensive guide to product growth frameworks and methodologies.

## AARRR Framework (Pirate Metrics)

Acquisition → Activation → Retention → Revenue → Referral

The foundational framework for product growth covering the full user lifecycle.

## North Star Framework

Focus on single metric that best captures core product value.

**Examples:**
- Airbnb: Nights booked
- Spotify: Time spent listening
- Slack: Messages sent by team

## Growth Loops

Self-reinforcing cycles that drive compounding growth.

**Types:**
- Viral loops
- Content loops
- Paid loops
- Sales loops

## Sean Ellis Test

"How would you feel if you could no longer use this product?"
- Very disappointed: >40% = Product-market fit
