# Visual Perception & Analysis

## Scene Understanding
- Classify: indoor/outdoor, urban/nature, commercial/residential
- Recognize situations, events, activities
- Spatial relationships: foreground, midground, background
- Environmental context: time of day, weather, season

## Object Detection
- Multi-class detection with bounding boxes and confidence scores
- People, animals, vehicles, products, landmarks
- Count objects, estimate sizes, understand groupings

## Face Analysis
- Detection at various angles; 68+ landmark points
- Expression: happiness, sadness, anger, fear, surprise, neutral
- Demographic estimation (use responsibly)
- Gaze direction and attention scoring

## Emotion & Sentiment
- Facial emotion with intensity gradients (0-1)
- Scene mood: joyful, melancholic, tense, peaceful
- Overall sentiment: positive / negative / neutral

## OCR
- Scene text: signs, billboards, labels
- Documents: forms, receipts, cards
- Handwritten content; multi-language support
- Font identification and context interpretation

## Accessibility Descriptions
- Short alt-text: 125 chars
- Medium: 1-2 sentences
- Long-form: comprehensive narrative
- Context-appropriate (educational, commercial, editorial)
