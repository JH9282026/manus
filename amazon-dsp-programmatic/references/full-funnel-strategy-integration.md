# Full-Funnel Strategy and Cross-Channel Integration for Amazon DSP

## Introduction to Full-Funnel Marketing

Full-funnel marketing recognizes that customers go through multiple stages before making a purchase decision. Amazon DSP excels at supporting objectives across the entire marketing funnel, from initial awareness through post-purchase retention. This guide covers how to build comprehensive full-funnel strategies and integrate DSP with other marketing channels.

## The Marketing Funnel Framework

### Traditional Funnel Stages

**Awareness (Top of Funnel - TOFU):**
- Customer doesn't know about your brand or product
- Goal: Introduce brand and create recognition
- Audience: Cold, broad audiences
- Metrics: Impressions, Reach, Brand Lift

**Consideration (Middle of Funnel - MOFU):**
- Customer is aware and researching options
- Goal: Demonstrate value and differentiation
- Audience: Engaged users, category shoppers
- Metrics: Engagement, Detail Page Views, Video Completion

**Conversion (Bottom of Funnel - BOFU):**
- Customer is ready to purchase
- Goal: Drive immediate sales
- Audience: High-intent, remarketing
- Metrics: Purchases, ROAS, Conversion Rate

**Retention (Post-Purchase):**
- Customer has purchased
- Goal: Encourage repeat purchases and loyalty
- Audience: Past purchasers
- Metrics: Repeat purchase rate, Lifetime Value

### Modern Funnel Considerations

**Non-Linear Journey:**
- Customers move back and forth between stages
- Multiple touchpoints across devices and channels
- Research happens continuously
- Purchase decisions are complex

**Micro-Moments:**
- I-want-to-know moments (awareness)
- I-want-to-go moments (local intent)
- I-want-to-do moments (how-to)
- I-want-to-buy moments (purchase intent)

**Omnichannel Behavior:**
- Research online, buy in-store (ROPO)
- Research in-store, buy online (showrooming)
- Cross-device shopping journeys
- Social media influence

## Full-Funnel DSP Strategy

### Awareness Stage Strategy

#### Campaign Objectives
- Build brand recognition
- Reach new audiences
- Establish brand positioning
- Create mental availability

#### Recommended Tactics

**Audience Targeting:**
- Broad in-market audiences (category-level)
- Lifestyle segments aligned with brand values
- Demographic targeting (age, income, location)
- Contextual targeting (relevant content categories)
- Lookalike audiences (broad similarity, 5-10%)

**Ad Formats:**
- **OTT/Streaming TV:** High-impact, full-screen, non-skippable
  - 15-30 second spots
  - Brand storytelling
  - Emotional connection
  - Premium environment

- **Online Video (In-Stream):** Engaging video content
  - 15-30 second pre-roll
  - Product introduction
  - Brand values communication
  - Broad reach

- **Display (Large Formats):** Visual brand presence
  - Billboard (970x250)
  - Large rectangle (336x280)
  - Brand-focused creative
  - Lifestyle imagery

**Creative Strategy:**
- Focus on brand story and values
- Emotional resonance
- Problem identification
- Lifestyle association
- Minimal product focus
- Strong brand presence (logo, colors)

**Bidding and Budget:**
- vCPM bidding (optimizing for impressions)
- Larger budget allocation (40-50% of total)
- Lower CPM targets acceptable
- Focus on reach over efficiency

**Success Metrics:**
- Unique reach: 500K-1M+
- Frequency: 3-5 impressions
- Brand lift: 5-10%
- Aided awareness increase
- Video completion rate: 60-70%

### Consideration Stage Strategy

#### Campaign Objectives
- Drive product interest
- Encourage research and comparison
- Increase detail page views
- Build preference

#### Recommended Tactics

**Audience Targeting:**
- Specific in-market audiences (product-level)
- Users exposed to awareness campaigns
- Competitor ASIN viewers
- Category browsers
- Lookalike audiences (moderate similarity, 3-5%)
- Website visitors (non-converters)

**Ad Formats:**
- **Display (Product-Focused):** Showcase specific products
  - Medium rectangle (300x250)
  - Leaderboard (728x90)
  - Product imagery
  - Key features highlighted

- **Dynamic E-Commerce Ads:** Personalized product recommendations
  - Automatic product selection
  - Real-time pricing
  - Customer reviews
  - Star ratings

- **Video (Product Demos):** Show product in action
  - 15-30 seconds
  - Feature demonstrations
  - Use case scenarios
  - Benefit-focused

**Creative Strategy:**
- Highlight product benefits and features
- Demonstrate differentiation
- Show product in use
- Include social proof (reviews, ratings)
- Clear value proposition
- Comparison messaging (vs. competitors)

**Bidding and Budget:**
- CPC bidding (optimizing for engagement)
- Moderate budget allocation (30-40% of total)
- Target efficient CPCs
- Dynamic bidding (up and down)

**Success Metrics:**
- Click-through rate: 0.15-0.3%
- Detail page view rate: 2-5%
- Add-to-cart rate: 1-3%
- Time on site: 2-5 minutes
- Pages per session: 3-5

### Conversion Stage Strategy

#### Campaign Objectives
- Drive immediate purchases
- Maximize return on ad spend
- Acquire new customers
- Convert high-intent users

#### Recommended Tactics

**Audience Targeting:**
- Cart abandoners (last 1-7 days)
- Product detail page viewers (last 7-14 days)
- Website visitors (last 14-30 days)
- High-intent in-market audiences
- Lookalike audiences (high similarity, 1-3%)
- Competitor purchasers

**Ad Formats:**
- **Dynamic E-Commerce Ads:** Personalized product reminders
  - Products viewed or in cart
  - Real-time pricing and availability
  - Reviews and ratings
  - Prime badge

- **Display (Conversion-Focused):** Direct response messaging
  - Standard IAB sizes
  - Strong call-to-action
  - Urgency messaging
  - Promotional offers

**Creative Strategy:**
- Strong, clear call-to-action ("Buy Now", "Shop Now")
- Urgency and scarcity ("Limited Time", "While Supplies Last")
- Promotional offers (discounts, free shipping)
- Product-specific messaging
- Minimal distractions
- Direct path to purchase

**Bidding and Budget:**
- CPC or dynamic bidding (up and down)
- Optimize for conversions
- Higher bids acceptable for high-intent audiences
- Smaller budget allocation (20-30% of total)
- Focus on efficiency (ROAS, CPA)

**Success Metrics:**
- Conversion rate: 2-5%
- Return on ad spend: 4:1 to 6:1
- Cost per acquisition: Within target margins
- New-to-brand rate: 60-80%
- Cart abandonment recovery rate: 10-20%

#### Campaign Objectives
- Encourage repeat purchases
- Increase customer lifetime value

#### Recommended Tactics

**Audience Targeting:**
- Past purchasers (30-90 days ago)

- Subscription renewal windows

**Ad Formats:**

**Bidding and Budget:**
- Moderate bids (lower than acquisition)

**Success Metrics:**
- Repeat purchase rate: 20-40%
- Time to second purchase
- Customer lifetime value increase
- Average order value increase
- Subscription conversion rate

## Sequential Messaging Strategy

### Concept

Deliver different messages to users based on their previous interactions with your ads, guiding them through the funnel with relevant messaging at each stage.

### Implementation

**Step 1: Awareness Message**
- Audience: Cold, broad audiences
- Message: Brand introduction, problem identification
- Creative: Video or lifestyle display
- Duration: Expose for 7-14 days

**Step 2: Create Exposed Audience**
- Build audience of users who:
  - Saw awareness ad (impression)
  - Clicked awareness ad
  - Completed video view
- Exclude: Users who converted

**Step 3: Consideration Message**
- Audience: Exposed to awareness, not converted
- Message: Product benefits, differentiation
- Creative: Product-focused display or demo video
- Duration: Expose for 7-14 days

**Step 4: Create Engaged Audience**
- Build audience of users who:
  - Engaged with consideration ad
  - Visited website
  - Viewed product detail page
- Exclude: Users who converted

**Step 5: Conversion Message**
- Audience: Engaged with consideration, not converted
- Message: Offer, urgency, call-to-action
- Creative: Dynamic e-commerce ads, promotional display
- Duration: Expose for 14-30 days

**Step 6: Measure Lift**
- Compare conversion rate of sequential audience vs. control
- Measure time to conversion
- Analyze cost per acquisition
- Calculate incremental ROAS

### Best Practices

- Frequency cap each stage (3-5 impressions)
- Allow sufficient time between stages (3-7 days)
- Ensure creative consistency (branding, messaging)
- Test different message sequences
- Measure incrementality vs. standard remarketing

## Cross-Channel Integration

### Amazon DSP + Sponsored Ads

**Strategy:**
- DSP for awareness and consideration (off-Amazon reach)
- Sponsored Products for high-intent keyword capture
- Sponsored Brands for branded search defense
- Sponsored Display for on-Amazon remarketing

**Audience Flow:**
1. DSP introduces brand to cold audiences
2. Users search on Amazon
3. Sponsored Products captures search traffic
4. Sponsored Display retargets non-converters

**Measurement:**
- Use Amazon Attribution to track DSP impact on Sponsored Ads performance
- Analyze search volume increase for branded terms
- Measure new-to-brand rate across ad types

### Amazon DSP + Social Media (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)

**Strategy:**
- Social for community building and engagement
- DSP for purchase-intent targeting
- Coordinate messaging and creative themes
- Cross-channel remarketing

**Audience Syncing:**
- Upload social engagers to DSP as custom audience
- Use Amazon Attribution pixels on social campaigns
- Retarget social ad viewers with DSP
- Exclude Amazon converters from social campaigns

**Measurement:**
- Amazon Attribution for social impact on Amazon sales
- Multi-touch attribution across channels
- Incremental reach analysis

### Amazon DSP + Search (Google, Bing)

**Strategy:**
- Search for high-intent keyword capture
- DSP for audience-based targeting
- Coordinate on branded vs. non-branded keywords
- Sequential messaging (DSP awareness → Search conversion)

**Tactics:**
- Use DSP to build awareness, driving branded search volume
- Retarget search ad clickers with DSP
- Use Amazon Attribution to measure search impact on Amazon
- Optimize search bids based on DSP exposure

**Measurement:**
- Branded search volume lift from DSP campaigns
- Conversion rate of DSP-exposed users on search
- Cross-channel ROAS

### Amazon DSP + Email Marketing

**Strategy:**
- Email for owned audience communication
- DSP for reaching email subscribers off-site
- Coordinate promotional timing
- Reinforce email messages with display ads

**Tactics:**
- Upload email list to DSP as custom audience
- Retarget email openers with DSP ads
- Exclude email converters from DSP
- Use DSP to re-engage inactive email subscribers

**Measurement:**
- Lift in email engagement from DSP exposure
- Conversion rate of multi-channel exposed users
- Incremental revenue from coordinated campaigns

### Amazon DSP + Retail Media Networks

**Strategy:**
- DSP for Amazon ecosystem
- Other retail media (Walmart, Target, Instacart) for their ecosystems
- Coordinate category-level strategies
- Avoid audience fatigue across networks

**Considerations:**
- Different purchase behaviors on each platform
- Platform-specific creative and messaging
- Unified measurement challenges
- Budget allocation based on sales distribution

## Unified Measurement Framework

### Data Integration

**Centralized Data Warehouse:**
- Aggregate data from all channels
- Standardize metrics and dimensions
- Enable cross-channel analysis
- Support unified reporting

**Tools:**
- Amazon Marketing Cloud (for Amazon data)
- Google BigQuery or AWS Redshift (for multi-source data)
- Data integration platforms (Fivetran, Stitch)
- BI tools (Tableau, Looker, Power BI)

### Attribution Modeling

**Multi-Touch Attribution:**
- Assign credit to all touchpoints in customer journey
- Use data-driven attribution when possible
- Compare different attribution models
- Understand channel interactions

**Incrementality Testing:**
- Holdout groups (PSA - Public Service Announcement)
- Geo-based testing (test vs. control markets)
- Time-based testing (on vs. off periods)
- Measure true incremental impact

### Unified KPIs

**Channel-Specific KPIs:**
- Each channel has unique strengths and metrics
- Optimize within channel for channel-specific goals

**Blended KPIs:**
- Overall ROAS across all channels
- Total customer acquisition cost
- Customer lifetime value
- Marketing efficiency ratio (MER)

**Formula:**
```
Marketing Efficiency Ratio (MER) = Total Revenue / Total Marketing Spend
```

### Budget Allocation Optimization

**Marginal ROAS Analysis:**
- Calculate incremental ROAS for each channel
- Allocate budget to channels with highest marginal ROAS
- Continuously test and rebalance

**Process:**
1. Establish baseline performance for each channel
2. Incrementally increase budget in one channel
3. Measure incremental return
4. Calculate marginal ROAS
5. Reallocate budget to highest marginal ROAS channels
6. Repeat monthly or quarterly

## Key Takeaways

1. Full-funnel strategies address customers at every stage of their journey
2. Each funnel stage requires different audiences, creative, and metrics
3. Sequential messaging guides customers through the funnel with relevant messages
4. Cross-channel integration amplifies reach and effectiveness
5. Amazon DSP works best when coordinated with Sponsored Ads and external channels
6. Unified measurement is essential for understanding true channel impact
7. Budget allocation should be based on incremental ROAS, not last-click attribution
8. Seasonal planning ensures campaigns align with customer needs and shopping behavior
9. Test and optimize continuously across the full funnel
10. Balance short-term conversion goals with long-term brand building
