# Lead Generation Advanced

Implement sophisticated lead generation strategies using intent data, ABM targeting, AI prospecting, and multi-channel orchestration to identify and convert high-value B2B prospects.

## Overview

Advanced lead generation in 2026 moves beyond basic inbound/outbound to leverage intent signals, predictive analytics, and multi-channel orchestration. This skill covers the strategies, tools, and frameworks needed to generate pipeline from high-value accounts at scale while maintaining personalization.

## Intent Data Strategy

### Intent Data Sources

| Source Type | Examples | Signal Strength | Cost | Use Case |
|------------|---------|-----------------|------|----------|
| First-party | Website visits, content downloads, pricing page views | Very high | Free (own data) | Retargeting, lead scoring |
| Second-party | G2/TrustRadius reviews, partner data sharing | High | $$ | Competitive conquest, in-market identification |
| Third-party | Bombora, TechTarget, ZoomInfo Intent | Medium-high | $$$–$$$$ | Net-new demand identification |
| Technographic | BuiltWith, Wappalyzer, HG Insights | Medium | $$–$$$ | Technology-based targeting |
| Hiring signals | LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, company pages | Medium | $–$$ | Budget/priority identification |
| News/events | Funding rounds, leadership changes, expansions | Medium | $–$$ | Trigger-based outreach |

| Tier | Accounts | Approach | Channels | Content | Budget/Account |

### AI Personalization at Scale

1. **Research automation** — Use AI to scan prospect's LinkedIn, company news, tech stack, and recent content in seconds
2. **Dynamic email generation** — AI writes unique first lines and value propositions per prospect based on research
3. **Optimal timing** — ML models predict best send times per recipient based on historical engagement
4. **Channel selection** — AI recommends best outreach channel based on prospect behavior patterns
5. **Response classification** — Automatically categorize replies (interested, objection, not now, unsubscribe) and route accordingly

## Best Practices

1. **Lead with insight, not product** — Share a relevant finding or perspective before pitching
2. **Orchestrate, don't blast** — Coordinate timing across channels so prospects feel surrounded, not spammed
3. **Prioritize by intent signals** — Warm outreach to high-intent accounts converts 5–10x better than cold
4. **Build for the buying committee** — B2B deals involve 6–10 stakeholders; generate leads from multiple personas per account
5. **Track full-funnel attribution** — Measure pipeline and revenue, not just MQLs
6. **Align sales and marketing on ICP** — Misalignment here wastes the entire lead gen investment
