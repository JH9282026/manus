# Corporate & Brand Video Production

Create video content that communicates organizational identity, values, culture, and messages to build long-term brand equity and stakeholder connection.

## Overview

Unlike product-focused marketing videos, corporate video builds brand equity, shapes perception, and creates emotional connections. Video increases understanding of products/services by 74% and reaches audiences more effectively than text alone.

## Production Process

### Pre-Production
1. Discovery and objective clarification
2. Concept and script development
3. Storyboarding and planning
4. Location scouting and talent booking

### Production
1. Equipment and location setup
2. B-roll and interview capture
3. On-set review and adjustments
4. Backup recording

### Post-Production
1. Footage assembly and rough cut
2. Color correction and graphics
3. Audio mixing and music
4. Review cycles and final approval

## Storytelling Framework

### Hero's Journey (Simplified)
1. Ordinary world (status quo)
2. Call to adventure (challenge)
3. Guidance (brand/solution)
4. Transformation (result)
5. New world (improved state)

### Brand Story Elements
- Why we exist (purpose)
- What we believe (values)
- How we act (proof)
- Impact we create (results)
