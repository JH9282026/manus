# Corporate Video Types

Specifications and best practices for different corporate video formats.

---

## Company Overview Videos

### Purpose
Introduce organization to external audiences.

### Key Elements
- Company history/founding story
- Mission and values
- Products/services overview
- Differentiators
- Customer impact
- Call to action

### Specifications
- Length: 2-4 minutes
- Tone: Professional, confident, authentic
- Applications: Website, sales presentations, trade shows

---

## Culture Videos

### Purpose
Showcase company culture and work environment.

### Content Elements
- Real office/workspace footage
- Employee interviews
- Team activities and events
- Values in action
- Diversity representation

### Authenticity Tips
- Use real employees, not actors
- Capture genuine moments
- Minimal scripting
- Show diversity naturally
- Avoid corporate jargon

---

## Recruitment Videos

### Purpose
Attract qualified candidates.

### Content Elements
- Day-in-the-life footage
- Role descriptions
- Growth opportunities
- Culture highlights
- Benefits overview
- Location features

### Distribution
- Careers page
- LinkedIn
- Job postings
- University recruiting
- Career fairs

---

## Executive Messaging

### Types
- CEO messages
- Quarterly updates
- Strategic announcements
- Crisis communications
- Celebratory messages

### Best Practices
- Authentic delivery
- Clear, concise messaging
- Professional setting
- Teleprompter support
- Multiple takes
