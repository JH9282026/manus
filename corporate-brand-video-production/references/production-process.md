# Production Process

Detailed workflow for corporate video production.

---

## Pre-Production

### Discovery Phase
1. Stakeholder interviews
2. Objective clarification
3. Audience definition
4. Message refinement
5. Reference review
6. Success metrics definition

### Creative Development
1. Concept exploration (3-5 directions)
2. Treatment/approach document
3. Script development
4. Storyboard creation
5. Approval milestones

### Planning
- Budget finalization
- Schedule creation
- Location scouting
- Talent booking
- Equipment planning
- Legal releases

---

## Production Phase

### Equipment Essentials
- Camera: Cinema or professional mirrorless
- Audio: Lavalier and shotgun microphones
- Lighting: LED panels with modifiers
- Grip: Tripods, sliders, stabilizers

### Shooting Guidelines
- Capture variety (wide, medium, close)
- Get more B-roll than needed
- Multiple takes for interviews
- On-set audio monitoring
- Daily backup procedures

### Interview Tips
- Pre-interview conversation
- Open-ended questions
- Comfortable environment
- Eye contact with interviewer
- Pause for clean edits

---

## Post-Production

### Edit Process
1. Footage organization and selects
2. Rough cut assembly
3. Music placement
4. Fine cut refinement
5. Color correction
6. Graphics and titles
7. Audio mixing
8. Final review and delivery

### Review Cycle
1. Rough cut review (structure)
2. Fine cut review (pacing)
3. Final review (polish)
4. Sign-off and delivery

### Delivery Formats
- Master: ProRes 422 HQ / DNxHD
- Web: H.264 / MP4
- Social: Platform-specific specs
