# Brand Storytelling

Narrative frameworks for creating emotional connection through video.

---

## Story Elements

### Protagonist
Who is the hero?
- The customer (most effective)
- The founder
- The employee
- The community

### Conflict/Challenge
- Problem to solve
- Obstacle to overcome
- Question to answer
- Journey to complete

### Resolution
- How the brand helps
- Transformation achieved
- Problem solved
- Better future realized

---

## Storytelling Frameworks

### Hero's Journey
1. Ordinary world
2. Call to adventure
3. Refusal/Doubt
4. Meeting the mentor
5. Crossing the threshold
6. Tests and allies
7. Ordeal
8. Reward
9. Return transformed

### Before-After-Bridge
1. Before: Current pain point
2. After: Desired outcome
3. Bridge: How brand enables change

### Purpose-Led Story
1. Why we exist
2. What we believe
3. How we act
4. Impact we create

---

## Emotional Triggers

### Universal Emotions
- Belonging and community
- Achievement and success
- Security and trust
- Discovery and possibility
- Care and compassion

### Building Emotion
- Real people, real stories
- Authentic moments
- Music and pacing
- Visual beauty
- Relatable situations

---

## Authenticity Checklist

✓ Real employees and customers
✓ Genuine emotions captured
✓ Honest challenges acknowledged
✓ Diverse perspectives included
✓ Minimal corporate jargon
✗ Staged or fake moments
✗ Over-scripted content
✗ Exclusionary imagery
