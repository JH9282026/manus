# Funnel Types & Architectures

## Sales Funnels

### Low-Ticket Sales Funnels ($1-$100)
- Minimal friction, impulse-friendly checkout
- Simple flow: Ad → Sales Page → Order Form → Thank You
- Volume-focused strategy with order bumps to increase AOV
- One-click upsell pages post-purchase

### High-Ticket Sales Funnels ($1,000+)
- Multi-step qualification: Content → Application → Call → Proposal → Close
- Trust-building content throughout the journey
- Sales calls or consultations required
- Longer nurture sequences with case studies

### Tripwire Funnels
- Low-cost entry offer ($7-$47) converts leads into customers quickly
- Front-end offer → Upsell sequence → Core offer
- Leverages psychology: converting someone from "lead" to "customer" changes the relationship

## Lead Generation Funnels

### Opt-in / Squeeze Page Funnels
- Focus on email capture with minimal form fields
- Clear value proposition for subscribing
- Flow: Traffic → Opt-in Page → Thank You → Email Sequence

### Lead Magnet Funnels
- Offer valuable content (ebook, checklist, template) in exchange for contact info
- Qualify leads by topic interest
- Flow: Ad → Squeeze Page → Delivery Page → Nurture Sequence

## Webinar Funnels
- Registration page highlighting webinar value and speaker authority
- Reminder sequence building anticipation
- Replay access for non-attendees with urgency
- Pitch transition in final 20% of webinar
- Flow: Ad → Registration → Reminders → Live/Replay → Offer → Follow-up

## Product Launch Funnels
- Pre-launch: build anticipation, early access lists, seed content
- Launch: time-limited availability, cart open/close mechanics, special pricing
- Post-launch: evergreen transition, testimonial collection

## Survey / Quiz Funnels
- Leverage micro-commitments and personalization
- Progressive questions build investment
- Route to appropriate offers based on responses
- Personalized pitch based on survey answers

## Challenge Funnels
- Multi-day challenge combining education with community
- Registration → Daily content → Community → Quick wins → Offer
- Excel for coaching, fitness, and educational businesses

## Application Funnels
- For high-ticket services and consulting
- Qualification page → Application form → Booking page → Confirmation → Call

## E-commerce Funnels
- Product → Cart → Checkout → Upsell/Cross-sell → Thank You
- Abandoned cart recovery sequences at 1hr, 24hr, 48hr, 7 days

## Evergreen Funnels
- Automated, always-on funnels simulating live experiences
- Automated webinars with simulated live elements
- Deadline funnels with rolling expiration
- Continuous traffic and conversion year-round

## Funnel Page Types

### Opt-in Pages (Squeeze Pages)
- Minimal distractions, no navigation
- Clear value proposition and compelling headline
- Simple form (email only or name + email)
- Strong action-oriented CTA button text

### Sales Pages
- **Long-form**: 3,000-10,000+ words; AIDA or PAS framework; for complex high-ticket offers
- **Short-form**: 500-1,500 words; for simple low-ticket offers
- **VSL (Video Sales Letter)**: Video-driven; 15-60 minutes; controls often hidden

### Upsell / Downsell Pages
- Post-purchase one-time offers (OTOs) with one-click purchase
- Downsell offers lower price or payment plan when upsell declined

### Thank You Pages
- Confirm action, set expectations, provide next steps
- Present additional offers or sharing opportunities

### Bridge Pages
- Warm cold traffic before sales page
- Pre-selling and context setting; story-driven

### Order Forms
- Clean, trustworthy design with security indicators
- Clear pricing, multiple payment options, order bump placement
