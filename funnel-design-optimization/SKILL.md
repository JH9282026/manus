# Funnel Design & Optimization

Design, build, and optimize multi-step customer journeys that maximize conversions at every stage from awareness through advocacy.

## Overview

This skill provides frameworks, architectures, and optimization strategies for creating high-converting marketing and sales funnels. It covers funnel types, page design, copywriting, email sequences, analytics, and platform selection to build predictable revenue engines.

## Core Funnel Principles

1. **One page, one goal** — eliminate distractions; every element serves the conversion objective
2. **Match message to market** — ensure ad copy, landing page, and offer are aligned
3. **Reduce friction** — remove unnecessary form fields, steps, and decisions
4. **Build trust progressively** — layer social proof, guarantees, and authority at each stage
5. **Create urgency ethically** — use legitimate scarcity and deadlines
6. **Optimize for mobile** — over 50% of traffic is mobile; design accordingly
7. **Test relentlessly** — headlines, CTAs, page layout, and email sequences

## Funnel Stages (TOFU → MOFU → BOFU)

### Top of Funnel (Awareness)
- Drive cold traffic via paid ads, SEO, social, or referrals
- Focus on value-first messaging and pattern interrupts
- Capture attention with lead magnets or compelling hooks

### Middle of Funnel (Consideration)
- Nurture with email sequences, retargeting, and content upgrades
- Build relationship through storytelling and case studies
- Warm prospects with testimonials and proof elements

### Bottom of Funnel (Decision)
- Present irresistible offers with clear value stacking
- Handle objections via FAQs, guarantees, and social proof
- Create urgency with deadlines, bonuses, and scarcity

### Post-Purchase (Retention & Advocacy)
- Onboard with welcome sequences and quick wins
- Upsell and cross-sell complementary products
- Turn customers into advocates via referral programs

## Key Funnel Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures | Target Benchmark |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Conversion Rate | % completing desired action | 2-5% cold, 10-25% warm |
| Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) | Cost to acquire one customer | Varies by industry |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Revenue per transaction | Increase via upsells/bumps |
| Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) | Total revenue per customer | 3x+ CPA |
| Funnel Velocity | Days from first touch to purchase | Reduce over time |
| Drop-off Rate | Where people exit the funnel | Identify and fix leaks |
