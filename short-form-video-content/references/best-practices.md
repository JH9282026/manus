# Best Practices

Detailed reference content for the Short Form Video Content skill.

---

## Best Practices


### Content Creation Best Practices

1. **Hook first, always**: Every video needs a strong opening
2. **Deliver value quickly**: Respect viewers' time
3. **Stay niche**: Focused content builds dedicated audience
4. **Maintain authenticity**: Audiences detect fakeness
5. **Embrace imperfection**: Done beats perfect
6. **Batch create**: Efficiency in production
7. **Study your niche**: Learn from successful creators in your space
8. **Engage genuinely**: Respond to comments, build community
9. **Experiment constantly**: Test new formats and approaches
10. **Analyze and iterate**: Use data to improve


### Platform Best Practices

1. **Post consistently**: 1-3+ times daily for growth
2. **Use native features**: Platforms reward native content
3. **Optimize timing**: Post when your audience is active
4. **Cross-promote wisely**: Adapt content for each platform
5. **Stay current**: Keep up with platform changes
6. **Engage authentically**: Be active beyond just posting
7. **Monitor trends**: Act on relevant trends quickly
8. **Test everything**: Don't assume—measure


### Brand and Business Best Practices

1. **Lead with value**: Educate and entertain, don't just promote
2. **Humanize the brand**: Show real people and behind-the-scenes
3. **Adapt to platform culture**: Don't force corporate content
4. **Engage with community**: Respond, collaborate, participate
5. **Track ROI**: Connect short-form efforts to business outcomes
6. **Build funnel integration**: Connect short-form to larger strategy
7. **Leverage UGC**: Encourage and share user content
8. **Stay compliant**: Follow platform advertising guidelines
