# Common Mistakes

Detailed reference content for the Short Form Video Content skill.

---

## Common Mistakes


### Content Mistakes

1. **Weak or missing hook**: Failing to grab attention in first 3 seconds
2. **Too much setup**: Taking too long to get to the point
3. **No clear value**: Viewer left wondering "why did I watch this?"
4. **Overproduction**: Polished but inauthentic content that doesn't resonate
5. **Inconsistent posting**: Sporadic activity hurts algorithm favor
6. **Ignoring trends**: Missing discovery opportunities
7. **Forcing trends**: Participating in irrelevant trends damages authenticity
8. **Poor audio**: Unclear speech, bad music choices, volume issues
9. **No CTA**: Missing opportunity to convert viewers to followers/customers


### Technical Mistakes

1. **Wrong aspect ratio**: Not optimized for vertical viewing
2. **Low resolution**: Blurry or pixelated content
3. **Platform watermarks**: TikTok logo on Instagram, etc.
4. **Poor lighting**: Dark or unflattering lighting
5. **Text in wrong places**: Covered by platform UI
6. **Too slow pacing**: Viewers scroll before value is delivered
7. **Captions missing**: Losing viewers who watch without sound


### Strategic Mistakes

1. **Chasing virality over consistency**: Random content instead of building niche
2. **Ignoring analytics**: Not learning from performance data
3. **No content pillars**: Unfocused content strategy
4. **Copying instead of adapting**: Replicating without adding value
5. **Wrong platform focus**: Investing in platforms where audience isn't
6. **Expecting immediate results**: Short-form success takes time and volume
