## Analytics and Metrics


### Key Metrics to Track

**Reach Metrics**:
- Views (total plays)
- Reach (unique accounts reached)
- Impressions (total times displayed)

**Engagement Metrics**:
- Likes, comments, shares, saves
- Engagement rate (engagements/reach or views)
- Follower growth

**Retention Metrics**:
- Average watch time
- Percentage watched
- Drop-off points
- Replay rate

**Conversion Metrics**:
- Profile visits
- Link clicks
- Follows from content
- Website traffic from platform


### Platform Analytics Tools

**TikTok Analytics** (Creator/Pro Account):
- Overview, Content, Followers, Live tabs
- Video-specific performance
- Audience demographics
- Traffic sources

**Instagram Insights**:
- Reels-specific analytics
- Reach and engagement breakdowns
- Audience demographics
- Activity patterns

**YouTube Studio** (Shorts Analytics):
- Shorts performance tab
- Audience retention graphs
- Traffic source analysis
- Subscriber growth tracking


### Using Analytics for Improvement

1. **Identify patterns**: What content performs best?
2. **Study outliers**: Why did certain videos over/underperform?
3. **Analyze retention**: Where do viewers drop off?
4. **Test hypotheses**: Use data to inform experiments
5. **Track trends**: Monitor performance over time
6. **Compare platforms**: What works differently on each?
