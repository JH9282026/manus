## Content Formats



### Educational Content

**Quick Tips**: Single, actionable piece of advice
**Tutorials**: How-to in 60 seconds or less
**Myth Busting**: Correct common misconceptions
**Facts and Statistics**: Surprising information presented engagingly
**Explainers**: Complex topics made simple
**Process Walkthroughs**: Show steps of a process quickly

**Educational Content Tips**:
- Start with the outcome/result
- Use visual demonstrations over verbal explanation
- Break complex topics into series
- Use text overlays to reinforce verbal points
- Include a clear takeaway



### Entertainment Content

**Comedy**: Skits, parodies, reactions, relatable humor
**Drama**: Micro-stories, cliffhangers, emotional moments
**Satisfying Content**: ASMR, organization, completion content
**Fails and Bloopers**: Behind-the-scenes mishaps
**Reactions**: Response content to other videos or events
**Challenges**: Participation in viral challenges

**Entertainment Content Tips**:
- Commit fully to the bit
- Timing is everything—edit ruthlessly
- Unexpected endings outperform predictable ones
- Relatable situations resonate broadly
- Music and sound effects enhance comedic timing



### Behind-the-Scenes Content

**Day in the Life**: Show authentic daily activities
**Process Content**: How products are made, how work gets done
**Workspace Tours**: Physical space reveals
**Team Content**: Employee highlights, team dynamics
**Raw Moments**: Unpolished, authentic situations

**BTS Content Tips**:
- Authenticity over polish
- Narration adds context and personality
- Show struggles, not just successes
- Humanize business and brands
- Make viewers feel like insiders



### Trend Participation

**Sound Trends**: Videos using trending audio
**Format Trends**: Replicating viral video structures
**Challenge Trends**: Participating in viral challenges
**Hashtag Trends**: Creating content around trending topics
**Effect Trends**: Using trending filters and effects

**Trend Content Tips**:
- Speed matters—trends have short lifespans
- Put unique spin on trends; don't copy exactly
- Only participate in trends relevant to your niche
- Monitor trend emergence tools and accounts
- Some trends are better skipped



### Storytelling Content

**Personal Stories**: Share experiences and lessons
**Customer Stories**: Testimonials and transformations
**Brand Stories**: Origin stories, mission content
**News Commentary**: React to industry or cultural news
**Serial Content**: Ongoing narratives across multiple videos
