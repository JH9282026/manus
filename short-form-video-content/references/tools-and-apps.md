# Tools And Apps

Detailed reference content for the Short Form Video Content skill.

---

## Tools and Apps


### Creation Tools

**Filming**:
- Smartphone (modern phones sufficient for most content)
- Ring light for consistent lighting
- Mini tripod or phone holder
- External microphone (optional, but improves audio)

**Editing Apps**:
- CapCut (full-featured, free)
- InShot (versatile)
- VN Editor (advanced features)
- Adobe Premiere Rush (cross-platform)
- Platform native editors

**Design Tools**:
- Canva (graphics, thumbnails)
- Unfold (Instagram-focused templates)
- Mojo (animated templates)
- Photoshop/Illustrator (advanced design)


### Planning and Management

**Content Planning**:
- Notion (content calendars, idea storage)
- Trello (content workflow boards)
- Asana (team content management)
- Google Sheets (simple tracking)

**Scheduling**:
- Later (multi-platform)
- Buffer (multi-platform)
- Hootsuite (enterprise)
- Platform native scheduling (varies)


### Analytics and Research

**Analytics**:
- Platform native analytics
- Social Blade (growth tracking)
- Not Just Analytics (Instagram deep dive)
- Third-party dashboard tools

**Trend Monitoring**:
- TokBoard (TikTok trends)
- Google Trends (broader trends)
- Social listening tools
- Competitor monitoring


### Music and Sound

**Royalty-Free Music**:
- Epidemic Sound (subscription)
- Artlist (subscription)
- Platform music libraries (free with platform)
- Pixabay/Mixkit (free options)

**Sound Effects**:
- Epidemic Sound (includes SFX)
- FreeSound.org (free)
- Platform libraries
