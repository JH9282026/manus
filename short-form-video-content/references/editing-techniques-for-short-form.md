# Editing Techniques For Short Form

Detailed reference content for the Short Form Video Content skill.

---

## Editing Techniques for Short-Form


### Pacing and Cuts

**Cut on Action**: Transition during movement for seamless flow
**Match Cuts**: Connect visually similar elements
**Jump Cuts**: Remove pauses to maintain energy
**L-Cuts and J-Cuts**: Audio precedes or follows video for smooth transitions
**Rapid Cuts**: Multiple quick cuts for high energy
**Beat Cuts**: Time cuts to music beats


### Recommended Cut Frequency

- High energy content: Cut every 1-3 seconds
- Tutorial/educational: Cut every 3-5 seconds
- Storytelling: Cut based on narrative beats
- Talking head: Cut to remove pauses, vary with B-roll


### Essential Editing Software

**Mobile Apps**:
- CapCut (free, full-featured, TikTok integration)
- InShot (versatile, user-friendly)
- VN Video Editor (advanced features, free)
- Splice (intuitive interface)

**Desktop Software**:
- Adobe Premiere Pro (professional standard)
- Final Cut Pro (Mac, professional)
- DaVinci Resolve (free professional option)
- Adobe Premiere Rush (simplified cross-platform)


### Speed Manipulation

**Speed Ramping**: Varying speed within a clip for dynamic effect
**Slow Motion**: Emphasize important moments
**Time-Lapse**: Condense long processes
**Freeze Frames**: Pause for emphasis or comedy
