# Industry Benchmark Standards

Reference benchmarks by industry and function.

---

## SaaS Industry Benchmarks

### Rule of 40 Performance

| Percentile | Growth + Margin |
|------------|----------------|
| Top Quartile | >50% |
| Median | 25-40% |
| Bottom Quartile | <15% |

### Key Ratios

| Metric | Good | Great | Best-in-Class |
|--------|------|-------|---------------|
| LTV:CAC | 3:1 | 4:1 | 5:1+ |
| Net Revenue Retention | 100% | 110% | 120%+ |
| Gross Margin | 65% | 75% | 80%+ |
| CAC Payback (months) | 18 | 12 | <8 |
| Magic Number | 0.5 | 0.75 | 1.0+ |
