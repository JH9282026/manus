# Benchmarking Methodology

Detailed frameworks for conducting rigorous benchmarking studies.

---

## Data Collection Framework

### Source Hierarchy

1. **Primary Sources** (highest reliability)
   - Direct company disclosures
   - Commissioned surveys
   - Executive interviews
   - Site visits and audits

2. **Secondary Sources**
   - Industry analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester)
   - Financial databases (S&P Capital IQ, Bloomberg)
   - SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q)
   - Trade publications

3. **Tertiary Sources**
   - Estimates and projections
   - Proxy calculations
   - Expert interviews

### Normalization Techniques

| Method | Use Case | Formula |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Per Employee | Labor productivity | Metric / FTE count |
| Per Revenue | Efficiency ratios | Metric / Annual revenue |
| Per Unit | Manufacturing | Metric / Units produced |
| Per Customer | Service metrics | Metric / Customer count |

### Currency & Accounting Adjustments

- Apply consistent exchange rates (period average or spot)
- Adjust for accounting standard differences (GAAP vs IFRS)
- Normalize for fiscal year timing
- Account for geographic cost differences

---

## Gap Analysis Framework

### Performance Differential Calculation

```
Gap = (Subject Performance - Benchmark) / Benchmark x 100%
```

### Significance Testing

- Sample size considerations
- Confidence intervals (typically 95%)
- Materiality thresholds (industry-specific)

### Prioritization Matrix

| Gap Size | Implementation Ease | Priority |
|----------|--------------------|---------|
| Large | High | Quick Win |
| Large | Low | Strategic Initiative |
| Small | High | Fill-in |
| Small | Low | Deprioritize |

## Best Practice Documentation

### Practice Profile Template

1. **Practice Name**: Clear, descriptive title
2. **Source Organization**: Who does this well
3. **Practice Description**: What they do differently
4. **Results Achieved**: Quantified outcomes
5. **Implementation Requirements**: Resources, timeline
6. **Transferability Assessment**: Applicability to your context
7. **Adaptation Considerations**: Required modifications
