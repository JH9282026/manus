# Benchmarking Research

Systematic competitive and performance benchmarking for identifying best practices and establishing industry standards.

## Overview

This skill transforms comparative data into strategic insights by systematically comparing organizational performance, processes, and outcomes against competitors, industry leaders, cross-industry exemplars, and historical baselines. It answers: "How do we compare?" and "What does good look like?"

## Benchmarking Types

| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Competitive | Direct competitor comparison | Market positioning, differentiation |
| Functional | Same function across industries | Best practice adoption |
| Internal | Across business units or time | Performance standardization |
| Generic | World-class processes any industry | Excellence inspiration |

## Core Methodology

### 1. Scope Definition
- Define benchmarking subject (unit, process, metric)
- Select benchmarking type and rationale
- Identify performance dimensions and KPIs

### 2. Comparison Set Selection
- Define peer group criteria (industry, revenue, geography)
- Name 5-15 specific comparators with rationale
- Identify best-in-class performers
- Document exclusion criteria

### 3. Data Collection
- Primary sources: direct company data, surveys
- Secondary sources: industry reports, filings
- Tertiary sources: estimates, proxies
- Apply normalization (per employee, per revenue)

### 4. Gap Analysis
- Calculate performance differentials
- Assess statistical significance
- Prioritize by impact vs achievability

## Common Benchmark Metrics

### SaaS Operating Metrics
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV) and LTV:CAC ratio
- Net Revenue Retention
- Gross Margin
- Rule of 40 (growth + profitability)

### Manufacturing Metrics
- Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
- Defects per Million (PPM)
- Inventory turns
- Changeover time
- Energy consumption per unit

### Service Metrics
- First Contact Resolution (FCR)
- Average Handle Time (AHT)
- Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Cost per contact

## Quality Standards

- Minimum 70% metrics from verifiable sources
- All normalizations documented with rationale
- Statistical significance indicated for gaps
- Top 10 insights summarized for executives
