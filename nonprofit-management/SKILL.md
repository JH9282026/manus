# Nonprofit Management

Lead mission-driven organizations through effective governance, fundraising, program delivery, and operational excellence.

## Overview

Provide comprehensive frameworks for nonprofit leadership covering board governance, strategic planning, fundraising, volunteer management, program evaluation, and regulatory compliance. Balance mission impact with organizational sustainability.

## Nonprofit Revenue Mix

| Source | Typical Share | Effort Level |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Individual donations | 30-50% | High (cultivation) |
| Grants (foundation) | 15-30% | High (applications) |
| Government contracts | 10-25% | Medium (compliance) |
| Events | 5-15% | High (logistics) |
| Corporate sponsorships | 5-15% | Medium (relationships) |
| Earned revenue | 5-20% | Medium (operations) |

## Strategic Planning Framework

1. **Mission review** — Reaffirm or refine mission, vision, values
2. **Environmental scan** — SWOT, stakeholder input, community needs
3. **Goal setting** — 3-5 strategic priorities for 3-year horizon
4. **Action planning** — Initiatives, owners, timelines, resources
5. **Resource allocation** — Budget alignment with strategic priorities
6. **Measurement** — KPIs, dashboards, reporting cadence
7. **Review cycle** — Quarterly progress review, annual refresh

## Key Performance Indicators

| Category | Metric |
|----------|--------|
| Mission impact | People served, outcomes achieved |
| Financial health | Operating reserve ratio, revenue diversity |
| Fundraising | Donor retention rate, cost per dollar raised |
| Programs | Participant satisfaction, completion rate |
| Operations | Overhead ratio, staff retention |
