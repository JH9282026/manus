# IT Operations Excellence

Optimize IT service delivery, infrastructure management, and operational efficiency.

---

## IT Operating Model

### Operating Model Design

| Model | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Centralized | Single IT organization serves all business units | Efficiency, standardization, cost control |
| Federated | Central IT + embedded business IT teams | Alignment, responsiveness, with some coordination |
| Decentralized | Each business unit has autonomous IT | Speed, customization, business ownership |
| Hybrid | Central platform + business-specific capabilities | Balance of efficiency and agility |

### Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) Principles

Apply SRE practices for modern operations:
- Define service level objectives (SLOs) for every critical service
- Error budgets — allow controlled risk for innovation
- Automate toil — eliminate manual, repetitive operational tasks
- Blameless post-mortems — learn from failures without blame
- Capacity planning — data-driven infrastructure sizing

## IT Vendor Management

### Vendor Evaluation Framework

| Criterion | Weight | Assessment Method |
|---|---|---|
| Capability | 25% | Product demo, proof of concept |
| Financial stability | 15% | Financial statements, analyst reports |
| References | 15% | Customer interviews, case studies |
| Total cost | 20% | TCO analysis over contract period |
| Support and SLAs | 15% | SLA commitments, support model |
| Strategic fit | 10% | Roadmap alignment, partnership potential |

### Contract Negotiation Best Practices

- Negotiate multi-year discounts with annual opt-out clauses
- Include SLA penalties (service credits) for underperformance
- Require data portability and exit provisions
- Cap annual price increases (typically 3-5%)
- Include rights to audit vendor compliance
- Negotiate favorable terms for scaling up AND down

## IT Organizational Design

### Key IT Roles

| Role | Focus | Reports To |
|---|---|---|
| CIO | IT strategy, business alignment | CEO |
| CTO | Technology vision, engineering | CIO or CEO |
| CISO | Cybersecurity strategy, risk | CIO or CEO |
| VP Engineering | Software development, delivery | CTO |
| VP Infrastructure | Operations, cloud, networking | CIO |
| Enterprise Architect | Architecture standards, governance | CIO |
| VP Data/Analytics | Data platform, BI, AI/ML | CIO or CDO |
| IT PMO Lead | Project governance, resource management | CIO |

### IT Talent Development

**Critical skill areas for modern IT:**
- Cloud architecture and DevOps
- AI/ML and data engineering
- Cybersecurity
- Product management (for IT-as-a-product)
- Business analysis and relationship management
- Agile and lean practices
