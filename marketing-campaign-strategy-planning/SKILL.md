# Marketing Campaign Strategy & Planning

Develop end-to-end marketing campaign strategies with audience targeting, channel mix, and measurement frameworks.

## Overview

Design data-driven marketing campaigns from objective setting through execution and optimization. Cover audience definition, channel selection, messaging, budget allocation, timeline planning, and KPI frameworks.

## Campaign Planning Framework

1. **Set objectives** — SMART goals tied to business outcomes
2. **Define audience** — Segments, personas, targeting criteria
3. **Craft messaging** — Value proposition, key messages, creative brief
4. **Select channels** — Channel mix based on audience and budget
5. **Allocate budget** — Channel allocation with testing reserves
6. **Build timeline** — Phases, milestones, dependencies
7. **Execute** — Launch, monitor, optimize in real-time
8. **Measure** — Report results against KPIs

## Channel Selection Matrix

| Channel | Best For | Cost Model | Typical CAC |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Search (Google Ads) | Intent-driven demand | CPC | $30-150 B2B |
| Social (Meta, LinkedIn) | Awareness, targeting | CPM/CPC | $20-200 |
| Email | Nurture, retention | Per send | $5-15 |
| Content/SEO | Long-term organic | Time investment | $15-50 |
| Events | Relationship building | Fixed + variable | $100-500 |
| PR | Credibility, reach | Retainer/project | Variable |
| Direct mail | High-value targets | Per piece | $50-200 |

## Budget Allocation Guide

| Campaign Phase | Allocation |
|---------------|-----------|
| Strategy and creative | 10-15% |
| Media spend | 50-65% |
| Technology/tools | 10-15% |
| Testing reserve | 10-15% |
| Measurement/analytics | 5-10% |
