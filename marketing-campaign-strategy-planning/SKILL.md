# Marketing Campaign Strategy & Planning

Plan, execute, and measure integrated marketing campaigns that drive measurable business results through strategic audience targeting, channel selection, and creative execution.

## Overview

A marketing campaign is a coordinated set of activities designed to achieve a specific business objective within a defined timeframe and budget. Effective campaigns aren't about being on every channel — they're about reaching the right audience with the right message at the right time. This skill covers end-to-end campaign planning: from defining objectives and audience to selecting channels, creating briefs, managing execution, and measuring results.

## Objective Setting with SMART Goals

| Objective Type | SMART Example | Primary KPI | Secondary KPIs |
|---------------|--------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Brand awareness | Increase aided brand recall from 23% to 35% in Q3 | Aided recall (survey) | Impressions, reach, social mentions |
| Lead generation | Generate 500 MQLs at <$75 CPL in 60 days | MQL count | CPL, landing page conversion rate |
| Product launch | Achieve 10,000 trial signups in first 30 days | Trial signups | CAC, activation rate |
| Revenue/sales | Drive $500K in attributed pipeline from Q2 campaign | Pipeline generated | ROAS, conversion rate, AOV |
| Engagement | Increase email list by 2,000 subscribers in 45 days | Net new subscribers | Email signup rate, content downloads |
| Retention | Reduce churn by 15% among at-risk accounts in Q4 | Churn rate reduction | NPS improvement, feature adoption |

## Channel Mix Strategy

| Channel | Funnel Stage | Budget Allocation (Typical) | Strength |
|---------|-------------|---------------------------|----------|
| Paid search (Google/Bing) | Mid–Bottom | 25–35% | Captures active intent |
| Paid social (Meta, LinkedIn) | Top–Mid | 20–30% | Targeting precision, creative formats |
| Content marketing/SEO | Top–Mid | 10–20% | Long-term organic traffic |
| Email marketing | Mid–Bottom | 5–10% | Highest ROI channel (avg $36:$1) |
| Display/programmatic | Top | 10–15% | Reach and retargeting |
| Events/webinars | Mid–Bottom | 5–15% | Relationship building, pipeline |
| Influencer/partnerships | Top–Mid | 5–10% | Trust transfer, new audiences |

### Channel Selection Decision Matrix

| Factor | Paid Search | Paid Social | SEO | Email | Display | Events |
|--------|------------|------------|-----|-------|---------|--------|
| Speed to results | Fast | Fast | Slow (months) | Medium | Fast | Slow |
| Cost to start | Medium | Low–Medium | Low (time-intensive) | Low | Medium | High |
| Targeting precision | High (intent) | High (demo/behavioral) | Low (topic) | High (list) | Medium | Medium |
| Scalability | High | High | Medium | Limited by list | High | Low |
| Attribution clarity | High | Medium | Low | High | Low | Low |

## Creative Brief Template

| Section | Content | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Campaign name | Internal reference name | "Q3 2026 Enterprise Pipeline Campaign" |
| Objective | What this campaign should achieve | Generate 200 MQLs at <$80 CPL |
| Target audience | Who we're reaching | VP+ at companies with 500+ employees in tech/finance |
| Key message | One thing the audience should remember | "Cut cloud costs 40% without sacrificing performance" |
| Supporting proof | Evidence for the claim | Customer case study, analyst report |
| Tone/personality | How the brand sounds | Confident, knowledgeable, not salesy |
| CTA | What we want them to do | Request a personalized cost audit |
| Deliverables | What creative team needs to produce | 3 ad variations, landing page, email sequence, white paper |
| Budget | Production + media | $15K production, $50K media |
| Timeline | Key dates | Brief due: April 7, Launch: April 28 |

## Budget Allocation Guide

| Budget Range | Channel Strategy | Team Size |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------|
| <$10K | 1–2 channels maximum, test-and-learn | 1–2 people |
| $10K–$50K | 2–3 channels, primary + retargeting | 2–4 people |
| $50K–$200K | 3–5 channels, integrated campaign | 4–6 people |
| $200K+ | Full funnel, multi-channel, ABM + broad | 6+ people + agency |

## Post-Campaign Analysis Framework

| Analysis Area | Questions | Metrics |
|--------------|-----------|---------|
| Goal achievement | Did we hit the SMART objective? | Actual vs. target KPIs |
| Channel performance | Which channels drove the most efficient results? | CPL/CPA by channel |
| Creative performance | Which messages and formats resonated? | CTR, conversion rate by creative |
| Audience insights | Which segments performed best? | Conversion rate by segment |
| Budget efficiency | Did we spend optimally? | ROAS, actual vs. planned spend |
| Lessons learned | What would we do differently? | Qualitative retrospective |

## Best Practices

- **Start with the objective, not the channel** — "We need a TikTok campaign" is not a strategy
- **Define success before launch** — agree on KPIs and targets with stakeholders upfront
- **Allocate 70% to proven, 20% to emerging, 10% to experimental** — don't bet everything on new tactics
- **Build a measurement plan before spending** — tracking pixels, UTM parameters, and attribution must be ready at launch
- **Test one variable at a time** — changing audience + creative + channel simultaneously tells you nothing
- **Run a retrospective after every campaign** — document what worked and what didn't for future campaigns
