# Campaign Strategy Frameworks

## Objective Setting (SMART Goals)

| Component | Definition | Example |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Specific | Clear, well-defined | Generate qualified leads |
| Measurable | Quantifiable metric | 500 MQLs |
| Achievable | Realistic given resources | Based on historical conversion |
| Relevant | Aligned to business goals | Supports Q3 pipeline target |
| Time-bound | Clear deadline | By end of Q2 |

### Objective Hierarchy
1. **Business objective**: Revenue, market share, customer growth
2. **Marketing objective**: Leads, awareness, engagement
3. **Campaign objective**: Specific metric for this campaign
4. **Channel objectives**: Per-channel contribution targets

---

## Audience Definition

### Targeting Layers
1. **Firmographic** (B2B): Industry, company size, revenue, location
2. **Demographic** (B2C): Age, gender, income, education
3. **Behavioral**: Purchase history, engagement, website activity
4. **Psychographic**: Values, interests, lifestyle, motivations
5. **Intent**: Search behavior, content consumption, buying signals

### Persona Development
- Name and role/description
- Demographics and firmographics
- Goals and challenges
- Information sources
- Decision-making process
- Messaging preferences
- Objections and concerns

---

## Messaging Framework

### Message Hierarchy
1. **Brand promise** — Overarching value proposition
2. **Campaign theme** — Specific campaign message
3. **Channel messages** — Adapted for each channel
4. **Segment messages** — Tailored to each audience
5. **CTAs** — Specific action per stage

### Creative Brief Template
- **Objective**: What must this creative accomplish?
- **Audience**: Who are we talking to?
- **Insight**: What do we know about them?
- **Message**: Single most important thing to communicate
- **Support**: Proof points and evidence
- **Tone**: How should this feel?
- **CTA**: What do we want them to do?
- **Mandatories**: Brand guidelines, legal requirements

## Multi-Channel Campaign Architecture

### Awareness Phase
- Social media ads (reach optimization)
- Display/programmatic advertising
- PR and media outreach
- Influencer partnerships
- Content marketing (blog, video)

### Consideration Phase
- Search ads (intent keywords)
- Retargeting campaigns
- Email nurture sequences
- Webinars and events
- Case studies and whitepapers

### Decision Phase
- Product demos and trials
- Sales enablement content
- Comparison guides
- Customer testimonials
- Limited-time offers

### Retention Phase
- Onboarding email sequences
- Customer success content
- Loyalty programs
- Upsell/cross-sell campaigns
- Referral programs
