# Healthcare Administration & Management

Analyze, plan, and optimize healthcare organization operations across clinical, financial, and regulatory domains.

## Overview

This skill integrates clinical operations, regulatory compliance, financial management, and analytics to improve patient outcomes, efficiency, and financial performance while maintaining strict regulatory adherence.

## Analysis Type Selection

| Challenge | Approach | Key Metric | Reference |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| High denial rates / AR days | Revenue Cycle Optimization | Days in AR, clean claim rate | `/references/revenue-cycle-quality.md` |
| Upcoming accreditation survey | Survey Preparation | Compliance gap count | `/references/compliance-accreditation.md` |
| Low patient satisfaction | HCAHPS Improvement | Domain percentile rankings | `/references/revenue-cycle-quality.md` |

## Core Operational Metrics

| Metric | Description | Benchmark |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Average Length of Stay | Days per discharge | Peer comparison |
| Bed Utilization | % beds occupied | 75-85% optimal |
| ED Throughput | Door-to-disposition | Under 4 hours |
| Days in AR | Average collection days | Under 45 |
| Denial Rate | % claims denied | Under 5% |
| HCAHPS Overall | Top-box percentile | 50th+ percentile |

## HIPAA Essentials
- De-identify or authorize all patient data access
- Apply minimum necessary standard
- Execute BAAs for data sharing
- Log all access for audit
