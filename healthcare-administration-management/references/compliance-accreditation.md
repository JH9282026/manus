# Compliance & Accreditation

## Joint Commission Preparation
- Individual patient tracers across 10+ clinical areas
- System tracers: medication, infection control, environment of care
- 8-month timeline: gap ID -> corrective action -> education -> mock survey -> remediation
- Focus: life safety, medication reconciliation, restraints, infection prevention

## HIPAA Compliance
- Privacy: designate officer, NPP, patient access, minimum necessary, workforce training
- Security: annual risk analysis, administrative/physical/technical safeguards, contingency plans
- Breach: notify individuals (60 days), HHS, media (500+)

## Staffing Models
- Calculate ratios by unit type and acuity
- Workload-based: hours per patient day
- Account for productive vs. non-productive time
- Flex pools for census variability

## Compliance Program
- Written policies, designated officer, committee
- Regular training, internal audits
- Corrective action protocols
- Reporting hotline with non-retaliation policy
