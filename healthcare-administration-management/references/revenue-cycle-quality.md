# Revenue Cycle & Quality

## Revenue Cycle Optimization
1. Map full cycle: registration -> charge capture -> coding -> billing -> collection
2. Measure: pre-registration completion, clean claim rate, denial rate by category
3. Root causes: authorization denials, coding errors, timely filing, eligibility
4. Strategies: automate eligibility (target 85%+), CDI program, scrub rules (95%+ clean), denial prevention protocols, POS collection

## HCAHPS Improvement
1. Analyze scores by unit, shift, demographic
2. Correlate with staffing, acuity, discharge timing
3. Benchmark against high performers
4. Interventions: hourly rounding, bedside shift report, nurse leader rounding, real-time feedback, communication training

## Population Health
1. Risk-stratify by HCC score
2. Identify high-cost patients and care gaps
3. Design programs: complex care, chronic disease, transitional care
4. Provider scorecards and referral management
5. Monitor total cost, utilization, quality metrics
