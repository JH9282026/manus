## Best Practices

1. **Use design systems as references**, not templates — Adapt patterns to your brand
2. **Check licenses** — Verify icon and illustration licenses for commercial use
3. **Optimize assets** — Compress images with TinyPNG or Squoosh before shipping
4. **Limit font families** — 2–3 maximum for performance and consistency
5. **Test accessibility tools early** — Don't wait until the end to check contrast and focus states
