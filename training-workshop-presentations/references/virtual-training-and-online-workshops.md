## Virtual Training and Online Workshops


### Virtual Training Challenges

- Limited non-verbal feedback
- Technology distractions
- Attention maintenance difficulty
- Reduced social connection
- Practice limitations


### Virtual Training Solutions

**Engagement Strategies:**
- Use polls and chat frequently
- Require cameras when possible
- Call on participants by name
- Break content into shorter segments
- Include frequent interaction points

**Technology Best Practices:**
- Test all technology beforehand
- Have backup plans for technical issues
- Use platform features effectively
- Provide technical support resources
- Keep slides and materials simple

**Virtual Practice Activities:**
- Breakout room exercises
- Shared document collaboration
- Virtual whiteboards
- Screen-sharing demonstrations
- Role-plays via video


### Hybrid Training Considerations

- Ensure equal participation for in-person and remote learners
- Use technology that bridges both groups
- Design activities that work for both formats
- Consider separate facilitators for each group
- Test all equipment thoroughly
