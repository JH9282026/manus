## Measuring Training Effectiveness


### Quantitative Metrics

**Learning Metrics:**
- Pre/post test score improvement
- Skill assessment scores
- Certification pass rates
- Knowledge retention rates

**Performance Metrics:**
- Productivity improvements
- Error rate reductions
- Quality measure improvements
- Time-to-competency reduction

**Business Metrics:**
- Revenue impact
- Cost savings
- Customer satisfaction changes
- Employee retention rates


### Qualitative Metrics

- Participant feedback themes
- Manager observations
- Peer feedback
- Self-reported confidence levels
- Application success stories


### Calculating Training ROI

**Formula:** ROI = ((Benefits - Costs) / Costs) × 100

**Benefits to Consider:**
- Productivity gains
- Error reduction savings
- Reduced turnover costs
- Quality improvements
- Compliance cost avoidance

**Costs to Consider:**
- Trainer time
- Participant time
- Materials and technology
- Facility costs
- Development time


### Continuous Improvement

- Regularly review evaluation data
- Identify patterns in feedback
- Update content based on results
- Test new approaches
- Share best practices with other trainers
