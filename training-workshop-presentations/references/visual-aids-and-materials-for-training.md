## Visual Aids and Materials for Training



### Slide Design for Training

**Training Slides vs. Presentation Slides:**
- Training slides support learning, not impress
- Include practice instructions
- Provide visual references for activities
- Use as discussion prompts
- Leave room for annotation

**Training Slide Best Practices:**
- One concept per slide
- More visuals, fewer words
- Include activity instructions clearly
- Use consistent navigation cues
- Provide printable versions when helpful
