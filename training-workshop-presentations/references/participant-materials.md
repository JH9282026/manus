# Participant Materials

Detailed reference content for the Training Workshop Presentations skill.

---

## Participant Materials


### Workbook Design

**Essential Components:**
- Session objectives and agenda
- Key content summaries
- Activity worksheets
- Note-taking space
- Reflection prompts
- Action planning templates
- Resource lists and references

**Design Principles:**
- Clean, professional appearance
- Adequate white space
- Clear organization and navigation
- Durable construction for reference use
- Consistent with organizational branding


### Job Aids

**Effective Job Aid Characteristics:**
- Task-specific and focused
- Easy to access when needed
- Clear and uncluttered
- Accurate and up-to-date
- Durable for workplace use

**Job Aid Types:**
- Step-by-step procedures
- Decision trees and flowcharts
- Checklists
- Quick reference cards
- Troubleshooting guides


### Handouts

**When to Use Handouts:**
- Complex information for reference
- Data or statistics for analysis
- Supplementary reading material
- Post-training resources
- Materials not included in workbooks

**Handout Best Practices:**
- Distribute at the right time (not too early)
- Indicate how they'll be used
- Keep organized and numbered
- Make available digitally as well
