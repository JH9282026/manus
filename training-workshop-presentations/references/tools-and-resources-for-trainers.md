# Tools And Resources For Trainers

Detailed reference content for the Training Workshop Presentations skill.

---

## Tools and Resources for Trainers


### Presentation and Content Tools

- **PowerPoint/Keynote/Google Slides:** Presentation creation
- **Canva:** Visual design for training materials
- **Articulate/Captivate:** E-learning development
- **Prezi:** Dynamic, non-linear presentations
- **Miro/Mural:** Virtual whiteboarding and collaboration


### Engagement and Interaction Tools

- **Mentimeter:** Live polls and word clouds
- **Kahoot!/Quizizz:** Gamified quizzes
- **Poll Everywhere:** Audience response system
- **Slido:** Q&A and polling
- **Padlet:** Collaborative boards


### Virtual Training Platforms

- **Zoom:** Video conferencing with breakout rooms
- **Microsoft Teams:** Integrated collaboration
- **Webex:** Enterprise virtual training
- **Google Meet:** Simple video meetings
- **Butter/Whereby:** Purpose-built for workshops


### Learning Management Systems

- **Moodle:** Open-source LMS
- **Canvas:** Educational LMS
- **Docebo:** Corporate learning platform
- **TalentLMS:** Small business LMS
- **Cornerstone:** Enterprise learning


### Trainer Resources

- **ATD (Association for Talent Development):** Professional development
- **Training Magazine:** Industry news and best practices
- **LinkedIn Learning:** Trainer skill development
- **Books:** "Design for How People Learn" by Julie Dirksen, "Tell Me So I Can Hear You" by Cynthia Ulrich Tobias
