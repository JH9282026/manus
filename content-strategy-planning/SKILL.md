# Content Strategy & Planning

Define, organize, and execute cohesive content programs that achieve specific business objectives.

## Overview

Transform scattered content efforts into coordinated campaigns that build brand authority, attract qualified audiences, and support revenue goals across multiple channels and formats. Create content ecosystems where pieces reinforce and amplify each other.

## Strategy Components

| Component | Purpose | Deliverable |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Content Pillars | Establish authority themes | 3-5 core topics |
| Topic Clusters | Support SEO and navigation | Pillar + cluster structure |
| Editorial Calendar | Plan and schedule | 30/60/90 day calendar |
| Distribution Plan | Maximize reach | Channel-content matrix |
| Measurement Framework | Track success | KPIs and dashboards |

## Content Pillar Development

### Selection Criteria
1. Aligns with business expertise
2. Addresses audience pain points
3. Has sufficient search volume
4. Differentiates from competitors
5. Supports content variety

### Topic Cluster Model
- **Pillar Page**: Comprehensive resource (3000+ words)
- **Cluster Content**: Supporting articles linked to pillar
- **Internal Links**: Hub-and-spoke structure

## Content Mapping

| Journey Stage | Content Goal | Content Types |
|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Awareness | Attract, educate | Blog posts, videos, social |
| Consideration | Engage, nurture | Guides, webinars, case studies |
| Decision | Convert | Demos, comparisons, testimonials |
| Retention | Delight, expand | Tutorials, exclusive content |

## Editorial Calendar Elements

- Topic and title
- Content pillar alignment
- Target keyword(s)
- Format and length
- Author and deadline
- Distribution channels
- Promotion plan
