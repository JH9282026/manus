# Distribution & Amplification

Channel strategy and promotion tactics for content reach.

---

## Distribution Channels

### Owned Media
- Company blog/website
- Email newsletters
- Social media accounts
- Mobile app
- Podcast/YouTube

### Earned Media
- Guest posting
- PR/media coverage
- Influencer shares
- User-generated content
- Reviews and mentions

### Paid Media
- Social advertising
- Content promotion
- Sponsored content
- Retargeting
- Influencer partnerships

---

## Content Repurposing

### One-to-Many Strategy
From one blog post:
- Social media thread
- LinkedIn carousel
- Short-form video
- Email newsletter
- Podcast episode
- Infographic
- Quote graphics

### Platform Adaptation
- LinkedIn: Professional, longer
- Twitter: Concise, conversational
- Instagram: Visual, lifestyle
- TikTok: Entertaining, authentic

---

## Promotion Timeline

### Launch Day
- Social posts (all platforms)
- Email to subscribers
- Internal sharing
- Community posting

### Week 1
- Repurposed formats
- Paid promotion (if budget)
- Influencer outreach
- Forum sharing

### Ongoing
- Evergreen social sharing
- Email sequence inclusion
- Internal linking
- Update and republish

---

## Amplification Tactics

### Employee Advocacy
- Easy sharing tools
- Pre-written copy
- Incentives/recognition

### Community Building
- Engage commenters
- Share user content
- Build relationships
- Create exclusivity
