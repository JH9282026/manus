# Content Audit Methodology

Systematic approach to evaluating and optimizing existing content.

---

## Audit Process

### 1. Inventory Creation
Compile all content:
- URLs and titles
- Publish dates
- Authors
- Content type/format
- Word count
- Target keywords

### 2. Performance Data
Collect metrics:
- Page views
- Organic traffic
- Bounce rate
- Time on page
- Conversions
- Backlinks
- Social shares

### 3. Quality Assessment
Evaluate each piece:
- Accuracy and currency
- Alignment with strategy
- SEO optimization
- User experience
- Brand voice consistency

---

## Action Categories

### Keep
- High-performing content
- Evergreen value
- Strong backlinks

### Update
- Outdated information
- Thin content to expand
- SEO improvement opportunities

### Consolidate
- Similar topics to merge
- Competing pages to combine
- Redirect old to new

### Remove
- Outdated with no traffic
- Off-brand content
- Duplicate content

---

## Gap Analysis

### Coverage Gaps
- Missing pillar topics
- Competitor content you lack
- Customer questions unanswered
- Journey stage gaps

### Keyword Gaps
- High-volume, low-coverage
- Competitor rankings you miss
- Long-tail opportunities

---

## Audit Frequency

- **Comprehensive**: Annually
- **Rolling**: Quarterly spot-checks
- **Trigger-based**: After major changes
