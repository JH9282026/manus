# Risk Assessment Methodology

Framework for evaluating and prioritizing contract risks.

---

## Risk Categories

### Financial Exposure
- Unlimited liability clauses
- Broad indemnification
- Penalty provisions
- Hidden cost escalators

### Operational Risk
- Unrealistic SLAs
- Inadequate termination rights
- Single-source dependencies
- Insufficient flexibility

### Legal/Regulatory
- Compliance gaps
- Jurisdiction issues
- Regulatory changes
- Data privacy concerns

### Intellectual Property
- Ownership ambiguity
- License limitations
- Third-party IP exposure
- Competitor restrictions

---

## Risk Scoring Matrix

### Impact Assessment
| Level | Financial Impact | Description |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| Critical | >$1M or material | Business-threatening |
| High | $100K-$1M | Significant loss |
| Medium | $10K-$100K | Manageable |
| Low | <$10K | Minimal |

### Probability Assessment
- **High**: Likely to occur
- **Medium**: Possible
- **Low**: Unlikely

### Priority = Impact × Probability

---

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Negotiate
- Request modified language
- Add carve-outs or caps
- Clarify ambiguity
- Add protections

### Accept
- Low impact, low probability
- Standard market terms
- No leverage to change

### Transfer
- Insurance coverage
- Subcontractor flow-down
- Indemnification rights

### Walk Away
- Unacceptable risk level
- No negotiation progress
- Better alternatives exist
