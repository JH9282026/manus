# Commercial Contracts

Key provisions for master service agreements and commercial relationships.

---

## Master Service Agreement (MSA)

### Structure
- MSA: General terms governing relationship
- SOW: Specific project terms and pricing
- Exhibits: Technical specifications, SLAs

### Critical Provisions

**Scope Definition**
- Clear deliverable descriptions
- Acceptance criteria
- Change order process
- Out-of-scope handling

**Payment Terms**
- Payment timing and milestones
- Expense reimbursement
- Late payment penalties
- Disputed amount process

**Intellectual Property**
- Pre-existing IP treatment
- Work product ownership
- License grants
- Third-party IP handling

---

## Liability Provisions

### Limitation of Liability
- Cap amount (typically contract value)
- Carve-outs (IP, confidentiality, willful misconduct)
- Consequential damage waivers
- Super-cap for certain claims

### Indemnification
- Scope of indemnity
- Defense obligations
- Third-party claim handling
- Insurance requirements

---

## Termination Provisions

### Termination Types
- For cause (material breach)
- For convenience (with notice)
- For insolvency
- Automatic expiration

### Wind-Down
- Transition assistance
- Return of materials
- Payment reconciliation
- Survival provisions

---

## Market Standards

| Provision | Buyer-Favorable | Balanced | Seller-Favorable |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|------------------|
| Payment Terms | Net 60-90 | Net 30-45 | Due on receipt |
| Liability Cap | 3x+ contract value | 1-2x contract value | Direct damages only |
| Termination | For convenience, 30 days | 60-90 day notice | For cause only |
