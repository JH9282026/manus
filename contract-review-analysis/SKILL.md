# Contract Review & Analysis

Examine contracts to extract key terms, identify potential risks, flag non-standard provisions, and highlight areas requiring negotiation attention.

## Overview

Transform complex legal documents into structured, actionable intelligence. This skill serves as a first-pass analysis tool that identifies issues requiring attorney attention, extracts commercial terms, and provides comparative analysis against standard market practices.

## Review Process

### 1. Initial Assessment
- Identify contract type and parties
- Determine review perspective (buyer/seller)
- Note term and value

### 2. Term Extraction
- Commercial terms (pricing, payment, scope)
- Legal provisions (liability, indemnification)
- Operational requirements (SLAs, deliverables)
- Compliance elements (data privacy, audit rights)

### 3. Risk Analysis
- Identify risk categories and severity
- Compare against market standard
- Assess probability and impact
- Prioritize by materiality

### 4. Deliverable Creation
- Executive summary with key findings
- Clause-by-clause analysis
- Negotiation playbook
- Risk matrix

## Risk Rating Framework

- **Critical**: Immediate legal attention required
- **High**: Significant business/legal exposure
- **Medium**: Deviation from standard, manageable
- **Low**: Minor concern, acceptable

## Important Disclaimer

This skill provides analytical support but does not constitute legal advice. All contract decisions should involve qualified legal professionals.
