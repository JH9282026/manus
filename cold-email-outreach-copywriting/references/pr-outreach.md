# PR and Media Outreach

Write effective media pitches and link building outreach.

---

## Media Pitch Templates

### Template 1: Story Pitch

```
Subject: Story idea: [compelling angle]

Hi [Journalist name],

I read your recent piece on [article topic] and thought you might be interested in a related angle.

[One paragraph pitch: What's the story? Why now? Why does it matter to their readers?]

Sources I can provide:
- [Your spokesperson] — [credibility, title]
- [Data point or research finding]
- [Customer/user story]

Want me to send more details?

[Your name]
[Your title, company]
[Phone number]
```

### Template 2: Expert Commentary

```
Subject: Expert source for [topic] coverage

Hi [Name],

Following your coverage of [topic], I wanted to offer [expert name] as a source for future stories in this space.

[Expert name] is [credentials] with [relevant experience].

Recent commentary topics:
- [Topic 1 — one sentence insight]
- [Topic 2 — one sentence insight]

Available for interviews, background briefings, or quick quotes on deadline. Just reply or call [number].

[Your name]
```

## Link Building Outreach

### Template: Resource Link Building

```
Subject: Resource for your [page topic] article

Hi [Name],

I came across your article on [topic] — great resource, especially the section on [specific point].

We just published [content piece] that covers [related angle] with [unique element — original data, expert interviews, step-by-step guide].

It might be a useful addition to your [specific section] — here's the link: [URL]

Either way, keep up the great work on [site name].

[Your name]
```

### Pitch Best Practices

- Research the journalist/editor's beat and recent articles
- Explain why the story matters to their specific audience
- Lead with the story angle, not your company
- Provide ready-to-use assets (data, quotes, visuals)
- Keep pitches under 150 words
- Follow up once, maximum twice
- Respect editorial calendars and deadlines
- Never ask "Did you get my email?" — add new value instead
