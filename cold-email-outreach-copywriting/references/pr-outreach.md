# PR and Media Outreach

## PR Pitch Framework

### Key Principles
- Lead with the news hook
- Be relevant to their beat
- Offer exclusivity when appropriate
- Make it easy to say yes
- Respect their time

---

## News Hook Types

| Hook Type | Example | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Data/research | Original survey findings | Trade, business press |
| Trend commentary | Expert perspective on news | News, industry |
| Company milestone | Funding, launch, growth | Business, tech |
| Human interest | Founder story, customer impact | General, lifestyle |
| Contrarian take | Against-the-grain perspective | Opinion, thought pieces |

---

## Subject Line Examples

```
"Data: [Surprising finding from your research]"
"Expert source for your [topic] coverage"
"Story idea: [Compelling angle]"
"Exclusive: [Newsworthy announcement]"
"Quick pitch: [One-line summary]"
```

---

## Pitch Structure

### Opening (Why Them)
```
"Your recent piece on [topic] raised important questions 
about [specific point]. I have a source who can add 
a unique perspective."
```

### The Hook (What's Newsworthy)
```
"Key talking points:
• [Unique insight 1]
• [Unique insight 2]
• [Data point or surprising finding]"
```

### The Source (Who/Why Credible)
```
"About [Expert Name]:
[Brief credentials—1-2 sentences]"
```

### The Ask (Clear CTA)
```
"Would this be valuable for an upcoming piece?
Happy to arrange an interview or provide written commentary."
```

## PR Best Practices

### Do
- Research the journalist's recent work
- Personalize every pitch
- Offer exclusives to top-tier targets
- Provide assets (images, data, quotes)
- Be responsive and helpful

### Don't
- Mass-pitch generic stories
- Follow up more than 2-3 times
- Send attachments in first email
- Pitch irrelevant journalists
- Be pushy or demanding

---

## Building Media Relationships

### Long-Term Approach
1. Follow and engage on social media
2. Share their work (genuinely)
3. Offer value before asking
4. Be a reliable, responsive source
5. Respect their time and deadlines
