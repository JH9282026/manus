### Toasts and Roasts
**Purpose**: Celebrate an individual or occasion with warmth and often humor.

**Characteristics**:
- Personal anecdotes and stories
- Appropriate humor (roasts push boundaries; toasts stay warmer)
- Emotional connection
- Brief (toasts: 2-3 minutes; roasts: 5-10 minutes)
- Ends with a tribute or raising glasses

**Example contexts**: Weddings, retirement parties, milestone celebrations, farewell events
