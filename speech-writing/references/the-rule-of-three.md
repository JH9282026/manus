### The Rule of Three

Humans naturally process information in threes:

**Why It Works**:
- Memorable and complete
- Creates rhythm and pattern
- Feels satisfying and balanced
- Easy to follow and recall

**Applications**:
- Three main points
- Three examples per point
- Three-part phrases
- Three-act structure

**Famous Examples**:
- "Life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness"
- "Government of the people, by the people, for the people"
- "Tell them what you're going to tell them, tell them, tell them what you told them"
