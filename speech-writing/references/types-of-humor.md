### Types of Humor

**Self-Deprecating**: Safest form; shows humility
> "When I was asked to speak about success, I called my mom. She said, 'Finally, something you know about.'"

**Observational**: Shared experiences the audience recognizes
> "We've all sat through that meeting that could have been an email..."

**Anecdotal**: Funny stories from personal experience
> Real, specific moments that naturally produce laughter

**Situational**: References to the event or context
> Jokes about the venue, timing, or shared experience
