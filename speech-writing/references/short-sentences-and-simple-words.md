### Short Sentences and Simple Words

Clarity trumps sophistication:

**Guidelines**:
- Average sentence length: 15-20 words
- Prefer one-syllable words when possible
- Avoid jargon unless universal
- Choose concrete over abstract
- Test by reading aloud

**Example**:
- Written: "The implementation of the strategic initiative resulted in optimization of operational efficiency."
- Spoken: "We made one change. It made everything work better."
