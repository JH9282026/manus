### Call-to-Action

What should the audience do next?

**Effective Calls-to-Action**:
- Specific and clear
- Achievable and realistic
- Time-bound when appropriate
- Emotionally motivated
- Easy to remember

**CTA Examples**:
- "Before you leave today, I want you to text one person who believed in you when you didn't believe in yourself."
- "Monday morning, schedule one 15-minute meeting with someone outside your department."
- "Tonight, have one conversation with your child without any screens present."
