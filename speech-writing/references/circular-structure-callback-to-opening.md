### Circular Structure (Callback to Opening)

Return to where you started for satisfying closure:

**Circular Technique**:
- Reference the opening story or statement
- Show how understanding has deepened
- Create sense of completion
- Demonstrate the journey traveled

**Example**:
- Opening: "Three years ago, I stood in an empty warehouse with nothing but a dream and a maxed-out credit card."
- Closing: "That empty warehouse is now our headquarters, employing 200 people. Your empty warehouse is out there too. The question is: will you walk through the door?"
