### Understanding the Audience

Before writing a single word, research your audience thoroughly:

**Demographics**:
- Age range and generational considerations
- Professional background and expertise level
- Cultural and geographic diversity
- Political or social affiliations
- Education level

**Psychographics**:
- What do they care about most?
- What problems keep them up at night?
- What are their aspirations?
- What shared experiences unite them?
- What language and references resonate?

**Relationship to Speaker**:
- How well do they know the speaker?
- What's their existing perception?
- What authority does the speaker have with this audience?
- Are there skeptics who need convincing?

**Audience Questions to Answer**:
- Why should I listen?
- What's in it for me?
- How does this apply to my situation?
- What do you want me to do?
