### Conclusion

The conclusion creates lasting impact:

**Functions of the Conclusion**:
- Summarize key points
- Deliver call to action
- Create emotional climax
- Provide memorable closing
- Leave audience with clear takeaway

**Time Allocation**: 10-15% of total speech

## Conclusion

Speech writing is a craft that combines strategic thinking, emotional intelligence, storytelling ability, and technical skill. The best speeches don't just convey information—they move people to think differently, feel deeply, and act decisively.

Remember: every speech is written for a specific speaker, delivered to a specific audience, on a specific occasion. There are no templates for greatness, only principles to guide you. Master the fundamentals, study the greats, practice relentlessly, and always keep the audience at the center of every word you write.

The goal isn't a perfect speech on paper—it's a perfect moment between speaker and audience, where words become connection, information becomes inspiration, and a message becomes a memory that changes something in the world.
