### Personal Stories vs. Third-Party Stories

Both have their place:

**Personal Stories**:
- Build authenticity and vulnerability
- Establish credibility through experience
- Create emotional connection
- Risk: Can seem self-centered if overused

**Third-Party Stories**:
- Demonstrate broader principles
- Bring in diverse perspectives
- Can be more dramatic
- Risk: Less personal connection

**Balance**: Use personal stories to establish connection, third-party stories to illustrate principles.
