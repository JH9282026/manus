### Signposting and Transitions

Help the audience follow your journey:

**Signposting Techniques**:
- "I want to share three ideas with you today..."
- "First... Second... Finally..."
- "Now let's turn to..."
- "The most important point is..."

**Transition Types**:
- **Additive**: "Furthermore," "Additionally," "Building on this..."
- **Contrastive**: "However," "On the other hand," "Despite this..."
- **Causal**: "Therefore," "As a result," "This leads us to..."
- **Sequential**: "Next," "Following that," "The final step..."
