### Occasion and Context

The event context shapes every aspect of the speech:

**Formal vs. Informal**:
- Formal: Structured, polished, limited humor
- Informal: Conversational, flexible, warmer tone

**Event Timing**:
- Opening: Set energy, establish themes
- Mid-event: Build on previous content, maintain engagement
- Closing: Synthesize, inspire, call to action

**Physical Environment**:
- Large auditorium vs. intimate room
- Standing vs. seated audience
- Indoor vs. outdoor venue
- Presence of cameras or media

**Preceding and Following Content**:
- What comes before? Build on it.
- What comes after? Set it up.
- Other speakers' topics and styles
