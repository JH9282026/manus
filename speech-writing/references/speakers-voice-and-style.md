### Speaker's Voice and Style

The speech must sound like the speaker, not the writer:

**Researching the Speaker**:
- Listen to previous speeches and interviews
- Note vocabulary preferences and patterns
- Understand comfort with humor
- Identify signature phrases or expressions
- Learn their stories and experiences

**Matching Voice**:
- Use their natural speech patterns
- Incorporate their areas of expertise
- Reference their personal experiences
- Match their comfort level with emotion
- Align with their public persona
