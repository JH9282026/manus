### Attention-Grabbing Techniques

The first 30 seconds determine whether you have the audience:

**1. Startling Statistic**
> "Every eight seconds, someone dies from tobacco use. That's one person every eight seconds. In the time it takes me to give this speech, 225 people will have died from tobacco-related causes."

**2. Provocative Question**
> "What if everything you believe about success is wrong? What if the very habits you've cultivated are holding you back?"

**3. Compelling Story**
> "At 3 AM on a Tuesday morning, I found myself standing in a hospital corridor, about to make the hardest decision of my life..."

**4. Bold Statement**
> "Artificial intelligence will eliminate 40% of jobs in this room within the next decade. But that's not the bad news. The bad news is we're not preparing for what comes next."

**5. Vivid Image**
> "Imagine a world where your children's children have never seen a starry night sky—where light pollution has erased the universe from view."

**6. Historical Reference**
> "Exactly 50 years ago today, a man walked on the moon. Today, we stand at another frontier—one that will define the next 50 years."

**7. Personal Confession**
> "I failed. Three times. Publicly. Embarrassingly. And those failures taught me everything I know about success."
