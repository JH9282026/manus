### Defining the Purpose and Message

Every speech needs a clear purpose and central message:

**Purpose Types**:
- **Inform**: Share knowledge, explain concepts, present data
- **Persuade**: Change minds, motivate action, build support
- **Inspire**: Elevate thinking, create possibility, energize
- **Entertain**: Delight, amuse, create memorable moments
- **Commemorate**: Honor, remember, mark occasions

**The Core Message**:
- Distill to one sentence: "If the audience remembers only one thing..."
- Make it specific and actionable
- Ensure it's relevant to the audience
- Test: Can someone repeat it to another person?

**Example**:
- Weak: "Innovation is important for business success."
- Strong: "Small experiments that fail fast lead to breakthrough innovations that last."
