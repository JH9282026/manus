### Data and Statistics

Numbers need context to resonate:

**Making Data Memorable**:
- Round numbers when precision isn't critical
- Use comparisons: "That's the population of Texas"
- Visualize: "If you stacked those dollars, they'd reach the moon"
- Personalize: "That's 10 people in this room"
- Repeat the key number

**Example**:
- Weak: "The program saved $47.3 million."
- Strong: "The program saved enough money to hire 500 teachers for 10 years. That's 50,000 students who could have a better future."
