### Pauses and Emphasis

Silence is a powerful tool:

**Use Pauses**:
- Before important statements (creates anticipation)
- After important statements (allows absorption)
- To replace "um" and "uh"
- To let laughter or emotion settle
- To regain composure

**Emphasis Markers**:
- "The most important thing I'll say today is..."
- "I want you to really hear this..."
- "Let me say that again..."
