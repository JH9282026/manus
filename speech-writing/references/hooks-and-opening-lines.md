### Hooks and Opening Lines

The first sentence matters most:

**Effective Opening Lines**:
- Direct and specific
- Create curiosity or tension
- Promise value to the audience
- Set the emotional tone
- Avoid clichés ("Webster's dictionary defines...")

**Weak vs. Strong**:
- Weak: "Today I'd like to talk about leadership."
- Strong: "The best leader I ever met couldn't read."
