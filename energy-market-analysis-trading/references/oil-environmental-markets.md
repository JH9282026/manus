# Oil and Environmental Markets

## Crude Oil Market Analysis

### Key Price Benchmarks
- **WTI (West Texas Intermediate)**: US benchmark, Cushing OK delivery
- **Brent**: International benchmark, North Sea
- **Dubai/Oman**: Asian benchmark
- **WTI-Brent spread**: Indicator of US vs global supply-demand balance

### OPEC+ Analysis
- Monitor production quotas and compliance
- Track spare capacity (primarily Saudi Arabia)
- Assess geopolitical risks to member production
- Watch for quota negotiation dynamics and market signaling

### Supply-Demand Balance

| Supply Factor | Demand Factor |
|---|---|
| OPEC+ production | Global GDP growth |
| US shale production | Transportation fuel demand |
| Non-OPEC supply growth | Petrochemical feedstock |
| Strategic reserve actions | Seasonal patterns |
| Supply disruptions | EV adoption rate |
| Refinery utilization | Aviation recovery |

### Oil Price Scenario Framework
- **Bull case**: Supply disruption + strong demand + OPEC discipline
- **Base case**: Balanced market with moderate growth
- **Bear case**: Demand destruction + OPEC oversupply + recession

## Refined Products

### Key Crack Spreads
- **3-2-1 crack**: 3 barrels crude → 2 barrels gasoline + 1 barrel distillate
- **Gasoline crack**: RBOB gasoline price - crude oil price
- **Distillate crack**: Heating oil/diesel price - crude oil price

### Seasonal Patterns
- Gasoline: Strong spring-summer (driving season), maintenance turnarounds
- Heating oil: Winter demand spike in Northeast US
- Diesel: Industrial demand indicator, relatively stable
- Jet fuel: Travel season patterns, airline profitability proxy

## Environmental Markets

### Renewable Energy Credits (RECs)

| Market | Type | Driver | Price Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| Compliance RECs | State RPS mandates | Regulatory requirements | $5-50/MWh |
| Voluntary RECs | Corporate sustainability | ESG commitments | $1-10/MWh |
| SRECs (Solar) | Solar carve-outs | State-specific solar mandates | $10-300/MWh |

### Carbon Markets

| Market | Coverage | Mechanism | Price Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| EU ETS | European emissions | Cap-and-trade | €50-100/tonne |
| RGGI | US Northeast power | Cap-and-trade | $10-15/tonne |
| California Cap-and-Trade | CA economy-wide | Cap-and-trade | $25-40/tonne |
| Voluntary carbon | Global | Offset credits | $5-50/tonne |

### Carbon Market Analysis
- Monitor regulatory changes and cap tightening schedules
- Track allowance supply (auctions, allocations, banking)
- Assess demand from covered entities
- Evaluate voluntary market growth and credit quality standards
- Watch for Article 6 (Paris Agreement) market linkage developments
