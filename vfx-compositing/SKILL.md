# VFX Compositing

Composite 3D renders with live action for film and VFX production. Combine multiple visual elements into cohesive, realistic scenes.

## Overview

VFX compositing combines 3D renders, live-action footage, and effects into final shots using node-based workflows in software like Nuke, After Effects, and Fusion. This skill covers the full compositing pipeline from keying and tracking through color grading and final assembly.
