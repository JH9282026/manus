# Amazon Sponsored Brands

Create brand-focused advertising campaigns featuring your logo, custom headline, and multiple products on Amazon.

## Overview

This skill covers Sponsored Brands campaign creation, ad formats, keyword targeting, and brand building strategies. Use it for increasing brand awareness, driving traffic to your Amazon Store, and showcasing product collections.

## Ad Formats

### Product Collection

**Components**:
- **Brand Logo**: Square, 400x400px minimum
- **Headline**: 50 characters
- **Products**: 3 products (auto-selected or manual)
- **Landing Page**: Amazon Store or product list page

**Best For**: Brand awareness, product discovery

### Store Spotlight

**Components**:
- **Brand Logo**: Square, 400x400px
- **Headline**: 50 characters
- **Store Pages**: 3 subpages from your Store
- **Landing Page**: Amazon Store homepage

**Best For**: Store traffic, brand exploration

### Video

**Components**:
- **Video**: 6-45 seconds, 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- **Headline**: 50 characters
- **Landing Page**: Product detail page or Store

**Best For**: Product demonstrations, storytelling

## Campaign Strategy

### Brand Defense

**Objective**: Protect brand searches from competitors

**Keywords**:
- [brand name]
- [brand name + product]
- [brand name + category]

**Bid Strategy**: High bids (top of search placement)

### Category Conquest

**Objective**: Capture generic category searches

**Keywords**:
- "product category"
- "product type"
- "use case + product"

**Bid Strategy**: Medium bids, test and optimize

### Competitor Conquest

**Objective**: Target competitor brand searches

**Keywords**:
- [competitor brand]
- [competitor brand + product]

**Bid Strategy**: Test bids, monitor ACOS carefully

## Keyword Targeting

### Match Types

| Match Type | Reach | Control | Use Case |
|------------|-------|---------|----------|
| **Broad** | High | Low | Discovery |
| **Phrase** | Medium | Medium | Balanced |
| **Exact** | Low | High | Brand terms |

### Keyword Strategy

**Tier 1: Brand Keywords** (Exact Match)
- [your brand name]
- [your brand + product]
- High bids, protect brand

**Tier 2: Category Keywords** (Phrase Match)
- "product category"
- "product type + modifier"
- Medium bids, scale reach

**Tier 3: Competitor Keywords** (Phrase/Exact)
- "competitor brand"
- Test carefully, monitor ACOS

## Creative Best Practices

### Headline Formulas

1. **Value Proposition**: "Premium Running Shoes - Free Shipping"
2. **Product Range**: "Shop 100+ Styles of Running Shoes"
3. **Brand Promise**: "Trusted Running Shoes Since 1995"
4. **Offer**: "Save 20% on Running Shoes Today"

### Product Selection

- **Best Sellers**: Highest conversion rate
- **New Arrivals**: Drive awareness
- **High Margin**: Maximize profitability
- **Complementary**: Cross-sell opportunity

## Performance Metrics
