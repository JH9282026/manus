# The Trade Desk Creative Management

Managing creatives across multiple channels.

## Display Creatives

### Standard Banners
- Common sizes: 300x250, 728x90, 160x600, 300x600
- Format: JPG, PNG, GIF, HTML5
- File size: Under 200KB
- Animation: Max 30 seconds

### Native Display
- Headline + description + image
- Adapts to publisher layout
- Looks like editorial content

### High Impact Units
- Expandable banners
- Interstitials
- Rich media
- Custom formats

## Video Creatives

### Standard Video
- Pre-roll, mid-roll, post-roll
- Duration: 15, 30, 60 seconds
- Format: MP4, MOV
- Resolution: 1080p recommended
- VAST tag support

### Out-Stream Video
- In-feed video
- In-article video
- Autoplay (muted)

### Video Best Practices
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Include captions
- Clear branding
- Strong call-to-action
- Mobile-optimized

## Connected TV (CTV)

### CTV Specs
- Duration: 15, 30 seconds
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1080p or 4K
- VAST 4.0 support

### CTV Best Practices
- High production quality
- TV-appropriate content
- Clear audio
- Brand-focused messaging
- Lean-back viewing experience

## Audio Creatives

### Audio Specs
- Duration: 15, 30 seconds
- Format: MP3, WAV
- Bitrate: 128kbps minimum
- VAST support

### Audio Best Practices
- Clear voiceover
- Memorable audio branding
- Simple message
- Strong call-to-action
- Companion banner (optional)

## Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH)

### DOOH Specs
- Various sizes (billboard-specific)
- High resolution
- Static or video
- Location-aware content

### DOOH Best Practices
- Large, readable text
- High contrast
- Simple message
- Location-relevant content
- Consider viewing distance

## Creative Assignment

### Ad Group Level
- Assign creatives to specific ad groups
- Multiple creatives per ad group
- Algorithm optimizes delivery

### Creative Rotation
- Even rotation: Equal delivery
- Optimized rotation: Best performers get more delivery

## Creative Testing

### A/B Testing
- Test different messages
- Test different visuals
- Test different CTAs
- Test different formats

### Multivariate Testing
- Test multiple variables simultaneously
- Identify winning combinations

## Creative Performance

### Key Metrics
- Impressions
- Viewability rate
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Video completion rate (VCR)
- Conversion rate

### Optimization
- Pause low performers
- Scale high performers
- Refresh creatives regularly
- Test new variations
- Monitor creative fatigue

## Cross-Channel Creative Strategy

- Consistent branding across channels
- Channel-appropriate messaging
- Unified campaign narrative
- Sequential messaging
- Frequency management across channels
