# The Trade Desk Campaign Setup Guide

Guide for creating programmatic campaigns on The Trade Desk.

## Campaign Hierarchy

### Partner Level
- Main Trade Desk account
- Account-wide features
- Private Marketplace Deals
- Audiences and inventory lists

### Advertiser Level
- Individual business or brand
- Can segment by business unit, product, market
- Houses creatives and first-party data

### Campaign Level
- Budget and flight dates
- Frequency caps
- Programmatic tactics
- KPI selection
- Seed audience for optimization

### Ad Group Level
- Targeting parameters
- Bidding strategies
- Creative assignments
- Budget allocation

## Creating a Campaign

### Step 1: Campaign Setup
- Campaign name
- Campaign goal (awareness, consideration, conversion)
- Budget (daily or total)
- Flight dates (start and end)
- Time zone
- KPIs (impressions, clicks, conversions, ROAS)

### Step 2: Seed Audience
- Select audience to guide algorithm
- Can use first-party data
- Helps Koa AI optimize delivery

### Step 3: Default Rails
- Brand safety settings
- Device type preferences
- Fraud prevention
- Geographic boundaries
- Operating system filters

### Step 4: Create Ad Groups
- Name ad group
- Select ad format (display, video, CTV, audio, DOOH)
- Set targeting parameters
- Configure bidding strategy
- Assign creatives

## Budget Configuration

- **Daily Budget**: Maximum spend per day
- **Total Budget**: Lifetime campaign budget
- **Pacing**: Even or ASAP
- **AutoAllocator**: Automatic budget distribution across ad groups

## Best Practices

- Use campaign templates for consistency
- Set up conversion tracking before launch
- Start with seed audience for better optimization
- Apply brand safety rails
- Break out exchanges into separate ad groups
- Monitor cross-channel performance
- Leverage Koa AI for optimization
