# The Trade Desk Campaign Management

Manage programmatic advertising campaigns on The Trade Desk DSP platform.

## Overview

The Trade Desk is an enterprise-level demand-side platform (DSP) for programmatic advertising across multiple channels. This skill covers campaign hierarchy, omnichannel ad types, advanced targeting, RTB bidding, Koa AI optimization, and cross-channel attribution.

## Campaign Hierarchy

1. **Partner**: Main Trade Desk account
2. **Advertiser**: Business or brand
3. **Campaign**: Budget, dates, KPIs
4. **Ad Group**: Targeting, bidding, creatives
5. **Creative**: Actual advertisements

## Ad Types & Channels

- **Display**: Standard banners, native
- **Video**: Pre-roll, mid-roll, out-stream
- **Connected TV (CTV)**: Streaming TV ads
- **Audio**: Streaming audio, podcasts
- **Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH)**: Digital billboards

## Targeting Capabilities

- **Audience**: Demographics, interests, behaviors, purchase intent
- **Contextual**: Content relevance, keywords
- **Geotargeting**: Country, region, city, radius
- **Cross-Device**: Consistent messaging across devices
- **Retail Data**: Transaction-based targeting
- **Lookalike**: AI-driven audience expansion

## Bidding & Optimization

- **Real-Time Bidding (RTB)**: Bid on individual impressions
- **Koa AI**: AI-driven optimization and budget allocation
- **Custom Algorithmic**: Proprietary bid logic
- **Bid Factors**: Adjust bids by placement value
- **AutoAllocator**: Automatic budget distribution
