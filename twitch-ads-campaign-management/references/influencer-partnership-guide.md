# Twitch Influencer & Creator Partnership Guide

Strategies for collaborating with Twitch streamers and creators to amplify brand reach and authenticity.

---

## Bounty Board Program

### Overview

**What It Is:**
- Official Twitch marketplace connecting brands with creators
- Brands post "bounties" (sponsored content opportunities)
- Creators apply to participate based on eligibility criteria
- Brand-safe, vetted partnership platform

**How It Works:**
1. Brand creates bounty campaign
2. Sets objectives, compensation, eligibility requirements
3. Posts to Bounty Board
4. Eligible creators see and apply
5. Brand reviews applications, selects creators
6. Creators produce sponsored content
7. Brand monitors and measures performance

**Access:**
- Available through Twitch sales team
- Part of managed service offering
- Requires minimum budget commitment

### Best Use Cases

**Game Launches:**
- New game releases
- DLC/expansion announcements
- Beta access promotions
- Gameplay reveals

**Product Demonstrations:**
- Gaming peripherals (mice, keyboards, headsets)
- Gaming chairs, desks, setups
- Tech products (monitors, PCs, components)
- Software and apps

**Brand Awareness:**
- Introducing new brands to gaming audience
- Repositioning existing brands
- Building credibility in gaming space
- Reaching niche communities

**Trailer Promotions:**
- Movie trailers (gaming-related films)
- Game trailers and cinematics
- Product launch videos
- Event announcements

### Setting Up a Bounty

**Define Objectives:**
- Awareness: Reach and impressions
- Engagement: Clicks, interactions, chat mentions
- Conversions: Sales, sign-ups, downloads
- Content creation: Clips, highlights, social shares

**Set Compensation:**
- Flat fee per creator
- Performance-based (CPM, CPC, CPA)
- Hybrid (base fee + performance bonus)
- Product seeding (free products as compensation)

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Minimum follower count
- Average concurrent viewers
- Content category (game genre, topic)
- Geographic location
- Language
- Streaming frequency
- Partnership status (Affiliate, Partner)

**Creative Guidelines:**
- Key messages to communicate
- Required disclosures (#ad, #sponsored)
- Brand assets (logos, graphics)
- Do's and don'ts
- Content approval process (if any)
- Streaming duration requirements

**Deliverables:**
- Stream length (e.g., minimum 2 hours)
- Number of mentions
- Hashtags to use
- Social media posts (Twitter, Instagram)
- Clips or highlights
- VOD retention period

### Managing Bounty Campaigns

**Creator Selection:**
- Review applications based on:
  - Audience demographics alignment
  - Engagement rate (not just follower count)
  - Content quality and production value
  - Brand safety and reputation
  - Previous sponsored content performance
  - Authenticity and creator fit

**Communication:**
- Clear, timely communication with selected creators
- Provide all necessary assets and information
- Answer questions promptly
- Set expectations clearly
- Build positive relationships

**Monitoring:**
- Track streams in real-time (when possible)
- Monitor chat sentiment
- Capture clips and highlights
- Verify deliverables completed
- Ensure disclosure compliance

**Measurement:**
- Reach: Total viewers, unique viewers
- Engagement: Chat activity, clip views, social shares
- Conversions: Clicks, sales, sign-ups (tracked via unique links/codes)
- Sentiment: Positive/negative chat reactions
- Content quality: Creator enthusiasm, integration quality

## Direct Creator Sponsorships

### Channel Takeover

**What It Is:**
- Immersive brand experience integrated into creator's channel
- Full stream sponsorship with branded elements
- Custom overlays, alerts, graphics, segments
- Exclusive partnership for defined period

**Components:**
- **Branded Overlays:** Custom stream overlays featuring brand
- **Alerts:** Branded follower, subscriber, donation alerts
- **Segments:** Dedicated sponsored segments within stream
- **Giveaways:** Brand-sponsored prizes for viewers
- **Exclusive Content:** Behind-the-scenes, early access, etc.

**Best For:**
- Major product launches
- Long-term brand partnerships
- Deep creator collaborations
- Building strong brand association

**Pricing:**
- Typically higher than standard sponsorships
- Based on creator's reach and engagement
- Negotiated directly or through agencies
- Can include revenue share models

### Sponsored Streams

**Integration Approaches:**

**Product Placement:**
- Creator uses product naturally during stream
- Gaming peripheral in use
- Energy drink on desk
- Apparel worn
- Minimal scripting, maximum authenticity

**Dedicated Segment:**
- 5-15 minute sponsored segment
- Creator discusses/demonstrates product
- Q&A with chat about product
- Detailed feature showcase
- Clear disclosure before segment

**Full Stream Sponsorship:**
- Entire stream sponsored by brand
- Periodic mentions throughout
- Branded graphics and overlays
- Giveaways and promotions
- "This stream brought to you by [Brand]"

**Challenge/Event:**
- Creator completes brand-related challenge
- Community participation (chat votes, suggestions)
- Prizes for creator and viewers
- Shareable, memorable moments
- Viral potential

**Unboxing/First Impressions:**
- Creator receives and unboxes product on stream
- Authentic first reactions
- Setup and initial use
- Viewer questions and interaction
- Honest feedback (builds trust)

### Creator Selection Criteria

**Audience Alignment:**
- Demographics match target market (age, gender, location)
- Interests align with product/brand
- Community values compatible with brand
- Viewer purchasing power appropriate

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Average concurrent viewers (CCV)
- Chat activity rate
- Subscriber count and growth
- Follower-to-viewer ratio
- Social media engagement
- VOD view counts

**Content Quality:**
- Production value (overlays, camera, audio)
- Consistency (streaming schedule)
- Professionalism
- Entertainment value
- Unique personality/style

**Brand Safety:**
- Content appropriate for brand association
- No controversial history
- Positive community reputation
- Complies with Twitch ToS
- Professional conduct

**Authenticity:**
- Genuine enthusiasm for relevant products
- Not over-saturated with sponsorships
- Maintains authentic voice
- Trusted by community
- Selective about partnerships

**Previous Performance:**
- Track record with sponsored content
- Conversion rates (if available)
- Audience reception to sponsorships
- Professionalism in partnerships
- Deliverable completion

## Partnership Best Practices

### Give Creative Freedom

**Why It Matters:**
- Creators know their audience best
- Authentic content performs better
- Scripted content feels forced and inauthentic
- Community can detect inauthenticity
- Trust is paramount on Twitch

**How to Do It:**
- Provide guidelines, not scripts
- Share key messages, not exact wording
- Allow creator's personality to shine
- Trust their content expertise
- Encourage natural integration

**What to Provide:**
- Brand overview and values
- Product features and benefits
- Key messages to communicate
- Do's and don'ts (brand safety)
- Examples of good integrations (not to copy, but for inspiration)

**What NOT to Provide:**
- Word-for-word scripts
- Overly restrictive guidelines
- Unrealistic expectations
- Excessive approval layers
- Micromanagement

### Set Clear Expectations

**Deliverables:**
- Stream length (e.g., minimum 2 hours)
- Number of product mentions
- Specific hashtags or @mentions
- Social media posts (quantity, platforms)
- Clips or highlights to create
- VOD retention period

**Disclosure Requirements:**
- FTC compliance (#ad, #sponsored, verbal disclosure)
- Twitch's "Sponsored" label
- Timing of disclosures (beginning, periodic)
- Clarity and conspicuousness

**Content Approval:**
- Whether pre-approval required
- Turnaround time for approvals
- What requires approval (scripts, graphics, etc.)
- Revision process

**Performance Metrics:**
- How success will be measured
- Reporting requirements
- Data sharing expectations
- Payment tied to performance (if applicable)

**Timeline:**
- Campaign start and end dates
- Content delivery deadlines
- Payment schedule
- Exclusivity period (if applicable)

### Build Long-Term Relationships

**Why Long-Term Works Better:**
- Audience becomes familiar with brand
- Creator develops genuine affinity
- More authentic endorsements over time
- Better performance as creator learns what works
- Cost-effective (negotiated rates for ongoing partnerships)

**Strategies:**

**Multi-Stream Contracts:**
- Commit to multiple streams over time (e.g., 4 streams over 3 months)
- Allows creator and audience to build familiarity
- Better ROI than one-off sponsorships

**Recurring Sponsorships:**
- Monthly or quarterly sponsorships
- Consistent brand presence
- Builds strong association
- Predictable for both parties

**Exclusive Partnerships:**
- Creator becomes brand ambassador
- Exclusive to your brand in category
- Deep integration across all content
- Premium pricing but strong association

**Provide Value Beyond Payment:**
- Early access to products
- Exclusive perks for creator and community
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Invitations to events
- Opportunities for creator growth

**Invest in Relationships:**
- Regular communication beyond campaigns
- Seek creator feedback on products
- Involve creators in product development
- Celebrate creator milestones
- Build genuine partnerships, not transactional relationships

## Disclosure & Compliance

### FTC Requirements

**Material Connection Disclosure:**
- Must disclose when there's a material connection (payment, free products, etc.)
- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous
- Can't be buried or hidden
- Must be understandable to average viewer

**How to Disclose:**
- **Verbal:** "This stream is sponsored by [Brand]" at beginning and periodically
- **Text:** #ad, #sponsored, "Sponsored by [Brand]" in stream title/description
- **Visual:** Twitch's built-in "Sponsored" label
- **All of the above:** Recommended for full compliance

**When to Disclose:**
- At the beginning of sponsored content
- Periodically throughout (every 15-30 minutes recommended)
- Can't be just once at the start
- Before any call-to-action

**What Doesn't Count as Disclosure:**
- Buried in description
- Hidden in hashtag list
- Vague language ("Thanks to [Brand]" without "sponsored")
- Only in VOD description (not during live stream)

### Twitch Requirements

**Sponsored Content Label:**
- Use Twitch's built-in "Sponsored" stream label
- Visible on stream thumbnail and page
- Required for all sponsored streams

**Verbal Disclosure:**
- Announce sponsorship verbally during stream
- Recommended at start and periodically

**Panel Disclosure:**
- Add sponsorship information to channel panels
- Visible to all viewers

**Ongoing Disclosure:**
- Not just once, but throughout sponsored content
- Especially before CTAs or product discussions

### Best Practices

**Be Transparent:**
- Honesty builds trust
- Viewers appreciate transparency
- "I'm excited to partner with [Brand] for this stream"
- Explain why you chose to partner (genuine reasons)

**Make It Natural:**
- Disclosure doesn't have to be grudging
- Integrate naturally into content
- "This stream is sponsored by [Brand], who sent me this awesome [product] to try"

**Educate Creators:**
- Provide clear disclosure guidelines
- Explain FTC and Twitch requirements
- Give examples of compliant disclosures
- Make it easy for creators to comply

**Monitor Compliance:**
- Check that creators are disclosing properly
- Provide feedback if needed
- Protect both brand and creator from regulatory issues

## Measurement & ROI

### Key Performance Indicators

**Reach Metrics:**
- Total viewers (unique)
- Peak concurrent viewers
- Average concurrent viewers
- Total watch time
- VOD views
- Clip views

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Chat messages per minute
- Follower growth during/after stream
- Subscriber growth
- Social media mentions
- Shares and retweets
- Community sentiment (positive/negative)

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Clicks (via unique tracking links)
- Coupon code usage
- Sales attributed to campaign
- Sign-ups or downloads
- Landing page visits
- Add-to-cart actions

**Content Metrics:**
- Clips created
- Highlights generated
- Social media posts
- User-generated content
- Viral moments

**Brand Metrics:**
- Brand mention volume
- Sentiment analysis
- Brand awareness lift
- Purchase intent increase
- Brand association strength

### Tracking Methods

**Unique Links:**
- Creator-specific tracking URLs
- UTM parameters for analytics
- Shortened links for easy sharing
- Track clicks, conversions, revenue

**Coupon Codes:**
- Creator-specific discount codes
- Easy to remember and share
- Track usage and revenue
- Incentivize conversions

**Affiliate Links:**
- Revenue share with creator
- Automatic tracking
- Incentivizes creator promotion
- Win-win model

**Pixel Tracking:**
- Place tracking pixel on landing pages
- Attribute conversions to campaigns
- Retargeting opportunities
- Detailed conversion funnel analysis

**Social Listening:**
- Monitor brand mentions
- Track hashtags
- Sentiment analysis
- Identify viral moments

### ROI Calculation

**Formula:**
ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100%

**Costs to Include:**
- Creator fees
- Production costs (graphics, assets)
- Product seeding (if applicable)
- Platform fees (Bounty Board, etc.)
- Agency fees (if using intermediary)
- Internal labor costs

**Revenue to Attribute:**
- Direct sales (tracked via links/codes)
- Estimated brand lift impact
- Long-term customer value (LTV)
- Earned media value (clips, social shares)

**Benchmarks:**
- Positive ROI: >100%
- Good ROI: 200-400%
- Excellent ROI: 500%+
- Awareness campaigns: May have lower direct ROI but high brand value

**Beyond Direct ROI:**
- Brand awareness value
- Content creation (clips, highlights)
- Community building
- Credibility in gaming space
- Long-term brand association
- Influencer relationships for future campaigns

## Creator Outreach & Negotiation

### Finding the Right Creators

**Research Tools:**
- Twitch directory (browse by category)
- TwitchTracker, SullyGnome (analytics)
- Social media (Twitter, Instagram)
- Creator agencies and networks
- Recommendations from other creators

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Audience size and demographics
- Engagement rate
- Content quality
- Brand fit
- Professionalism
- Availability

### Outreach Best Practices

**Personalized Approach:**
- Watch their streams before reaching out
- Reference specific content or moments
- Explain why they're a good fit
- Show genuine interest in their work

**Professional Communication:**
- Clear, concise email or DM
- Introduce brand and campaign
- Explain opportunity and benefits
- Provide contact information
- Follow up appropriately (not spammy)

**What to Include:**
- Campaign overview
- Deliverables and expectations
- Compensation offer
- Timeline
- Next steps
- Contact for questions

### Negotiation Tips

**Understand Creator Value:**
- Engagement rate more important than follower count
- Niche audiences can be more valuable
- Quality over quantity
- Long-term potential

**Be Fair:**
- Research market rates
- Don't lowball experienced creators
- Consider creator's value beyond numbers
- Build goodwill for future partnerships

**Flexible Compensation:**
- Flat fee
- Performance-based (CPA, CPM)
- Hybrid models
- Product seeding + payment
- Revenue share (affiliate)

**Negotiate Win-Win:**
- Understand creator's goals
- Find mutual benefits
- Be open to creator input
- Build partnership, not transaction

**Contract Essentials:**
- Scope of work (deliverables)
- Compensation and payment terms
- Timeline and deadlines
- Disclosure requirements
- Content rights and usage
- Exclusivity (if applicable)
- Termination clauses
- Liability and indemnification

## Case Studies & Examples

### Successful Gaming Peripheral Launch

**Brand:** Gaming Mouse Company
**Objective:** Launch new wireless gaming mouse
**Strategy:**
- Bounty Board campaign targeting FPS streamers
- 20 creators selected (mix of sizes)
- Product seeding + flat fee compensation
- 2-hour minimum stream requirement
- Unique discount codes per creator

**Results:**
- 500K+ total viewers reached
- 15K+ clicks to product page
- 2,500+ sales (tracked via codes)
- 300% ROI
- 50+ organic clips created
- Positive sentiment (85% positive mentions)

**Key Success Factors:**
- Product genuinely appealing to audience
- Creators had creative freedom
- High-quality product (authentic enthusiasm)
- Competitive pricing with discount
- Strong creator selection

### Energy Drink Brand Awareness

**Brand:** Energy Drink Company
**Objective:** Build awareness in gaming community
**Strategy:**
- Long-term partnerships with 5 top streamers
- 6-month contracts, monthly sponsored streams
- Branded overlays and alerts
- Product placement (drink on desk)
- Community giveaways

**Results:**
- 2M+ total reach over 6 months
- 40% increase in brand awareness (surveyed)
- 25% increase in purchase intent
- Strong brand association with gaming
- Creators became genuine brand advocates

**Key Success Factors:**
- Long-term commitment built authenticity
- Creators genuinely enjoyed product
- Natural integration, not forced
- Community engagement (giveaways)
- Consistent presence over time

### Game Launch Campaign

**Brand:** Indie Game Developer
**Objective:** Drive awareness and sales for new game launch
**Strategy:**
- Bounty Board + direct outreach
- 30 creators across various sizes
- Early access keys for creators
- Launch day coordinated streams
- Unique affiliate links for revenue share

**Results:**
- 1M+ viewers on launch day
- 10K+ game sales attributed to campaign
- 500% ROI
- Trending on Twitch (top 10 games)
- Sustained interest post-launch

**Key Success Factors:**
- Early access created exclusivity
- Launch day coordination created momentum
- Affiliate model incentivized creators
- Game quality (creators genuinely enjoyed it)
- Diverse creator selection (broad reach)
