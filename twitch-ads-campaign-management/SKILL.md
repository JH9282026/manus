# Twitch Ads Campaign Management

Manage advertising campaigns on Twitch through Amazon Ads to reach gaming, streaming, and entertainment audiences with video and display ad formats.

## Overview

Twitch advertising is managed through Amazon Ads (not self-service Twitch platform). Advertisers access Twitch inventory via Amazon DSP for programmatic buying or work with Twitch sales team for managed service (budgets $35,000-$50,000+). The platform offers premium video placements, interactive formats, and access to highly engaged audiences spending 100+ minutes daily on the platform.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Premium video ads (pre-roll, mid-roll) across all devices
- High-impact display placements integrated into streaming experience
- Demographic targeting (72% aged 18-34, gaming/lifestyle interests)
- Amazon first-party shopper data for precise audience targeting
- Unskippable video formats with 100% viewability
- Creator partnerships and sponsorship opportunities

## Campaign Access Methods

| Budget Level | Access Method | Features | Best For |
|--------------|---------------|----------|----------|
| $35,000-$50,000+ | Managed Service | White-glove support, custom influencer partnerships, premium units (Homepage Headliner), dedicated account team | Large brands, major campaigns, custom solutions |
| Any budget | Amazon DSP (Self-Service) | Programmatic buying, granular bid control, full targeting options, campaign automation | Performance marketers, agencies, programmatic buyers |

**Setup Process:**
1. Contact Twitch account executive through Amazon Advertising site
2. Share company information, campaign goals, budget estimates
3. Twitch team recommends ad formats and targeting strategy
4. Submit creative assets (must comply with Twitch Advertising Guidelines)
5. Twitch team handles campaign setup, scheduling, targeting configuration
6. Monitor performance through Amazon Ads reporting dashboard

## Ad Format Selection Guide

### High Impact Media

**First Impression Takeover**
- Exclusive placement: one advertiser per day per geo
- Displays during viewer's first watched broadcast of the day
- Up to 30 seconds, unskippable
- Premium pricing, sold on per-day, per-geographic basis
- Best for: Major product launches, brand awareness campaigns, exclusive announcements

**Headliner**
- Premium cross-screen placement on Twitch homepage and mobile app landing page
- Above the fold, clickable
- High visibility for all Twitch visitors
- Best for: Homepage takeovers, maximum reach campaigns

### Premium Video Formats

**Premium Video**
- 100% above the fold, unskippable
- Inventory: desktop, mobile, tablet, console, streaming TV
- Pre-roll and mid-roll placements
- Lengths: 6s, 15s, 20s, 30s, 60s (fees vary by length)
- Delivered via SureStream technology (bypasses ad blockers)
- Best for: Brand storytelling, product demonstrations, broad reach

**Vertical Video Ads (9:16)**
- Desktop: appears silently beside livestream
- Mobile: unmuted, skippable, full-screen in feed
- Lengths: 6s, 15s, 20s, 30s
- Native mobile-first format
- Best for: Mobile-optimized campaigns, social-style content, younger demographics

### Display Ad Formats

**Stream Display Ads**
- Renders between live stream and chat OR directly below stream
- Clickable, multiple IAB standard sizes available
- Integrated into viewing experience
- Best for: Direct response, website traffic, complementary messaging

**Mid-Feed Ads**
- Mobile-optimized, renders within Twitch mobile app feed
- Full-screen, clickable
- Native feed integration
- Best for: Mobile campaigns, app installs, mobile commerce

**Super Leaderboard**
- IAB standard wide banner unit
- Keeps viewers engaged while browsing content
- Clickable
- Best for: Brand awareness, homepage visibility

**Medium Rectangle**
- IAB standard unit embedded in search content
- Maximum 2 per page
- Clickable
- Best for: Search-related campaigns, contextual targeting

### Sponsorships & Promotions

**Streamables**
- 30-second ad experience viewers opt into on Twitch Partnered mobile games
- Full-screen, non-skippable livestream
- Viewer-initiated engagement
- Best for: Gaming brands, high engagement campaigns

**Homepage Carousel**
- Promotes livestream channel content on homepage
- Rotating carousel slots
- Maximum 240 characters
- Best for: Channel promotion, event marketing

**Bounty Board**
- Marketplace connecting brands with Twitch influencers
- Brand-safe influencer partnerships
- Best for: Game launches, trailer promotions, authentic creator integrations

**Channel Takeover**
- Creator-centric solution for immersive brand experiences
- Direct partnerships with verified streamers
- Best for: Deep creator collaborations, sponsored streams

## Targeting Strategy

### Demographic Targeting

**Age Ranges:**
- 72% of users aged 18-34 (as of Dec 2023)
- Strong Gen Z and Millennial presence
- Highly engaged younger demographics

**Gender:**
- Historically male-dominated, now 35% female (up from 18% in 2017)
- Diversifying audience base
- Consider gender targeting based on content category

**Geographic:**
- Global audience with strong North America and Europe markets
- Growing Asia presence
- Target by country, region, or specific geos

### Interest & Behavioral Targeting

**Content Categories:**
- Gaming (core): specific game titles, genres, esports
- Music: live performances, DJ sets, music production
- Art: digital art, traditional art, creative streams
- Cooking: recipe streams, cooking shows
- Lifestyle: "Just Chatting", IRL streams, talk shows
- Fitness, education, technology, and more

**Viewing Habits:**
- Target by specific gaming categories
- Streaming preferences and watch time
- Device usage patterns
- Time of day/week viewing behaviors

**Amazon First-Party Data:**
- Shopper purchase behavior ("Bought gaming mouse in last 30 days")
- Product category interests
- Shopping intent signals
- Cross-platform Amazon audience segments

## Bidding & Budget Management

### Bidding Strategies (Amazon DSP)

**Fixed Bids**
- Set default bid, remains constant regardless of conversion likelihood
- Predictable budget management
- Best for: New product launches, visibility goals, budget certainty
- Limitation: May result in fewer conversions per dollar spent

**Dynamic Bidding – Down Only**
- Amazon automatically lowers bids up to 100% if ad unlikely to convert
- Cost-effective, recommended for beginners
- Better conversion rates with potentially less traffic
- Best for: Testing campaigns, optimizing for efficiency
- Recommendation: Start here, test for 2 weeks before switching strategies

**Dynamic Bidding – Up and Down**
- Amazon adjusts bids up or down by up to 100% based on real-time conversion likelihood
- Increases conversion potential, less predictable budget
- Best for: Managing excess inventory, boosting underperforming products, maximizing conversions
- Caution: Budget can fluctuate significantly

**Rule-Based Bidding (Advanced)**
- Set specific ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) targets
- Create custom "if/then" rules for bid adjustments
- Amazon adjusts bids to stay within ROAS target
- Best for: Experienced advertisers, performance-driven campaigns, complex optimization

### Budget Allocation

**Minimum Budgets:**
- Managed Service: $35,000-$50,000+ (varies by region)
- Amazon DSP: No strict minimum, but $5,000+ recommended for meaningful data

**Budget Distribution:**
- Allocate 60-70% to proven formats (Premium Video)
- Reserve 20-30% for testing (Vertical Video, new placements)
- Keep 10% for optimization and scaling winners

**Pacing:**
- Even pacing: Spread budget evenly across campaign duration
- Accelerated pacing: Spend budget as quickly as possible for time-sensitive campaigns
- Dayparting: Concentrate spend during peak viewing hours (evenings, weekends)

## Creative Best Practices

### Understanding Twitch Culture

**Authenticity is Critical:**
- Twitch community values genuine, non-corporate messaging
- Avoid overly polished, traditional TV-style ads
- Self-aware, humorous content performs better
- UGC-style ads blend more naturally

**Community-First Mindset:**
- Respect the streaming experience
- Don't interrupt with jarring, off-brand content
- Speak the platform's language (gaming terminology, memes, references)
- Acknowledge you're advertising to a savvy audience

### Video Creative Guidelines

**Technical Requirements:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum (Premium Video)
- Bitrate: 4,000 kbps minimum, 10,000 kbps maximum
- Audio: -9dB peak audio level
- Format: H.264 (MP4)
- Frame Rate: 24-30fps
- Lengths: 6s, 15s, 20s, 30s, 60s (60s has extra charge, mid-roll only)

**Content Best Practices:**
- Front-load key message (first 3 seconds critical)
- Include logo/brand name early and throughout
- Use subtitles (many viewers watch muted initially)
- Keep under 30 seconds when possible
- Make it entertaining, not just promotional
- Consider interactive elements (QR codes, polls, mini-games)

**Vertical Video Specific (9:16):**
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Optimize for mobile viewing
- Design for sound-off (desktop) and sound-on (mobile)
- Use bold, readable text
- Vertical framing for faces and products

### Display Creative Guidelines

**General Requirements:**
- SSL-compliant (all components must use HTTPS)
- Clickable with clear CTA
- Brand logo prominently featured
- Complies with Twitch Advertising Guidelines and Community Guidelines

**Design Best Practices:**
- High contrast for visibility during streams
- Minimal text, bold typography
- Gaming-relevant imagery or lifestyle content
- Avoid white backgrounds (blend with Twitch's dark theme)
- Test creatives in context (beside live stream)

### Interactive Ad Strategies

**Why Interactive Works:**
- Gamers expect interaction
- Transforms passive viewing into active engagement
- Significantly higher CTR and dwell time
- Memorable brand experiences

**Interactive Elements:**
- Mini-games within ads
- Polls and voting
- QR codes for instant mobile engagement
- Clickable hotspots
- Gamified experiences

**Measurement:**
- Track "dwell time" (more meaningful than impressions)
- Interaction rate
- Completion rate for interactive experiences
- Post-interaction conversion rate

## Campaign Optimization

### Key Performance Metrics

**Awareness Metrics:**
- Impressions: Total ad views
- Unique Reach: Unduplicated viewers
- Video Completion Rate (VCR): Percentage who watched entire ad
- Brand Lift: Measured increase in brand awareness

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Dwell Time (for interactive ads)
- Interaction Rate
- Engagement Rate

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Detail Page Views (DPV): Amazon product page visits
- Branded Searches: Increase in brand search volume
- New-to-Brand (NTB) Conversions: First-time customers
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)

### Optimization Tactics

**Creative Optimization:**
- Test multiple video lengths (15s vs 30s)
- A/B test messaging approaches (humorous vs informative)
- Rotate creatives every 2-3 weeks to combat ad fatigue
- Analyze VCR by creative to identify winners
- Refresh underperforming creatives

**Targeting Refinement:**
- Analyze performance by demographic segment
- Identify top-performing content categories
- Exclude low-performing audience segments
- Layer Amazon shopper data with Twitch behavioral data
- Test lookalike audiences based on converters

**Placement Optimization:**
- Compare performance across devices (mobile vs desktop vs console)
- Analyze pre-roll vs mid-roll effectiveness
- Shift budget to top-performing placements
- Test different ad formats for same campaign goal

**Timing Optimization:**
- Identify peak performance hours/days
- Adjust dayparting based on conversion data
- Align with major gaming events, esports tournaments
- Consider seasonal gaming trends (new releases, holidays)

**Budget Reallocation:**
- Move budget from low ROAS campaigns to high performers
- Scale winning ad groups by 20-30% increments
- Pause campaigns with CPA above target threshold
- Increase bids on high-converting keywords/audiences

Read `/references/optimization-strategies.md` for advanced optimization techniques.

## Influencer & Creator Partnerships

**Bounty Board Program:**
- Marketplace connecting brands with Twitch creators
- Brands post "bounties" for sponsored content opportunities
- Best for: Game launches, product demos, brand awareness

**Direct Creator Sponsorships:**
- Channel Takeover: Immersive brand experiences with verified streamers
- Sponsored Streams: Creator integrates product naturally into content
- Selection based on audience alignment, engagement rate, brand safety

**Partnership Best Practices:**
- Give creative freedom (guidelines, not scripts)
- Set clear expectations (deliverables, disclosures, FTC compliance)
- Build long-term relationships (recurring sponsorships perform better)

## Measurement & Reporting

**Amazon Ads Dashboard Metrics:**
- Campaign performance, impressions, unique reach
- Video completion rates, click-through rates
- Detail page views, branded search lift
- New-to-brand metrics, ROAS, conversion data

**Content Transparency Reports (via API):**
- Content type, rating, title/stream data
- Brand safety verification, contextual relevance

**Third-Party Tracking:**
- Supported: Impressions, clicks, quartiles, completes
- VAST 2.0/3.0 supported (VAST 4.0, VPAID not supported)
- All tracking must be SSL-compliant (HTTPS)

**Performance Benchmarks:**
- VCR: 70-85% (unskippable), CTR: 0.3-0.8%
- ROAS target: 3:1 minimum, 5:1+ excellent
- Brand Lift: 5-15% increase

## Compliance & Guidelines

### Twitch Advertising Guidelines

**Creative Requirements:**
- Must include advertiser logo or brand name
- Content must be accurate and verifiable
- Comply with Twitch Community Guidelines
- No misleading or deceptive content
- Age-appropriate for platform (13+)

**Prohibited Content:**
- Illegal products or services
- Weapons, tobacco, drugs
- Adult content or services
- Gambling (restrictions apply)
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Shocking or disturbing imagery

**Disclosure Requirements:**
- Sponsored content must be clearly labeled
- Creator partnerships require FTC-compliant disclosures
- Affiliate links must be disclosed
- Material connections must be transparent

### Technical Compliance

**SSL Requirements:**
- All ad components must use HTTPS
- Third-party vendors must be SSL-compliant
- Tracking pixels must use secure protocols

**File Specifications:**
- Adhere to format-specific technical requirements
- Maximum file sizes must be respected
- Proper encoding and compression
- Quality assurance testing before submission

**Lead Times:**
- Submit creatives 5-7 business days before campaign start
- Allow time for review and approval
- Plan for potential revision requests
- Rush approvals may not be available
