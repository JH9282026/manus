# Customer Segmentation & Persona Development

Transform customer data and market insights into structured frameworks for understanding and targeting distinct customer groups.

## Overview

Combine quantitative analysis with qualitative behavioral understanding to create segmentation models and buyer personas that inform marketing strategy, product development, and sales targeting. Layer demographic, behavioral, psychographic, and needs-based dimensions to build rich, multi-dimensional customer portraits.

## Segmentation Methodology Selection

| Approach | Data Required | Best For | Complexity |
|----------|--------------|----------|------------|
| Demographic | CRM/firmographic data | Initial segmentation, B2B targeting | Low |
| Behavioral (RFM) | Transaction history | Customer value tiers, retention | Medium |
| Psychographic | Surveys, interviews | Messaging, content strategy | Medium |
| Needs-based | Customer research | Product development, innovation | High |
| Predictive/ML | Large datasets | Scalable, dynamic segmentation | High |

## RFM Analysis Framework

Score customers on three dimensions (1-5 scale each):

| Dimension | Definition | Scoring Logic |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Recency** | Time since last purchase | More recent = higher score |
| **Frequency** | Number of purchases in period | More frequent = higher score |
| **Monetary** | Total spend in period | Higher spend = higher score |

### RFM Segment Interpretation

| Segment | RFM Score | Strategy |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Champions | 5-5-5 | Reward, upsell, ask for referrals |
| Loyal | 4-4-4 | Loyalty programs, new product previews |
| Potential Loyalists | 5-3-3 | Nurture, increase engagement |
| At Risk | 2-4-4 | Win-back campaigns, special offers |
| Hibernating | 1-1-2 | Reactivation or sunset |

## Persona Development Process

1. **Aggregate data** — Combine quantitative segments with qualitative research
2. **Identify patterns** — Find recurring motivations, behaviors, and pain points
3. **Draft persona profiles** — Create 3-5 distinct personas
4. **Validate** — Test personas against real customer data and interviews
5. **Distribute** — Share across marketing, product, sales, and support teams

### Persona Template

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Name & Photo | Memorable, humanizing identifier |
| Demographics | Age, role, company, location |
| Goals | What they want to achieve |
| Pain Points | Frustrations and challenges |
| Buying Behavior | Decision process, influencers, criteria |
| Preferred Channels | Where they consume content and engage |
| Objections | Common reasons for not buying |
| Quote | Representative statement capturing their perspective |

## Segment Prioritization Matrix

| Criteria | Weight | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Market size | 25% | Revenue potential of segment |
| Growth potential | 20% | Projected segment growth rate |
| Competitive intensity | 20% | Number and strength of competitors |
| Strategic fit | 20% | Alignment with company capabilities |
| Accessibility | 15% | Ability to reach and serve segment |
