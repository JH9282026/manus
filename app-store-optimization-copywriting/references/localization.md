# ASO Localization

Expand to international markets with proper localization.

---

## Beyond Translation

Localization is not just translation:
- Keywords differ by language
- Search behavior varies
- Cultural references matter
- Competition differs

---

## Localization Checklist

### Research Phase

- [ ] Research keywords in target language
- [ ] Analyze local competitors
- [ ] Understand cultural norms
- [ ] Check trademark availability

### Execution Phase

- [ ] Hire native speakers (not just translators)
- [ ] Adapt tone for cultural norms
- [ ] Update screenshots with localized text
- [ ] Verify app name works locally
- [ ] Test with native speakers

## Keyword Research Per Market

### Different Keywords Per Language

English "photo editor" might be:
- German: "Bildbearbeitung"
- French: "retouche photo"
- Japanese: "写真編集"

### Process

1. Research top keywords in each language
2. Analyze local competitor keywords
3. Validate with native speakers
4. Test and iterate

---

## Cultural Considerations

### Adapt References

- Idioms don't translate
- Color meanings vary
- Holidays differ
- Reading directions vary

### Tone Adjustments

- Formal vs informal varies by culture
- Humor may not translate
- Direct vs indirect communication
- Professional norms differ

---

## Screenshot Localization

### Must Localize

- All text on screenshots
- Feature callouts
- CTA text

### Consider Adapting

- Imagery for cultural fit
- Device shown (popular local devices)
- Content examples

## Testing Localization

### Quality Checks

- Native speaker review

- Conversion rate monitoring

- Monitor performance by market
- A/B test localized elements

- Update for local trends
