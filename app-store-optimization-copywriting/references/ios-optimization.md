# iOS App Store Optimization

Apple App Store specific optimization strategies.

---

## Character Limits

| Element | Limit | Indexed |
|---------|-------|---------|
| App Name | 30 chars | Yes |
| Subtitle | 30 chars | Yes |
| Keyword Field | 100 chars | Yes (hidden) |
| Promotional Text | 170 chars | No |
| Description | 4,000 chars | No |

---

## Keyword Field Optimization

### Rules

- Separate keywords with commas (no spaces after commas)
- Do not repeat words from title/subtitle
- Do not use plurals if you have singular
- Do not use "app" or category name
- Use only lowercase
- No trademarked competitor terms

### Character Efficiency

**Inefficient** (wastes characters):
```
cooking, recipes, meal planning, meal prep
```

**Efficient**:
```
cooking,meal,prep,planning,dinner,healthy,easy
```

### Strategy

1. Use shortest form of words
2. Combine words that work together
3. Prioritize by search volume

## Subtitle Strategy

### Options

- **Benefit-focused**: "Reduce Stress & Anxiety"
- **Feature-focused**: "Guided Meditations & Sounds"
- **Audience-focused**: "For Beginners & Experts"
- **Differentiator-focused**: "Award-Winning & Science-Backed"

### Best Practices

- Include keywords not in title
- Communicate key benefit
- Complement, don't repeat title
- 30 characters maximum

---

## Promotional Text

### Purpose

- 170 characters that appear above description
- Can be changed without app update
- NOT indexed for search
- Great for timely messaging

### Use Cases

**Seasonal Promotions**:
"Holiday Special: 50% off annual subscription. Limited time only!"

**Feature Launches**:
"NEW: Widgets! Track your goals from your home screen."

**Awards**:
"Apple Design Award Winner 2024. See why millions trust us."

**Social Proof**:
"Just hit 10 million downloads! Join the community today."

---

## Description Writing

### Not Indexed - Focus on Conversion

Since iOS descriptions aren't indexed:
- Write for humans, not algorithms
- Focus on persuasion and clarity
- Use formatting for scannability

### Structure

1. **Hook** (first line visible)
2. **Problem/Solution** statement
3. **Feature bullets** with emojis
4. **Social proof** section
5. **Call to action**

---

## In-App Purchases

### Indexing Benefit

In-app purchase names ARE indexed:
- Use keyword-rich IAP names
- Example: "Premium Meditation Pack" vs "Pack 1"

### Strategy

- Include relevant keywords in IAP titles
- Keep names user-friendly
- Consider search potential
