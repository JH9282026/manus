# ASO Keyword Research

Methods and tools for effective app store keyword research.

---

## Research Methods

### 1. Brainstorming

Start with comprehensive list:
- Core app functions
- User problems solved
- Benefits delivered
- Alternative terms
- Related concepts

### 2. Competitor Analysis

Analyze top competitors:
- What keywords are they targeting?
- What appears in their titles/subtitles?
- What gaps exist?

### 3. Auto-Suggest

Use app store search:
- Type partial terms
- Note suggestions
- Indicates search volume
- Find long-tail opportunities

### 4. User Research

Survey actual users:
- How did you find us?
- What did you search for?
- How would you describe this app?

### 5. Analytics Review

Check current traffic sources:
- Which keywords drive installs?
- Which have high impressions but low conversions?
- Which convert best?

## Keyword Evaluation

### Criteria Matrix

| Keyword | Relevance | Volume | Difficulty | Score |
|---------|-----------|--------|------------|-------|
| [term] | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | Calculate |

### Scoring Guide

- **Relevance**: How closely matches your app
- **Volume**: How many people search
- **Difficulty**: How hard to rank

### Priority Categories

**Primary Keywords**:
- High volume, high relevance
- Target in title and subtitle
- Worth fighting for

**Secondary Keywords**:
- Medium volume, good relevance
- Keyword field (iOS) or description
- Build foundation

**Long-Tail Keywords**:
- Lower volume, very specific
- Higher conversion potential
- Less competition

## Long-Tail Strategy

### Benefits

- Lower competition
- Higher relevance
- Better conversion
- Clearer intent

### Examples

| Short-Tail | Long-Tail |
|------------|-----------|
| meditation | 10 minute morning meditation |
| fitness app | home workout app no equipment |
| photo editor | photo editor with filters free |

### Finding Long-Tail Keywords

1. Add modifiers (free, best, easy)
2. Add specifics (for beginners, at home)
3. Add use cases (for travel, for sleep)
4. Analyze auto-suggest deeply
