# Keyword Research & Strategy

Finding and optimizing the right keywords.

---

## Research Methods

### Brainstorming
- List all relevant terms
- Think like your users
- Consider pain points and solutions

### Competitor Analysis
- Analyze top competitors' keywords
- Identify gaps and opportunities
- Note what's working

### Auto-Suggest
- Type partial terms
- Capture long-tail suggestions
- Note popular searches

### User Research
- Survey users about search behavior
- Check support tickets for language
- Analyze reviews for terms

## Keyword Evaluation

| Criterion | Question |
|-----------|----------|
| Relevance | Does it match your app? |
| Volume | How many searches? |
| Competition | How hard to rank? |
| Intent | Are they looking for apps? |

### Priority Matrix

| Priority | Characteristics |
|----------|----------------|
| Primary | High volume, high relevance |
| Secondary | Medium volume, good fit |
| Long-tail | Lower volume, very specific |

## iOS Keyword Field Optimization

### Efficiency Techniques
- Use shortest word forms
- Combine working words
- No wasted characters
- No punctuation except commas

### Example Optimization

**Inefficient:**
```
cooking, recipes, meal planning, meal prep, dinner ideas
```

**Efficient:**
```
cooking,recipes,meal,prep,planning,dinner,ideas,easy,quick
```
