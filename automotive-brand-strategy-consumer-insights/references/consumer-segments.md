# Automotive Consumer Segments

Psychographic profiles and purchase journey mapping.

---

## Luxury Consumer Segments

### The Achiever

**Profile**:
- Success-driven professional
- Uses vehicle as status symbol
- Values prestige and recognition

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Brand reputation
- Premium features
- Social signaling

**Target Brands**: BMW, Mercedes-Benz

---

### The Enthusiast

**Profile**:
- Driving passion as lifestyle
- Performance-focused decision maker
- Values driving dynamics over luxury

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Performance specs
- Driving experience
- Motorsport heritage

**Target Brands**: BMW M, Porsche

---

### The Connoisseur

**Profile**:
- Appreciates craftsmanship
- Heritage-oriented buyer
- Values authenticity and quality

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Build quality
- Brand heritage
- Exclusivity

**Target Brands**: Mercedes, Porsche

---

### The Innovator

**Profile**:
- Technology early adopter
- Forward-thinking mindset
- Values cutting-edge features

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Latest technology
- EV capability
- Digital features

**Target Brands**: All brands (EV lines)

---

## Mass-Market Consumer Segments

### The Practical Family

**Profile**:
- Safety and reliability focused
- Value-conscious decision maker
- Space and utility priority

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Safety ratings
- Total cost of ownership
- Cargo/passenger capacity

**Target**: Ford/Chevy SUVs

---

### The Working Professional

**Profile**:
- Capability and durability needs
- Uses vehicle for work
- Values toughness and reliability

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Towing capacity
- Durability reputation
- Resale value

**Target**: Truck buyers (F-150, Silverado)

---

### The First-Time Buyer

**Profile**:
- Entering car market
- Budget-constrained
- Values accessibility

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Financing options
- Entry price
- Reliability

**Target**: Chevy entry models

---

### The Value Maximizer

**Profile**:
- Features-per-dollar focused
- Research-intensive buyer
- Negotiates actively

**Purchase Drivers**:
- Feature comparison
- Incentives/deals
- Long-term value

**Target**: Both mass-market brands
