## Campaign Framework

### Luxury Formula

1. **Emotional Connection**: Aspiration, achievement
2. **Heritage/Innovation**: Balance history with future
3. **Exclusivity Signal**: Premium positioning
4. **Experience Focus**: Test drive, events

### Mass-Market Formula

1. **Value Proposition**: What you get for the price
2. **Reliability Message**: Trust and durability
3. **Lifestyle Fit**: How it fits your life
4. **Accessibility**: Easy to own
