## Cultural Considerations

### Messaging Adaptation

| Market | Value Emphasis | Avoid |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| US | Performance, Freedom | Over-formality |
| Germany | Engineering, Quality | Flashiness |
| China | Technology, Prestige | Western assumptions |
| Middle East | Luxury, Status | Restraint messaging |

### Visual Adaptation

- Imagery appropriate to market
- Family vs individual focus
- Professional vs lifestyle
- Climate considerations
