# Financial Analysis & Forecasting

Perform comprehensive financial analysis and build data-driven forecasts that support investment decisions, budgeting, valuation, and strategic financial planning.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for financial statement analysis, ratio computation, trend identification, and forecasting model construction. Cover income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow analysis with forward-looking projection techniques.

## Financial Statement Analysis Framework

| Statement | Key Questions | Primary Metrics |
|---|---|---|
| Income Statement | Is revenue growing? Are margins expanding? | Revenue CAGR, gross/operating/net margin |
| Balance Sheet | Is the company financially healthy? | Current ratio, D/E ratio, ROE, ROA |
| Cash Flow | Is the business generating cash? | FCF, operating cash flow, cash conversion |

## Key Financial Ratios

| Category | Ratio | Formula | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Profitability | Gross Margin | Gross Profit / Revenue | Industry-specific |
| Profitability | Operating Margin | Operating Income / Revenue | Industry-specific |
| Profitability | Net Margin | Net Income / Revenue | Industry-specific |
| Profitability | ROE | Net Income / Shareholders' Equity | >15% strong |
| Liquidity | Current Ratio | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | >1.5 healthy |
| Liquidity | Quick Ratio | (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities | >1.0 |
| Leverage | Debt-to-Equity | Total Debt / Shareholders' Equity | <2.0 moderate |
| Leverage | Interest Coverage | EBIT / Interest Expense | >3.0 comfortable |
| Efficiency | Asset Turnover | Revenue / Total Assets | Industry-specific |
| Efficiency | Inventory Turnover | COGS / Average Inventory | Higher = better |
| Valuation | P/E Ratio | Stock Price / EPS | Relative to peers |
| Valuation | EV/EBITDA | Enterprise Value / EBITDA | Relative to peers |

## Forecasting Methodology

### Revenue Forecasting Approaches

| Method | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Top-down | Market size × market share × price | New markets, strategic planning |
| Bottom-up | Units × price per unit by segment | Established businesses |
| Trend-based | Historical CAGR extrapolation | Stable, mature businesses |
| Driver-based | Key business drivers model | Granular operational planning |
| Cohort-based | Customer cohort behavior | Subscription/SaaS businesses |

### Standard Forecast Structure
1. Revenue by segment/product/geography
2. Cost of goods sold (variable + fixed)
3. Operating expenses by category
4. EBITDA and operating income
5. Interest, taxes, and net income
6. Working capital changes
7. Capital expenditures
8. Free cash flow
