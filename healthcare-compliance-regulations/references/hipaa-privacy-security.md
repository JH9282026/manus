# HIPAA, GDPR & Data Privacy

## HIPAA Privacy Rule
- Applies to covered entities and business associates
- Protects PHI; requires NPP, patient access rights, minimum necessary
- Authorization required for marketing, sale of PHI, psychotherapy notes

## HIPAA Security Rule
- Administrative: risk analysis, training, access management
- Physical: facility controls, workstation security, device controls
- Technical: access controls, audit logs, encryption, transmission security
- Annual risk analysis mandatory

## Breach Notification
- Notify individuals within 60 days
- HHS immediately for 500+; annually for smaller
- Media for 500+ in a state
- Document risk assessment

## GDPR Compliance
- Lawful basis required for processing EU/EEA personal data
- DPIA for high-risk processing
- SCCs or adequacy decisions for transfers
- Post-Schrems II: assess destination protections
- Appoint DPO for large-scale health data

## CMS Conditions of Participation
- Patient rights, quality assessment, infection control, records, staffing
- Enforced via state surveys and deemed status organizations
