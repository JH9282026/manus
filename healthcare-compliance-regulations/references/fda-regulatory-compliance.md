# FDA & Clinical Research

## FDA Inspection Preparation
1. Assess GMP/GLP/GCP compliance
2. Review recent warning letters in area
3. Prepare document response templates
4. Train staff on investigator protocols
5. Mock inspection on focus areas
- Focus: data integrity (ALCOA+), process validation, supply chain, CAPA, lab controls

## Digital Health Regulatory Strategy
- Classify under SaMD framework (IMDRF risk categories)
- Evaluate predetermined change control plan pathway
- Identify clinical evidence requirements
- Develop risk-based QMS

## Clinical Trial Regulations
- Part 50: informed consent
- Part 56: IRB requirements
- Part 312: IND applications, safety reporting
- Part 812: IDE requirements
- Part 11: electronic records/signatures

## Quality Management System
- Document control, CAPA system, training records
- Internal audits, management review
- Supplier qualification, change control
