# Healthcare Compliance & Regulations

Navigate the complex healthcare regulatory landscape to ensure compliance across multiple jurisdictions and frameworks.

## Overview

This skill monitors regulatory developments, interprets requirements, assesses compliance status, and generates implementation guidance across FDA, HIPAA, HITECH, GDPR, CMS, and state regulations.

## Domain Selection

| Domain | Key Regulations | Risk | Reference |
|--------|----------------|------|-----------|
| Patient Privacy | HIPAA Privacy/Security | $100-$50K/violation | `/references/hipaa-privacy-security.md` |
| Drug/Device Safety | FDA 21 CFR | Warning letters, prosecution | `/references/fda-regulatory-compliance.md` |
| EU Data Protection | GDPR | Up to 4% global revenue | `/references/hipaa-privacy-security.md` |
| Clinical Research | FDA Parts 50, 56, 312, 812 | Clinical hold, debarment | `/references/fda-regulatory-compliance.md` |
| Quality Standards | CMS Conditions of Participation | Loss of certification | `/references/hipaa-privacy-security.md` |

## Assessment Process
1. Scope: identify applicable frameworks
2. Inventory: document current controls
3. Gap analysis: compare against requirements
4. Risk rank: prioritize by likelihood and impact
5. Remediate: corrective action plans with owners
6. Monitor: ongoing compliance reporting
