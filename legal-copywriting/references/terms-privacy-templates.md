# Terms of Service & Privacy Policy Templates

## Terms of Service Structure

### Essential Sections

#### 1. Acceptance of Terms
- Clear statement that using the service = accepting terms
- Age requirement specification (typically 13 or 18)
- Authority to bind organization (for business users)

#### 2. Service Description
- What the service provides
- Service availability and uptime commitments
- Geographic restrictions

#### 3. User Accounts
- Registration requirements
- Account security responsibilities
- Account termination conditions

#### 4. Acceptable Use
- Permitted activities
- Prohibited activities (detailed list)
- Content standards for user-generated content

#### 5. Intellectual Property
- Company IP ownership statement
- User content license grant
- DMCA/copyright procedures

#### 6. Payment Terms (if applicable)
- Pricing and billing cycles
- Refund policy reference
- Price change notification requirements
- Auto-renewal disclosure

#### 7. Limitation of Liability
- Disclaimer of warranties (AS IS)
- Limitation of damages
- Exclusion of consequential damages
- Cap on total liability

#### 8. Dispute Resolution
- Governing law specification
- Arbitration clause (if applicable)
- Class action waiver (jurisdiction-dependent)
- Venue and jurisdiction

#### 9. Termination
- User termination rights
- Company termination rights
- Effect of termination on data
- Survival clause

## Privacy Policy Structure

### GDPR-Compliant Sections

#### 1. Information We Collect
**Personal Data Categories**:
- Identity data (name, username, DOB)
- Contact data (email, phone, address)
- Technical data (IP, browser, device)
- Usage data (pages visited, features used)
- Financial data (payment details)
- Marketing data (preferences, consents)

**Collection Methods**:
- Direct interactions (forms, accounts)
- Automated technologies (cookies, analytics)
- Third-party sources (social logins, data providers)

#### 2. How We Use Your Information
Map each use to a lawful basis:
| Purpose | Lawful Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Service delivery | Contract performance |
| Account management | Contract performance |
| Payment processing | Contract performance |
| Marketing emails | Consent |
| Analytics | Legitimate interest |
| Legal compliance | Legal obligation |
| Fraud prevention | Legitimate interest |

#### 3. Data Sharing
- Service providers and processors
- Legal requirements and law enforcement
- Business transfers (M&A)
- With user consent

#### 4. Data Subject Rights (GDPR)
- Right of access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object
- Rights related to automated decision-making
- How to exercise rights
- Response timeframe (30 days)

#### 5. Data Retention
- Retention periods by data category
- Criteria for determining retention
- Deletion procedures

#### 6. International Transfers
- Transfer mechanisms (SCCs, BCRs)
- Adequacy decisions relied upon
- Safeguards in place

## Cookie Policy Template

### Required Sections
1. What cookies are (plain-language explanation)
2. Types of cookies used:
   - Strictly necessary (no consent needed)
   - Performance/analytics
   - Functionality
   - Targeting/advertising
3. Specific cookies table (name, provider, purpose, expiry)
4. Third-party cookies
5. How to manage cookies (browser settings, opt-out links)
6. Consent mechanism description
7. Contact information
