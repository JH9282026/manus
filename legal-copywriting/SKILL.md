# Legal Copywriting

Draft accessible, compliant legal documents that protect organizations while remaining user-friendly.

## Overview

Create legal documents at the intersection of law, communication, and UX. Balance legal precision with plain-language readability to meet regulatory requirements (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, LGPD) while building user trust.

## Core Principles

1. **Plain language first** — Write at 8th-grade reading level, then layer legal precision
2. **Regulatory compliance** — Map each section to applicable regulations
3. **Layered disclosure** — Summary → Key points → Full legal text
4. **Consistent structure** — Use headers, numbered lists, and tables for scanability
5. **Brand voice** — Maintain organizational tone even in legal contexts
6. **Version control** — Date all documents, maintain change logs

## Document Structure Pattern

Every legal document should follow this hierarchy:
1. **Title and effective date**
2. **Plain-language summary** (1-2 paragraphs)
3. **Key terms table** (definitions)
4. **Substantive sections** (numbered for reference)
5. **User rights section**
6. **Contact information**
7. **Last updated date**

## Regulatory Compliance Checklist

| Regulation | Key Requirement | Document Impact |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|
| GDPR | Lawful basis, data subject rights | Privacy Policy must detail all 8 rights |
| CCPA | Right to know, delete, opt-out | Privacy Policy needs CA-specific section |
| PIPEDA | Consent, accountability | Privacy Policy must name DPO |
| ePrivacy | Cookie consent | Separate Cookie Policy required |
| CAN-SPAM | Unsubscribe mechanism | Email terms must include opt-out |
