# CRE Property Type Strategies

Specialized strategies for each major commercial property type.

---

## Office Properties

### Market Dynamics
- Hybrid work has reset office demand permanently (10-20% lower than pre-2020)
- Flight to quality — Class A/A+ properties outperforming
- Amenity-rich buildings commanding premium rents
- Suburban office recovering as satellite/flex space

### Strategy Considerations
- Invest in amenities (fitness, food, outdoor space, conferencing)
- Flexible lease structures (shorter terms, expansion/contraction options)
- Technology infrastructure (high-speed connectivity, smart building systems)
- ESG and wellness certifications increasingly important
- Conversion feasibility assessment for underperforming assets

---

## Retail Properties

### Market Dynamics
- E-commerce continues shifting spending but physical retail remains 80%+ of total
- Experiential retail, food/beverage, and services gaining share
- Grocery-anchored centers most resilient
- Outlet and luxury retail performing well

### Strategy Considerations
- Curate experiential tenant mix (entertainment, dining, fitness, services)
- Integrate omnichannel capabilities (BOPIS, curbside, ship-from-store)
- Mixed-use redevelopment for underperforming enclosed malls
- Outparcel development for pad sites
- Percentage rent structures for high-performing retail tenants

---

## Industrial Properties

### Market Dynamics
- E-commerce driving sustained demand for logistics space
- Last-mile delivery facilities in high demand near population centers
- Rent growth moderating from peak but still above historical averages
- Cold storage and specialized facilities commanding premium rents

### Strategy Considerations
- Location near population centers and transportation infrastructure
- Clear height (32-40 ft.) and dock configuration for modern logistics
- Trailer parking and yard space increasingly valuable
- Speculative development feasible in supply-constrained markets
- ESG features (solar, EV charging, LED) increasingly expected

---

## Multifamily Properties

### Market Dynamics
- Housing affordability driving rental demand
- Supply pipeline elevated in many markets
- Rent growth moderating after post-pandemic surge
- Build-to-rent (BTR) single-family emerging as alternative

### Strategy Considerations
- Value-add renovation programs (kitchen, bath, common areas)
- Technology amenities (smart home, package lockers, EV charging)
- Revenue management systems for dynamic pricing
- Pet-friendly policies and amenities (increasing tenant pool)
- Workforce housing strategies for suburban markets
