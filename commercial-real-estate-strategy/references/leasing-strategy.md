# Leasing Strategy

## Lease Analysis Framework

### Key Lease Economics

| Metric | Calculation | Use |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| Face rent | Stated rent per SF | Headline number |
| Effective rent | NPV of cash flows / term | True economics |
| TI/LC | Tenant improvement + commission | Upfront cost |
| Free rent | Months of abatement | Concession value |
| Net effective rent | Effective rent - TI/LC | Bottom line |

### Mark-to-Market Analysis
1. Compare in-place rents to market
2. Identify upside at rollover
3. Calculate roll risk for above-market
4. Factor in lease-up costs

## Tenant Mix Strategy

### Mix Considerations

| Factor | Office | Retail |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Credit quality | Diversification | Anchor stability |
| Size mix | Large vs. small tenants | Anchor, in-line, pad |
| Industry | Diversification | Synergy, traffic |
| Term | Stagger expirations | Anchor long-term |

### Optimal Mix Principles
- Balance credit quality with rent potential
- Diversify to reduce concentration risk
- Create synergy (retail) or avoid conflict
- Stagger lease expirations
- Match tenant to building positioning

## Deal Structuring

### Lease Term Negotiation

| Element | Landlord Preference | Tenant Preference |
|---------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Term length | Longer | Shorter/flexible |
| Escalations | Higher, annual | Lower, fixed |
| Options | Fewer, at market | More, fixed rate |
| TI allowance | Lower | Higher |
| Free rent | Less | More |

### Concession Guidelines
- TI: Based on credit, term, rent
- Free rent: Trade-off with TI
- Commissions: Market standard
- Cap exposure on unrated tenants
- Require guarantees when appropriate

## Rollover Management

### Rollover Risk Assessment
1. Map lease expirations by year
2. Identify concentration risk
3. Assess renewal probability
4. Model rollover scenarios
5. Plan proactive renewals

### Renewal Strategy
- Engage tenants 12-18 months early
- Understand tenant needs and alternatives
- Prepare competitive proposals
- Balance retention with rent growth
- Know walk-away point
