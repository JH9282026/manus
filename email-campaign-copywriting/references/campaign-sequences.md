# Email Campaign Sequences

## Welcome Series (E-commerce)

### Email 1: Instant Welcome (Day 0)
- Subject: "Welcome to [Brand] — here's your [incentive]"
- Content: Thank them, deliver promised incentive, set expectations
- CTA: Shop now with discount code

### Email 2: Brand Story (Day 1-2)
- Subject: "Why we started [Brand]"
- Content: Origin story, mission, values — build emotional connection
- CTA: Learn more about us / browse bestsellers

### Email 3: Social Proof (Day 3-4)
- Subject: "See why [number] customers love [Brand]"
- Content: Customer reviews, testimonials, user-generated content
- CTA: Shop customer favorites

### Email 4: Education (Day 5-7)
- Subject: "How to get the most from [product category]"
- Content: Usage tips, styling guides, how-to content
- CTA: Explore the collection

### Email 5: Urgency (Day 7-10)
- Subject: "Your [incentive] expires soon"
- Content: Reminder of welcome offer with deadline
- CTA: Use your discount before it expires

## Cart Abandonment Sequence

### Email 1: Reminder (1 hour post-abandonment)
- Subject: "You left something behind"
- Content: Show cart items with images and prices
- CTA: Complete your purchase
- Tone: Helpful, no pressure

### Email 2: Overcome Objections (24 hours)
- Subject: "Still thinking it over?"
- Content: Address common concerns — reviews, guarantee, shipping info
- CTA: Return to cart

### Email 3: Incentive (48-72 hours)
- Subject: "We saved your cart + a little something extra"
- Content: Offer discount or free shipping, create scarcity
- CTA: Complete purchase with [incentive]

## B2B Cold Outreach Sequence

### Email 1: Value-First Introduction
- Subject: "[Specific insight about their company]"
- Keep under 100 words
- Reference specific company detail (shows research)
- One clear value proposition
- Soft CTA: "Would it make sense to chat for 15 minutes?"

### Email 2: Social Proof Follow-Up (Day 3)
- Subject: "How [similar company] achieved [result]"
- Brief case study or result
- Connect their situation to the success story
- CTA: "Want to see how this could work for you?"

### Email 3: Different Angle (Day 7)
- Subject: "Quick question about [their challenge]"
- Ask an insightful question about their business
- Share a relevant insight or resource
- CTA: "Curious if you've thought about this approach?"

### Email 4: Breakup Email (Day 14)
- Subject: "Should I close your file?"
- Keep very short (2-3 sentences)
- Acknowledge they're busy, no hard feelings
- One final CTA with easy response option (yes/no)

## Product Launch Sequence

### Pre-Launch Phase (7-14 days before)
1. **Teaser**: "Something big is coming" — build curiosity
2. **Reveal**: Show the product with key benefits
3. **Social proof**: Early reviews, beta tester feedback

### Launch Phase (Launch day + 3 days)
4. **Launch announcement**: "It's here!" with full details and early-bird offer
5. **Deep dive**: Detailed features, use cases, demo video
6. **Urgency**: "Launch pricing ends in [X] hours"

### Post-Launch (Days 4-14)
7. **Results**: Customer success stories from early adopters
8. **FAQ**: Address common questions and objections
9. **Last chance**: Final call for any introductory offer
