# Talent Acquisition Technology and Analytics

## Overview

Technology and data analytics are transforming talent acquisition, enabling more efficient processes, better candidate experiences, and data-driven decision-making. This guide explores the key technologies, tools, and analytics approaches that modern talent acquisition teams leverage for competitive advantage.

## Talent Acquisition Analytics

### Descriptive Analytics

Understanding what happened.

**Key Metrics:**

**Efficiency Metrics:**
- **Time-to-Fill:** Days from requisition to offer acceptance
- **Time-to-Hire:** Days from application to offer acceptance
- **Cost-per-Hire:** Total recruitment costs / number of hires
- **Applicants per Opening:** Total applications / open positions

**Quality Metrics:**
- **Quality of Hire:** Performance ratings, hiring manager satisfaction
- **Offer Acceptance Rate:** Offers accepted / offers extended
- **New Hire Retention:** Percentage retained after 90 days, 1 year
- **Hiring Manager Satisfaction:** Survey feedback on process and quality

**Source Effectiveness:**
- **Source of Hire:** Which channels produce hires
- **Source Quality:** Performance by source
- **Source Cost:** Cost per hire by source
- **Source Diversity:** Diversity metrics by source

**Pipeline Metrics:**
- **Conversion Rates:** Percentage advancing at each stage
- **Drop-off Points:** Where candidates exit process
- **Interview-to-Offer Ratio:** Offers / interviews conducted
- **Qualified Candidates per Opening:** Qualified applicants / positions

**Diversity Metrics:**
- **Diversity of Applicant Pool:** Demographics of applicants
- **Diversity at Each Stage:** Conversion rates by demographic
- **Diversity of Hires:** Demographics of new hires
- **Representation Goals:** Progress toward diversity targets

**Reporting:**
- Executive dashboards
- Recruiter scorecards
- Hiring manager reports
- Source effectiveness reports
- Diversity and inclusion reports

### Diagnostic Analytics

Understanding why something happened.

**Analysis Techniques:**

**Funnel Analysis:**
- Conversion rates at each stage
- Identify bottlenecks
- Compare across roles, sources, teams
- Root cause investigation

**Cohort Analysis:**
- Group candidates by characteristics
- Compare performance and outcomes
- Identify patterns and trends
- Inform strategy adjustments

**Correlation Analysis:**
- Relationship between variables
- Predictive factors for success
- Assessment validation
- Process optimization

**Benchmarking:**
- Compare to industry standards
- Identify gaps and opportunities
- Set realistic targets
- Track improvement over time

### Predictive Analytics

Predicting future outcomes.

**Applications:**

**Quality of Hire Prediction:**

**Attrition Risk:**

**Demand Forecasting:**

**Process Optimization:**
