# Affiliate Marketing (Advanced)

Scale revenue through strategic affiliate partnerships and performance-based marketing.

## Overview

Advanced affiliate marketing involves building and optimizing performance-based partnerships where affiliates earn commissions for driving sales, leads, or other desired actions.

## Affiliate Program Strategy

### Program Models

**Pay-Per-Sale (PPS)**:
- Commission on completed sales
- Most common model
- Typical rates: 5-30% of sale
- Best for: Ecommerce, SaaS

**Pay-Per-Lead (PPL)**:
- Commission for qualified leads
- Fixed amount per lead
- Typical rates: $5-$100+ per lead
- Best for: B2B, financial services, insurance

**Pay-Per-Click (PPC)**:
- Payment for clicks
- Lower rates, easier to achieve
- Risk of low-quality traffic
- Less common today

**Hybrid Models**:
- Combination of above
- Base fee + performance bonus
- Tiered commissions
- Recurring commissions (subscriptions)

### Commission Structure Design

**Factors to Consider**:
- Product margins
- Customer lifetime value
- Competitive rates
- Affiliate effort required
- Attribution window

**Tiered Commissions**:
```
Tier 1 (0-10 sales/month): 10%
Tier 2 (11-25 sales/month): 15%
Tier 3 (26-50 sales/month): 20%
Tier 4 (51+ sales/month): 25%
```

**Recurring Commissions** (SaaS):
- First month: 30%
- Months 2-12: 10%
- Lifetime: 5%

**Performance Bonuses**:
- Monthly sales targets
- New customer bonuses
- Seasonal promotions
- Exclusive deals

- Largest product selection
- Trusted brand
- 1-10% commissions
- 24-hour cookie
- Best for: Product reviews, content sites

**CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)**:

- Premium publishers
- Global reach
- High-quality traffic
- Higher costs
- Best for: Established brands

**Impact.com**:
- Modern platform
- Partnership automation
- Advanced tracking
- Influencer integration

## Fraud Prevention

### Common Fraud Types

**Cookie Stuffing**:
- Placing cookies without user action
- Inflating affiliate credit
- Detection: Unusual conversion patterns

**Click Fraud**:
- Fake clicks to earn PPC commissions
- Bot traffic
- Detection: Click-to-conversion ratio

**Trademark Bidding**:
- Bidding on brand keywords (if prohibited)
- Stealing branded traffic
- Detection: Paid search monitoring

**Fake Leads**:
- Submitting false information
- Low-quality leads
- Detection: Lead validation

**Coupon Code Abuse**:
- Unauthorized code distribution
- Last-click attribution gaming
- Detection: Code usage monitoring

### Prevention Strategies

**Technical**:
- Fraud detection software
- IP address monitoring
- Conversion validation
- Lead verification
- Traffic quality analysis

**Policy**:
- Clear terms and conditions
- Prohibited practices list
- Trademark bidding rules
- Coupon code guidelines
- Penalties for violations

**Monitoring**:
- Regular audits
- Conversion pattern analysis
- Traffic source review
- Affiliate activity monitoring
- Customer feedback

**Tools**:
- Anura
- ClickCease
- Fraudlogix
- Network built-in tools

## Attribution and Tracking

### Attribution Models

**Last-Click**:
- Credit to final touchpoint
- Most common in affiliate
- Simple but incomplete

**First-Click**:
- Credit to initial touchpoint
- Rewards awareness drivers

**Linear**:
- Equal credit to all touchpoints
- Fair but may overvalue minor touches

**Time-Decay**:
- More credit to recent touchpoints
- Balances journey

**Position-Based (U-Shaped)**:
- 40% first, 40% last, 20% middle
- Values awareness and conversion

### Cookie Duration

**Standard Durations**:
- 24 hours: Amazon Associates
- 30 days: Common for ecommerce
- 60-90 days: Higher-value products
- 120+ days: B2B, long sales cycles
- Lifetime: Subscription services (first purchase)

**Considerations**:
- Sales cycle length
- Competitive landscape
- Product consideration time
- Customer journey complexity
