## Video Accessibility and SEO


### Accessibility Improves SEO

Accessible video practices often improve SEO:
- Captions provide text for indexing
- Transcripts add indexable content
- Audio descriptions add context
- Alt text for thumbnails aids discovery


### Accessibility Best Practices

**Captions and Subtitles**:
- Accurate, synced closed captions
- Speaker identification
- Sound effect descriptions

**Audio Descriptions**:
- Describe visual information for blind viewers
- Include in educational/instructional content

**Color and Visual Accessibility**:
- Sufficient contrast in thumbnails
- Don't rely solely on color for information
- Clear, readable text

**Transcripts**:
- Full text version of video content
- Include speaker identification
- Describe relevant visual elements
