# Youtube Studio Analytics

Detailed reference content for the Video Seo Optimization skill.

---

## YouTube Studio Analytics


### Key Analytics to Monitor

**Overview Metrics**:
- Views
- Watch time (hours)
- Subscribers (gained/lost)
- Estimated revenue (if monetized)

**Reach Metrics**:
- Impressions
- Impressions click-through rate
- Traffic sources

**Engagement Metrics**:
- Average view duration
- Average percentage viewed
- Likes, dislikes, comments

**Audience Metrics**:
- Unique viewers
- Returning viewers
- Subscription source
- Demographics


### Using Analytics for Optimization

**Identify Top Performers**:
- Which videos have highest CTR?
- Which have best retention?
- Which drive most subscribers?
- Replicate success factors

**Identify Improvement Opportunities**:
- Low CTR with good retention = thumbnail/title issue
- Good CTR with low retention = content doesn't match promise
- Specific drop-off points = content issues at those moments

**Audience Insights**:
- When are your viewers online? (posting schedule)
- What other channels do they watch? (content ideas)
- What are the demographics? (content customization)


### Custom Analytics Reports

Create monitoring routines:
- Weekly content performance review
- Monthly channel health check
- Quarterly strategy assessment
- Annual trend analysis
