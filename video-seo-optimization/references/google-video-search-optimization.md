## Google Video Search Optimization


### How Videos Rank in Google

Google shows videos in:
- Main search results (video carousel or featured)
- Dedicated Video tab
- Image search
- News results
- Discover feed

**Ranking Factors**:
- YouTube SEO signals (watch time, engagement)
- Traditional SEO signals (relevance, authority)
- Video-specific signals (thumbnails, chapters)
- Source authority (YouTube videos often preferred)


### Optimizing for Google Video Search

**YouTube Advantage**: YouTube videos have inherent advantage in Google video search

**Additional Optimization**:
- Create content for queries where Google shows video results
- Optimize for Google Featured Snippets format
- Include transcripts for text indexing
- Build backlinks to YouTube videos
- Embed videos on authoritative websites


### Video Featured Snippets

Google may show video results as featured snippets:
- "How to" queries often trigger video snippets
- Video chapter timestamps can appear
- Thumbnail and title shown prominently

**Optimization for Featured Snippets**:
- Create clear, structured how-to content
- Use chapters with keyword-rich titles
- Provide direct answers to common questions
- Include transcript for Google to extract answers
