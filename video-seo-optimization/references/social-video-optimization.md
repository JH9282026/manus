## Social Video Optimization


### Platform-Specific Optimization

Each social platform has different optimization requirements:


### TikTok Optimization

**Discovery Factors**:
- Completion rate and rewatch rate
- Engagement velocity (likes/comments/shares in first hour)
- Account relevance to content category
- Use of trending sounds and hashtags

**Optimization Tactics**:
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- Encourage rewatches (loop-worthy content)
- Use trending sounds early in trend lifecycle
- Strategic hashtag usage (3-5 relevant)
- Post during peak audience hours


### Instagram Reels Optimization

**Discovery Factors**:
- Account category and content relevance
- Engagement metrics
- Use of Instagram native features
- Audio trends

**Optimization Tactics**:
- Use native editing features
- Trending audio integration
- Strong opening hook
- Hashtag strategy (5-10)
- Cross-promote to Stories and Feed


### Facebook Video Optimization

**Discovery Factors**:
- Watch time and completion
- Engagement (reactions, comments, shares)
- Page category and audience relevance
- Native upload vs. link sharing

**Optimization Tactics**:
- Always upload native (not YouTube links)
- Design for sound-off (captions essential)
- First 3 seconds critical for autoplay
- Optimize for Facebook's audience demographics


### LinkedIn Video Optimization

**Discovery Factors**:
- Profile and content relevance
- Engagement velocity
- Professional audience targeting
- Native video preference

**Optimization Tactics**:
- Professional but authentic content
- Caption everything (office viewing)
- Strong first frame for feed preview
- Business-relevant topics
- Employee advocacy distribution
