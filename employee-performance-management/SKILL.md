# Employee Performance Management

Design, execute, and optimize performance management systems that drive organizational effectiveness through goal alignment, continuous feedback, and development planning.

## Overview

Provide frameworks for modern performance management covering goal-setting methodologies (OKR, SMART, MBO), review cycle design, calibration processes, feedback systems, and performance improvement plans. Balance accountability with development focus.

## Goal-Setting Frameworks

| Framework | Structure | Best For | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| OKRs | Objective + 3-5 Key Results | Innovation, alignment | Quarterly |
| SMART Goals | Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound | Individual targets | Annual/Quarterly |
| MBO | Management by Objectives cascade | Hierarchical orgs | Annual |
| KPIs | Key Performance Indicators | Operational roles | Ongoing |
| Balanced Scorecard | Financial, Customer, Process, Learning | Strategic alignment | Annual |

## Performance Review Cycle Design

| Element | Traditional | Modern |
|---|---|---|
| Frequency | Annual | Quarterly + continuous |
| Focus | Past performance | Future development |
| Ratings | 5-point scale | No ratings or simplified |
| Feedback | Manager only | Multi-source (360°) |
| Documentation | Paper forms | Digital platform |
| Calibration | Forced ranking | Relative calibration |

## Rating Scale Options

| Scale | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| 5-point | Familiar, granular | Central tendency bias |
| 3-point | Forces differentiation | May feel limiting |
| No rating | Reduces anxiety, focuses on growth | Hard to tie to compensation |
| Narrative only | Rich, contextual | Time-intensive, inconsistent |

## Performance Conversation Framework

1. **Prepare** — Review goals, gather data, solicit self-assessment
2. **Acknowledge** — Start with strengths and contributions
3. **Assess** — Discuss performance against goals with specific examples
4. **Develop** — Identify growth areas and development actions
5. **Align** — Set forward-looking goals and expectations
6. **Document** — Record discussion, agreements, and next steps
