### Key Differences from Other Formats

**vs. Blog Posts/Articles:**
- Must sound conversational, not academic
- Shorter sentences and simpler vocabulary
- Repetition is acceptable and often necessary
- Can't use visual formatting (bold, bullets, headers)

**vs. Video Scripts:**
- No visual demonstrations to rely on
- Must paint pictures with words alone
- Pacing depends entirely on audio elements
- Transitions are purely auditory

**vs. Radio Broadcasting:**
- Generally longer-form content
- More intimate, personal tone
- On-demand listening changes structure
- Greater creative freedom

**vs. Speeches:**
- Lacks live audience feedback
- Can be edited and refined
- Often includes multiple voices
- Sound design enhances content
