### Scene Writing

Write individual scenes with clear purpose and sensory detail.

**Scene Elements:**
- Setting description (for sound design)
- Character introduction
- Dialogue
- Action description
- Emotional beats
- Transition to next scene

**Example Scene Script:**
```
SCENE 3: THE WAREHOUSE - NIGHT

[SFX: Rain on metal roof, distant thunder]

NARRATOR: The warehouse had been abandoned for years. Sarah pushed through the rusted door, her flashlight cutting through the darkness.

[SFX: Metal door creaking]

SARAH: (whispering) Hello? Is anyone here?

[SFX: Footsteps on concrete, echo]

NARRATOR: The beam of light found something in the corner. Something that shouldn't be there.

[MUSIC: Tension building]
```
