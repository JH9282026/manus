### Cold Open/Teaser

A compelling opening that hooks listeners before the main introduction. Usually 30-90 seconds of engaging content.

**Cold Open Types:**
- Audio clip from later in episode
- Provocative question or statement
- Dramatic scene opening
- Surprising fact or statistic
- Guest quote preview

**Example Cold Open:**
```
[AUDIO CLIP]
"And that's when I realized—everything I'd believed about success was completely wrong."
[MUSIC STING]
That's bestselling author James Clear. And today, he's going to challenge everything you think you know about building habits.
```
