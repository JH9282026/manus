### Music Cues

Script music as integral content element.

**Music Cue Format:**
```
[MUSIC: Upbeat theme, 10 seconds, fade under]

HOST: Welcome to the show...

[MUSIC: Fade out]
```

**Music Types:**
- Theme music (open/close)
- Transition stings
- Underscore (beneath narration)
- Emotional enhancement
- Period-appropriate (historical content)
