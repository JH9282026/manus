### Fully Scripted

Every word is written out and read verbatim. Best for narrative podcasts, scripted fiction, and content requiring precision.

**When to Use:**
- Legal or medical information
- Narrative storytelling
- Fiction podcasts
- Complex explanations
- Sponsored content

**Pros:**
- Complete control over content
- Consistent quality
- Efficient recording
- Easy to time

**Cons:**
- Can sound stiff if not practiced
- Less spontaneous
- Requires more prep time
- May lose natural energy
