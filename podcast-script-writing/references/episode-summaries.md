### Episode Summaries

Write compelling summaries that drive listens.

**Summary Elements:**
- Episode topic (clear and specific)
- Key guests or featured content
- What listeners will learn
- Teaser without spoilers
- Keywords for searchability

**Example Summary:**
```
How do you build a million-dollar business while working a full-time job? In this episode, serial entrepreneur Marcus Chen shares his exact strategy for launching three successful companies on the side. You'll learn his "5-hour founder" framework, the biggest mistakes part-time entrepreneurs make, and how he eventually made the leap to full-time. Plus, Marcus reveals the one book that changed his entire approach to business.
```
