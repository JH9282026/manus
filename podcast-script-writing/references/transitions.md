### Transitions

Bridges between sections that maintain flow and signal changes to listeners.

**Transition Types:**
- Musical stings or bumpers
- Verbal bridges ("Now let's shift to...")
- Sound effects
- Segment introductions
- Summary and preview

**Example Transitions:**
- "That brings us to our next topic..."
- "But here's where it gets interesting..."
- "Now, you might be wondering..."
- [MUSIC TRANSITION] "Welcome back..."
