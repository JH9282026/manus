# Ethical AI Principles

## Overview

Ethical AI development requires considering societal impact, fairness, transparency, and accountability.

---

## Core Ethical Principles

**Beneficence**: AI should benefit humanity.

**Non-Maleficence**: AI should not harm.

**Autonomy**: Respect human agency.

**Justice**: AI should be fair and equitable.

**Explicability**: AI decisions should be explainable.

**Accountability**: Clear responsibility for AI outcomes.
