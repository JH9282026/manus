# Healthcare, Financial, Technology & Legal

## Healthcare
- Conference: IMRaD format; 8-15 min oral, 45-60 min grand rounds
- Visuals: clean, no clipart; forest plots, Kaplan-Meier, CONSORT diagrams
- HIPAA: no identifiers; de-identify; obscure faces
- Patient education: plain language (6th-8th grade), 24pt+ text

## Financial Services
- Earnings: highlights, YoY/QoQ, segments, guidance, non-GAAP reconciliations
- Investment: thesis, market, financials, projections, risks, use of funds
- Disclosures: past performance disclaimers, risk factors, safe harbor
- Visuals: navy/gray, waterfall charts, no truncated axes

## Technology
- Demos: problem -> solution -> live demo -> features -> pricing
- Architecture: C4 model, UML, system flows
- Code: syntax highlighting, 24pt+, incremental reveal
- Roadmaps: themes not features externally; relative timing

## Legal
- Courtroom: court-approved equipment, evidence authentication
- Design: 36pt+ text, black on white, sequential evidence
- Client: plain language, risk in business terms, action items
