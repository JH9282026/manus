# Clinical Research & Analysis

Analyze clinical trial data and medical research to generate actionable healthcare insights.

## Overview

This skill provides systematic evaluation of clinical trials, observational studies, and medical research programs. Process complex clinical datasets, protocol documentation, and regulatory submissions to drive evidence-based healthcare decisions and pharmaceutical development.

## Analysis Type Selection

| Research Context | Analysis Type | Key Methods | Reference |
|------------------|---------------|-------------|----------|
| Phase III trial completion | Efficacy/safety analysis | ITT, Kaplan-Meier | `/references/statistical-methods.md` |
| Multiple study synthesis | Meta-analysis | Random effects, heterogeneity | `/references/meta-analysis.md` |
| Post-market monitoring | Pharmacovigilance | Signal detection, PRR | `/references/safety-analysis.md` |
| Regulatory submission | Integrated summaries | ISS, ISE, CSR | `/references/regulatory-standards.md` |
| HTA evaluation | Comparative effectiveness | NMA, cost-effectiveness | `/references/meta-analysis.md` |

## Core Competencies

### Clinical Trial Analysis
- Process Phase I-IV trial datasets
- Conduct intention-to-treat and per-protocol analyses
- Perform survival analysis (Kaplan-Meier, Cox models)
- Evaluate primary and secondary endpoints
- Assess subgroup effects

### Safety Analysis
- Analyze adverse event data
- Detect safety signals
- Calculate incidence and relative risk
- Prepare safety summaries
- Conduct benefit-risk assessments

### Meta-Analysis
- Conduct systematic literature reviews
- Extract and synthesize study data
- Perform fixed and random effects modeling
- Assess heterogeneity (I², Q-test)
- Generate forest plots and funnel plots

### Regulatory Documentation
- Prepare clinical study reports (ICH E3)
- Create integrated summaries (ISS, ISE)
- Develop benefit-risk frameworks
- Support IND/NDA submissions
- Prepare periodic safety reports

## Key Deliverables

### Analysis Reports
- Statistical Analysis Report with efficacy/safety summaries
- Tabulated results (TFLs: Tables, Figures, Listings)
- Forest plots for meta-analyses
- Kaplan-Meier survival curves
- CONSORT flow diagrams

### Regulatory Documents
- Clinical Study Report (CSR) sections
- Integrated Summary of Safety (ISS)
- Integrated Summary of Efficacy (ISE)
- Periodic Safety Update Reports (PSURs)
- Benefit-risk assessment frameworks

### Quality Metrics
- Data quality assessment scores
- Protocol deviation summaries
- Site performance analytics
- Query resolution metrics

## Study Design Assessment

| Design | Strengths | Limitations | Key Considerations |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| RCT (double-blind) | Gold standard for efficacy | Cost, generalizability | Randomization quality |
| Open-label | Practical, ethical | Bias risk | Blinded endpoint assessment |
| Observational | Real-world data, large N | Confounding | Adjustment methods |
| Cross-over | Within-subject comparison | Carryover effects | Washout adequacy |
