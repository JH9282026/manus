# Packaging Design Fundamentals

## Overview

Packaging design balances aesthetics, functionality, and brand communication. Effective packaging protects the product, attracts customers, and communicates brand values.

---

## Packaging Functions

**Protection**: Primary function is protecting product during shipping, storage, and handling.

**Information**: Communicate product details, ingredients, instructions, warnings.

**Brand Communication**: Packaging is often first touchpoint with customer.

**Differentiation**: Stand out on shelf among competitors.

**Convenience**: Easy to open, use, store, and dispose.

**Sustainability**: Environmental impact increasingly important.
