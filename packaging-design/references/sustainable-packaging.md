# Sustainable Packaging Design

## Overview

Sustainable packaging minimizes environmental impact while maintaining functionality.

---

## Material Selection

**Recyclable Materials**: Paper, cardboard, glass, aluminum.

**Biodegradable Materials**: Break down naturally.

**Compostable Materials**: Break down in composting conditions.

**Recycled Content**: Using post-consumer recycled materials.

**Renewable Materials**: Plant-based materials like bamboo, sugarcane.
