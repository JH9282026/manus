### Structuring Steps

**Numbering Conventions:**
- Number every distinct step
- Use sub-steps when needed (1a, 1b)
- Match verbal and visual numbering
- Consider on-screen step indicator

**Step Size:**
- Each step = one clear action
- Don't combine too many actions
- Don't make steps too granular
- Match viewer's cognitive load

**Transitions Between Steps:**
- Clear verbal cue ("Now, step 2...")
- Visual transition or indicator
- Brief pause for processing
- Optional recap before continuing
