### Camera Placement for Physical Tutorials

**Main Angles:**
- **Overhead/Bird's Eye**: Ideal for tabletop work (cooking, crafts)
- **Eye Level**: Natural perspective for face-to-camera
- **Side Angle**: Shows depth and procedure
- **Close-Up**: Detailed work and small objects

**Multi-Camera Setups:**
- Wide establishing shot
- Medium working shot
- Close-up detail shot
- Face for talking segments
