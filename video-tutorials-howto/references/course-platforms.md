### Course Platforms

**Udemy:**
- Marketplace model
- Platform handles marketing
- Revenue share (37-97% depending on sale source)
- High competition

**Teachable/Thinkific:**
- Hosted platform
- You own customer relationship
- Monthly platform fee
- Full pricing control

**Skillshare:**
- Subscription-based
- Paid by watch minutes
- Built-in audience
- Must attract viewers
