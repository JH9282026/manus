### Screen Recording Techniques

**Cursor Movement:**
- Move deliberately, not frantically
- Pause before clicking
- Use highlights or zoom for emphasis
- Avoid unnecessary movement

**Zoom and Pan:**
- Zoom to focus attention
- Pan smoothly between areas
- Give viewers time to orient
- Return to full view periodically

**Annotations:**
- Use arrows and shapes to highlight
- Add text callouts for emphasis
- Draw attention to key areas
- Don't overuse (clutters screen)
