### Audio Recording Tips

**Environment:**
- Quiet room (minimize outside noise)
- Soft surfaces (reduce echo)
- Consistent background
- HVAC off during recording

**Technique:**
- Consistent distance from microphone
- Proper gain levels (-12 to -6 dB peaks)
- Monitor with headphones
- Record room tone for editing

**Common Issues:**
- **Echo/reverb**: Add soft materials, record closer to mic
- **Background noise**: Noise gate in editing, better environment
- **Popping (plosives)**: Pop filter, angle microphone
- **Inconsistent levels**: Compression in post-production
