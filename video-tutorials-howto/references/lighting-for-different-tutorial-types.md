### Lighting for Different Tutorial Types

**Talking Head:**
- Soft, flattering front light
- Minimal shadows on face
- Appropriate color temperature
- Background appropriately lit

**Screen Recording (with facecam):**
- Match lighting to screen brightness
- Avoid screen reflections
- Position light to not reflect in glasses
- Consider ring light for simplicity

**Physical Demonstrations:**
- Illuminate the workspace evenly
- Minimize shadows on work area
- Additional lights for detail areas
- Avoid glare on materials

**Cooking Videos:**
- Bright, appetizing light
- Overhead light for flat work
- Front light for face
- Color temperature affects food appearance
