### Thumbnail Design

**Effective Tutorial Thumbnails:**
- Show the end result
- Include text reinforcing topic
- High contrast and readability
- Consistent style for series

**Design Elements:**
- Bold, readable text (3-5 words max)
- Relevant imagery
- Facial expression (curiosity, accomplishment)
- Brand consistency

**Technical Specs:**
- Resolution: 1280×720 (minimum)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- File size: Under 2MB
- Format: JPG or PNG
