### Title Optimization

**Title Formula:**
```
How to [Outcome] in [Timeframe/Steps] | [Platform/Tool]
```

**Examples:**
- "How to Edit Videos in Premiere Pro for Beginners (2024)"
- "How to Make Sourdough Bread | Step-by-Step Guide"
- "Excel Tutorial: 10 Formulas You Need to Know"

**SEO Considerations:**
- Include primary keyword early
- Match search intent
- Specific > generic
- Include modifiers (beginner, complete, fast, etc.)
