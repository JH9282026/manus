### Keeping Viewers Engaged

**Opening Techniques:**
- Show the end result immediately
- Promise specific value
- Establish credibility quickly
- Create curiosity

**Throughout Video:**
- Vary visual presentation
- Use B-roll and cutaways
- Include unexpected tips
- Maintain energy

**Interactive Elements:**
- Ask questions (rhetorical or comment-prompting)
- Include challenges
- Reference comments/community
- Polls and engagement features
