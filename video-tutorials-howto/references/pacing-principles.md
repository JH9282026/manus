### Pacing Principles

**General Guideline:**
- Speak more slowly than normal conversation
- Allow silence after key points
- Give viewers time to follow along
- Vary pace for interest (but don't rush)

**Pacing by Content Type:**
- **Technical/complex**: Slower, more pauses
- **Simple/familiar**: Moderate pace
- **Recap/summary**: Faster is acceptable
- **New concepts**: Extra time for processing
