### Essential Angles

**Talking Head:**
- Medium shot (chest up)
- Eye-level camera
- Slight off-center framing
- Consistent throughout video

**Demonstration Shot:**
- Shows hands/workspace
- Excludes or minimizes face
- Focus on the action
- May be overhead or front-facing

**Wide/Establishing:**
- Shows full workspace/context
- Sets the scene
- Good for transitions
- Lower importance for tutorials

**Detail/Close-Up:**
- Tight focus on specific area
- Shows fine details
- Used sparingly for emphasis
- May be macro lens for small items
