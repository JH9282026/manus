### Editing Techniques for Tutorials

**Jump Cuts:**
- Remove dead space and mistakes
- Keep pacing tight
- Common in YouTube style
- May feel less polished to some

**Cutaways:**
- Cut to B-roll while continuing audio
- Shows what you're talking about
- Covers edit points
- Adds visual interest

**Speed Changes:**
- Speed up repetitive actions
- Slow down for emphasis
- Use for time-lapse effect
- Match to narration pace

**Split Screen:**
- Show two things simultaneously
- Before/after comparisons
- Multiple perspectives
- Efficient use of time
