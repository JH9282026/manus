### Audio Editing for Tutorials

**Noise Reduction:**
- Remove background hum
- Reduce room noise
- Apply subtly (avoid artifacts)
- Use noise gate for silence

**Compression:**
- Even out volume levels
- Improve clarity
- Don't over-compress
- Voice-appropriate settings

**Music:**
- Use sparingly in tutorials
- Lower volume behind narration
- Fade in/out smoothly
- Appropriate mood
