### Screen Recording Software

**For Mac:**
- **ScreenFlow**: Professional editing and recording
- **Camtasia**: Full-featured, cross-platform
- **OBS Studio**: Free, powerful, streaming-focused
- **QuickTime**: Basic, free, built-in
- **Loom**: Quick sharing, cloud-based

**For Windows:**
- **Camtasia**: Industry standard
- **OBS Studio**: Free, open-source
- **Flashback**: Windows-specific
- **Screencast-O-Matic**: Simple, cloud-based
- **Loom**: Quick sharing

**For Both:**
- **Camtasia**: Best all-around
- **OBS Studio**: Best free option
- **Loom**: Best for quick screencasts
- **Screenpal**: Good balance of features
