### Key Metrics to Track

**Audience Metrics:**
- Views and unique viewers
- Watch time (total and per video)
- Average view duration
- Audience retention curve

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Likes, comments, shares
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Subscriber gain/loss
- End screen click rate

**Discovery Metrics:**
- Traffic sources
- Search rankings
- Suggested video performance
- External traffic
