### Scripting Approaches

**Full Script:**
- Word-for-word written content
- Read from teleprompter
- Most polished delivery
- Risk: Can sound unnatural

**Outline Script:**
- Key points and structure
- Talk points, not sentences
- More natural delivery
- Requires more skill

**Bullet Points:**
- Minimal notes
- Maximum flexibility
- Most natural feel
- Risk: Rambling or missing points
