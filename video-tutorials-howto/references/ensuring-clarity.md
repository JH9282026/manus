### Ensuring Clarity

**Verbal Clarity:**
- Use simple, direct language
- Define technical terms
- Avoid jargon unless appropriate for audience
- Repeat important information

**Visual Clarity:**
- Show exactly what you're talking about
- Zoom to relevant areas
- Minimize visual distractions
- Use annotations to highlight

**Structural Clarity:**
- Follow logical sequence
- Signal transitions clearly
- Relate parts to whole
- Summarize periodically
