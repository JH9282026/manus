# Microsoft Audience Network Targeting Options and Strategies

## Overview

Microsoft Audience Network (MSAN) provides one of the most sophisticated audience targeting ecosystems in digital advertising, combining search intent data, LinkedIn professional information, demographic signals, and customer data to deliver highly relevant ads. This comprehensive targeting capability enables advertisers to reach specific customer segments with precision and scale.

## Core Targeting Categories

### 1. Intent-Based Targeting

#### In-Market Audiences

In-market audiences consist of users actively researching and demonstrating purchase intent within specific product or service categories.

**How It Works**:
- Analyzes search queries on Bing
- Tracks clicks and browsing behavior
- Monitors page views across Microsoft services
- Identifies patterns indicating purchase readiness

**Performance Impact**:
- **42% higher conversion rate** compared to non-targeted campaigns
- Ideal for mid-to-lower funnel marketing
- Captures users at peak consideration stage

**Available Categories**:
- Automotive
- Consumer Electronics
- Financial Services
- Home & Garden
- Travel & Tourism
- And many more industry-specific segments

**Best Practices**:
- Combine with relevant ad messaging that addresses purchase intent
- Use competitive pricing or unique value propositions
- Include strong calls-to-action
- Monitor conversion rates closely to identify highest-performing segments

### 2. Professional Targeting (LinkedIn Integration)

Unique to Microsoft Advertising, LinkedIn Profile Targeting leverages professional data for B2B and career-focused campaigns.

**Targeting Dimensions**:

1. **Company Targeting**:
   - Target employees of specific companies
   - Ideal for account-based marketing (ABM)
   - Reach decision-makers at target accounts

2. **Industry Targeting**:
   - Focus on specific business sectors
   - Examples: Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing
   - Align messaging with industry-specific pain points

3. **Job Function Targeting**:
   - Reach professionals in specific roles
   - Examples: Marketing, IT, Finance, Operations, HR
   - Tailor messaging to functional responsibilities

4. **Seniority Level**:
   - Target by organizational hierarchy
   - Options: Entry-level, Manager, Director, VP, C-Suite
   - Adjust messaging complexity and value proposition accordingly

**Performance Impact**:
- **179% higher conversion rate** for LinkedIn-targeted campaigns
- Particularly effective for B2B software, services, and high-value products
- Enables precise account-based marketing strategies

**Strategic Applications**:
- Enterprise software sales
- Professional services marketing
- B2B lead generation
- Recruitment advertising
- Industry-specific product launches

### 3. Remarketing Audiences

Remarketing enables re-engagement with users who have previously interacted with your brand.

#### Standard Remarketing

**Requirements**:
- Universal Event Tracking (UET) tag implementation
- Minimum audience size thresholds
- Defined lookback windows

**Audience Types**:
- All website visitors
- Specific page visitors
- Users who completed specific actions
- Shopping cart abandoners
- Past converters

#### Dynamic Remarketing

Highly personalized remarketing based on specific products or content viewed.

**Features**:
- Displays ads for exact products users viewed
- Automatically updates based on product feed
- Personalizes creative with product images and details
- Tracks specific product IDs

**Performance Impact**:
- **164% higher conversion rate** compared to standard display
- Significantly higher relevance and engagement
- Ideal for e-commerce and large product catalogs

**Implementation Requirements**:
- Product feed integration
- UET tag with product ID tracking
- Dynamic ad templates
- Regular feed updates

#### Impression-Based Remarketing

Targets users who saw your ads but didn't click or visit your website.

**Strategic Value**:
- Sequential messaging across channels
- Brand story continuity
- Multi-touch attribution
- Awareness to conversion journey

**Use Cases**:
- Follow up awareness campaigns with conversion messaging
- Reinforce brand messages
- Address potential objections
- Provide additional information or incentives

#### Auto-Generated Remarketing Lists

For advertisers without time to build custom lists, Microsoft automatically creates:
- All website visitors list
- All converters list
- Smart remarketing segments based on behavior patterns

### 4. Customer Data Targeting

#### Customer Match

Upload customer email addresses to re-engage existing customers across MSAN.

**Performance Impact**:
- **134% higher conversion rate**
- Highly relevant to known customers
- Enables personalized messaging

**Strategic Applications**:
1. **Customer Retention**:
   - Re-engage lapsed customers
   - Promote loyalty programs
   - Share exclusive offers

2. **Upsell and Cross-Sell**:
   - Promote complementary products
   - Introduce premium tiers
   - Highlight new features or services

3. **Brand Awareness**:
   - Keep brand top-of-mind
   - Share company news and updates
   - Build long-term relationships

**Privacy and Compliance**:
- Email addresses are hashed for privacy
- Must comply with data protection regulations
- Requires proper consent and opt-in
- Minimum list size requirements apply

#### Custom Audiences

Advanced remarketing lists created using Data Management Platform (DMP) integration.

**Segmentation Criteria**:
- Purchase history and frequency
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Tenure and relationship length
- Subscriber type or membership level
- Time since last purchase
- Product category preferences
- Engagement level

**Performance Impact**:
- **102% higher conversion rate**
- Enables sophisticated customer segmentation
- Supports personalized messaging strategies

**Implementation**:
- Requires DMP integration
- Custom data mapping
- Regular audience updates
- Compliance with data policies

### 5. Expansion Targeting

#### Similar Audiences (Lookalike Audiences)

Expands reach by finding new users similar to your best customers.

**How It Works**:
- Analyzes characteristics of seed audience (remarketing lists)
- Identifies patterns and commonalities
- Finds new users with similar attributes
- Continuously refines based on performance

**Performance Impact**:
- **24% lower cost per acquisition**
- Maintains quality while expanding reach
- Discovers new customer segments

**Best Practices**:
- Use high-value customer lists as seed audiences
- Start with converters rather than all visitors
- Monitor quality metrics closely
- Adjust similarity threshold based on performance

**Seed Audience Options**:
- Past converters
- High-value customers
- Engaged users (multiple visits, long sessions)
- Specific product purchasers

#### Predictive Targeting

AI-driven targeting that identifies new audiences likely to convert.

**How It Works**:
- Analyzes ad content and landing pages
- Combines with audience intelligence signals
- Predicts conversion likelihood
- Automatically finds and targets high-potential users

**Performance Impact**:
- **46% higher conversion rate**
- Discovers audiences you might not have considered
- Reduces manual targeting work

**Best For**:
- Audience discovery
- Scaling successful campaigns
- Testing new market segments
- Complementing existing targeting strategies

### 6. Demographic Targeting

Target or exclude specific age ranges:
- 18-24
- 25-34
- 35-44
- 45-54
- 55-64
- 65+
- Unknown

**Strategic Considerations**:
- Align with product/service demographics
- Adjust messaging tone and style
- Consider life stage relevance

#### Gender Targeting

Options:
- Male
- Female
- Unknown

**Applications**:
- Gender-specific products

- Performance optimization

### 7. Geographic Targeting

#### Location Targeting

**Granularity Levels**:
- Countries
- States/Provinces
- Cities
- Metro areas
- Zip/Postal codes
- Custom radius around locations

**Location Intent Options**:
1. **People in, searching for, or viewing pages about the location** (default)
   - Broadest reach
   - Includes physical presence and interest

2. **People in the location**
   - Physical presence only
   - More precise but smaller audience

**Strategic Applications**:
- Local business promotion
- Regional campaigns
- Market-specific offers
- Event marketing
- Store locator campaigns

**Exclusions**:
- Exclude specific locations
- Exclusions always override inclusions
- Useful for avoiding unprofitable markets

## Advanced Targeting Strategies

### Combined Lists

Create sophisticated audience segments using Boolean logic:

**Operators**:
- **AND**: Users must be in all specified lists
- **OR**: Users in any of the specified lists
- **NOT**: Exclude users in specified lists

**Example Combinations**:
- (In-market for cars) AND (Income $75K+) NOT (Past converters)
- (Website visitors) AND (LinkedIn: IT professionals) NOT (Current customers)
- (Product viewers) OR (Cart abandoners)

**Benefits**:
- Highly precise targeting
- Avoid audience overlap
- Create exclusion strategies
- Sophisticated segmentation

### Layered Targeting Approach

Combine multiple targeting dimensions for maximum precision:

**Example Strategy**:
1. **Base Layer**: In-market audience (e.g., CRM software)
2. **Professional Layer**: LinkedIn targeting (e.g., Marketing Managers)
3. **Company Layer**: Target specific company sizes or industries
4. **Geographic Layer**: Focus on key markets
5. **Exclusion Layer**: Remove past converters or competitors

**Benefits**:
- Highly qualified audiences
- Improved conversion rates
- Better ROI
- Reduced wasted spend

**Caution**:
- Don't over-restrict audience size
- Ensure sufficient reach for algorithm optimization
- Monitor audience size estimates
- Balance precision with scale

## Targeting Best Practices

### 1. Start Broad, Then Refine
- Begin with broader targeting parameters
- Gather performance data
- Identify top-performing segments
- Gradually narrow focus to best performers
- Maintain sufficient audience size

### 2. Audience Size Considerations
- Ensure minimum audience sizes for delivery
- Allow algorithm room to optimize
- Balance precision with reach
- Monitor "audience too small" warnings

### 3. Testing and Optimization
- Test different audience combinations
- Run A/B tests with audience variations
- Compare performance across segments
- Allocate budget to top performers
- Continuously refine based on data

### 4. Messaging Alignment
- Tailor ad copy to audience characteristics
- Address specific pain points
- Use appropriate language and tone
- Highlight relevant benefits
- Include audience-specific CTAs

### 5. Performance Monitoring
- Track conversion rates by audience type
- Monitor cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Analyze engagement metrics
- Review audience performance reports
- Adjust targeting based on insights

### 6. Privacy and Compliance
- Respect user privacy
- Comply with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Follow platform policies
- Obtain proper consent for customer data
- Avoid prohibited targeting practices

## Conclusion

Microsoft Audience Network's targeting capabilities provide advertisers with powerful tools to reach the right audiences with relevant messages. By combining intent signals, professional data, customer information, and demographic attributes, advertisers can create highly effective campaigns that drive superior performance. The key to success lies in strategic targeting selection, continuous testing and optimization, and alignment between audience characteristics and ad messaging.
