### Poetic Documentary

**Characteristics**:
- Emphasis on mood, tone, and visual poetry
- Less concerned with argument or narrative
- Focuses on rhythmic and aesthetic qualities
- Often experimental or artistic

**Techniques**:
- Lyrical editing and pacing
- Symbolic imagery
- Emphasis on sound design and music
- Abstract or impressionistic approach

**Examples**: "Koyaanisqatsi" (1982), "Baraka" (1992), "Samsara" (2011)

**Best for**: Artistic expression, meditation on themes, visual-driven storytelling
