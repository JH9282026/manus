#### Three-Act Structure
Traditional narrative structure applies to documentaries:

**Act One (Setup)**:
- Introduce characters and world
- Establish central question or conflict
- Hook audience interest
- Set stakes and context

**Act Two (Confrontation)**:
- Characters pursue goals
- Obstacles and complications arise
- Tension builds
- Midpoint shift or revelation

**Act Three (Resolution)**:
- Climax of conflict
- Resolution (or acknowledgment of ongoing nature)
- Reflection and meaning
- Emotional closure
