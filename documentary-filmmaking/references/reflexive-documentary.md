### Reflexive Documentary

**Characteristics**:
- Draws attention to documentary construction
- Questions documentary conventions
- Makes filmmaking process visible
- Self-aware and meta-commentary

**Techniques**:
- Showing cameras, crew, editing
- Discussing documentary ethics on screen
- Breaking fourth wall conventions
- Acknowledging constructed nature of film

**Examples**: "Man with a Movie Camera" (1929), "F for Fake" (1973)

**Best for**: Films about filmmaking, media critique, experimental work
