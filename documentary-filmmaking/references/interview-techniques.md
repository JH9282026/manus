#### Interview Techniques

**Building Rapport**:
- Casual conversation before recording
- Explain process and ease concerns
- Show genuine interest
- Create safe space for openness

**Asking Questions**:
- Open-ended questions (not yes/no)
- One question at a time
- Allow silence—subjects often fill it
- Follow up on interesting responses
- Listen actively, don't just wait to ask next question

**Getting Great Answers**:
- Ask subjects to include question in answer
- Request specific stories and examples
- "Tell me about a time when..."
- Follow emotions and energy
- Don't be afraid to ask again differently
