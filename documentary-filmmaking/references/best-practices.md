## Best Practices

1. **Research deeply before shooting**: Understanding enables access
2. **Prioritize audio quality**: Clean sound is non-negotiable
3. **Build real relationships**: Authentic connection with subjects
4. **Capture abundantly**: More footage provides editing options
5. **Structure in the edit**: Let story emerge from material
6. **Honor your subjects**: Ethical filmmaking builds trust
7. **Test with audiences**: Screening feedback improves films
8. **Plan distribution early**: Audience strategy from beginning
9. **Document everything**: Rights, releases, sources
10. **Allow time for story to develop**: Patience yields authenticity
