#### Interview Best Practices

1. **Look for stories**: Anecdotes are more powerful than abstractions
2. **Embrace specificity**: Details make stories vivid
3. **Follow tangents**: Some best material comes unexpectedly
4. **Get reactions**: "How did that make you feel?"
5. **Circle back**: Return to important topics from different angles
6. **Know when to stop**: Don't exhaust subject or material
