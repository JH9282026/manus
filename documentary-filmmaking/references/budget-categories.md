### Budget Categories

| Category | Percentage | Includes |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Pre-production | 5-10% | Research, development, travel |
| Production | 40-50% | Shooting, equipment, crew, travel |
| Post-production | 30-40% | Editing, sound, music, graphics |
| Marketing/Distribution | 10-15% | Festival fees, marketing, deliverables |
| Contingency | 10% | Unexpected expenses |
