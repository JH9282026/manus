#### Camera Selection
| Type | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Cinema cameras | Maximum quality | Size, cost, complexity | Feature documentaries |
| Mirrorless | Excellent quality, portable | Limited recording time | Most documentaries |
| Camcorders | Long record time, built-in audio | Lower image quality | Events, extended shooting |
| Smartphones | Always available, discreet | Quality limitations | Run-and-gun, social content |
