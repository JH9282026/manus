## Common Mistakes

1. **Starting without a story**: Shooting before understanding narrative
2. **Neglecting audio**: Poor sound quality undermines entire film
3. **Over-interviewing**: Too much talking, not enough showing
4. **No visual strategy**: Boring, repetitive visual approach
5. **Ethics shortcuts**: Damaging trust and reputation
6. **Ignoring structure**: Interesting content without compelling arc
7. **Self-indulgence**: Filmmaker's interests over audience engagement
8. **Underestimating post-production**: Time and budget miscalculation
9. **Poor archival rights management**: Legal issues from unlicensed material
10. **No distribution plan**: Making films without audience strategy
