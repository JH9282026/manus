### Participatory Documentary

**Also known as**: Interactive documentary

**Characteristics**:
- Filmmaker actively engages with subjects
- Filmmaker presence acknowledged on screen
- Interviews and conversations central
- Documentary maker as character or guide

**Techniques**:
- On-camera presence of filmmaker
- Direct questioning and interaction
- Acknowledgment of filmmaking process
- Personal perspective explicitly shared

**Examples**: "Roger & Me" (1989), "Bowling for Columbine" (2002), "Fahrenheit 9/11" (2004)

**Best for**: Investigation, advocacy, or stories where the filmmaker's perspective is part of the narrative
